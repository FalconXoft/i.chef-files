About Us

Started almost 50 years ago by Vinnie himself, a renewal is now breathing life into a solid foundation of the Northside community. With great respect for the soul of the pizzeria, and by staying true to time-honored traditions of the trade, Vinnies, simply put, keeps it real! The service is filled with pride and integrity, the food with freshness and flavor. Our marinara sauce is marinated on simmer with a long list of hearty ingredients. The eggplant and chicken are cut, breaded and cooked, right here. Meatballs too. Our Vegan selection is expansive, creative, and includes a variety of textures and flavors. Sooo many options to choose from. And the dough…THE DOUGH…our blank canvas of creation. Pure, soft, crispy, delicious. And that’s only the beginning. We’ve raised the art and sport of pizza-making to new heights, a feat which could not have been accomplished without our loyal patrons. Whether you’re after the classic NYC slice, or want to get down with some real creative, adventurous stuff, the choice is yours.
