About Us

Ammos Estiatorio is a theme restaurant serving Greek and Mediterranean cuisine, a wide variety of menus, fresh seafood delicacies and authentic Greek products such as extra virgin olive oil, honey, strained yogurt, barrel-aged feta cheese and many more. Right in the bustle of midtown Manhattan you can unwind and relax and just imagine you are on a holiday on a remote Greek island. Rest assured we will do our best to satisfy your appetite.
