About Us

Plum is a modern neighborhood restaurant, located in Oakland’s Uptown district. The food, our interpretation of California cuisine, uses the best wild and cultivated ingredients we can find in our area. The beverage list features delicious and interesting wines, beers, cocktails and non-alcoholic drinks. The restaurant is dramatic and inviting, with reclaimed wood tables and benches, original artwork and a large chandelier with hand-blown glass shades.
