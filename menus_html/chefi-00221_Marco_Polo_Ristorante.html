Don Melchor

Since 1987, Vina Concha y Toro has been producing "Don Melchor", Chile's most internationally acclaimed Cabernet Sauvignon. Named after the founder of the winery, Don Melchor de Concha y Toro, the wine has become a symbol of the best of the land and the wine maker's ability can produce in Chile. Don Melchor Cabernet Sauvignon comes from the grapes grown in a select area of the well known vineyards of Puente Alto, located in the very heart of the Maipo Valley, an area deamed ideal for planting Cabernet Sauvignon. It's poor soil, perfect climatic conditions, and proximity to the Andes Mountains combine to produce the finest Cabernet in this region. The grapes are genlty harvested by hand. After fermentation, the wine is aged for 13 months in French oak and then another year in the bottle before release

About Us

Marco Polo is located in the heart of Carroll Gardens and is one of Brooklyn's most popular and acclaimed eateries. Marco Polo is famous for it's Northern and Southern Italian Cuisine. Daily Chef specials also top the list. Opened since 1983, Marco Polo is celebrating its 29th Anniversary! Your Proud Host Joseph Chirico invites you to join him in celebration and welcomes you to a delightful evening of fine food and live piano playing nightly. Marco Polo Ristorante's Dining Room sparkles with a beautiful fireplace, mahogany wood finishing, extensive bar and murals of Marco Polo's travels.
