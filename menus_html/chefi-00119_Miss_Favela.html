About Us

Miss Favela is a botequim, a Brazilian neighborhood bistro, located on the edge of Wiliamsburg, right next to the Williamsburg Bridge. We serve traditional Brazilian dishes, including Moqueca de Peix e Pirão, a fish stew with onions, tomatoes, green peppers, fresh cilantro and coconut milk; Carne seca come mandloca, Brazilian beef jerky with fried yucca; and Feijoada, rice and beans served with yucca and collard greens. We have most nights a DJ or live music featuring Brazilian music. Our mission is to bring an authentic Brazilian experience to Brooklyn.
