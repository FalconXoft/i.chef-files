About Us

Bistro Ten 18 is our family owned and operated American Bistro located in one of New York City's greatest neighborhoods, Morningside Heights. Our main dining room is a bustling bistro that looks onto St. John the Divine while our rear dining room is cozy and warm complete with a fireplace, perfect for a quiet evening.We are located minutes away from Barnard, Columbia University, Riverside Church, the Upper West Side and Harlem. We strive to create seasonal, simply prepared, flavorful dishes utilizing local meats, fish, dairy and produce whenever possible. Our service team are part of the family and it shows in their dedication to not only efficient service but their friendly demeanor, treating everyone like one of our "neighborhood regulars".
