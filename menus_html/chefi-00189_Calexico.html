About Us

Come visit our newest Brooklyn location at 645 Manhattan Avenue (corner of Bedford) in Greenpoint. The menu features familiar items like carne asada tacos, pulled pork burritos, and our signature rolled quesadillas. We are also debuting some new dishes like baja fish tacos, mole chicken, and some fantastic desserts. This location also has a full bar, featuring fresh margaritas, micheladas, and our very own beer made exclusively for Calexico by the brewing geniuses over at Sixpoint Craft Ales. We are open for lunch and dinner, with take out and delivery available now.
