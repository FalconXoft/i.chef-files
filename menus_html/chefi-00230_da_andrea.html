About Us

Da Andrea Restaurant has recently moved from Hudson Street to a new location on 13th street. The new location features a larger sidewalk cafe, larger dining room, with exposed brick walls and vintage hardwood floors. The kitchen is also more spacious, allowing for a more expedite service, hosting private party for lunch up to 60 people. Our pastas are still made daily on the premises and we continue to serve traditional dishes from our region, Emilia Romagna.
