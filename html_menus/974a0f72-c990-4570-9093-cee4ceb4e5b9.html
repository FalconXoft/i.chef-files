Little Owl Restaurant

about

welcome

A corner gem with a big porkchop and an even bigger heart. Celebrating almost two decades in Greenwich Village, Little Owl offers bold Mediterranean cuisine with friendly yet professional service that captures neighborhood dining in NYC.

Menus flow with the seasons featuring daily specials and Little Owl classics. Seafood from chef Joey Campanaro’s fishing trips and local varietals make appearances as well as treasured dishes from his childhood cooking alongside his grandmother.

Lunch offers an ideal background for a special occasion or a business meeting. Brunch has an upbeat vibe serving interesting twists on the classics. Our wine list offers a unique selection of full and half bottles intended to match the cuisine with experience and budget in mind.

Thanks for being a part of the Little Owl Family.

visit

We’re located at 90 Bedford St. on the corner of Grove St. in the West Village of New York City.

We’re in walking distance of our event space, Little Owl the Townhouse, as well as our sister restaurants The Mary Lane, Market Table & The Clam.

directions

via subway

1 train to Christopher St. and walk west on Grove St. until you reach Bedford St. or A, B, C, D, E, F, M trains to West 4th St & walk north on West 4th, take a left on Grove Street.

via driving

South on 7th Ave, right on Barrow St., right on Bedford St. The closest parking garage is on the corner of 7th Ave & Morton St.
