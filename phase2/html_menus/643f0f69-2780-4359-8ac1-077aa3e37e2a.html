I was waiting for this restaurant to open for over a year, imagine a Pho restaurant in the neighborhood, was afraid that it would disappoint like the previous Pho restaurant we had not too far in Huguenot. The Pho soup was great, just a suggestion if you are taking it to go, get the broth into the bowl as soon as you get it home. I let it sit for half an hour, it wasn&#39;t as hot as it should be which would help combine the soup and noodles together, nonetheless it was very good, I would suggest to restaurant a little more protein, or at least add a button to the ordering screen to add extra protein like they have for the sandwiches. The duck breast vermicelli. Unfortunately we were not fans of the duck breast itself, the vermicelli noodles with the salad and the dressing it comes with was excellent, next time will try another meat and pretty sure it will be better than the duck. Also hopefully they would allow to order half salad half vermicelli to make the dish even lighter. The summer rolls were excellent, comes with peanut sauce which was very good. Suggestion to the restaurant is to add seafood to the pho options. Overall great option.
