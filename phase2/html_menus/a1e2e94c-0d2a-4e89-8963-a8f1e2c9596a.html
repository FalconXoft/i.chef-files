Chef’s Counter

An intimate ten-seat omakase experience featuring authentic Edomae-style sushi.

$365 (tax and gratuity not included). Please allow approximately 2.5 hours for the dining experience.

We accept reservations up to 2 weeks in advance through Resy or by phone at (646) 478 8282 Tuesday through Sunday. We kindly ask that our guests arrive promptly for their reservation to ensure the full experience. The chef’s tasting will commence when all patrons for the evening have arrived. Furthermore, we are unable to accommodate customers with a gluten allergy, shellfish allergy, soy allergy, or kosher guests, due to the heavy presence of shellfish and soy on the menu. Thank you for your understanding.

Dress Code The dress code is smart casual. Please refrain from wearing shorts, hats and sandals.

Cancellation Policy $365 will be charged per person for any cancellations, party adjustments or rescheduling within 48 hours of your reservation time.

Corkage Fee Corkage is $100 per bottle with a two bottle maximum. We do not allow bottles that are in our current list. Please contact us prior to your reservation for confirmation.
