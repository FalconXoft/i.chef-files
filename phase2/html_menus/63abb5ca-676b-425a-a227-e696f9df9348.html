Our Story

AB Fish Market Restaurant offers authentic and delicious tasting Sushi & Asian cuisine in New York, NY. AB Fish Market's convenient location and affordable prices make our restaurant a natural choice for dine-in or take-out meals in the New York community. Our restaurant is known for its variety in taste and high quality fresh ingredients. Come and experience our friendly atmosphere and excellent service.
