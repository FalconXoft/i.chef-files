Explore Our Vegan Restaurant Menus in NYC

At the heart of our culinary philosophy lies a commitment to sourcing the finest and freshest plant-based ingredients, ensuring that each dish reflects the authenticity and diversity of Mexican cuisine. Our menu at Sentir, a vegan restaurant in Flatiron, NYC, is an artfully curated symphony of flavors, including tacos, ceviches, shared plates, entrees, desserts, and crafted cocktails. Everything is thoughtfully prepared for modern plant-based dining in New York City.
