Where Comfort Meets Culture on the UWS.

Elevated Flavors, Inviting Atmosphere

Haven is a vibrant yet cozy polished-casual neighborhood restaurant and bar located on the Upper West Side. Renowned for its elevated and unique preparations of familiar dishes, Haven offers a menu that delights with creativity and flavor. Chef/owner Brian Staub and his talented team are dedicated to crafting culinary experiences that tantalize the taste buds, providing a fresh take on classic comfort foods and innovative cocktails. Each dish is thoughtfully prepared to showcase high-quality ingredients and imaginative combinations that leave a lasting impression.
