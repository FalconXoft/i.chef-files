COVID-19 Update

Fidi, 36th & the Bronx are all open for socially distant indoor and outdoor dining! It’s the same outdoor space you know and love, with all of the necessary safety precautions to keep you as safe and healthy as possible during these times. Masks are required upon entry and must be worn until you are seated at your table. Please keep 6-feet away from other guests at all times – our staff will be doing the same! Contactless takeout and delivery is also available – all you have to do is click the “ ORDER NOW ” button on our website!

Please stay tuned for updates on our other locations as they will be opening in the upcoming weeks and months. We appreciate your understanding during these times. The wellbeing and safety of our guests and staff is still our absolute highest priority. Follow us on Instagram @clintonhallny for all announcements. We hope you’re staying healthy and safe and we can’t wait to see you soon!
