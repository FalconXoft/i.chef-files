Vinatería is about neighborhood, community, and bringing people together

We create vibrant yet comforting dishes rooted in the rich culinary traditions of Italy and Spain. Whether you’re stopping by for an intimate meal or a big celebration, when you walk through our doors, you’ll feel like a longtime friend, welcomed with a glass of wine and a familiar face.

Make a reservation

We are proudly Black-owned, women-owned, and rooted in Harlem since 2013

Vinatería was created by Yvette Leeper-Bueno. A lifelong New Yorker, Yvette maintains long-standing roots in Harlem- from her parent's first meeting on 122nd Street in the 1950s to the neighborhood townhouse she has shared with her husband and sons for over two decades.
