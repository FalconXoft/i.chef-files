Welcome

Seek cover from the bustling West Harlem bar scene and head downstairs to The Honey Well.


A laid back basement bar with some dope cocktails and small bites inspired by Mom’s best entertaining dishes. The Honey Well is a comfortable, low key alternative to its sister bars, Harlem Public and At the Wallace . We are currently open with indoor and outdoor drinking and dining everyday. Our outdoor garden is open weather permitting. Reservations are available through RESY.

Frequently Asked Questions

DO YOU HAVE HAPPY HOUR?
We do! Everyday from 4pm-6pm. $11 classic cocktails, $1..50 deviled eggs, $10 cocktail meatballs, $12 cajun shrimp.

IS YOUR BACKYARD HEATED?
Due to restrictions with the DOB, we are unable to provide heaters in our backyard. Please keep this in mind when booking. The backyard is open weather permitting.

HOW DO I MAKE A RESERVATION FOR GROUPS LARGER THAN 12?
Groups over 12 must fill out the party form below. We are a very small bar so large group seating is limited inside. There are options outdoors for these groups, or full buyouts for our back room. These require minimum spends. Please fill out the from below and we will get back to you.

DO YOU ALLOW WALK INS OR ARE YOU RESERVATION ONLY?
We do hold tables and barseats for walk-ins, though we love it when you make reservations as it helps us plan our flow out better.

WHAT HAPPENS IF IT RAINS? I HAVE RESERVATIONS. DO I HAVE TO PAY A CANCELLATION FEE?
If rain is in the forecast and you don’t feel comfortable with the risk, just shoot us an email to let us know you are cancelling. We will not charge you any cancellation fee. If we get rained out, you will not be charged either.

ARE DOGS ALLOWED IN YOUR BACKYARD GARDEN?
At this time, only service animals are allowed in our yard.

"I CAN’T FIND YOUR PHONE NUMBER. YOU'RE NOT ANSWERING YOUR PHONE. WHAT DO I DO?!"
No phone here. Please email us at thehoneywellnyc@gmail.com and we will get back to you asap.

HOW LATE IS YOUR KITCHEN OPEN?
Our kitchen is open until 10:45PM Sunday-Thursday and 11:45PM on Friday and Saturday..

DO YOU HAVE OTHER LOCATIONS?
We have two sister bars on the block.. "Harlem Public" and "At the Wallace" are two doors down. If there's a wait, grab a drink there!
