A New York City Destination

Home of amazing food and decadent desserts such as the world-famous Frrrozen Hot Chocolate, Serendipity 3 restaurant first opened its doors on the Upper East Side in 1954. It’s well known as an enchanting place where artists and actors of both the past and the present come for inspiration.

After 70 years, Serendipity 3 proudly opens an additional location in the heart of NYC—Times Square. This expansion reinforces the restaurant’s status as a beloved staple in New York history, offering its legendary dining experience to a broader audience.
