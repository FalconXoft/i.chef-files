Food & Drink

Our food is made fresh, daily, with love.

Our menu changes often due to the availability of the highest quality ingredients, which we insist on.

Most of our ingredients are imported directly from Italy.

Please call (347) 763-0899 for pickup and to-go orders.

All day

We proudly serve Miscela d’Oro espresso drinks and brewed coffee , available hot or iced, regular or decaf. We also have homemade hot chocolate and affogato (seasonal) , Italian gelato and a variety of Italian sparkling water, bottled spring water, juices, and sodas.

We offer a variety of freshly made pastries & baked goods , most ranging in price from $2.50-$5.

  • Croissants & Cornetto (plain or with choice of fillings including prosciutto, fontina, fresh cream, marmalade, mascarpone, Nutella)
  • Bombolone alla crema
  • Bombolone alla Nutella
  • Babà alla Panna
  • Torta al Cioccolato
  • Crostada
  • Pastiera

Afternoon & Evening

Most of our lunch/dinner entrees range in price from $10-$20.
Most of our sandwiches range in price from $7-$15.
Seafood dishes (branzino, langoustine, etc) usually range from $20-$25.

  • Bianca (Focaccia w/ mortadella)
  • Amacord (Focaccia w/ prosciutto)
  • La Grande Bellezza (mozzarella di bufala, arugula)

  • Mediterraneo (Italian tuna, arugula, sweet marinated onions, tomato)
  • Splendor (mixed mushrooms, arugula, speck & fontina cheese)
  • Paisa (Italian tuna, artichokes, extra virgin olive oil)
  • Malena (Prosciutto cotto, mozzarella di bufala, grilled zucchini, sundried tomato)
  • Sciuscia (sundried tomato, grilled zucchini, mozzarella di bufala)

  • Blow Up (Prosciutto cotto, fontina cheese, arugulaFettuccine)
  • Novecento (mortadella, artichoke, arugula, gran padano cheese)

Entrees

Some of our favorites are almost always available upon request, including:

  • Fettuccine al Porcini (egg pasta with porcini mushrooms , cream, and Parmigiano)
  • Regina Salad (Rucola/arugula, Italian beans , tomatoes, marinated sweet onions, Italian tuna, hard boiled egg )
  • Caprese di Bufala ( mozzarella di bufala, tomatoes, fresh basil , balsamic)
  • Prosciutto Bufala ( Prosciutto di Parma, mozzarella di bufala, tomatoes, fresh basi l, balsamic
  • Cannelloni (fresh pasta with spinach, mozzarella , tomato sauce & Parmigiano)
  • Fettuccini with Speck and Zucchini , parmesan cream sauce
  • Fettuccini with Speck and Carciofi (i.e. artichokes), parmesan cream sauce
  • Polpette al Sugo (beef meatballs in tomato sauce )
  • Polpette all Parmigiana (beef meatballs in a Parmegiano cream sauce )
