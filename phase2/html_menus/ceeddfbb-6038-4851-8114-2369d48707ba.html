MEZCALS

Mezcal Unión Uno | 14

Mezcal Unión Uno is a silver mezcal distilled from 2 types of agave: Espadin and Cirial. It's produced by a group of mezcal families and Master Mezcalero Pedro Hernandez in San Baltazar, Oaxaca.

Montelobos Espadín | 14

Montelobos Joven is an artisanal Mezcal made in Santiago Matatlán produced with 100% organically certified agave Espadín. This Mezcal is crafted by biologist and distiller Iván Saldaña and mezcalero Don Abel Lopez. It is made according to Saldaña’s vision and passion for agave and artisanal mezcal with respect for traditional production methods, sustainability, and the quality of life in the communities and families who have produced mezcal for generations.

Montelobos Ensamble | 20

Montelobos Ensamble is approximately 53% agave Cupreata and 35% maguey Espadin (agave Angustifolia) along with the tails from Montelobos Tobala. This mezcal is produced in Puebla using cultivated agaves.

Montelobos Tobala | 25

This mezcal has aromas of lemon, green pepper and pear. On the palate, there are notes of citrus, basil, roasted fig, macadamia, and truffle.

Montelobos Pechuga | 30

Montelobos Pechuga is made with agave Espadin. Before the third and final distillation, a turkey breast is suspended in the still and local fruits and spices are added to the distillate. Traditionally, a Pechuga mezcal like this is drunken at celebrations like weddings, quinceañeras, or other celebratory events.

Ilegal Mezcal | 15

Handcrafted in small batches by fourth-generation mezcaleros, their Joven, Reposado, and Añejo mezcals are all made with perfectly ripe, sustainable Espadín agave, double distilled in the Santiago Matatlan Valley of Oaxaca, Mexico.

Del Maguey Vida Mezcal | 15

This mezcal is distilled twice and hand-crafted. Del Maguey uses wood-fired copper stills, which bring out fruity aromas and other sweet notes such as honey and vanilla.

Del Maguey Vida de Muertos | 16

Del Maguey Vida de Muertos is a special 45% ABV edition of Vida inspired by the batches of mezcal that are distilled in autumn and shared for the celebration of Dia de los Muertos.

Del Maguey Chichicapa | 18

The Del Maguey Chichicapa has a relatively light nose, yet it is deep and sweet on the tongue with lots of citrus and a complex character. It has a long finish with developing smokiness and a hint of chocolate and mint at the end. The town of Chichicapa is dry and tropical, depending on the time of year, with banana trees, guava, and mangoes.

Del Maguey Las Milpas | 18

Del Maguey Las Milpas is produced by Anastacio Cruz Antonio and his sons Rigoberto, Abel and Pedro in the remote village of Las Milpas. Agave Espadin is roasted in an underground oven carved into a granite hillside. It is milled using a horse-drawn tahona, naturally fermented in open-air wooden fermentation vats, and twice distilled in small copper stills. Las Milpas has aromas of slate and minerals. On the palate there are notes of tropical fruit, citrus, lavender, and tarragon with a long and dry finish.

Del Maguey Minero | 18

The Del Maguey Minero from Santa Catarina Minas has a nose full of flower essence, vanilla, and figs with a burnt honey flavor and a bit of lemon. The Mezcal Minero is deep and warm, sweet all the way to the finish. The name Minero can be a bit confusing at first. It refers to a Mezcal from Santa Catarina Minas, and the name Minero is used by several brands that have production in the small town.

Del Maguey San Pablo Ameyaltepec | 31

Del Maguey San Pablo Ameyaltepec is also known as the Papalote de Puebla and is part of the Del Maguey Vino de Mezcal collection. This mezcal is made by mezcalero Aurelio Gonzalez Tobon. This is a very floral mezcal that has notes of spice, lilac and tropical fruit. The agave Tobala gives this mezcal bright citrus notes that balance with its creamy texture.

Del Maguey Tobaziche | 31

Del Maguey Tobaziche is produced by Paciano Cruz Nolasco and Marcos Cruz Mendez in San Luis Del Rio using wild maguey Tobaziche which is a rare member of the agave Karwinskii family. This mezcal is naturally fermented in open-air wooden vats. This mezcal is peaty, earthy and smoky with an underlying sweetness of citrus fruit. Del Maguey Tobaziche was released as part of a series of bottlings for La Maison du Whisky’s Cellar Book series.

The Lost Explorer Espadín 8 yr | 16

The Espadín Agave grows for 8 years before harvesting begins. A subtly sweet and herbaceous mezcal, well-balanced with hints of sweet apple, ripe fruits, and a mild smoky layer to finish.

The Lost Explorer Tobalá 10 yr | 24

The Tobalá Agave grows for 10 years before harvesting begins. An earthy mezcal expression, with hints of tobacco, cocoa, vanilla, and leather, offering a unique balance between wood aromas and umami flavours

The Lost Explorer Salmiana 12 yr | 32

Produced in Oaxaca, Mexico, Lost Explorer Salmiana is a rare mezcal bottle, made from 100% Salmiana agave, which is aged for 12 years before harvested. A sweet and spicy profile with notes of green chilli, grapefruit and fresh agave.

TEQUILAS

Casamigos Blanco | 14

Casamigos Blanco is a premium, small-batch tequila made from 100% Blue Weber agave, grown in the Jalisco Highlands of Mexico. It's known for its crisp, clean taste with notes of citrus, vanilla, and sweet agave, and a smooth finish. The tequila is unaged, giving it a clear appearance and a vibrant flavor profile.

Casamigos Reposado | 15

Casamigos Reposado is a premium tequila that is aged for seven months in American white oak barrels, resulting in a smooth and refined flavor profile with notes of caramel, vanilla, and oak, balanced by the sweetness of agave. It's known for its silky texture and clean finish, making it suitable for both sipping neat or mixing

Don Julio Blanco | 15

Don Julio Blanco Tequila is a "silver" or unaged tequila, meaning it's not aged in oak barrels after distillation. It's made from 100% blue agave, a plant native to the Jalisco region of Mexico. Don Julio Blanco is known for its crisp, clean flavor with agave notes and hints of citrus, making it a popular choice for margaritas or enjoyed neat.

Don Julio Reposado | 16

Don Julio Reposado Tequila is a tequila that has been aged in American white oak barrels for eight months. It's a golden amber color and offers a rich, smooth finish with mellow, elegant flavors and aromas. It's made from 100% blue Weber agave.

Espero Blanco | 12

Espero Blanco Tequila is a 100% agave tequila that roasts their agave in brick ovens in Juanacatlán, Jalisco.

Espolon Blanco | 14

Espolón Blanco is a 100% Blue Weber agave tequila, known for its smooth, balanced taste and clear, platinum color. It's unaged, making it the purest expression of Espolón tequila, and is double-distilled using both column and pot stills. It offers a nose of sweet agave, tropical fruit, and lemon zest with a hint of pepper, and a palate with notes of pepper, vanilla bean, and grilled pineapple,

Espolon Anejo | 15

Espolòn Añejo is a tequila aged in new American oak for 10 months, then aged for an additional 2 months in deeply charred bourbon barrels. This process gives it a rich character with notes of caramel, vanilla, dried fruit, and chocolate. It has a reddish-gold hue and a velvety mouthfeel. The aroma is a balanced mix of roasted agave and wood, with hints of dried fruits and butterscotch.

Teremana Blanco | 14

Teremana Tequila is a super premium 100% Blue Weber Agave grown in the Highlands of Jalisco Mexico. Roasted in authentic brick ovens and distilled in copper pot stills.

Teremana Reposado | 15

Teremana Tequila Reposado has notes of oak and vanilla with a smooth rich finish.

Libelula Tequila Joven | 14

Libelula is a blend of Blanco 80% and Reposado (20%). The Blanco is cooked in autoclaves and distilled in continuous column stills. The Reposado is cooked in brick ovens, distilled in copper pot stills, and aged minimum 8 months in American whiskey barrels

Corralejo Silver | 15

100% agave tequila, obtained from the double distillation of fermented musts from weber's blue agave. Corralejo Tequila has a mild flavour with a strong character.

Corralejo Reposado | 16

Made from 100% Blue Weber Agave and aged for 4 months in French, American and Encino barrels, Corralejo Reposado is light straw in color and has an aroma of sweet vanilla, honey, spice and hints of oak.

Corralejo Añejo | 17

Corralejo Anejo is double distilled and aged for twelve months in charred American oak barrels. Corralejo Anejo has a sweet and smokey flavor.

Cincoro Blanco | 26

Cincoro Blanco Tequila has Vivid scents of pineapple, salted pears, vanilla, and fresh agave. Creamy on the palate, with a whisper of earth and white pepper. Succulent, clean notes of agave with white chocolate and Key lime. Elegance defined.

Gran Centenario Plata | 15

Gran Centenario is a family owned product that has transcended time and is recognized for its exceptional quality and taste. Gran Centenario is made from the finest 100% blue agave plants grown on their estates high altitudes and submitted to a proprietary process called “Seleccion Suave”

Nosotros Blanco | 14

Nosotros blanco is the result of blending highland agaves with lowland agaves from two different regions. Highland agaves give it its sweet and citrusy introduction while lowland agaves bring an herbal and savory finish. It is full of flavor and impossibly smooth.

Nosotros Reposado | 15

This tequila features a strong vanilla aroma and a taste identified by a distinct cinnamon beginning followed by a robust vanilla and oak finish. The spirit is rested in toasted French Oak barrels for up to 11 months.

Cabo Wabo Blanco | 14

Enough spice to give a kick, but goes down so clean, you’ll want another. The double-pot distilled blanco was baked in a wood-fired adobe oven which allows for the smooth and rich texture. Enjoy anytime.

Cabo Wabo Reposado | 20

Bright slivery straw color. Creamy, nutty, toasty aromas and flavors of nougat, chocolate, roasted pineapple, and pecan with a soft, shy, dryish light body and a warming, brisk finish displaying accents of vanilla tapioca, vanilla toffee, and spiced papaya. A lovely, tingling reposado with a fine sweetness that will work well in cocktails.

Cabo Wabo Añejo | 24

Amber color invites you in. A whiff of vanilla raises an eyebrow. Hints of honey wake up your taste buds. With age comes wisdom - and more rich taste. After maturing in American oak barrels for at least 14 months, Cabo Anejo becomes even bolder with flavor. You can mix it if you want, but this old dog doesn't need to learn any new tricks. It's best enjoyed neat or on the rocks.

Milagro Select Barrel Silver | 15

Milagro Select Barrel Reserve Silver is an estate-grown silver tequila made from 100% blue agave that has been rested for thirty days in American and French oak barrels.

Milagro Select Barrel Reposado | 16

Select Barrel Reserve Reposado is an estate-grown, 100 percent blue agave tequila which is rested in both American and French oak barrels for a minimum of 3 months and a maximum of six months, yielding a smooth, complex spirit that is a perfect balance of agave and oak

Milagro Select Barrel Añejo | 17

Milagro Select Barrel Reserve Añejo tequila is aged in both American and French oak barrels for 18-24 months, making it an intriguing mix of sweetness and oak that is smooth and full-bodied.

Espanita Blanco | 15

Espanita Blanco is silky smooth, harmonious, and creamy in texture. Vibrant, fruity body with rich flavors of hazelnut spread, black peppercorn, rosemary, and thyme with intense herbaceous zesty notes of agave fibers.

Espanita Reposado | 17

Succulent and creamy with wafting scents of spiced butter, vanilla butterscotch, jasmine tea, crystallized ginger and underlying saline, zesty and slightly peppery herbal presence of freshly roasted agave.

Don Fulano Fuerte Blanco | 17

Don Fulano Blanco is the purest expression of blue agave from the highlands of Jalisco. … This Blanco showcases the rich red soil that the agaves were planted in and which gives the tequila a unique sense of origin and of terroir.

Mayenda Blanco | 17

Crafted at Casa San Nicolas, in Arandas, Jalisco by Maestro Tequilero Jesus Susunaga and his team, each bottle of this small-batch high-end tequila is made from 100% Agave Azul and produced with the aim of capturing the aromatic, caramelized agave notes that are typically lost in the production process. The result is a unique, agave-forward sipping experience with new and unexplored flavors from the agave. This ultra-premium tequila offers aromas of honey, cinnamon, and orange blossom, followed by caramelized agave notes on the palate with notes of citrus, honey, and baking spices at the finish. Mayenda Tequila Blanco is best enjoyed at room temperature, neat.

Siempre Plata | 16

Award-winning, 100% agave tequila that's handcrafted in small batches. Crystal clear in appearance. Complex aromas and flavours of spice, herb, pepper, brine/sea salt and citrus with candied, floral notes and hints of caramel and sweet fruit.

Siempre Anejo | 18

Siempre Anejo Tequila is aged for 24 months in American Oak bourbon barrels. It features flavor notes of toffee, salted caramel, chocolate, vanilla and peppercorns that lead into a long-lasting warm finish.

123 Organic Uno Blanco | 16

The unaged “Uno” uses 100% blue agave sourced from the Tequila Valley (Lowlands). The bottles are made from 100% recycled glass, with the labels printed on 100% recycled paper.

123 Organic Dos Reposado | 18

Dos Reposado tequila rests in white oak barrels for six months before its timely release. This complex spirit reveals aromatic raw and cooked agave tempered by the warmth of oak aging with spicy notes of vanilla and hint of citrus. The most versatile of the 123 Tequilas.

123 Organic Tres Anejo | 20

Aged for 18 months in white oak barrels, 123 Organic Tequila Añejo (Tres) emerges with a prominent woody character and deep golden hue. It initially sends heady notes of tannin and wood oil to the nose, yet the aromas mellow in the glass, giving way to rich caramel and chocolate elements.

Clase Azul Reposado | 42

Clase Azul Tequila Reposado is a symbol of Mexican tradition and culture. Made with slow-cooked 100% Blue Weber Agave, this ultra-premium reposado tequila is unique and incomparable. This Reposado is masterfully aged for eight months in American whiskey casks creating its unique hazelnut and vanilla flavors as well as exceptional smoothness.
