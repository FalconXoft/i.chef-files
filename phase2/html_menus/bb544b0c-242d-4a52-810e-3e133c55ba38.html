About Us

Welcome to Sathi: Friend

Sathi, a friend; is the result of a couple hardworking friends who are practicing wonderful Indian and Himalayan kitchen past few years. We believe in great a home-style taste and welcoming service. It is a less business place and more service place. We warmly welcome all indian food lovers with decent flavors and family environment. We have some dishes for northeastern indians and Nepali/Himalayan patrons as well. Customers satisfaction is our guarantee.

Wanna try Indian Foods? Then remember 'SATHI' is there for you. We serve all kinds of Indian dishes with our special flavours.
