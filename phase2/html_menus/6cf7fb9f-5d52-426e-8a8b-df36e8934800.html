Welcome

Located in Ditmas Park, George's is your landmark family eatery for over 65 years. Visit us and share our family's legacy of serving quality and delicious food to our community.

Established in 1956, George's is now under the ownership of Michael, cousin of the original owning family. The new George's opened in December 2015 and is located in the historic Ditmas Park neighborhood in Brooklyn. The restaurant has been completely renovated on the inside while maintaining its iconic facade.

George's has renewed its commitment to serving quality interpretations of classic dishes and providing the best dining experience for our customers. Come on by!
