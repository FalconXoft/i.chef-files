205 Allen St

Kisa Sikdang, known as the "driver restaurant," is a laid-back diner in Korea that has been catering to taxi drivers since the 1980s. Renowned for its flavorful and affordable meals, Kisa Sikdang offers authentic Korean cuisine that satisfies both the palate and the wallet.

"Kisa" brings the authentic taste of Korea to New York City, complete with seasonally curated banchan (side dishes) and perfected main entrée such as bulgogi, spicy pork, bibimbap, spicy squid.
