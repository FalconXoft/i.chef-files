More About Us

Established in 1980, the APPLEJACK DINER has been family owned and operated since opening day offering you a great taste of Greek hospitality! We are proudly located on Broadway, in the heart of New York City, overlooking Times Square, Central Park South, Columbus Circle and Hell’s Kitchen.

The Applejack Diner is a neighborhood icon, servicing local residents and visitors from all over the world and is often referred to as the ‘Diner to the Stars’. Our delicious dishes, friendly service, relaxing atmosphere, outdoor sidewalk cafe, and much more make Applejack Diner a must for your next dining experience.

Our full menu is offered all day along with many house specials. We welcome you 7am- 10pm 7 days a week. We look forward to seeing you soon!!!
