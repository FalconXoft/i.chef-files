Frequently asked questions

Got questions? We’ve got answers! Here are some of the most common inquiries from our guests. If you don’t find what you’re looking for, feel free to contact us directly

What are the restaurant’s opening hours?

We’re open daily from 7:00 AM to 15:00 PM. Come and enjoy our delicious dishes at any time of the day

Do you offer vegetarian options on the menu?

Yes, we have a variety of vegetarian options, including fresh salads, veggie burgers, and more. Ask our staff for details

Can I make a reservation for breakfast or lunch?

We don’t take reservations, but we welcome walk-ins. Come by and enjoy our delicious breakfast and lunch offerings

Do you offer home delivery?

Yes, we provide fast home delivery service. You can place your order online through our website or call us directly to arrange your delivery

Do you offer takeout service?

Yes, you can order any dish from our menu for takeout. Place your order in person or by phone, and we’ll have it ready for you
