Levante is a casual-cool, pizza-centric Italian restaurant and Bar

Just two blocks from historic Court Square, Levante brings artisanal Italian flavors to a vibrant, sociable, and dynamic setting. The dining room features a lively 16-seat full bar , soaring 32 ft ceilings, and a spacious backyard , creating the perfect backdrop for any occasion. For more intimate gatherings, our private dining room offers an exclusive setting to enjoy our signature dishes and drinks.
