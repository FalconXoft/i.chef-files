Our Story

South Slope Ramen Restaurant offers authentic and delicious-tasting Japanese cuisine in Brooklyn, NY. South Slope Ramen's convenient location and affordable prices make our restaurant a natural choice for eat-in or take-out meals in the Brooklyn community. Our restaurant is known for its variety in taste and high quality fresh ingredients. Come and experience our friendly atmosphere and excellent service.
