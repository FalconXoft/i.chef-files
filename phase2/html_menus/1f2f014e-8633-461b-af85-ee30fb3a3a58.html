Our story

ABOUT US

Founded in 2003, Fushimi Japanese Group has been an atelier for contemporary Japanese-fusion transformation, serving masterfully crafted Japanese dishes with innovative French tastes and techniques in a modern-meets-traditional style. Fushimi was first established in Staten Island and currently has five locations spread throughout Staten Island, Bay Ridge, Williamsburg, and our newest location, Manhattan.

Fushimi aims to produce visionary Japanese-fusion dishes, using the highest quality ingredients and state-of-the-art methods. Hand-picked, fresh ingredients from local farms are shipped weekly to each location and are artistically handled by our expert chefs. The restaurant flaunts its exceptional menu, premium service, and its trendy ambiance and nightlife, making Fushimi Staten Island, Bay Ridge, Williamsburg, and the newly opened Times Square location a MUST-GO! Highly recommended by the Zagat Survey & Michelin Guide, Fushimi welcomes customers looking for innovative developments of traditional cuisines, Japanese food lovers, or simply anyone wanting to try something new.
