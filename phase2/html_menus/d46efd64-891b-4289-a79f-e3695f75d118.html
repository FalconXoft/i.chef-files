Cecconi's Dumbo, New York

Set inside Empire Stores, between the Brooklyn and Manhattan bridges, Cecconi’s DUMBO serves lunch and dinner, as well as a weekend brunch feast. Take in the Manhattan skyline on the terrace outside while enjoying an aperitivo, or dine inside the former warehouse and choose from a selection of Northern Italian dishes inspired by the original Mayfair restaurant.
