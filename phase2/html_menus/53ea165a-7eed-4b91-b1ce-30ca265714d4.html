Dinner Prix-Fixe Menu – Levant on Smith

Enjoy a 3-course prix-fixe dinner in Brooklyn at Levant on Smith,
available Sunday through Thursday nights for just $59 per person (plus tax & gratuity).
Our French-inspired bistro menu offers classic flavors
in the heart of Cobble Hill,Carroll Gardens Brooklyn,NYC

Begin with favorites like French Onion Soup, Crispy Goat Cheese, or Sesame Tuna Tartare,
followed by entrées such as Coq au Vin, Mediterranean Seabass,
Steak Frites, Beef Bourguignon, or French Onion Spaghetti.
End your meal with Crème Brûlée, San Sebastián Cheesecake, or Gluten-Free Almond Cake.

Enhance your dinner with an optional two-drink pairing for $16 (wine, cocktails, beer, or mocktails).
While our prix-fixe is perfect for weeknights,
the full à la carte menu is always available.

Experience one of the best prix-fixe dinners in NYC, right here in Cobble Hill ,Brooklyn
