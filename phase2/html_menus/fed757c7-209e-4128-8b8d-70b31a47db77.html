OUR STORY

Usha Foods, Usha Sweets & Usha Snacks is fully owned, operated and managed by Mr. Anil Mathur & Mrs. Indira Mathur, who both continuously strive with the utmost diligence, commitment and heartfelt enthusiasm to provide the entire community with authentic high quality South Asian sweets, snacks, and full course meals.

It was word of mouth that grew Usha Foods Inc. manifold over the last 20 years, until we became what we are today: an establishment synonymous with taste, quality and consistently great service.

Today, Usha Foods is well established as a leading brand of a wide variety of sweets, snacks, fast food, and a comprehensive range of catering and shipping services all throughout the United States.
