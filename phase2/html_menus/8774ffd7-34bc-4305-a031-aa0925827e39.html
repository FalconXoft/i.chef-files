About Us

At Momo Crave all of our momos (dumplings) are meticulously made from scratch, the same way they are made in Nepal. These momos venture out from the norm, however, with new and exciting twists that feature traditional Nepalese flavors intertwined with other cultural cuisines.

Come and experience this beautiful culinary fusion in our cozy atmosphere with prices that can't be beaten. It won't take long before you realize how we got our name... Momo Crave!
