Poke Bowl Station

Our Story

Poke Bowl Restaurant was born out of a craving for something fresh, authentic, and undeniably delicious. Tired of the fast-food frenzy dominating the New York City scene, three friends came together with a shared passion for Hawaiian cuisine. We envisioned a place where people could savor the true essence of poke, crafted with the highest quality ingredients and served in a vibrant, welcoming atmosphere.
