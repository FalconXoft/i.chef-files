Miso Ramen

Curly noodles served in hearty miso chicken broth topped with charbroiled slow-cooked pork or chicken chashu, marinated egg*, corn, bean sprouts, bamboo shoot, and scallions

Shio Classic Ramen

Curly noodles served in the simplest creamy chicken broth topped with charbroiled slow-cooked pork or chicken chashu, marinated egg*, nori seaweed, beansprouts, bamboo shoot, and scallions
