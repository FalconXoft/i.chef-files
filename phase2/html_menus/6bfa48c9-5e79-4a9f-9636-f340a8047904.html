In the heart of New York City, just before the world faced unprecedented challenges, a dream was born – Dao Dim Sum. Our doors opened with excitement, welcoming diners to experience the authentic taste of Hong Kong-style dim sum. Little did we know, the journey ahead would test our resilience and passion.

As the pandemic swept across the globe, our tight-knit team faced uncertainties and hardships. However, fueled by a collective dedication to preserving the essence of our craft, we persevered. Our chef, with over 30 years of expertise, led the charge with unwavering commitment. Together, we navigated through challenges, adapting and evolving while staying true to the traditions that define our dim sum.

Now, as the world embarks on a path to recovery, we are eager to share the flavors that sustained us through those trying times. Dao Dim Sum is more than a place to dine; it’s a story of resilience, passion, and the indomitable spirit of our team.

Join us in celebrating not just the artistry of dim sum but also the triumph of our journey. As we bring these authentic flavors to your table, we invite you to savor not just the taste but the story behind every bite. Together, let’s embrace the joy and warmth that dim sum brings, a testament to the strength of community and the enduring power of shared culinary experiences.
