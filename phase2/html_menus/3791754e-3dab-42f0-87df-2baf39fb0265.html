WHITE WINES

Castello Colle Massari, Montecucco Vermentino Melacce

$14 / $53

Clean Vermentino with a fruity-fresh first sip, Aromas of Granny Smith apple, white peach and sweet limes with a hint of exotic fruits. Crispy and dry on the palate with fantastic minerality.

Tramin, Sdtirol Alto Adige Pinot Grigio

$14 / $53

This wine displays a straw color with aromas of citrus fruits, pears, apples and white flowers. The rich and velvety texture of the wine is balanced by refreshing acidity leading to a long, lingering finish.

Abbazia di Novacella, Alto Adige, Valle Isarco, Kerner

$16 / $61

A particular specialty of the Valle Isarco. Greenish to bright yellow in color. Peaches, apricots and tones of tangerines on the nose. Very lively, juicy and sleek on the palate, with a mineral-rich finish.

RED WINES

Valle Reale, Montepulciano di d'Abruzzo

$12 / $45

Valle Reale Montepulciano d'Abruzzo is a bright ruby-red in color. This youthful red offers explosive aromas of blueberries, cherries, licorice and sweet spices.

Scopetone, Rosso Di Montalcino, Sangiovese

$17 / $65

Brilliant ruby red with pale garnet tones. Typical aroma, intense and strong with hints of berries and ripe plums which face in spicy licorice, tobacco and leather.

Cottanera, Etna Rosso Barbazzale

$14 / $53

Blend: 90% Nerello Mascalese, 10% Nerello Cappuccio. On the nose, aromatic scents of raspberries, blackberries and wildflowers intertwined with mineral shades. The palate is fresh and aromatic, balanced by good tannins.

SPARKLING & ROSE

Zardetto Prosecco Brut Organic

$13 / $49

Lemon-yellow in color with elegant, refined bubbles that form a hearty white froth. Aromas of white flowers, apricot and herbs create a delicately soft bouquet.

Fiorini, Lambrusco Grasparossa di Castelvetro Becco Rosso

$13 / $49

Purplish red color. Vinous with hints of wild strawberries, geranium, raspberry; It is dry, fresh and savory, with a pleasant effervescence on the palate. Balanced tannins typical of Lambrusco, with a pleasant bitter note in the finish.

Pico Maccario, Piemonte Lavignone Rosato

$13 / $49

Pale salmon in appearance with playful aromas of wild strawberry, watermelon, cranberry, fresh-cut grass. A refreshing, but modest, backbone of acidity supports a round mouth-feel and velvety texture.
