About KimGaNae

High Quality Casual Korean Fusion Food

Here at KimGaNae located in the heart of Flushing, NY, we specialize in a wide variety of Bunsik dishes, which are Korean comfort foods loved by all ages! Some dishes we serve that you may recognize from your favorite K-dramas are kimbap, dukbokki, ramen, and many more! If you like restaurants with high-quality food set at affordable prices, KimGaNae is the perfect place to visit!

OUR CHEF & STAFF

Our chefs at KimGaNae are exceptionally skilled, with over 20 years of experience cooking in first-class restaurants. By giving their 100%, our chefs hope to deliver your palate an indescribable adventure. Our friendly and hard-working staff will ensure your utmost satisfaction. We work passionately to provide our customers a sensational experience.

OUR FOOD

In order to preserve our restaurant's high quality food, we obtain our ingredients locally, ensuring that the food you eat is as fresh as possible. At the same time, we want our food to offer a comforting and nostalgic experience. Whether you are with your friends, family, or significant other, dive into the Korean culture and create lasting memories with our food.

WE DO CATERING

We are also available to cater for private events. Whether this be for your wedding, business lunch, dinner, cocktail reception, etc., we can provide for all of them. Contact us for more details and to plan out your next special occasion!
