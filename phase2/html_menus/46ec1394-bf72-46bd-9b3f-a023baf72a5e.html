An inventive and perennially packed brunch institution, Sadelles offers quintessential classics for breakfast and lunch, including its renowned bagels. After establishing locations in New York City, Las Vegas, and Paris, Major Food Group has partnered with The Boca Raton to bring Sadelles to our iconic resort.

An outpost of the original New York destination, the menu features sliced-to-order salmon and sturgeon, chopped salads, sandwiches, decadent French toast, crispy latkes, and other New York staples, served in an updated setting that is both refined and fun.
