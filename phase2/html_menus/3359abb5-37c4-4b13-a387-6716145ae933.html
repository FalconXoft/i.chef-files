Menu

USDA Prime, dry aged steaks are the cornerstone of our menu, but the menu is varied and offers many options beyond the signature exemplary steaks.  Guests can also select from a variety of market-fresh seafood, poultry, and lamb.  Delicious side dishes complete the Smith & Wollensky meal, such as creamed spinach, hashed browns, and seasonal vegetables.  The irresistible dessert selection ends the meal on a memorable note with a variety of decadent, housemade cakes, sorbets, and ice creams.
