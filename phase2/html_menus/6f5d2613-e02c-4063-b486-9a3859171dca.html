About

Cosme is Enrique Olvera’s restaurant in New York City’s Flatiron District.

Cosme serves contemporary cuisine rooted in Mexican flavors and traditions while celebrating local and seasonal ingredients.

Our beverage program mirrors our cuisine, focusing on artisanal spirits, high quality ingredients, and sourcing inspiration from our Mexican culture.

Events

The private dining room is located towards the back of the restaurant. As a separate room, it allows guests to enjoy Cosme in an intimate setting. You can select beverages from our extensive list or choose from one of our two beverage packages.

For large parties, we offer a set menu to ensure a seamless dining experience. A family-style approach allows guests to enjoy the best of what our team has to offer. The space can accommodate up to 14 guests.
