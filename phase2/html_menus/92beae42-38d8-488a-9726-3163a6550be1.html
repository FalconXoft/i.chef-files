About Us

In 1996, Bobby Van’s Steakhouse at Park Ave opened its doors as the first Manhattan establishment under Bobby’s name. Since the opening of this Bobby Van’s Steakhouse, Bobby Van’s name has grown to represent the best steakhouse in NYC. Bobby Van’s Steakhouse’s prime location is next to Grand Central Station in the landmark Helmsley Building, and the coveted location boasts views of Park Avenue which can only be attributed to the best steakhouse in NYC. The elegant menu options at Bobby Van’s offers the most superb manner of steaks, seafood and other delicious entrees, with an outstanding bar and an unparalleled, award-winning wine list for one’s eager taste buds.
