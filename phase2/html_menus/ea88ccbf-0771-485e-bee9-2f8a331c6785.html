﻿ Our menu was inspired by classic NYC pizzeria offerings including pizza by the slice, specialty pies, hot heroes, salads and an amazing calzone.

Our dough is made with flour, water, yeast, salt and olive oil -no chemicals or sugar are added.

We take pride in making most of our food including sauces and dressings from scratch in our kitchen daily.

All sauces are made with California tomatoes, fresh vegetables and herbs. They are naturally sweet and we never add sugar.

The food we serve our customers is food that we would be happy to serve our own children.
