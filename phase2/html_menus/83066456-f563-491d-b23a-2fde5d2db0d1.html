No Menu. Just Fish.

Elias Corner is the first to bring fresh seafood to Astoria!

We are also the last to accept credit cards or join the Internet...!


Ok, the latter is admittedly less impressive, but Elias was a blueprint that could not be duplicated. Things change, and now we take credit cards and have a website with buttons, but the process remains the same. Every day is a fight to get the best catch at the best price. You can't rely on anyone, and so we have to go to everyone. The idea is rooted in Greek culture, where there was no "Greek food", there was " whatever-you-can-get " food. ¬†That makes it rather difficult to promise a fixed menu, but we do promise to provide our best fish at our best price. For now, you can check out our online portal for what we are serving.
