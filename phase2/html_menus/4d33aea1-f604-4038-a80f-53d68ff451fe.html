About Us

The Ancient Greek queen of the Mediterranean was crowned with the title of Anassa, given only to unique women with goddess-like qualities. The title, similar to a “queen,” was reserved for women who exuded rare qualities and benevolent leadership.

Like the Ancient Greek Anassa, we believe our restaurant stands out, representing all of the finest recipes, culinary traditions and ingredients from Greece and the Mediterranean – always standing out for our quality and attention to detail.
