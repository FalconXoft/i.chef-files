About Park Side Restaurant

Nestled in the heart of Corona, NY, Park Side Restaurant is a treasure trove of authentic Italian cuisine. Our passion for traditional Italian dishes shines through in every meal we serve. From our handcrafted pasta to our succulent meat and fresh seafood selections, each dish is a celebration of Italy's rich culinary heritage. We pride ourselves on creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere where every guest is treated like family. Whether you're here for a casual lunch or a special dinner, Park Side Restaurant promises an unforgettable Italian dining experience.
