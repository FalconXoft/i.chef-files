Lorenzo’s Restaurant & Bar

When you come to Lorenzo’s, you come home. “Their staff is top notch. They truly know the meaning of hospitality and always make you feel like family.” Be assured that when dining at Lorenzo’s, you will see the familiar faces of our staff and management team that have been graciously welcoming you back for more than 20 years…

Lorenzo’s features happy hour Monday to Friday in our bar area serving small bites, as well as favorites like burgers, Flatbread Pizzas and more. We showcase craft seasonal cocktails along with an extensive wine list to match every dish. Our chef’s modern curated menu offers brilliance in every bite featuring our in-season Nicotra Grown Organic Rooftop Farm produce!

Fine Dining

Lorenzo’s Restaurant & Cabaret specializes in Italian & America cuisine, open for breakfast, lunch, dinner, and a lavish Sunday Brunch! Lois & Richard Nicotra and their tenured waitstaff welcome you to take a step back in time to get a taste of elegance…
