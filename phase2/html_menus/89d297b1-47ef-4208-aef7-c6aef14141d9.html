Cozy, intimate, candle-lit, authentic Italian cuisine.

Located on the UES Caffe Buon Gusto is a purveyor of fine Italian dining

Caffe Buon Gusto is a cornerstone in the UES communities and has been recognized for its outstanding Italian cuisine, excellent service and friendly staff.

Our cozy, intimate, candle-lit, authentic Italian restaurant is known for its modern interpretation of classic dishes and its insistence on only using high quality fresh ingredients. In addition, we offer our guests the option to choose any sauce they'd like with their pasta, chicken, veal, or fresh fish- making our experience unique, fun and ever-changing.

Caffe Buon Gusto offers delicious dining, takeout and delivery to the UES in Manhattan .

Our staff is always on hand to accommodate any requests you may have.
