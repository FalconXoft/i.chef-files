Our Menu

Vegan

To highlight the freshest and healthiest dishes, our menu is entirely vegan. Ingredients are local, organic, and sustainably sourced whenever possible.

*Gluten-free injera is available upon request.

Kosher

Our kitchen is now certified Kosher!

Feel-Good

We want our customers to feel nourished not only by our food but by our vibrant community of musicians, writers and artists.
