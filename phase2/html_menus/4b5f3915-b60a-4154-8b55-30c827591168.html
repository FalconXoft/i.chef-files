The Dram Shop Bar {Gueros’ sister restaurant} - nestled in the heart of Park Slope, Brooklyn - is a cherished locally-owned sports bar and restaurant that has been delighting patrons for over 15 years. Famed for its delicious blend of classic American fare with a Tex-Mex twist, Dram Shop offers a wide variety of fresh, daily-made favorites, including the popular Dram Burger, chicken wings, tacos, sandwiches, salads, and more. With an impressive selection of beers, liquors, and cocktails, there's always something to satisfy your cravings.
