Our food halls are the home of over 16 fast-casual brands, offering diners the opportunity to enjoy selections from a variety of “ craveable ” fast-casual restaurants!

Ranging from comfort food classics such as Pulled & Chopped BBQ, & The Whole Pie Pizza Co., to Ethnic Inspired Cuisine such as BONMi Vietnamese Baguettes & Bowls, & Vindy Indian, to Veg forward offerings like VEG 23 or D.G.B, you are sure to find the perfect meal every day!

Each item served at SVK Kitchen is a personalized creation made to order, in real-time! The ingredients may look familiar, but the combinations are endless and the flavors are unique.

We have spent years experimenting and building recipes that utilize clean ingredients. Our commitment is 100% satisfaction… every guest… every time!

Taste Matters

Our Food Philosophy:

  • We select only clean simple ingredients to bring you items crafted by hand.
  • We source fresh produce from local suppliers where possible.
  • We seek out partners who share our philosophy to deliver the best “bites” to our Guests.
  • We use natural and minimally processed meats; prepared using sous vide cooking.
  • We believe that offering Vegan, Vegetarian, Gluten Free, and Paleo-friendly options is just the right thing to do.

The best part about SVK Kitchen … We offer each of the restaurants on a rotating basis , so let us know if there is something you are CRAVING – and you may just find it in our food hall soon!
