Why Choose Table 87’s Heat & Serve Pizza?

Effortless Prep

No dough tossing, no complicated steps—just heat and serve! Our pizzas arrive frozen and fully cooked, so all you need to do is pop ‘em in a toaster or oven. In just 5 minutes, you’re serving up coal-fired pizza perfection. Your team can focus on the fun stuff (like happy customers), while we handle the pizza.

Simple & Cost-Effective

Forget pricey equipment and the whole pizza-making trouble. With Table 87, you get premium pizza with zero hassle. No extra staff, no fancy ovens—just grab a pizza, heat it up, and you’re ready to go! Our Heat & Serve pizzas keep costs low, profits high, and waste at zero.

Unmatched Flavor

This isn’t just pizza; it’s authentic Brooklyn coal-fired goodness made with ingredients you can trust. Each pie is topped with fresh mozzarella cheese, real plum tomatoes, fragrant basil, and a sprinkle of Grana cheese, all baked to perfection for that taste people crave. It’s the real deal, served in minutes, and your customers will taste the difference in every bite. Get ready for some rave reviews!

Flexible Serving Options

Whatever your vibe, we’ve got you covered. Want full pizzas for lunch? Done. Need slices for happy hour? Easy. Grab-and-go for the busy crowd? No problem! Our Heat & Serve pizzas fit any menu and keep customers happy across the board. More options, more happy guests, and way less hassle.

With Table 87 Heat & Serve pizzas , you can add a premium, authentic Brooklyn pizza option to your menu—effortlessly and profitably.
