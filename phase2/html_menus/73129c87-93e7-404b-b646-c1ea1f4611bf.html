Welcome to Le Sajj , your local Lebanese restaurant in the heart of Bay Ridge , Brooklyn, where tradition and taste come together to create an unforgettable dining experience. For over 40 years our cozy eatery has pride itself in serving up the best Lebanese cuisine in South Brooklyn. Crafted with love and generations of culinary expertise, every meal at Le Sajj is a celebration of Lebanon's rich food and culture.

A Taste of Home

At Le Sajj , we craft the iconic Lebanese Sajj bread, a cherished culinary gem, on an authentic sajj - a blazing hot dome that brings this exquisite bread to life. This Markouk bread, known for its unique smoky flavor and flaky texture, is just the start of our journey into authentic Lebanese cuisine.

Beyond bread, we serve a variety of traditional dishes, from juicy grilled meats and flavorful kebabs to fresh salads and a selection of mezze, all infused with the essence of the Mediterranean. Each dish celebrates Lebanon's rich culinary heritage, offering a taste that's designed to delight and transport you to the vibrant streets of Beirut with every bite.
