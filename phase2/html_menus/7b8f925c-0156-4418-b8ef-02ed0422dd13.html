Frozen Mac & Cheese Kit

Frozen Kit - $18.00 each

These kits are available for Grab & Go in a freezer and are a great way to ensure that you always have some S'MAC handy. Each of these kits feeds 3-4 people and includes...

2 Cheeses (Cheddar, Muenster)

Bechamel (regular or Vegan)

Elbow Macaroni (regular, low-carb or gluten-free)


Baking Instructions

Remove kit from freezer and place on counter for 10 minutes.

Pre-heat oven to 400F.

Cut open the bechamel package, place bechamel in a microwave safe bowl and heat for 3 minutes until bechamel is soft.

Put aside a handful of the shredded cheese (this is to top your dish later).

Add the remaining shredded cheese and pasta to the soft bechamel and mix everything up, making sure to break up lumps.

At this point you can add anything you want to this mixture to S'MAC it up!

Some suggestions are: 

Cooked meats such as tuna, ground beef, chicken, ham, etc.
Cooked vegetables such as cauliflower, spinach, broccoli, etc.
Herbs such as cilantro, parsley, jalapenos, etc.

After mixing everything up, transfer to a baking dish and top with the shredded cheese that you had put aside earlier.

Sprinkle breadcrumbs on top if you like a crispy top.

Bake uncovered for 20 minutes.  Lastly, broil on high for 4 minutes.

ENJOY!!!

Take & Bake

Want to avoid waiting for your food to be prepared at S'MAC?

Want to take some home now but eat it later?

Then ask us to make yours Take & Bake . You can take it home cold and store it in the refrigerator until you are ready to eat it. Then, follow the steps below:


1. Remove plastic wrap from pan
2. Preheat your oven to 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes
3. Place on the center rack of the oven
4. Bake at 400 degrees F for approx:

10 minutes (Nosh)
12 minutes (Major Munch)
15 minutes (Mongo)

*If you are baking more than one pan, baking time may be longer*

Our Mac & Cheese refrigerates well and will taste just as good even after 3-4 days in your refrigerator. And if you need to store it longer, then just pop it in the freezer (but be sure to wrap it well in plastic).


It is a great way to ensure that you always have some S'MAC handy. Plus, you won't have to wait long for your food to be prepared in the restaurant since we can expedite Take & Bake orders.
