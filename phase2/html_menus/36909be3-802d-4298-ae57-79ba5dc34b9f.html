Explore Our Vegan Restaurant Menus in NYC

At Le Basque , every dish is a testament to the creativity and expertise of our chefs, who have meticulously crafted a menu that celebrates the essence of French and Spanish cuisine. As a vegan restaurant in New York City, we draw inspiration from the rich culinary traditions of both regions, using the finest plant-based ingredients to honor classic techniques while introducing modern twists. From the vibrant, bold flavors of Spain to the sophisticated nuances of French cooking, each plate is designed to provide a memorable vegan dining experience in the heart of NYC.
