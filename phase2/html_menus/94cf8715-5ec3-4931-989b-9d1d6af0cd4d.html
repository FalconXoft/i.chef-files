MENU

Welcome to Pico De Gallo, home to the best Mexican food in Manhattan and NYC. Our menu is a celebration of traditional Mexican cuisine , expertly crafted with a unique twist by our talented chefs. We pride ourselves on using fresh, quality ingredients to create dishes that will delight your palate.

From our all-day menu, featuring a variety of Mexican classics, to our specially curated brunch menu, there's something to satisfy every craving. Conveniently located near New York, NY, Pico De Gallo offers an immersive dining experience in a vibrant, Mexican pop-art setting.

Whether you're a local foodie or a visitor seeking the best Mexican food in NYC, we invite you to join us for an unforgettable culinary journey. Experience the flavors of Mexico right here in Manhattan at Pico De Gallo."
