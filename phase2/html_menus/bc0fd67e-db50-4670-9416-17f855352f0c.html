Giovanni Pizza & Grill

Welcome to Giovanni Pizza & Grill - the right place for a delicious pie in Newark, NJ! What makes us such a local favorite are our amazing food and service. So, if you're around Essex County, we joyfully invite you to stop by for a fresh and hot slice. Let's take a look at our selection of flavorsome pizzas. For a start, you can get our classic Cheese one, or even create yours with the toppings you prefer. But we also offer a number of other great options. These include our Stuffed, Pepperoni, Baked Ziti, and French Fry pie, to name a few. Just check our menu for all the details and make your pick from there. Furthermore, we got tasty appetizers, sandwiches, pasta, lasagna, seafood, and others. Every item on our menu is made with the best ingredients available, so whatever you get, you're in for a real treat. Feel free to join us for lunch or dinner at any time - we're open 7 days a week.
