LaRustica Pizza

It doesn't get much better than New York pizza, right? Well, one of the best places to grab a fresh slice in NYC is LaRustica Pizza! Located on Lexington Ave, we offer outstanding food and service to all hungry locals and tourists alike. Each pizza and dish on our menu is made with the highest quality ingredients available. Make sure to stop by or order online for pickup or delivery. Without further ado, let's take a closer look at our menu, starting with the fabulous pizza selection. Try our classic Cheese pie or even create your own with the toppings you prefer. On that note, we offer a fantastic Cauliflower Personal pie which you can customize however you want it. Indulge in our popular Grandma pizza with mozzarella, tomatoes, garlic, and basil on a thin pan-baked crust. Another favorite is our Chicken Bruschetta pie with chicken cutlet, tomatoes, olive oil, red onions, balsamic glaze, and mozzarella. Moreover, we suggest our Spicy Sausage pizza with Italian sausage, jalapeños, cherry peppers, and mozzarella. Likewise, there's our Rainbow pie with marinara, vodka, and pesto. Other great choices include our Veggie Lovers, Hell's Kitchen, and Chicken Parmigiana pizza, to name a few. But hey, we're more than just a pizza place! Start your meal with any of our irresistible appetizers and sides. We have everything from mozzarella sticks and fried calamari to garlic knots and meatball marinara. Plus, we have to mention our chicken fingers, chicken wings, and French fries. In addition, our menu includes an amazing selection of hero sandwiches. Enjoy our famous chicken ala vodka hero with chicken cutlets, vodka sauce, and melted mozzarella. Also, you can try our chicken parmigiana, grilled chicken Caesar, and fried shrimp heroes, among others. Refresh your taste buds with one of our delicious salads. Whether it's our House, Greek, or Caprese salads, you cannot go wrong with any. Furthermore, we offer a variety of homemade and baked pasta dishes. These include our meat lasagna, cheese ravioli, pasta carbonara, baked ziti, and more. On top of that, we have a great selection of dinner options. Order our eggplant rollatini, veal parmigiana, chicken piccata, or shrimp scampi, to give a few suggestions. Last but definitely not least, our menu features a number of delicious desserts. Indulge in our amazing Red Velvet cake made with real cream cheese. It's layers of creamy deliciousness. We also recommend our molten lava cake, tiramisu, zeppoles, and more. Feel free to join us for lunch or dinner anytime or order online for pickup or delivery if it's more convenient. See you!
