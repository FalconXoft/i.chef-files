Menus

You are cordially invited to join us for an afternoon or evening of relaxed elegance and exceptional cuisine. La Strada , a culinary gem located in the heart of Staten Island, has been providing temptingly delicious Italian cuisine for 30 years.  Influenced by the seasons and the freshest produce and ingredients, our talented chef/owner continuously strives to innovate and create beyond the conventional. The classics that you crave deftly share the menu with delicious contemporary selections. From our pasta and specialties to our delicious chicken, beef and veal dishes, there is something certain to please all of our guests.  As time marches on, we proudly continue to garner top marks for food and service and remain a local favorite year after year with our loyal customers.  We thank you and look forward to serving you soon!
