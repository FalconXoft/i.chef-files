Honey Specialty Drinks

Discover our Honey Specialty Drinks , a handcrafted lineup of café favorites infused with pure honey for a naturally smooth sweetness. Each drink is designed to balance rich espresso with the unique flavors of honey and carefully chosen ingredients.

Drinks in this category:

  • Honey Macchiato – A smooth blend of vanilla, honey, milk, and bold espresso, creating the perfect harmony of sweet and strong.

  • Yemeni Honey Latte – Espresso swirled with steamed milk, honey, cinnamon, and cardamom for a warm, spiced indulgence inspired by traditional Yemeni flavors.

  • Pistachio Honey Latte – A creamy fusion of espresso and pistachio cream, lightly sweetened with honey for a nutty, velvety finish.
