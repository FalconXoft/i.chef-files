The intriguing flavors and casual simplicity of the eastern Mediterranean combine at Pera Soho, culminating menus that are as inspired and varied as the region itself. We follow the tides of the market with seasonal ingredients guiding our traditional and original dishes and cocktails. Our culinary team, immersed in the history and gastronomy of Istanbul and the Mediterranean, has bridged culinary cultures to bring an adventurous journey through the eastern Mediterranean food ways.
