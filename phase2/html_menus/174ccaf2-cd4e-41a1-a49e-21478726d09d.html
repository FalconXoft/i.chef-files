    PANTRY

    • Pomegranate Molasses

      vegan

    • Extra Virgin Olive Oil

      vegan

    • Za’atar

      A Lebanese classic. Thyme, sumac, and toasted sesame seeds bring an aromatic touch to dips, marinades or rubs. Try a dash over NAYA’s labne, or combined with olive oil for dipping pita bread.

      vegan

      tree nut, wheat
