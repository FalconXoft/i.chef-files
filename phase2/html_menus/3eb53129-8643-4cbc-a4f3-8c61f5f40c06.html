Introducing the menu at Parrilladas Sunrise, a Tex-Mex restaurant located in Jackson Heights, NY. This vibrant spot offers a variety of dishes, from traditional Mexican favorites to unique blends of American flavors. The menu features appetizers like ceviche and empanadas, as well as entrees like grilled chicken with rice and beans. The bar serves up frozen margaritas, mojitos, Mexican Micheladas, and more, making it a perfect spot for a fun night out. Customers rave about the flavorful food, great drinks, and lively atmosphere. With options for vegetarians and a welcoming staff, Parrilladas Sunrise is a gem for those looking for delicious Mexican cuisine and entertainment. So gather your friends, enjoy a meal, sip on some drinks, and maybe even sing some karaoke at this lively spot in Jackson Heights.

Parrilladas Sunrise

Welcome to Parrilladas Sunrise, a vibrant Tex-Mex restaurant located in Jackson Heights, NY. Indulge in delicious grilled food, traditional Mexican dishes, and refreshing drinks while enjoying karaoke nights. With great prices, vegetarian options, and attentive staff, this hidden gem promises an authentic and enjoyable dining experience.
