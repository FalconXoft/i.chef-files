About Bahn Pho

At Bahn Pho , we are passionate about bringing the rich, vibrant flavors of Vietnam to your table. Nestled in the heart of Hoboken, our restaurant combines traditional Vietnamese cuisine with contemporary design, creating a sophisticated yet comfortable space to enjoy authentic dishes. We prepare our dishes with slow-simmered broths made from premium cuts of meat, and a medley of fresh herbs and spices that bring out the essence of Vietnamese comfort food.

Whether you're indulging in our aromatic broths or exploring our other menu offerings, each dish is crafted with the finest ingredients and attention to detail.
