Reservations

Reserve your table on Resy ! For guaranteed Peking Duck reservations , please see below. We cannot accommodate Peking duck requests left via Resy; please email us below.

CANCELLATION FEES There is a $20 cancellation fee per guest for reservations cancelled after 12pm the day of the reservation. 100% of the fees are donated to City Harvest.

LARGE PARTIES For parties larger than 6 guests, please email our events team: events@pinchchinese.com

SPECIAL ACCOMMODATIONS Please let us know if you will require a high chair as we are unable to accommodate full-size strollers in our dining room. Should you require space for a wheelchair or scooter, or your service animal, please let us know while making your reservation. We do not allow pets or emotional service animals indoors.

PEKING DUCK Please allow 72 hours notice to reserve a Peking Duck for your meal. Email us here to guarantee your duck reservation: nihao@pinchchinese.com

Wines + Corkage

Pinch Chinese is recommended by Star Wine List , the guide to great wine bars and restaurants in New York City. The most current version of our wine list can be accessed here .

We allow corkage of wines not currently offered on our wine list for $45 per 750mL portion for the first two portions, and $100 for every 750mL portion thereafter. We do not allow corkage of wines currently on our menu, regardless of vintage.

Allergies + Menu Accommodations

Please let us know if you have any allergies or severe dietary restrictions, and we will work with you to ensure an enjoyable experience at Pinch Chinese.

We have several temperance options for those who abstain from the consumption of alcohol.

Outside food or drink is not permitted in our restaurant.
