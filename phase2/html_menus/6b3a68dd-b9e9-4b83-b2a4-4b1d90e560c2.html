How we started

Rumba Cubana’s founders Alan & Nairelys Lopez, yearned to recreate and share the lost flavor of home. The business plan was straightforward: provide the community with high-quality food, large portions, incomparable service, and an exceptional atmosphere at an unbeatable price. Hence, the first Rumba Cubana opened its doors in June of 2009 on 75th & Broadway in North Bergen, New Jersey. Years later, it was relocated to a beautiful building overlooking New York City right on Boulevard East in Guttenberg, New Jersey.
Given the increased demand for authentic cuisine combined with exceptional service, Rumba Cubana opened additional New Jersey-based restaurants in North Bergen on Tonnele Avenue, Clifton, and Jersey City. We are very excited to announce our fifth location opening soon in Rochelle Park.
“We are truly grateful for the community’s embrace of Rumba Cubana. We are looking forward to serving you in the many years to come, and we cannot thank you enough for all your support.”
Eternamente agradecidos,
Alan & Nairelys
