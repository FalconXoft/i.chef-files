Servicing Manhattan’s bustling Financial District, Hole In The Wall Cliff Street is a culinary oasis in an area that’s typically filled with fast food. Offering an ever-changing menu that’s influenced by in-season ingredients, the food at Cliff St extends far beyond your average avocado toast. It’s the perfect location to conduct a power lunch, to enjoy a weekend brunch or even host an event for a mates birthday - our friendly staff will take care of you no matter what your needs are.
