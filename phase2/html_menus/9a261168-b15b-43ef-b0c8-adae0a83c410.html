    About

    Pappardella restaurant is a long-standing fixture in New York City’s upper west side since opening in 1983. Praised by The New York Times, equally applauded by Eater and crowned as an “inviting neighborhood joint" by Zagat, Pappardella offers authentic Italian cuisine with timeless energy. The family-owned and operated restaurant has built a large and loyal following of fans, offering an exceptional weekend brunch, 365-days of lunch and dinner service and a rotation of daily specials ranging from fresh pasta and pizzas to fishes and meats. Located steps from Central Park, the Museum of Natural History and the Beacon Theater, Pappardella is a favorite for in-the-know Upper West Siders, epicureans and tourists alike. Enjoy a charming Tuscan-inspired atmosphere or dine al freso with heated outdoor seating.

    Gluten Free Options

    Pappardella is happy to provide our guests with a separate glutenfree menu, to enjoy a diverse selection of pasta, pizza, sandwiches and all kinds of other dishes that gluten-intolerant guests otherwise would not be able to order. What started as a service for our neighbor regulars has grown since into an integrated part of our menu. Our staff is trained and diligent in providing a gluten-free meal as best as possible in a kitchen that cooks both gluten and gluten-free food.

    Outdoor Terrace

    A beautiful outdoor terrace makes for great people-watching in the warmer months. We are conveniently located near the Beacon Theater, the Museum of Natural History and one block from Central Park. We feature weekend brunch, lunch and dinner prix fixe menus and a busy happy hour.
