The Cabin NYC

Located in NYCs legendary East Village community, Alphabet CityDecked out with wood paneled walls, infinity mirrors reflecting birch trees, and leather banquettes lining the perimeter of the venue, The Cabin NYC is a cozy, rustic and comfortable cottage-like space with a full-service bar serving delicious and unique specialty cocktails. And rather than offering individual starters and main courses, The Cabin NYC serves mouthwatering American Classic dishes with their creative twist and are created for sharing. Come experience the #CabinLife!
