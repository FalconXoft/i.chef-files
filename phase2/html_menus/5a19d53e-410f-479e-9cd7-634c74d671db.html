gertrude's is a neighborhood bar & restaurant, inspired by our shared love for iconic New York restaurants, Jew-ish cuisine, and the vibrant atmosphere found in European bistros.

It's a place for everybody and for every occasion from family dinner parties and big brunch celebrations to date nights and solo noshes at the bar.

gertrude's is the second restaurant in the RAD Restaurants family. For this one, husband and wife duo Nate Adler and Rachel Jackson are partnering up with Chef Eli Sussman (owner of Samesa in 30 Rock, previously the Executive Chef of Mile End) to create their perfect neighborhood spot.
