Welcome

Welcome to Hylan Diner. We are located in the New Dorp neighborhood of Staten Island at 2561 Hylan Boulevard, Staten Island, NY 10306. We have been serving delicious food to our community for many years and look forward to many more. Please stop by for breakfast, lunch, or dinner or you can order online and eat at the comfort of your home. We hope to see you all soon.

Fresh Ingredients

Everything is prepared fresh daily using only the finest ingredients. We thrive on serving only the best and most delicious food to all of our customers. Our mission is to always deliver the best food and service.

Our Menu

Our extensive menu has something delicious for all of our guests. From our delicious appetizers, to soups, salads, burgers, steaks, seafood and much much more. We have worked hard to ensure that there is always something for everyone to enjoy, even the picky eaters. We hope to see you all soon!
