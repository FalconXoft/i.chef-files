FAQ

1. What is the maximum capacity for private events?

For our Manhattan , Upper East Side venue:
– A single private room can accommodate up to 25 guests for more intimate gatherings or small corporate events;
– The combined Garden and Middle rooms can host up to 50 guests;
– The Main room can accommodate up 40 guests.

If you’re considering a party above 50 guests at Manhattan location, we offer the entire venue consisting of 3 rooms, with no additional fee. Feel free to reach out to us with the number of guests you’re interested in, and we’ll provide you with the necessary information.

Don’t hesitate to contact us at admin@odahouse.com to make a reservation or if you have any questions.

2. Do you have a set menu for private events or can it be customized?

While we do offer a set menu options, we are more than happy to work with you to customize it according to your preferences.

3. Do you offer decoration services for private events?

Yes, we provide decoration services to make your event special. Our team is here to help with your vision.

4. Is it possible to organize a buffet style celebration? What are possible add-ons?

Certainly! We are pleased to offer buffet style celebrations.

Additionally, we can provide options like an extended open bar and stationary appetizer and dessert stations. We pride ourselves on flexibility and are ready to discuss all your preferences to ensure your event aligns perfectly with your vision.

5. Can you accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies for my guests?

Absolutely. We prioritize the dietary needs and safety of your guests. Just let us know about any restrictions or allergies ahead of time, and we’ll guide you to menu items that cater to those specific needs.

6. What are the prices and menu options for private events?

We offer a range of menu options tailored to different budgets and preferences. Please contact our event manager at admin@odahouse.com for detailed pricing and menu packages.
