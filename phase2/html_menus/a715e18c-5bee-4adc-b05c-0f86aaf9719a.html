Frequently Asked Questions

How can I make a reservation?

You can make an online reservation through our websites or by calling us directly.

  • Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu: Reservations accepted throughout the day.
  • Fri, Sat, Sun: Reservations accepted between 11am - 5pm only.

*Walk-ins are always welcomed!

*No Same day reservations allowed.*
*Minimum party number of 6*

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept cash and major credit cards
(Visa, Mastercard, American Express).

Does the restaurant offer delivery service?

Yes, we offer delivery service through Uber Eats & Grubhub platform.

Can I cancel or modify a reservation? Are there any fees associated with it?

If you need to cancel or modify a reservation, please contact us as soon as possible. Fees may apply depending on the cancellation or modification. For more information, please refer to our Cancellation Policy page.

How can I make a reservation for a large group?

If you're interested in making a reservation for a large group, please call us or inquire through our Group Reservation Inquiry page. We'll be happy to assist with any additional information or special requests.

Is there parking available near the restaurant?

If you have any questions not addressed in the FAQ, please don't hesitate to contact us. We'll do our best to assist you as soon as possible.
