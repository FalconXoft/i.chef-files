A SOUTHERN THAI AND SEAFOOD
BACK STORY

Chef Nate Limwong was born in Suratthani a culturally rich in Southern Thai cuisine and also known for its fresh seafood. She has been in the forefront of the culinary art world for decades. Having worked in three different continents (Asia, Australia, and North America), She has learned and mastered her skills in a way like no other. She has also mastered her skills in different cuisines such as French and Chinese. Coming to these days, She would love to return and celebrate her root that is Southern Thai cuisine where she grew up loving and cooking. Having lived in NYC for less than a decade, She had played the head chef of the success behind some of the famous Thai restaurants in NYC. Returning back and celebrating her root of Southern Thai cuisine would mean elevating southern local Thai tastes to the world-class NYC culinary platform. She would like to pioneer and dedicate Chalong as the first Southern Thai focus restaurant in NYC. Anyone who has been to the South of Thailand and has tasted the real freshness of spices and fresh seafood will reminisce their wonderful memory of Thailand.

"LONG DTAI"

COCKTAIL SERIE

(Taking the trip South)

Chalong’s Long Dtai cocktail series was inspired by a trip to the South of Thailand. Chef Nate Limwong, Surat Thani native, growing up in Thailand and surrounded herself with various exotic tropical fruits and ingredients, she has learned to skillfully and masterfully pair the flavors of the food with the drink. Together with Cici Pratamon; Chalong’s beverage director, they beautifully created the Chalong’s Long Dtai. Chalong’s Long Dtai cocktail series is not only just cocktails but it is also a story telling in itself. The names and ingredients of the cocktails were inspired by stories and tales of the South of Thailand; the pirates of Tarutao (Satun), the pearl of the Andaman (Phuket), the first evergrown rambutan tree in Thailand (Surat Thani), one of the best dive sites in the world Similan (Phang Nga), the bay made famous by Leonardo DiCaprio movie”The Beach” (Krabi) and more. At Chalong we believe in elevating your dining experience with authentic taste of Southern Thai culinary, extraordinary drinks and ethereal atmosphere. Chalong: Let Us Celebrate with You.
