About

Manouche Mediterranean & Lebanese Cuisine focuses its menu on traditional Mediterannean & Lebanese offerings, ranging from Hummus, Baba Ghanouj, Labneh, Shmandar to Shish Tawook, Kafta Kabob, Beef and Lamb Gyro and Chicken Shawarma.
If you try us for the first time, you get hooked because we have quality along with fair prices for our portions.
Looking for Mediterannean food in Bloomfield? Look no further. Manouche & Co is the place to visit to eat authentic Mediterannean & Lebanese food.
