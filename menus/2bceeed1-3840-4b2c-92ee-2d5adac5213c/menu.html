FAQ

Is PLANTA Queen Nomad a vegan restaurant?

Yes, PLANTA Queen in Nomad is a 100% plant-based vegan restaurant offering innovative Asian-inspired cuisine.

What makes PLANTA Queen Nomad a top plant-based restaurant?

Our New York City location features a creative plant-based menu with standout dishes like vegan sushi, nigiri, handrolls, handhelds, pasta, and seasonal bowls—all served in a vibrant, sustainable setting.

Does PLANTA Queen Nomad offer gluten-free vegan options?

Yes, we offer many gluten-free and vegan options at our New York City location, including gluten-free sushi rolls and 'Chilled & Raw' items. Ask your server for guidance.

What are the most popular dishes at PLANTA Queen Nomad?

Guest favorites include our Bang Bang Broccoli, Udon Noodles, Spicy Tuna Roll, PLANTA Burger, Unagi Eggplant Nigiri, and Beef & Broccoli. 

What plant-based desserts are on the menu?

Our menu features delicious plant-based desserts perfect for anyone seeking vegan and dairy-free sweet treats. You can enjoy our Matcha Cheesecake, which is gluten-free and topped with a tangy berry compote and creamy coconut whip. Another favorite is our Banana Pudding, made with rich banana custard, coconut caramel, dairy-free whipped cream, vanilla cake, and chocolate shavings.

Where in PLANTA Queen Nomad located?

We’re at 15 W 27th St, New York, NY 10001. Our cross streets are W 27th St and 5th Ave.

Where can I park when visiting PLANTA Queen Nomad?

The closest parking garage is located at 224 W 25th St, between 7th and 8th Avenue. Valet parking is also available.

Can non-vegans enjoy PLANTA Queen Nomad?

Absolutely. PLANTA Queen Nomad is designed for everyone—from committed vegans to curious food lovers. Our menu focuses on bold flavors, creative textures, and beautiful presentation that appeals to plant-based and non-plant-based guests alike. Many first-time visitors are surprised by how satisfying and delicious plant-based dining can be—even without traditional meat or dairy.

Does PLANTA Queen Nomad have good vegan food for date night?

Yes! PLANTA is one of the best spots for a vegan date night in New York City. With moody lighting, beautifully designed interiors, and an elevated plant-based menu that includes dishes like truffle sushi, dumplings, and handcrafted cocktails, PLANTA Queen offers a vibe that’s both intimate and impressive—perfect for a night out.

What vegan options are kid-friendly at PLANTA Queen Nomad?

PLANTA Queen Nomad offers a dedicated kids’ menu designed to make plant-based dining fun and approachable for younger guests. With dishes that are simple, flavorful, and 100% vegan, even picky eaters will find something to love. Kid-friendly favorites include the Avocado Cucumber Roll, Fried Rice, Udon Noodles, and Watermelon Juice—all crafted with the same quality and care as our full menu.

Is there a happy hour at PLANTA Queen Nomad?

Yes! PLANTA Queen offers one of the best happy hours in New York City—plus a late-night version. Join us for Happy Hour featuring specialty cocktails, martinis, and spritzes, vegan sushi, and shareable bites at low prices. Can’t make it early? Our Late-Night Happy Hour brings the same energy after dark. Whether you're meeting up after work or keeping the night going, PLANTA Queen is your go-to happy hour destination in New York City.
