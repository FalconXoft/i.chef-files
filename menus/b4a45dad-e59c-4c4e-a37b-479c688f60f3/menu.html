In Tagalog, Naks is an exclamation of delight and admirationsomething you say when youre impressed, moved, or just feeling the moment. Here, its all about good food, good vibes, and the unmistakable energy of the East Village. 

Naks by Unapologetic Foods is a love letter to Filipino comfort food, shaped by the personal memories of Chef Eric Valdez. 

We hope you enjoy it.
