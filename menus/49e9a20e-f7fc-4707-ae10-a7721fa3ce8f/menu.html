COCKTAILS, PIZZA & THE HITS

Welcome to Uva Next Door, the playful little sister to the original Uva - no, we are not the same!

Uva Next Door presents a cocktail-forward dining experience - specifically leaning into the creative niche of Aperitivi, Amari & Digestivi. Our food is still rooted in quality Italian classics, but with a more southern style and seasonal drive, as well as offering FANTASTIC Amalfi style pizza! We are super Veg & Vegan friendly, and make gluten-free pasta in house every day. Our vibe is eclectic nostalgia meets the new roaring 20s, our energy is high, and our cocktails swing! 

Yes, we do have a truffled gnocchi but we do it the Uva Next Door way!
