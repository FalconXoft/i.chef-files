Welcome to Parrilladas Sunrise

"Parrilladas Sunrise" in Jackson Heights, NY, is a Tex-Mex gem offering a diverse menu with a Mexican twist. With a casual atmosphere and options for delivery, takeout, and catering, it's a versatile spot for groups and families. Customers rave about the flavorful dishes like grilled meats, enchiladas, and tacos, along with the impressive array of drinks, including top-notch margaritas and sangria. The highlight of the experience is their lively karaoke nights, where guests can enjoy delicious food while showcasing their singing talents. The friendly staff and accommodating service make every visit to "Parrilladas Sunrise" a memorable and authentic dining experience.
