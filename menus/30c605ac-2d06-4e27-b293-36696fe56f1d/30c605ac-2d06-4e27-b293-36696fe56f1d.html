Large Parties

Groups of 7 to 16 guests can reserve our communal table in the back room of the restaurant for a set Chef's Tasting Menu, served family-style. Drinks can be enjoyed on consumption, or as part of optional beverage packages.

    • Small Group Set Menus

    Chef's Tasting Menus for groups of four to six guests are now available! Offered Mondays through Wednesdays by reservation only, these curated four course menus are served family-style and designed to make your evening extra special. There is limited availability each night, so book early via an email to hello@pdfnyc.com to secure your spot!

    Buyouts

    Backroom Buyout

    The back room at Place des Fêtes is ideal for birthday parties, engagement dinners, or festive family and friend gatherings. Events in the back room are considering a partial restaurant buyout. We offer a seated dinner package and a standing cocktail reception package.

    A seated dinner can accommodate up to 30 guests. The evening begins with a 30 minute cocktail reception followed by a Chef's Tasting family style dinner with a variety of beverage packages to choose from.

    A standing cocktail reception in the back room is perfect for groups up to 40. The evening includes select passed canapés and unlimited beer, wine and cider. Add ons such as charcuterie and cheese, a raw bar, and unlimited cocktails are available upon request.


    Full Restaurant Buyout

    A full restaurant buyout at Place des Fêtes is a wonderful option for weddings, birthday/holiday parties, or celebrations of any kind. We offer a seated dinner package and a standing cocktail reception package.

    For a seated dinner up to 50 guests, the evening begins with an hour long cocktail reception, featuring a selection of passed canapés and a beverage package of your choosing. Following the cocktail hour, dinner is served, featuring a family style Chef's Tasting Menu.

    A standing cocktail reception buyout can accommodate up to 100 guests and includes passed canapés and a beverage package of your choosing. Add ons such as charcuterie and cheese, a raw bar, and premium canapés are available upon request.

    • Private Dining

    L'Escala is the private dining room at Place des Fêtes. A unique space suited for intimate celebrations, holiday parties, baby showers, or even just a private dinner with friends.

    The PDF PDR is able to accommodate up to 18 guests for a seated dinner and up to 25 guests for a cocktail reception. 

    A seated dinner would consist of a standing cocktail reception for guests to mingle and settle in followed by a seated Chef's Tasting Menu dinner shared family style. 

    A cocktail reception includes passed canapés, a charcuterie station, and a beverage package of your choosing. Add ons such as a raw bar, and premium canapes or wines are available upon request.
