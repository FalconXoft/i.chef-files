    Authentic Mexican Cuisine

    Tacolmos Mexican Restaurant, owned by Chef Ulises Olmos, offers a taste of authentic Mexican flavors. As a native of Veracruz, Mexico, Olmos grew up learning from his mother's expertise in using local ingredients and crafting complex spice blends. After honing his skills at renowned establishments like Daniel Boulud Cafe, Olmos now brings his passion for Mexican cuisine to Tacolmos, offering diners an unparalleled culinary experience.
