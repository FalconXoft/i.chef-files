Nestled in the vibrant community of Flushing, New York, Panorama Restaurant stands as a culinary beacon for those seeking authentic Ecuadorian and Colombian cuisine. Located at 8710 Astoria Boulevard, this beloved establishment has carved out a distinctive place in the hearts of locals and visitors alike, offering a genuine taste of South American tradition in every dish.
The restaurant's foundation rests firmly on family values and cultural authenticity. Panorama takes pride in its family-oriented atmosphere, creating a welcoming space where generations can gather around tables laden with traditional delicacies. From the moment guests step through the doors, they encounter an environment that feels less like a typical restaurant and more like an extension of home—a place where community, conversation, and exceptional food intertwine seamlessly.
Panorama's menu reads like a love letter to Ecuadorian culinary heritage. Diners can savor classic dishes such as empanadas bursting with savory fillings, seco de chivo that melts in the mouth, and fresh ceviche that captures the essence of coastal Ecuador. The kitchen also celebrates Colombian flavors with equal passion, serving beloved staples including arepas, bandeja paisa, churrasco, and the signature picada de carne that keeps customers returning time and again.

Understanding the diverse needs of its clientele, Panorama offers flexible dining options including dine-in service, takeout, and delivery. Operating Monday through Friday from 10 AM to 10 PM, the restaurant ensures that whether customers have time for a leisurely meal or need authentic flavors on the go, their cravings can be satisfied. The establishment also features a private meeting room available for rental, perfect for family celebrations or special events where guests can enjoy fine catering in an intimate setting.
