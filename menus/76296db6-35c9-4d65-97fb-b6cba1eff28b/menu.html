Our Story

At Phở Hoàng, we pride ourselves on being a nostalgic and authentic mom-and-pop shop with decades of experience in the heart of downtown Flushing, New York. Our family is ethnically from Vietnam, Malaysia, and Canton.

We are known for our traditional Southeast Asian cuisine, combining these flavors to create a dining experience with a uniquely large selection of homemade dishes. Come for the phở, but stay for our crispy duck!

OUR WAY OF LIFE

Community Focus

Since 1997, nothing has changed about our goal to create and build a community for our guests and staff. We’re passionate about family and loyalty, aiming to offer a taste of these values in our food.

Sense of Belonging

We’ve been family-run for over 30 years. Our staff and regulars are a second family to us and we love it when guests create fond memories with us. Our hearts are warm when guests, who have cherished our dishes since their youth, start introducing their own families to the flavors they've grown up loving.

Fresh Produce

  • Our commitment to excellence extends to our choice of suppliers, with whom we share a warm and longstanding relationship. This includes our cherished roast duck supplier, who consistently delivers USDA Grade A, all-natural whole ducks.
