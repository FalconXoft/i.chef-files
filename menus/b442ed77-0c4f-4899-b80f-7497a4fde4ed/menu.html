About

Our chefs specialize in authentic and delicious food. The staff at Times Square Diner & Grill is devoted to serving customers mouthwatering dishes that feature the highest quality ingredients available. Times Square Diner & Grill uses only the freshest ingredients around, ensuring that every dish is of the highest possible quality. Not sure what you're in the mood for?
