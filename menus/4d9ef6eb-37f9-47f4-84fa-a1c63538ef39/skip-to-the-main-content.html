About Us

Welcome to Antojos Colombianos Bakery & Restaurant! Located in Harrison, NJ, we are proud to serve authentic Colombian cuisine and delightful bakery treats. From savory dishes like Bandeja Paisa to sweet delights like Pan De Bono, our menu is filled with traditional flavors that will transport you straight to Colombia.

Discover the Rich Flavors of Colombia at Antojos Colombianos Restaurant & Bakery

At Antojos Colombianos, we pride ourselves on bringing the vibrant taste of Colombia to Harrison. Our diverse menu features classic dishes like Empanadas De Carne and Bandeja Paisa alongside freshly baked Pan De Bono. Step into our cozy restaurant and indulge in an unforgettable culinary journey through Colombia.
