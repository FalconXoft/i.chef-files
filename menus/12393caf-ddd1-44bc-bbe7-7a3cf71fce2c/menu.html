When we signed the lease for the first Montesacro, I imagined a casual rustic place for people to gather together, enjoying a pinsa with seasonal toppings, that is gentle with the stomach, but a delicious experience for your palate. I couldnt believe pinsa had not yet been brought the United States.

Gianluca, Owner & Founder

A hardscrabble working-class quarter, Montesacro is the name of a neighborhood in Rome, where founder Gianluca Legrottaglie was born. We pay homage to the people, and their beautiful culinary traditions. We take inspiration from the classic "fraschette" of Lazio, old taverns where they only served the famous wine of the region, usually poured straight from the barrel, and patrons sat down on benches along long wooden tables.
