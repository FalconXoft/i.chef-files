Sipping Tequilas

  • Patron Anejo
  • Sherry cask aged—complex aromas of fresh apricot, medjool dates, stewed pears, aged pear cider, overripe bananas, baked agave, vanilla pudding, and honey-glazed walnuts.
  • Don Julio 1942
  • A complex, aromatic añejo tequila, aged longer than most añejos. Floral, candy and fruit notes accompany the expected grassy agave, oak and brown sugar.
  • Clase Azul Reposado
  • Intense amber in color, the Clase Azul Reposado presents aromas of wood, vanilla and toffee caramel. On the palate, it is silky, rich and smooth, offering notes of cooked agave, wood and vanilla.
  • 123 Organic Reposado
  • This complex spirit reveals aromatic raw and cooked agave tempered by the warmth of oak aging with spicy notes of vanilla and a hint of citrus.
  • Maestro Dobel
  • A blend of reposado, anejo & extra anejo double filtered to produce a clear tequila. A Complex mix of caramel, honey, maple as well as nutty & Vanilla notes.
  • Tequila Ocho Plata
  • Sweetly floral nose with extra ripe agaves and candied fruit presenting themselves. Citrus, cinnamon, dried fruit and almond on the palate with a lingering hint of spearmint on the finish.
  • Montelobos Mezcal Artesanal
  • The mezcal is light and very vegetal with a nose of lemon and green pepper, followed by citrus, roasted fig and basil on the palate.
  • Ilegal Mezcal Joven
  • Eucalyptus and mineral aromas compliment hints of green apple, fresh citrus, and red chiltepe.

Spanish Brandies

  • Cardenal Mendoza
  • Rich & Intensely flavored with notes of dried fruit, burnt caramel, oak and subtle sweet notes of plums and raisins.
  • Carlos I
  • A deep amber color with golden highlights. Very aromatic and elegant, with notes of cocoa and vanilla. It is complex and balanced, with a long finish.
  • Fundador
  • Rich spice and brandy butter. Hints of fruitcake and dried apricot. Pepper. Vanilla custard, baked apples and spice. Medium finish with lingering apricot and spice.
  • Felipe II
  • Bright, transparent and luminous, with the notes of a good spirit, touches of nuts, figs and slight hints of oak, vanilla and pralines. Smooth, velvety and long-lasting.
