ARTISANAL CUISINE & SOULFUL HOSPITALITY

Enjoy an eclectic dining experience featuring soulful food & signature crafted drinks with genuine hospitality at its core. Fandi Mata seeks to enchant each guest through an evocative journey blending craftsmanship with global influences, from its Mediterranean-inspired cuisine to its artisanal mezcaleria 'Little Oaxaca' on the mezzanine. Bringing back heartfelt hospitality to Brooklyn, Fandi Mata delivers an escapist experience in a warm and familial setting.
