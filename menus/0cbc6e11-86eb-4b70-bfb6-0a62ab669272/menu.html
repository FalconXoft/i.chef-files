Frequently Asked Questions

ALL YOUR EFFY’S QUESTIONS, ASKED & ANSWERED

What are the opening hours for Effy’s Café – UWS and York?
Effy’s Café – UWS and Effy’s Café – York are open daily, Monday through Sunday, from 8 AM to 9 PM.
What is the phone number for Effy’s Café – York?

Call Effy’s Café – York at 646-490-6751.

What is the phone number for Effy’s Café – UWS?

The phone number for Effy’s Café – UWS is 212-280-6200.

Is Effy’s Café open every day?
Yes – both Effy’s Café locations are open seven days a week.
Does Effy’s Café offer vegetarian options?
Absolutely! Some popular vegetarian picks include our Fresh Mozzarella Sandwich, Goat Cheese Salad, and Green Shakshuka. We also have vegan and gluten-free meals available.
How do I order online from Effy’s Café?
You can place an order online through our website by selecting the location closest to you.
Where are Effy’s Café locations in New York?

Effy’s Café has two locations in Manhattan:
– 104 W 96th St (Upper West Side)
– 1688 York Ave (Yorkville)

Can I schedule a delivery in advance at Effy’s Café?

Yes, you can schedule a delivery in advance through our online ordering system or by calling 212-280-6200.

Does Effy’s Café offer contact-free delivery?
Yes – contact-free delivery is available at both locations for your convenience.
What type of restaurant is Effy’s Café?
Effy’s Café is a Mediterranean-style Café offering healthy, meatless, scratch-made dishes in a casual, welcoming setting.
What are your delivery hours?
Delivery is available every day from 8 AM to 9 PM.
Can I track my delivery order online?
Yes! After your order is confirmed, you’ll receive a tracking link to follow your delivery in real time.
Do you offer any special deals or discounts on delivery orders?

We do! Check our website or follow us on social media for current delivery promotions – including first-time discounts, online-only offers, and time-based deals.
