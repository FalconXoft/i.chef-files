A Few Words About Our Restaurant

At Curry Heights, we believe in crafting an authentic and memorable dining experience inspired by the rich culinary traditions of India. Located in the heart of Brooklyn, our restaurant is a haven for food enthusiasts seeking flavorful dishes, vibrant spices, and warm hospitality.

We take pride in using the freshest ingredients to prepare each dish, ensuring every bite is packed with taste and quality. From the creamy indulgence of our chicken tikka masala to the aromatic perfection of our lamb biryani, our menu is designed to cater to a variety of palates, including vegetarian and vegan options.

Beyond the food, Curry Heights offers a cozy and inviting ambiance where friends, families, and colleagues can come together to enjoy delicious meals and create lasting memories. Our attentive service reflects our commitment to making every visit special for our guests.
