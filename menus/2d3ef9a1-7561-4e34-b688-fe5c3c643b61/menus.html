Browse our Authentic Menu Selections

La Catena Italian Restaurant is the finest of Italian restaurant in the Metro NY region.  Our authentic preparation of Italian delicacies will delight your senses from the start.

The fragrant aroma of imported Parmegiano Reggiano fills the air, courtesy of the table-side preparation of a specialty of the house:  our wonderful Homemade Pappardelle a la Catena!  Equally sensational are the savory notes of our famous Steak on a Stone, baked ravioli, calamari sautéed in marinara sauce, and broiled pork chop with sautéed vinegar peppers, to name just a few. 

We offer a large selection of lunch, dinner, and dessert options to ensure there is something to delight everyone.  We frequently feature special, seasonal, and holiday menus.  All of our delicious menu pairs wonderfully with our wine and spirits menu to complete your dining experience for any celebration.
