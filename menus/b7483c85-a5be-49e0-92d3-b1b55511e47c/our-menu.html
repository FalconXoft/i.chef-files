I visited for the first time on Sunday morning. Timed it just right because we waited about 15 minutes. Jack's is located in a small shopping strip, a few doors down from the post office.

Signage is kind of tricky because the sign has all of the other businesses on it, which makes it a lottle hard to see " Jack's".

There is plenty of parking and is across the street from the hardware store.

It is really cozy inside. The decor is that of Mel's diner or something late 70s to mid 80s.

I did not mind the decor because the staff was great and can I tell you about the food?

Ok, I will tell you about the food... The menu was abundant and accompanied by a special menu. The special menu included.... Orange Mimosa French Toast... WHAT??? Delicious is what.. I mean topped with sugar crystals and an orange garnished with the same and served with perfectly cooked chicken sausage. My heart smiled and my tummy fluttered when the waitress explained it.

Three thumbs up Jack's!!! I guess there is a reason why this place is listed in the top 5 breakfast spots in CT.

Oh yeah, I forgot to mention... they close at noon.

Totally worth the trip and is DEFINITELY on the repeat list.
