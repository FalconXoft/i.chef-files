About

Welcome to Pita Grill – Where Family, Flavor, and Freshness Meet

At Pita Grill, we’re more than just a restaurant — we’re a small, family-owned business that has proudly served our community with delicious, high-quality kosher food for over 15 years.

Rooted in tradition and built on passion, we've made it our mission to offer something for everyone — from authentic Mediterranean shawarma to protein-packed gym plates, fresh sushi rolls, and hearty, satisfying entrées. Every dish is made with care, using only the freshest ingredients, and always prepared to meet the highest kosher standards.

Over the years, we’ve weathered many storms — from rising costs and tough regulations to the unprecedented challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic. Through it all, one thing has never changed: our commitment to serving the community with warmth, resilience, and great food.

Every dish we serve is a reflection of our commitment to quality, authenticity, and care. Whether you're joining us for a quick bite or a family meal, you’re not just a guest— you’re part of our extended family. Thank you for supporting local. Thank you for supporting us.
