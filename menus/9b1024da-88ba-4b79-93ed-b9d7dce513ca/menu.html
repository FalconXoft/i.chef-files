About us

Welcome to the Wildflower Spa, a serene retreat located in Flushing and Manhattan. Our array of services includes massages, facials, and eyelash extensions. Beyond the treatments, Wildflower Spa is a sanctuary where you can escape the daily hustle and embrace the transformative power of self-care. Let us curate a personalized experience for you, ensuring each moment spent here is a harmonious blend of tranquility and refinement. Step into a haven where relaxation becomes an art form, and your well-being is our priority.

OUR SERVICES

Laser Genesis: A non-invasive treatment designed to target fine lines, wrinkles, redness, and uneven skin texture. Using advanced laser technology, Laser Genesis stimulates collagen production, promoting a smoother, more even complexion.

Facial Treatment: Indulge in the luxury of our rejuvenating facial treatments. Our skincare experts utilize premium products and advanced techniques to address your specific skincare concerns. From hydrating facials to anti-aging treatments, each session is designed to leave your skin glowing with vitality.

Massage: Immerse yourself in the healing touch of our skilled therapists. From therapeutic deep tissue massages to soothing Swedish techniques, our diverse range of massage options is tailored to meet your unique needs. Let the stresses of the day melt away as you experience the restorative power of our massages.
