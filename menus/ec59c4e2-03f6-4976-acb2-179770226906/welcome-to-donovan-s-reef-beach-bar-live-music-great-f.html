Join the Party!

Just one visit and you'll understand why Donovan's Reef was voted "One of the Top 21 Beach Bars in America."  Our landmark location in Sea Bright, NJ offers beautiful ocean views, an eclectic crowd and several open-air bars and decks. Spend the day on our private lifeguarded beach while sipping drinks in the sand, or grab a seat at our authentic beachfront Tiki Bar and enjoy the view.  Step inside to catch a game or stay outside to catch the breeze.  Add in live music, daily drink specials and fantastic food and we're confident you'll want to join the party like everyone else has since 1976!

Daily Specials

Jam to the Bands!

At Donovans Reef, live music fills the ocean air with an awesome line-up of musicians.  Feel the rhythm in the sand at our beachfront summer stage.  Dance the night away outside on the deck or catch the beat of the band inside at the bar. There's something for everyone at Donovan's Reef Beach Bar, so join the crowd and sing along, or just hang out and jam to the bands!

Enjoy Great Food!

Donovans Reef serves a variety of freshly made breakfast items, grilled classics, healthy salads and fresh seafoodperfect with your favorite drink and our amazing ocean views.  Our kitchen serves breakfast from 9-11am and stays open until 10:00pm.  Just place your order at our walk-up window and enjoy a delicious oceanside meal anytime of the day.  We've raised the bar on bar food, so raise your glass and enjoy great food at Donovans Reef!

Hit the Beach!

Join us for a day of fun on our private lifeguarded beach and enjoy all that Donovan's Reef Beach Bar has to offer.  It's a cool and casual scene, so sit back and catch some sun, or jump in the ocean and catch the waves.  Join your friends for lunch on the deck, or join the crowd down on the beach. There's music in the ocean air, so grab your beach chair and your towel, but most of all grab a drink, because Donovan's Reef is one of the only bars on the Jersey Shore that serves cocktails on the sand!
