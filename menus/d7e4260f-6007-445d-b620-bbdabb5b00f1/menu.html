WELCOME TO 175 FULTON DINER
 

175 FULTON DINER, at 175 Fulton Avenue in Hempstead, NY, features superb American Continental cuisine in a relaxed and welcoming environment. Our menu consists of home-cooked, made-from-scratch comfort food using local ingredients. We are celebrating the multicultural identity of Hempstead, NY.

The blend of textures and flavors are presented in appealing and delicious ways. The dessert menu offerings showcase a delectable selection of pastries that infuse the upscale American recipes with European influences.

We, at 175 FULTON DINER, look forward to providing our guests with the finest of our Cuisine and making your stay at our restaurant a pleasurable and satisfying dining experience. We offer a tantalizing cuisine in an attractive atmosphere that is ideal for enjoying that important business lunch, as well as dining with friends and family.

Dine In our restaurant's relaxed atmosphere or enjoy Take Out from our menu and Chef's Daily-Selected specials.
