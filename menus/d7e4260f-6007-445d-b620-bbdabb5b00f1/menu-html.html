175 FULTON DINER MENU
 

Our diner-style restaurant features friendly service and home-style cooking that includes a variety of appetizers, soups and salads; sandwiches; award winning seafood; vegetables; desserts and more! The menu is a collection of family favorites and comfort classics our skilled chef prepares daily.
