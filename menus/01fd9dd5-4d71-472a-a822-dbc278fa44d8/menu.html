Welcome

Welcome!

Chiddy’s is proud to be serving Long Islanders by offering the best cheesesteaks to be found on these shores. And, thanks to our valued customers, we’ve won the prestigious Best of Long Island Award for Cheesesteaks, too! But the great fact is, we’re far from just being the place to go for hot and juicy cheesesteak sandwiches for lunch and/or dinner. While that’s what we’re known for, we also offer homemade empanadas, gourmet hot dogs, tacos, fresh salads, hand-cut fries, and truly something for every member of your family and/or posse. Conveniently located, you’ll find us at: 

Chiddy’s in Commack: 189 Jericho Turnpike,
Hours: Monday - Sunday 11:30 AM - 9:00 PM 

Chiddy’s in West Islip: 510 Union Blvd,
Hours: Monday - Sunday 11:00 AM - 9:00 PM

Chiddy’s in Farmingdale: 191 Main Street,
Hours: Sunday - Thursday 11:30 AM - 9:00 PM
Friday and Saturday: 11:30 AM - 10:30 PM

Chiddy’s in Astoria: 37-03 31st Avenue,
Hours: Sunday - Thursday 11:00 AM - 11:00 PM,
Friday and Saturday 11:00 AM -Midnight

Chiddy’s in Oakdale: Coming soon to 1551 Montauk Hwy.

Be sure to look for Chiddy’s at Section 132 at Citi Field for Mets Home Games during the season. 

Chiddy’s Food Truck, located in Bay Shore at 1851 Sunrise Hwy! We not only have fabulous options for lunch and dinner. Don’t forget, that our food truck can be rented for all your private parties and events, too!
Hours: Open daily from 11:00 AM - 7:00 PM

Remember, you can order online for pickup, or arrange via UberEats for home delivery using their smartphone app. Hot. Fresh. Delicious. That’s what Chiddy’s is all about. So “Get Cheezy at Chiddy’s!” We can’t wait to serve you- come by and see for yourself soon!
