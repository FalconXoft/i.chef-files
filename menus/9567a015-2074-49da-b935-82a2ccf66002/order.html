Lillian Pizzeria

Lillian Pizzeria is currently located at 9601 69th Ave. Lillian Pizzeria has been serving pizza and classic Italian dishes to Forest Hills and adjacent communities for over 50 years. The tradition continues today as we welcome long-term neighbors and new arrivals to enjoy our home-style cooking. We’re best known for our grandma pizza, but you can’t wrong with classic cheese, Sicilian, margarita or any of our specialty menu pies. Complete the meal with any of our heroes, pasta dishes, entrees, salads, appetizers or kids meals. Follow us @lillianpizzeria
