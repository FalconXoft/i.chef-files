ABOUT

Cafe Salamanca has delighted diners with authentic Spanish cuisine for a quarter of a century. Our menu offers dishes from the various regions of Spain with a strong emphasis on fresh fish and seafood. Enjoy lunch and dinner, seven days a week; and on a Saturday night you’ll be serenaded by soothing live acoustic guitar while you dine with singing that creates a festive environment for all to enjoy.  Cafe Salamanca is open for lunch and dinner seven days a week. At the bar, you can enjoy a glass of wine, a cocktail or some tapas. We offer a dining room to accommodate parties from 20 up to 80 guests, a complete dinner special seven nights a week which includes homemade chicken soup, choice of a one-pound lobster or our signature paella, coffee and dessert, all for amazingly low prices. It’s a perfect place to meet your coworkers and friends to savor the delicious delicacies of Spain, all in a warm and friendly atmosphere.
