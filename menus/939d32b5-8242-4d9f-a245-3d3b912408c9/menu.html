Reservations

Please understand we are a small, neighborhood restaurant. Turning away walk-ins with the expectation that a reservation will arrive greatly affects our ability to sustain a successful business when a reservation decides to no-show or not cancel a reservation 12-hours in advance. Accommodating walk-ins and our waitlist is equally important. It is our best intention to always do our best to seat everyone.

On nights our reservation list is at full capacity, we only seat complete parties and allow a 15-minute grace period. If you are still incomplete after this time, your reservation will be forfeited and your party will be placed on the waitlist.

Cancel your reservation 12-hours in advance to avoid the $20 per person late/no-show cancelation fee. Sign into YELP or locate the original email or text confirmation you received upon making the reservation. Cancelations AFTER the 12-hour period will be a charge of $20 per person to the credit card holder on the reservation. 

We appreciate your understanding, are grateful for your support in our small business, and to be a part of your community. Mahalo Brooklyn!

Large parties 6 or more, please email: suzumebk@gmail.com

Waitlist

The waitlist becomes available the day of and only if the restaurant is fully seated. By adding your name to the wait list, YELP will send you a text with an estimated time, and will indicate how many parties are on the list before you.
