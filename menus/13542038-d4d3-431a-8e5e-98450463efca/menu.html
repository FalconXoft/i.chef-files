About Us

Straightforward Greek cuisine such as seafood & lamb served in warm digs with exposed-wood beams.

Step into a warm, inviting space where traditional Greek flavors take center stage. From succulent, slow-roasted lamb to fresh, perfectly prepared seafood, every dish is crafted with care and authenticity. The restaurant’s cozy interior, with its exposed-wood beams and rustic accents, evokes the charm of a Greek taverna, while soft lighting and thoughtful décor create an atmosphere that’s both relaxed and welcoming.
