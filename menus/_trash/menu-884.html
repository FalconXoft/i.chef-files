FAQs about Pizza Catering

What types of pizza do you offer for catering in New Jersey?

We offer classic Neapolitan pizzas made fresh with high-quality ingredients. Options include Margherita, Crispy Pepperoni, Plain Jane, Veggie Jackson, Hot Shot, and seasonal specialties. Whether it’s a small gathering or a big event, we’ve got flavors to match every crowd.

How long does it take to prepare and deliver pizza?

Preparation and delivery time usually depend on the size of the order and the delivery area. Nevertheless, our custom-designed pizza trucks come with authentic imported Italian wood-fired ovens, with which we can prepare pizzas in less than 90 seconds. We recommend booking in advance so that everything goes as planned.

Why is Neapolitan pizza catering a great choice for weddings and parties?

Freshly baked Neapolitan pizzas are an absolute crowd-pleaser. Guests simply adore the whole show where the pizzas are baked in minutes, straight from the mobile oven. It’s got that cool, fun feel but still sophisticated enough for weddings, birthdays, and everything in between.

How is Neapolitan pizza different from other styles?

A Neapolitan pizza has a thin, soft crust and a puffy edge, cooked fast in a super-hot wood-fired oven. It’s not heavy or greasy—just simple, fresh, and full of flavor. Neapolitan pizzas are nothing like your standard delivery pizzas.

What ingredients are used in authentic Neapolitan pizza?

Authentic Neapolitan pizza tends to keep things simple: Italian flour, San Marzano tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, basil, and extra virgin olive oil. No shortcuts, no wacky ingredients—just clean, fresh, real ingredients that come together in a way that makes a pizza truly delicious.
