Welcome to Oligarch

As one of the premiere catering halls and event spaces across NYC, Oligarch has cemented its status as one of the most in-demand venues for all types of gatherings and affairs. Located in the heart of Queens’ Richmond Hill section, Oligarch offers its patrons and clients an unmatched experience that few, if any, across the city can duplicate. A largescale restaurant and catering hall, Oligarch is the perfect setting for your next event or party, no matter if it’s a corporate team building event, birthday celebration, or the ideal space for your wedding reception. Your Dream Wedding Reception

At Oligarch, we strive to provide our clients with a one-of-a-kind experience, they won’t be able to find, anywhere else in New York City! Oligarch isn’t your average catering hall, and that has made it one of the most in-demand locations for wedding parties of all kinds. Our amazing décor, and luxurious design, provide you with the perfect backdrop for your wedding reception & celebration.
A blend of classic beauty, and modern amenities, Oligarch is the perfect place to celebrate your most special of days. One of the most important days in the life of a man or woman is their wedding day. And for such a special day, you need a venue to match! Oligarch is the ideal setting to bring in the rest of your life with your partner, friends, family and loved ones of all kinds. A Russian themed hall, not only is Oligarch one of the most beautiful and well-designed venues in the city, but it is also quite spacious and provides its clientele with an elite level of comfort and amenities. The venue has a capacity of over 300 people, perfect for wedding parties of all kinds.

Food, Catering & Amenities

A full ballroom, restaurant and event center, Oligarch is a modern marvel located in one of the most convenient areas in the borough of Queens. While many of our patron’s marvel at our outstanding décor and high-level, luxurious aesthetic, Oligarch is much more than just nice to look at! With a responsive, and attentive staff, a full restaurant with food and catering options, ballroom, wheelchair accessibility and much more – Oligarch is a full-service venue that you can count on, and ensure that each and every one of your guests will have a night they will never forget.

Bridal Showers & Other Wedding Events

Located in the heart of Queens, one of New York City’s most culturally diverse boroughs, Oligarch has been the setting for weddings, receptions, showers, and bridal events of all kinds! Despite its obviously Russian-inspired name, Oligarch has been the backdrop for wedding celebrations from individuals and couples of all backgrounds! With each and every culture having their own specialty parties and wedding traditions, you can count on Oligarch to an unforgettable venue, for whatever you and your party have in mind! Whether it’s a religiously themed, more somber affair, or an exciting bridal shower, Oligarch is the place for any, or even ALL your wedding’s events!
