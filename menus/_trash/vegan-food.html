Enjoy the Tastiest Vegan Food at the Leading Vegan Restaurant in Manhattan

Enjoy the authentic Indian cuisine in a stylish setting at Anand Indian Cuisine. We are the best Indian restaurant in Manhattan that offers a wide variety of traditional Indian dishes with exotic spices and ingredients that reflect the Indian heritage. The best part of Anand Indian Cuisine is, we are the most popular vegan Indian restaurant in Manhattan where you can find 100% vegan food.

Finding a good vegan restaurant in Manhattan is quite difficult. And if you find a restaurant, you never know if they are using the proper ingredients to prepare vegan food. that is why most people, who are vegan, don’t dare to dine-in outside. But when you are at Anand Indian Restaurant, you can be assured that the food is 100% vegan. Our chefs have enough experience to know the difference between the veg and the vegan food and they take the ultimate care while preparing vegan dishes. Our magnificent staff serve you the most delicious vegan food always with a smile on their face.

We believe the proverb ‘the way to the heart is through the stomach’ and our main motto is to be in your heart forever. This is the reason, our star-rated chefs prepare delicious vegan food and make your taste buds happy and satisfied.
