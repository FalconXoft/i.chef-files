Condominium Residences on the Williamsburg Waterfront

The condominium residences at One Domino Square are designed with sophisticated finishes and modern conveniences that enhance the experience of living in one of New York City’s most spectacular locations.

Each home has a generous open layout with high ceilings and oversized windows that complement the stunning views and natural light. Bespoke chef’s kitchens are available in two distinct designs curated by Annabelle Selldorf and the spa-like bathrooms soothe the senses with radiant floor heating, walk-in showers and large soaking tubs.
