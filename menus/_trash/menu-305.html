HOME OF THE WORLD FAMOUS 
INNER-MONGOLIAN HOT POT

Welcome to Happy Lamb, your gateway to the finest inner-Mongolian-style hot pot experience. Immerse yourself in a culinary journey where our rich broths, premium meats, and lively atmosphere will heighten all your senses.

Taste the happiness. Share your stories. Enjoy our beloved hot pot.

FALL IN LOVE WITH OUR BONE MARROW BROTH

Using more than 10 handpicked ingredients directly from their origins, our culinary masters meticulously combine the flavours of the broth. After countless cycles of boiling, stillnes and re-boiling and integrating a 6 step process - a pot of broth that can impact the world is born.

Happy Lamb is renowned for its 6 hour bone broth and its “no dipping” approach to hot pot. Crafted to perfection and incredibly flavorful that no dipping sauce is needed but can be enjoyed with our unlimited sauce bar.

Experience the richness of beauty contained in every sip with our dense collagen rich broth known for its high content of antioxidants and minerals.

THE BEST LAMB MEAT FROM ACROSS THE WORLD

Sourcing the highest quality premium natural lamb from Inner Mongolian China, U.K., Scotland, New Zealand, Ireland and Australia - Happy Lamb serves the best lamb meat in the world.

MORE THAN JUST HOT POT

Offering delicious, freshly cooked hot plates to accompany the flavors of our hot pot, there is something for everyone at Happy Lamb.
