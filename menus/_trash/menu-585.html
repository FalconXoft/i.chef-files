Antojitos BK

Located in the heart of Brooklyn's Sunset Park, Antojitos has been a neighborhood favorite for years. Passionate about authentic Dominican cuisine, with a specialty in sandwiches, Antojitos will be sure to put a smile on your face. Whether this is your first time trying or you're a lifelong fan of Caribbean comfort food, stop by Antojitos or place an order for delivery and see for yourself what all the buzz is about.
