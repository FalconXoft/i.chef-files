    Our Story

    Meet Mingge and Derike, the cool minds behind Mr KEKE, cooking up a storm in the world of Japanese and Asian flavors. From Mingge's bold move from selling utensils to crushing it in Sichuan cuisine to Derike's rad restaurant management skills, they've joined forces to bring you a ramen haven right in the heart of Long Island. Mr KEKE isn't just a spot; it's the ultimate Japanese fast-food vibe, rocking unique eats and cutting-edge processes. Get ready for a taste revolution where Mingge and Derike flip the script on Asian fast food!

    Our Mission

    Our mission at Mr KEKE is to redefine the Asian fast-food landscape in the USA by seamlessly blending tradition with innovation, ensuring an exhilarating culinary journey.

    Our Vision

    Fusion of Asian characteristics of noodles, dim sum, to create the first brand of healthy food fast food chain.
