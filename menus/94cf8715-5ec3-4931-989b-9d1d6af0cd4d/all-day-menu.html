ALL DAY MENU

Welcome to Pico De Gallo's Mexican Lunch & Dinner Menu, where traditional Mexican cuisine meets innovative culinary artistry. Our menu is a testament to the rich flavors and diversity of Mexican food, each dish crafted with fresh, quality ingredients to ensure an authentic taste. From the hearty comfort of enchiladas to the vibrant flavors of our tacos, our menu offers a unique culinary journey through Mexico's diverse regions.

Whether you're joining us for a leisurely lunch or an evening feast, our menu caters to a variety of tastes and preferences. Our chefs have expertly balanced traditional Mexican flavors with their own creative flair, resulting in a menu that is both familiar and exciting.

Set in the heart of Manhattan, Pico De Gallo offers a dining experience that goes beyond the food. Our vibrant, Mexican pop-art setting provides an immersive atmosphere, making your meal a truly memorable experience.

Experience the best of Mexican food in NYC with our Lunch & Dinner Menu. At Pico De Gallo, we're not just serving meals, we're sharing a piece of Mexican culture. Join us for a culinary adventure that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more.
