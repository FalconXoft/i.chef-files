Mayfield is a seasonal American restaurant with a focus on fresh, quality ingredients prepared in a simple yet creative way.

Mayfield Restaurant opened in Oct 2012 in Crown Heights, Brooklyn. Since then, owners Lev Gewirtzman and Jacques Belanger have been serving quality interpretations of classic American dishes in an atmosphere great for someone who wants to have a hamburger and a beer while watching the game at the bar, to a group of friends that want a four course meal while enjoying our hand-picked wines and cocktails.
