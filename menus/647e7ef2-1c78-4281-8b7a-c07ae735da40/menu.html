About Us

Welcome to Annie’s Kitchen located at Queens, NY, an Asian Fusion restaurant that brings together thebest of Easternand Westerncuisine. We serve the most flavorful and unique dishes in a contemporary atmosphere that will make you feel like you’re in the heart of Asia.

Our menu features a range of Asian Fusion dishes. Each dish is cooked to your preference by our experienced chefs, ensuring that each bite is bursting with flavor. We use only the freshest ingredients, resulting in a truly sublime dining experience.

In addition to great food, we also prioritize providing an enjoyable dining experience. Our restaurant is decorated in a modern yet cozy style, complete with comfortable tables and chairs.
