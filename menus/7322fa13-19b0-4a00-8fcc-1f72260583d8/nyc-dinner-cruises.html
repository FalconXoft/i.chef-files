FAQs

How long is the New York Bateaux Premier Plus Dinner Cruise?

The New York Bateaux Premier Plus Dinner Cruise lasts 3 hours.

What sights can you see while on the New York Bateaux Premier Plus Dinner Cruise?

While on the New York Bateaux Premier Plus Dinner Cruise, you can enjoy views of the New York City skyline, including the Empire State Building, the Brooklyn Bridge, and the Statue of Liberty.

What should I wear on the New York Bateaux Premier Plus Dinner Cruise?

Semi-formal attire is recommended for this cruise.

How much is the New York Bateaux Premier Plus Dinner Cruise?

The cost of the New York Dinner Cruise varies based on the package and booking time. Please check the website or contact customer service for the most current pricing.

Where to have dinner on the water in New York?

For a memorable dining experience with a view, the New York Bateaux Premier Plus Dinner Cruise offers a delightful evening with stunning views of the New York City skyline.

What type of dining experience is offered on the New York Bateaux Premier Plus Dinner Cruise?

While on the New York Bateaux Premier Plus Dinner Cruise, you can enjoy a delicious plated dinner with freshly prepared entrees, salads, and desserts. Beer, wine, and creative cocktails are also available from the fully stocked bar for guests 21 and older.

Is there live entertainment on the New York Bateaux Premier Plus Dinner Cruise?

Yes, there is a live band performance to enhance your dining experience.

Are there any upgrades available for the New York Bateaux Premier Plus Dinner Cruise?

Yes, there are several upgrades available including creative cocktails, wine, and beer from a well-stocked cash bar, as well as window table reservations for the best views.

Is the New York Bateaux Premier Plus Dinner Cruise a good option for a romantic night out?

Absolutely! The cruise offers a perfect blend of delicious dining, stunning views, and a festive atmosphere, making it an ideal way to enjoy a romantic night out on the water.
