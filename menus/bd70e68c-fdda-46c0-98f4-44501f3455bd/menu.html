Fast Food Delivery & More!

Finally a fast food menu that doesnt disappoint! Jollibee is known for Fried Chicken, Burgers and Pies. Our menu is designed to bring joy to the whole family, perfect for lunch or dinner. How do we do it? With sweetness, spiciness, Filipino flavor and plenty of joy. We know you'll love or Original and Spicy chicken recipes. Jollibee Menu FAQ

  • Where can I learn more about your food (ingredients, allergen, and nutrition information)?

    You can download/view the complete nutritional information and allergen information of all our food offerings.

  • Do you use hormones or antibiotics in your chicken?

    The chicken we serve does not contain added hormones. Federal regulations prohibit the use of added hormones in poultry in the US and Canada.

    We are currently looking into using chicken raised without antibiotics which we plan to implement in the future.

  • Is your chicken free-range?

    Jollibee does not use free-range chicken; however, all chickens used are raised according to the highest standards of quality and safety from USDA-approved facilities.

  • Do you serve halal or kosher chicken?

    Jollibee does not guarantee any of its products to be halal or kosher due to variation in food suppliers and ingredient sources, use of shared kitchen equipment and various requirements for halal certification.

  • Do you have vegetarian/vegan options in your menu?

    We do have food offerings that will fit different dietary and lifestyle choices:

    Lacto-vegetarian (excludes meat and eggs; allows dairy): Peach Mango Pie, French Fries, Rice, Mashed Potato, Gravy, Pineapple Quencher, Coffee (select stores), Soda

    Ovo-vegetarian (no meat, fish, eggs, dairy): Rice, Pineapple Quencher, Coffee (select stores), Soda

    Vegan (no meat, fish, eggs, dairy): Rice, Pineapple Quencher, Coffee (select stores), Soda

    Gluten-free: Rice, Mashed Potato (Without Gravy), Ranch Dip, Honey Mustard Dip, Halo-Halo, Pineapple Quencher, Coffee (select stores), Soda
