ABOUT US

Da Andrea, your neighborhood Italian ristorante.
Located in the West Village of NYC, now in Chelsea as well, weve been capturing hearts and palates for over 20 years.
Experience the beauty of authentic Italian dining with a menu that specializes in the art of homemade pasta, traditional Italian dishes, delicious risottos, and more.
Each dish we serve you is a masterpiece, meticulously prepared with fresh, locally sourced ingredients and an abundance of experience.
Dining is a shared experience and every detail matters. Whether youre in the mood for a glass of wine or a full four-course meal, were here to take you on a delectable journey in a warm and inviting ambiance.
