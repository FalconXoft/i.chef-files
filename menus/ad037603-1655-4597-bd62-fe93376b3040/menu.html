      Our Story

      For over 30 years, Texas Rotisserie & Grill has been bringing authentic Southern flavors to Manhattan’s Upper West Side. What started as a dream to bring real Texas-style BBQ to New York has evolved into a neighborhood institution where comfort food meets culinary expertise.

      Over three decades, we’ve proudly served the community and our commitment to quality has earned us recognition as one of Manhattan’s premier BBQ destinations and a loyal customer base spanning three decades.

      Today, we continue our tradition of bringing Southern hospitality to New York City, combining time-honored smoking techniques with the dynamic energy of Manhattan’s food scene.

      Why Choose Texas Rotisserie & Grill?

      Always Fresh

      Every morning starts with preparing our signature rubs, firing up the smokers, and marinating our famous rotisserie chicken. We believe in doing things the right way, not the easy way.

      Made From Scratch

      From our cornbread to our BBQ sauces, everything is made in-house daily. Our sides aren’t just accompaniments; they’re a celebration of Southern cooking traditions.

      Traditional Smoking Process

      Our meats are smoked daily using traditional cooking methods, following authentic Texas BBQ traditions. This patient process, sometimes lasting up to 14 hours, ensures that every bite of brisket, rib, or pulled pork captures the genuine taste of Texas.
