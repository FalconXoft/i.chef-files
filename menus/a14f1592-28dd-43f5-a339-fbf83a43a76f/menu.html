About "El Gauchito"

Since 1979, El Gauchito is the place where you can find a little piece of Argentina in New York. In a family atmosphere, you can share una parillada (an Argentine grill) or taste traditional Argentine dishes. We offer an extensive menu, delicious desserts and the best Argentine wines. In our butcher shop, you will find top quality beef with Argentine-style cuts, along with sausages, blood sausages and matambre (rolled stuffed flank steak).

We also offer a wide variety of authentic Argentine products, from yerba mate (unique tea blend) to dulce de leche in various brands. We welcome you to discover our shop and find the authentic delicacies you need.

Our goal is to provide best quality and service at an unbeatable price.
