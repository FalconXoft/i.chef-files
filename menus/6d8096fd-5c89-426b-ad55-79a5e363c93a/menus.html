    • Washington, DC Menus

    Whether you choose one of our tasting menus curated by Zaytinya Concept Chef Michael Costa or create your own mezze meal, prepare for a culinary voyage across the Mediterranean. Make sure to visit Zaytinya on the weekends for our famous RoZAY Brunch, including a trio of rosés and four courses of mezze with dessert to share. Pair your meal with our wide selection of Mediterranean wines or try one of our delicious craft cocktails like the 3PM In Istanbul – that pairs lemon lavender mint tea-infused vodka, aperol, vanilla, lime and grapefruit.
