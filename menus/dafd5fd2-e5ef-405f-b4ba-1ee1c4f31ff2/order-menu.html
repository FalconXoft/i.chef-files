OUR TABLE BBQ POLICY
For Table BBQ, a Minimum of 2 AYCE’s must be ordered per table.
This requirement is similar to ordering 2 orders of meat for a table BBQ experience at any other Korean restaurant in NYC.
Since we are an All You Can Eat Korean barbecue restaurant,
We require a minimum of 2 orders of AYCE per table.
All guests at the BBQ table must choose the original or premium.
Diners cannot combine the choices since It’s AYCE.
This applies to groups and single diners who visit our restaurant.
We only offer table BBQ since we are an AYCE restaurant.
