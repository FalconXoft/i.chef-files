About Our Authentic Spanish
Restaurant & Cuisine

ABOUT OUR CHEF

Lolo Manso

In 2008, Lolo Manso created a place in New York City to share his love for Spain. Since then, Socarrat has brought the essence of Spanish cuisine to New Yorkers and guests from around the world.

LEARN OUR STORY

Socarrat (Sok-ah-rht):

Derived from the Valencian/Catalan verb “socarrar,” which means “to scorch” or “to singe,” it refers to the seductive caramelization of the crispy bottom layer of rice created at the bottom of a perfectly cooked paella.

SPANISH CULTURE & ESSENCE

A Journey through Spain's Culinary Traditions

Spains cuisine is deeply rooted in its regions, each with distinct flavors shaped by history, geography, and local ingredients. From the seafood-rich coastlines to the agricultural heartlands, every dish reflects the traditions of the people who have perfected it over generations.

DISCOVER OUR MENUS

Pulpo a la Gallega

An iconic Galician dish, this northwestern Spanish staple features tender octopus with olive oil, sea salt, and paprika, reflecting the region's Atlantic heritage.

Gambas al Ajillo

A classic tapa from southern Spain, this dish features sizzling shrimp in garlic-infused olive oil with guindilla peppers, a coastal favorite for its fresh seafood.

Patatas Bravas

A Madrid tapas staple, crispy potato cubes topped with smoked tomato sauce and aioli are a must-try at any Spanish gathering.

Tortilla

A Spanish classic, this potato and onion mixture is a versatile staple found in homes, cafs, and bars nationwide.

Bacalao a la Plancha & Berbecheros

A Basque cuisine staple, codfish is often paired with shellfish, lemon, and white wine sauces to enhance it delicate flavor.

Jarrete & Chuletas de Cordero

A staple in northern Spain, lamb is slow-roasted or braised for tenderness and rich flavor.

Paella

Spain's iconic paella, from Valencia, is cooked with saffron, sofrito, and a mix of meats, seafood, or vegetables. The prized socarrat, the crispy rice bottom layer, adds depth and flavor.

Croquetas

Golden and crispy with a creamy bchamel base, croquetas are a Spanish must-have tapa, filled with jamn, seafood, or mushrooms.
