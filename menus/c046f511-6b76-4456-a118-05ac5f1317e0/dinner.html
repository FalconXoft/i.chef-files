The Evening Dining Tradition,
Inspired by Spain

Whether its a quiet dinner for two or a lively gathering of friends, dinner at Socarrat is an invitation to slow down, enjoy good company, and experience the soulful spirit of Spain through food. From classic tapas to our signature paellas, every dish is meant to bring people together just like at the best tables in Spain.
