Family Style

For TABLES of 7 or more in our dining room (NO PRIVATE) we offer a FAMILY STYLE MENU for $70pp for 3 courses (Antipasti - Primi - Dolci) or $90 for 4 courses (Antipasti - Primi - Secondi - Dolci)

Antipasti

Choice of 3 antipasti to be served family style:

Chef’s selection of cured market Italian meats (+$5 per person)

Meatballs In "Rosso Gargano" Tomato Sauce

Arugula, cherry tomatoes, shaved parmigiano reggiano, walnuts * 

Red lettuce, baby spinach, pomegranade, grilled scamorza cheese, orange, honey and mustard dressing

Fritto Misto with crispy zucchini, curry and dill mayo

Crispy artichokes with shaved parmigiano reggiano, crumbled pistachio, magic sauce

Crostini Misti

Primi

Choice of 3 pasta to be served family style:

Trofie with arugula pesto, shrimp, candied tomatoes, burrata cream 

Mafaldine carbonara with crispy guanciale

Spaghetti with tomato sauce

Spaghetti garlic, extra vergin olive oil, 'nduja, breadcrumbs 

Tagliatelle with beef & pork ragout

Cappelletti with stracchino cheese, arugula and crispy prosciutto 

Gnocchi gorgonzola and walnuts

Risotto "alla Milanese", saffron cream and italian sausage

Secondi

($90 menu option)

Choice of 3 Individual entrée to be served family style:

Eggplant parmigiana with scamorza cheese & tomatoes “conserva” *

Shell steak “tagliata” with roasted potatoes and mustard sauce

Roasted cauliflower "steak" serve with endive, guanciale and chimichurri sauce

Branzino "al cartoccio" (oven-baked branzino filet, cherry tomatoes, olives, capers)

Grilled octopus, heirloom tomatoes, roasted cipollini onions, baked potatoes

Pollo Arrosto (roasted “cage free” organic chicken, sauteed broccoli rabe)

Dolci

Desser to be served family style:

Piccola Pasticceria (Chef's Selection of Assorted Homemade Petit Pastries)

Mixed cheese selection with homemade jams (+$10 per person)
