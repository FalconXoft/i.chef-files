Welcome to Casa Di Isacco, his journey began in the Canary Islands, it is here his love for fresh caught seafood and his passion for Elvis Presley began. His desire for food brought him to Geneva where Isaac honed his skills as a chef and Sommelier. The passion Isaac had for Elvis and entertainment brought him to the United States where he began performing as an Elvis impersonator in Las Vegas. Isaac’s musical talent brought him all across America while performing and recording songs. Isaac landed in New York City and opened Trattoria Casa Di Isacco, his ability to mix fresh seafood, fine wines and entertainment is displayed each day. Come in and enjoy New York’s best kept secret, we are located on 9th avenue between 39th and 40th street.
In the heart of New York City lies a hidden gem, Trattoria Casa Di Isacco, a charming Italian restaurant that transports its guests straight to Italy's warm, rustic landscapes. This delightful find is the brainchild of Isacco, the charismatic proprietor, who treats each guest like royalty and is known to serenade his guests in a celebration of good food and company. Trattoria Casa Di Isacco is more than a restaurant; it's a journey of sensory delight. The menu, boasting of Isacco's original recipes, has received wide acclaim. From the comforting warmth of tortellini di lanona and the hearty gnocchi Bolognese to the sweet temptation of homemade lemon sorbet, each dish is a testament to authentic Italian cuisine. The ambience, replete with eclectic charm and an unmistakable Italian vibe, further enhances the dining experience.
