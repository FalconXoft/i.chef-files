About

China Restaurant

Welcome to China Restaurant, a cherished culinary gem nestled in the vibrant neighborhood of Brooklyn, NY. Located at 937 Utica Ave, this spot has become a local favorite, renowned for its outstanding wings, affectionately dubbed as "Snyder Wings" by loyal patrons. With a contact line of +1 718-498-5108, it's easy to get in touch for takeout or inquiries.

When you step into China Restaurant, you're entering a realm where flavor reigns supreme. Notably, the chicken wings have garnered a reputation that transcends conventional accolades. Customers rave about their crispy perfection and rich taste, with Dee proclaiming them as the best in Brooklyn. Janysa “Jany” Rodriguez even suggests the wings are so good that she hasn’t felt the need to explore the rest of the menu, a sentiment echoed by many.

Lisa Lewis attests to the restaurant's exceptional offerings beyond wings, enjoying their chicken lo mein and soy sauce chicken as well. The combination of richly flavored dishes has made it a go-to for gatherings and special events. It's not just food; it's a celebration of flavors that bring joy to your palate.

As you consider a visit, be mindful that China Restaurant operates on a cash-only basis—a small caveat for a big reward. The warmth of the food, the joy it brings to patrons, and the community it fosters make this restaurant a unique dining experience.

In summary, China Restaurant is more than just a name on Utica Avenue; it’s a culinary destination providing delightful dishes, particularly their legendary wings. Whether you're picking up an order for a gathering or just satisfying a craving, this establishment promises to deliver memorable flavors that keep customers returning time and again.
