Welcome to Karakatta

Karakatta is a trendy and intimate ramen spot in New York, offering a fusion-style menu with options for vegans and vegetarians. The menu includes delicious appetizers like Miso Edamame and Pork Belly Buns, along with a variety of ramen dishes ranging from Vegan to Shoyu. Customers praise the flavorful and spicy options, as well as the cozy atmosphere and attentive staff. The restaurant also offers a selection of beers, cocktails, and sakes to complement the meal. Overall, Karakatta is a must-visit for those looking for a unique and satisfying ramen experience in the city.

Welcome to karakatta, a cozy ramen restaurant located in the heart of New York City. Offering a fusion-style ramen experience, karakatta is known for its spicy options and an extensive selection of delicious vegetarian and vegan dishes. The restaurant prides itself on providing a diverse menu to cater to a variety of dietary preferences.

When you step into karakatta, you'll be greeted by a small yet intimate space that can accommodate around 20-25 people comfortably. The atmosphere is casual, trendy, and classy, making it the perfect spot for a low-key meal or a gathering with friends. The restaurant is wheelchair accessible and offers outdoor seating for those who prefer dining al fresco.

One of the highlights of karakatta is its impressive selection of appetizers, including mouth-watering options like Miso Edamame, Pork Belly Bun, and Fried Gyoza. The menu also features a range of vegetarian and vegan-friendly dishes, ensuring that everyone can find something they love.

Customers rave about the spicy ginger stamina ramen, a must-try dish for those who enjoy a kick of heat. The broth is flavorful and rich, while the noodles are cooked to perfection. Pair your meal with a refreshing beer or sake from karakatta's extensive drink menu, featuring options like Sapporo Premium Beer and Kikusui Karakuchi Cup Sake.

With exceptional service and a warm, welcoming environment, karakatta is the ideal spot for a cozy night out or a casual meal with loved ones. Whether you're a ramen enthusiast or looking to explore new flavors, karakatta is sure to impress with its delicious dishes and inviting ambiance.
