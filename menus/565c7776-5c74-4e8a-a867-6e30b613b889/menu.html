About Us

Check our story

Life is surprising! Who thought we three people come up together and commence a new joint venture of opening a restaurant in New York? Now, here we are with “Okawa Asian Cuisine”. Okawa means a big river in Japanese.

Okawa provides a blend of various Asian cuisines such as Japanese, Indo-Chinese, Nepali, and Indian, and of course Himalayan food flavors. Chef Dendup Sherpa, from Sikkim, brings his own signature dishes to the table. Dendup has more than ten years of experience in the food industry. Similarly, Ang Chhiri Sherpa brings more than ten years of sushi chef experience. Ang is an expert sashimi maker.

Pashupati Shrestha, CPA brings his financial and entrepreneurial expertise to the restaurant. He has various tax and business experience that complements the cooking skills of chefs. Pashupati Shrestha also owns his own tax CPA firm “Buddha Tax and Accounting” in Jackson Heights. If you need any tax and accounting support, please visit Buddha Tax at 7226 Broadway, Fl3, Jackson Heights, NY 11372. Buddha Tax files tax for all 50 states.

Please visit our restaurant at 7705a 37th Ave, Jackson Heights, NY 11372, and support small businesses in Jackson Heights.
