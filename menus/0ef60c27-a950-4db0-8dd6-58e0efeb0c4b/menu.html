SIMPLE DIRECTIONS

Farmington Gardens is conveniently located on Rt 4 in Farmington, Connecticut.

From Points East and South

Take exit 39 off Interstate 84, continue onto Rt 4 West.

Farmington Gardens will be 2.5 miles on the left.

From Points West

Farmington Gardens is located 3.2 miles from Unionville Center on the right.

From Points North

Take Rt 10 South to Rt 4. Turn right onto Rt 4 West

Farmington Gardens will be 0.7 Miles on the left.
