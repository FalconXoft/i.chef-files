Moghul Express brings the diverse and flavorful cuisines of India to your plate, offering a wide array of North and South Indian, Street Food, Indo-Chinese, and Pan Asian delicacies, along with an exquisite selection of Indian and fusion desserts.

Delectable Indian Delights

Indulge in the rich and aromatic flavors of authentic Indian cuisine, where each dish is a harmonious blend of spices, herbs, and traditional cooking techniques, transporting your taste buds on a delightful journey through the diverse culinary landscape of India.

Moghul Express, originating from the iconic Moghul Restaurant, has expanded its authentic Indian cuisine across New Jersey now boasting of locations in Edison, Kendall Park, Piscataway & Parsippany.
