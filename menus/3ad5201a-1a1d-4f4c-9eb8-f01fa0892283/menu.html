About

CJ Cafe is your go-to destination for bold, authentic Caribbean flavors in New York. From juicy jerk chicken and crispy fried chicken to seasoned rice & peas and vibrant salads, our menu brings comfort and flavor to every plate.


With options like rasta pasta, curry goat, and oxtail, we serve meals that are flavorful, filling, and always made with care. Whether you're dining in or grabbing takeout, CJ Cafe delivers a true taste of the Caribbean in every bite.
