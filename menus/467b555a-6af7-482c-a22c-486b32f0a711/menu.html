About Us

Menya Sandaime

We thrive to provide authentic taste of Japanese Ramen to our customers by using fresh and natural ingridients only. Our Tokyo style pork broth and house-made noodles are prepared daily with great amount of time and effort.
Taste the difference. Quality is our Recipe.

    Pan-fried noodles. Served with Ankake sauce mixed in with stir-fried vegetables, shrimp, & calamari. Topped with scallion & marinated egg.

    Soy sauce based soup & in house-made noodles. Topped with bean sprouts, scallions, spinach, marinated egg, grilled pork belly, and bamboo shoots.

    Stir-fried vegetables (bean sprouts, spinach, onions, cabbage, carrots, and scallions), pork and shrimp. Over choice of noodles or rice.
