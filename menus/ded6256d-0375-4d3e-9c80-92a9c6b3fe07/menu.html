Hello, welcome to Gino's Pizza Restaurant. We have been doing business in West Hempstead since 1985. We have always been committed in bringing our customers the best product we can. Not only do we specialize in Pizza, but our assortment of appetizers, soups, salad, wraps, panini, pasta and entrees will also soothe your craving for a special Italian meal.

Let Gino's cater your next affair. We take great pride in our work so our customers can enjoy their event. Corporate lunches, birthday parties, religious events, or just a friendly get together, no problem just call Gino's.

Here at Gino's, we also give back to the community. We are proud sponsors of the West Hempstead Little League, soccer, baseball, and football clubs. We believe that the children of the community have to be active and involved with their friends and family. Being part of any club or sports team teaches them discipline and is a great way to interact with other children. 

So please, come on in and enjoy a slice of Amore!
