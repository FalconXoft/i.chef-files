Feast of the Seven Fishes?!

Feast of the Seven Fishes, Café Mars style! For the 3rd year in a row, we will close it out with an entire week of special services. Our version of the Feast is a 7-course, family-style (ish!) tasting menu, consisting of 12 different servings with 20+ products of the sea!  To tie a bow on and celebrate our year, each of the dishes will be pulled from one of 2025’s  Global Italian regions, including 2024’s Tropical Italian dinners.
We hope you will join us December 17-21 in celebrating the holidays with indulgent seafood, festive vibes, and perhaps a seasonal beverage pairing?! It’s the perfect night out for friends and family looking for an unusual take on an age-old Italian tradition.
