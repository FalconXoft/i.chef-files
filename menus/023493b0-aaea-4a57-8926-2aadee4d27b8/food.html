About Us

FOOD is an eclectic cafe with a distinct style that serves seasonal, healthy and delicious food. Our menu of morningfood, soups, sandwiches, signature salads and housemade sweets is supplemented by distinctly prepared daily specials showcasing seasonal farmers' market offereings. FOOD is a neighborhood market providing you with the new essentials. Whether you are looking for everyday items to stock your larder or choice ingredients to create a home-cooked meal, we've got the goods. Our prepared entrees, soups, sides and sweets as well as our cheese and wine selections, can make for a complete take-home meal or complement your own home-made dishes.We are caterers, ready to provide your company with breakfast, lunch and meeting food. In addition, our experienced professional staff can prepare and serve a special dinner for two, a cocktail reception for seventy-five or a celebration for several hundred.
