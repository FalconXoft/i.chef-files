Smiles from Every Direction

Imagine being surrounded by some of life's greatest treats. Authentic Italian gelato. Rich espresso drinks. Delicious sandwiches and pizza. Decadent desserts.


You don't have to dream, because all of this and more can be found at Pino Gelato Cafe, located within the Silver Sands Premium Outlets in Destin, FL.

Our scrumptious gelato is an out-of-the ordinary gourmet treat. Hey, you deserve it.

Sweets, Treats and Unique Eats

Authentic Italian Gelato & Sorbetto

Gelato is gourmet ice cream made in the Italian tradition. The result is a denser, richer and more intensely flavored experience than boring old ice cream. You can't buy this in your grocery store!

Premium Espresso Drinks

The coffee beans used to make our hot or cold espresso drinks are the finest in the world, and our house blend is selected to deliver a rich, aromatic coffee experience. Our machines are imported from Italy. 

Sandwiches, Pizza & More

Our specialty sandwiches are definitely "special." Try our world-famous Muffaletta, Or, our hot and cheesy Cubano with seasoned pulled pork. Don't forget our Italian Classico Panini with prosciutto and  flatbread. 

Decadent Desserts

We offer a variety of desserts to satisfy your sweet tooth. These range from Salted Caramel Cheesecake, to the Ultimate Chocolate Cake to our rich authentic Tiramisu. Pair any with a hot coffee and you're in heaven!

Seaside Sweeteries, Sandestin Resort

If you are staying in or visiting the Sandestin Resort, be sure and visit our sister store, Seaside Sweeteries.  Located in The Village at Baytowne Wharf , Seaside Sweeteries is a unique and wonderful place to get your sweet or coffee fix. We offer authentic gelato, premium hot and cold coffee drinks and an assortment of delicious desserts. Plus, you can relax on our covered back porch with a beautiful lakeside view.


Whether you’re a vacationer looking for a special place to make a memory, or you’re a local resident looking for your “go to” spot for coffee and dessert, Seaside Sweeteries is a great choice.

Pino Gelato Cafe at Silver Sands Premium Outlets

Silver Sands Premium Outlets is home to over 100 designer and name-brand outlet stores.  Visitor's can find a variety of shops ranging from women's apparel and accessories to housewares and sporting goods. 


Renowned as the premier shopping destination along Northwest Florida's Emerald Coast, the outlet mall is conveniently located off US Highway 98, one mile west of Sandestin Golf and Beach Resort. The complex features covered sidewalks, lush landscaping and a children's play area.  It's also the longtime home of Pino Gelato Cafe!
