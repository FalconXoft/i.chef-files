Reviews

Review by - Google

five star review by Julia R:

By far the most authentic Italian in an absolutely precious atmosphere with attentive, kind, spectacular service. We came into the city for just a few hours so our newborn could meet her uncle and only had time for one meal. It truly exceeded expectations - if you have time for 1 meal in New York City, this is the place. A 5pm Saturday reservation in the back dining room was truly impeccable. Menu highlights include mushroom truffle rice balls, ahi tuna salad, pesto gnocchi and lobster ravioli. Don't forget to stop at the ATM as it is cash only.

Review by - Google

five star review by Emma E:

Very quaint and warm place. Loved the large sky windows, really gave the restaurant this home feeling. The burrata with anchovies was so fresh. Very hard to find a spot that has a spicy red sauce without cream and wow was theirs so good!! Small drink selection but really great selection of unique options. Staff were very friendly and attentive. Will definitely be back!

Review by - Google

five star review by Annie S:

You could blindfold me, sit me down inside this restaurant and ask me where I am and I might say Italy. Skip the restaurants on the main strip in Little Italy and come here - you won't regret it. Incredibly authentic food, atmosphere and service at a very reasonable price. I had the best pasta here since I went to Italy 5 years ago and I would recommend this to anyone visiting NYC. An absolute can't-miss spot.

We have been a few times before and as usual.. the food was great. I really enjoyed the "home" made lasagna.. the mushroom appetizer was exceptional.. the service was good, maybe too good. It's always a difficult balance between great service and feeling like you are being pushed out... Will be back.
