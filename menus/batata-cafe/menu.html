About Us

Batata Cafe & Grille has been feeding the Long Island community since August of 2005. We are a woman-owned and operated small business located in Northport, NY.

Our extensive menu includes sandwiches, burgers, salads, soups, quesadillas, wings, smoothies & ACAI bowls.

Batata Café & Grille won “Best of Long Island” for our original veggie burger in 2019.

We are favored by the vegan and vegetarian community as well as families with a variety of taste buds.

Each menu item is made to order and prepared with fresh daily produce.

There is Food For Every Soul here at Batata Café & Grille. Stop by and see for yourself.
