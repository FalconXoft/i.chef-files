About

Located on the landmark corner of West 4th St. and 7th Avenue, our newest Little Ruby’s Cafe features an airy, multi-level space. A first for Little Ruby’s, West Village offers a full bar with an exclusive cocktail program alongside our classic menu with new dishes unique to the location. With an expansive terrace and sweeping windows, the West Village restaurant invites guests to absorb the energy of the neighborhood as they enjoy their morning brekkie or grab cocktails and dinner with friends. 

Now Open Reservations and walk-ins accepted. Little Ruby’s West Village is  open 7 days a week for brekkie, lunch, and dinner.
