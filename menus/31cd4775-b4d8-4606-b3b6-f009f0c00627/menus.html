Eating Well is Living Well

The Greek cuisine unlike other western diets, approaches food not simply as a need to satisfy hunger but as a means of enhancing and enjoying life itself. The combination of fruits and vegetables, fresh fish, lean meats, olive oil and grains produces a diet rich in antioxidants and unsaturated fats and offers uncompromised quality that celebrates the most basic of human desires which satisfies the palette as well as all the other senses.

Catering For All Occasions

In addition to our dine-in and takeout business, we also offer delicious, affordable catering. Our catering selections are ideal for office meetings, client presentations, home and office parties, and any other occasion that calls for dishes made with our own long-standing Greek recipes, homemade sauces and desserts, and fresh ingredients.
