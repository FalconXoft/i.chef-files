LET US TAKE YOU TO NAPOLI!

The mission at SIMÒ is to make pizza napoletana accessible to everyone without compromising size or quality. 

SIMÒ takes its name from its founder, Simone Falco, whose family nicknamed him Simò. Simone has channeled his roots from his hometown of Napoli to bring NYC SIMÒ PIZZA, a fast-casual dining concept.

OUR MENU

In NAPOLI, pizza is the most delicious and approachable food. It is a food that can be eaten everywhere and at any time. SIMÒ Pizza can be eaten on the go or in the restaurant. Ultimately, we want the essence of pizza to shine through.
