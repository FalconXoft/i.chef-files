90 seconds to napoli

“90 SECONDS” IS THE AMOUNT OF TIME IT WILL TAKE A PIZZA AT SIMÒ PIZZA TO COOK IN THE OVEN, but it is ALSO SIMÒ’S PROMISE TO ITS CUSTOMERS THAT YOUR DINING EXPERIENCE WITH US WILL BE QUICK AND EASY. 

The SIMÒ menu features pizzas (including vegetarian and vegan options) salads, specials, and desserts. Each of our personal-size pizzas are priced between $13 - $18, without compromising the size or quality of the pie. All dishes at SIMÒ are made to order with Italian ingredients. The drink menu includes white, red and rosé wine, prosecco, beers, and Italian sodas.

we make it gluten free, too!

Although we want you to experience our pizza the most traditional way, we understand everyone has their personal preferences and dietary needs. We offer gluten free pizza crust that has a blend of corn, rice, buckwheat and gluten free wheat. While this crust is gluten free, it is not certified for those with Celiac disease.
