About us

LET'S SPEAK GREEK WITH OUR DISHES

Located on Broadway, in the heart of Astoria, one of New York’s largest Greek communities, Amylos Taverna is redefining Greek traditional cuisine. Amylos celebrates the art, architecture, and food of Greek culture in a way that hasn’t yet been offered. Our setting from our bar/lounge to our dining area evokes feelings of the stylish mainland chic of old Athens.
