    About

    PASSION FOR PIZZA

    At Slice Astoria, we're all about sharing our passion for Italian cuisines & pizza with our beloved Astoria community. Our journey began with a mission to craft the perfect pizza, using only the finest, locally-sourced ingredients, and a dash of tradition. Our skilled artisans hand-stretch the dough and bake it to crispy perfection in wood-fired ovens, delivering a slice of Italy to your table. But we offer more than just pizza; our menu boasts a delectable array of Italian dishes and snacks, including mozzarella balls, pastas, wings, heroes, and more. We take pride in treating our customers like family and promise to make every visit a flavorful, unforgettable experience. Join us at Slice Astoria and savor the difference.
