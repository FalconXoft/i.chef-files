About Chelsea

The rich, hearty flavors of Italian cuisine

Chelsea Ristorante combines the rich tradition of Italian cuisine with antipasti, homemade pasta, and risotto which are considered some of the best, and with our famous wood burning brick oven, Chelsea Ristorante satisfies even the most discriminating tastes. Join us for Lunch, Brunch, Dinner, Cocktails or a quick bite at the bar.
