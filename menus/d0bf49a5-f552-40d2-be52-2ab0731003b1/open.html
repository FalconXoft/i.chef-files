Beyond Menu - Food Delivery Find food for pickup or delivery from the best local restaurants near you. Getting hungry? Order food from the best independent restaurants in your town and pay less while doing it. There are no hidden fees for you or your favorite restaurants, just low, transparent menu prices and good food. SUPPORT YOUR NEIGHBORHOOD You won’t find big chains on Beyond Menu. We specifically work with independent restaurants to support local community restaurants and help diners find the best food in their area. Browse the menu and find some amazing food to order. ORDER TAKEOUT OR DELIVERY Choose from thousands of different local restaurants and over 25 different cuisines. It’s time you try something new and explore the restaurant community around you to see what you’ve been missing out on all this time. Order it for pickup or stay in and order food delivered to your door. SIMPLE ORDERING & LOWER PRICES We don’t charge you or the restaurant huge fees and that translates to lower menu prices. Order foods from local restaurants at a better price and get it done in just a few simple clicks. LOCAL COUPONS It isn’t just about lower menu prices. Restaurants on Beyond Menu frequently offer coupons and free food. Get out and explore something new and you might get a free appetizer or other discount while you do it! AVAILABLE ALL OVER THE USA We work with independent restaurants so you can order the best local food no matter where you are! We’re in Baltimore, Denver, Los Angeles, New York, Phoenix, San Francisco, Boston, Houston, Miami, Omaha, Portland, Seattle, Chicago, Las Vegas, New Jersey, Philadelphia, San Diego, Washington DC, and more!

  • The OTHER food delivery app

    09/21/2020

    EricHopeSF

    I use BeyondMenu all the time. They’ve got my favorite Asian restaurants on it. Ordering delivery through BeyondMenu almost always turns out to be cheaper than doing the same thing with GrubHub, Seamless, Caviar, DoorDash, etc, as they don’t seem to add service fees ON TOP of your order, like those services do. And often, the minimum order is less than it is on those other services, too, so I don’t have to order quite as much food to get my meal delivered (I’m just a single guy. I can only eat SO much). I get the feeling that BeyondMenu also costs THE RESTAURANTS less, as I suspect they don’t charge as much of a fee as the others (I don’t KNOW this. I’m just guessing), so I suspect it’s better for the restaurant. So, if I can help it, I try to go with BeyondMenu first, and THEN the others. They definitely seem more geared towards Asian restaurants, though, so if you’re looking for a salad from MIXT, it might not work for you. In any case, I appreciate BeyondMenu.

  • Customer experience is lacking

    04/27/2018

    what^up

    If you are comparing this to uber eats you may be disappointed. First off, there is only one place where it shows you when your delivery is expected - it really should be listed in the summary of the order (though that is messed up as well since it says I paired with Google Pay even though I am on an iPhone...). There is no way to track your order’s progress in the app. They do offer text message updates but they are vague. Unfortunately part of my order was incorrect (missing items). I called Beyond Menu and the woman had to call the restaurant to fix anything (refund). She then called me back insisting that the restaurant sent the right order and that she cannot refund anything (stating she spoke to the cook and he was adamant it was correct). To me, you work with the customer and then deal with the business after you make sure the user is treated well, not insinuating they are a liar. I ended up calling the restaurant and they said he told Beyond Menu he wouldn’t issue a refund until he spoke with the cook, which he did and they admitted their mistake and refunded me the item. The restaurant was very kind, unlike the agent at Beyond Menu. The reason for 2 stars instead of 1 is because the menu interface is pretty smooth. Not great since you kind of have to guess on some thing (like swiping right gets you a different menu) but it didn’t glitch or anything. Overall, probably not my go to food delivery app.
