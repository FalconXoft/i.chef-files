About This Café

Paris Baguette is your neighborhood bakery café, bringing expertly crafted baked and brewed goods to the Queens community. Our bakers, cakers, and baristas are serving up handcrafted cakes, freshly baked pastries and breads, delicious Lavazza coffee, and more. Stop by and cozy up with a warm pastry or cool down with a gourmet iced latte. We look forward to bringing joy to your day! Sweeten your experience with PB Rewards, where you can enjoy exclusive offers on pastries, coffee, and more.
