Wings Over Nutrition & Allergen Information

At Wings Over, we believe in providing not only flavorful meals but also transparency about what goes into them. Our Nutrition & Allergen Guide offers detailed information on calories, ingredients, and potential allergens for all our menu items. Whether you’re counting calories, managing dietary restrictions, or simply curious about our offerings, this guide empowers you to make informed choices that suit your lifestyle.

Addition Menu Items & Services

Shareables

Our shareables are perfect for groups or anyone looking to round out their order with something extra. Choose from customizable trays of fresh Caesar salad, crispy fries, & 5-packs of cookies, ranch, or blue cheese. These crowd-pleasers are ideal for parties, team meals, or game day spreads!

Catering

Feeding a crowd? Wings Over Catering has you covered with crave-worthy trays of tenders, wings, fries, salads, and more—perfect for office lunches, team celebrations, parties, and game day spreads. Our customizable catering options make it easy to serve up bold flavors everyone will love.

Gift Cards

Give the gift of flavor with Wings Over gift cards. Whether you’re celebrating a birthday, saying thanks, or just spreading the wing love, our gift cards make it easy. Choose from digital cards for instant delivery or physical cards to hand-deliver the surprise – either way, it’s a crowd-pleaser.

A Wings Over Menu to Make Every Customer Happy

At Wings Over, our mission is simple: To serve the best chicken wings and tenders, and to make customers happy.

We’ve stayed true to our roots since 1999, offering the crispiest chicken wings and juiciest hand-breaded tenders, paired with the finest sauces, dry rubs, and our signature fresh-made ranch and blue cheese dips. But what really sets us apart is our commitment to listening to you, our customers.

Your feedback drives us to continuously improve and ensure we’re delivering exactly what you love. We’re here to make you happy and provide that magical Wings Over experience every time.
