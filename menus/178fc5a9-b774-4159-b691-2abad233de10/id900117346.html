10 points for every $1 spent when you order DIG for delivery or pickup on the DIG app. With our new DIG Rewards program, every purchase unlocks reward progress and points toward the next loyalty tier. Use the DIG app to get your lunch or dinner delivered fast, for free. Order food online, or simply see where your nearest restaurant is located and what’s on the menu. Save your favorite items or reorder from your order history to quickly satisfy your vegetable needs. Getting DIG has never been easier. Schedule orders for takeout, and we’ll let you know when to pick it up. Or, we’ll deliver your food directly to you. EARN REWARDS Earn and redeem rewards both in-store and online through the app. CUSTOMIZE YOUR ORDER Craving a bowl? Prefer farro over rice? Feeling more greens over charred chicken? Build your own meal. We’ll take care of the rest. FIND YOUR NEAREST DIG If you’re looking for food in New York, Boston, Philadelphia, or a few other select locations, we can show you your closest restaurant. BROWSE THE MENU Our menu changes seasonally with what we can get our hands on — so make sure to check out the menu to see what we’re offering. Our menu also shows our many vegan food options, as well as allergy warnings. TRACK YOUR PURCHASES Really into what you ordered the last time, but can’t quite remember what it was? Take a look, and order it again. EAT MORE VEGETABLES They just taste good — especially when they’re delivered to your doorstep. Questions or concerns?   • Crappy Service

    04/18/2020

    a-over

    I ordered the other day, and after 1.5 hours the order was marked as delivered. I went outside to look for my food, but it was nowhere to be found. I tried looking through the app for any helpful updates but couldn’t find anything (there’s no map in the app or delivery man phone number). Tried calling the restaurant and thankfully they picked up on the 2nd call. They then proceeded to tell me that the order was there but that they don’t deliver to my address anymore (i.e. an hour - hour and a half after I placed the order) so they can’t deliver it and they cancelled my order (on the app though they marked it as delivered) . The guy on the phone told me he’d give me a refund, but they only gave me a PARTIAL refund for an order THEY cancelled after 1.5 hrs. I contacted support through the app, but no response. I had several mistakes happen with Dig Inn in the past as well. The last order right before this one arrived completely wrong , and a bunch of things I paid for (and some i paid extra for) didn’t arrive with the order .

    Developer Response

    We’re so sorry to hear about this experience - it certainly doesn’t sound up to our standards. We’re working hard to answer customer emails asap, so be on the lookout for an email from our Guest Experience team to help make this right for you.
