- ABOUT US -

We are one of the few locations on Long Island that serves true authentic coal fired Neapolitan pizza and the only place to get pizza this unique. When the summer months hit, Port Jefferson Village is jumping and the hottest spot in town is The Pie. In the Winter, The Pie is the perfect cozy spot to get together with your loved ones. Whether you are part of the community or just visiting, our family friendly atmosphere is the place to be. Our pizza is hand-crafted thin, crispy, and well done. Each pie is made with only the freshest ingredients served by our courteous staff. A great selection of appetizers, fresh salads, homemade pastas, and desserts there is something here for everybody. We are located in the heart of Main Street, just a short walk from the marina.
