WE GAVE A NEW MEANING TO THE WORD "PIZZA"

Annadale Town is a great place where you can come to enjoy one of the best pizzas in Staten Island!!

Mona Lisa's goal is to provide our customers with the best service possible, because we care. We take pride in our food and only the best ingredients are employed to create our classic dishes. Homemade bread is baked everyday by 11:00am on our premises.

Mona Lisa Pizzeria was voted "The Best Pizzeria" in North East America National Pizza Competition in Atlantic City 2012. Since then and till today, Lenny Giodano is an official member of US Pizza Team competing around the world!
