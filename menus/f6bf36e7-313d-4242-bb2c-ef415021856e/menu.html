About Us

Sushi House located at 1562 Lemoine Ave, in Fort Lee, NJ was established over 15 years ago. Our experienced chef has over 25 years of experience, preparing sashimi & specialty maki rolls for dine-in and takeout orders!

Our restaurant is known for its modern interpretation of classic dishes and its insistence on only using high quality fresh ingredients.

It is our great pleasure to serve you with the highest quality food and excellent service at the best prices. So, the next time you’re craving for some sushi, come by Sushi House! We look forward to seeing you!
