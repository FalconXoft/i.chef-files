About

Georgian House

Welcome to Georgian House, a delightful culinary gem nestled at 129 Brighton 1st St, Brooklyn, NY. This charming establishment invites food enthusiasts to embark on a gastronomic journey through the rich and diverse flavors of Georgian cuisine. With its inviting atmosphere and commitment to authenticity, Georgian House stands out as a must-visit for anyone in the area, including those frequenting Brighton Beach.

The menu is a vibrant tapestry of classic Georgian dishes that cater to a variety of palates. Start your meal with a tantalizing Georgian Salad featuring crunchy cucumbers, ripe tomatoes, and aromatic walnuts, dressed in spices that are uniquely Georgian. Priced at just $14, it sets the stage for the culinary magic to come.

  • Khachapuri: No visit is complete without indulging in the famed Khachapuri. This golden-brown bread, adorned with a luscious layer of melted cheese, is a comfort food that will have cheese lovers rejoicing. Try the Adjaruli Khachapuri, a delightful boat-shaped version crowned with a sunny-side-up egg, for $18.
  • Shish Kebab: Meat enthusiasts can relish the succulent shish kebabs, available in various options including chicken, pork, and lamb. Each skewer is grilled to perfection and served with crispy French fries. The Chalahach Shish Kebab – lamb chops for $32 – is particularly noteworthy, delivering a satisfying depth of flavor.

Reviews from patrons echo the restaurant's commitment to quality. Hossam Eshmawy describes the khachapuri as a "must-try" echoing what many others feel about this fulfilling dish. Nigar Bek's experience highlights not only the delicious food but also the enchanting atmosphere created by live music, showcasing Georgian House as more than just a dining spot; it’s a destination for cultural immersion.

However, as with any culinary experience, not every review sings praises. Some guests have noted concerns with service speed and food consistency, suggesting that while the flavors can be rich and authentic, the execution may sometimes fall flat. Yet, amidst varying opinions, the general sentiment remains: Georgian House is a place where comfort food and memorable experiences collide.

In closing, my sentiments align with those who cherish Georgian House for its unique offerings and the shared cultural journey it provides. Whether you're there for a hearty meal or to soak in the vibrant atmosphere, this restaurant has something for every palate. Give yourself time to savor each dish and let Georgian House transport you to the heart of Georgia with every delightful bite.

For inquiries and reservations, don’t hesitate to reach out at +1 718-759-6555. Elevate your dining experience today at Georgian House!
