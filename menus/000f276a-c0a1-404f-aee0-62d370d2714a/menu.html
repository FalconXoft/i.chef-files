We’re a family-owned restaurant in Rockaway Beach, New York specializing in Central Asian cuisine. Our menu is inspired by the many diverse cultures found along the Silk Road and beyond, spanning everything from everyday street food to the fine dining traditions of Samarkand we grew up with. Opened in 2013, Uma’s has grown into a New York staple, known for its chill atmosphere and eclectic vibes. Come join us for lunch or dinner any day of the week, to experience a unique culinary experience that bridges centuries of eastern tradition.
