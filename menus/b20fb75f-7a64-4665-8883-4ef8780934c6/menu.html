Plant-Based Goodness

Tamam is a New York City-based fast-casual Israeli Mediterranean plant-based eatery. We source the freshest products, using organic and fair trade ingredients. We pride ourselves in preparing everything in our kitchen, and cooking all food to order.

Our goal is to become a NYC institution that serves tasty, plant-based, accessible food for all, because we believe eating healthy is the best way to show yourself love.
