    Jody’s Club Forest

    Staten Island West Brighton Forest Avenue

    Founded by Mary and Jody Haggerty on June 1, 1976.

    Starting out with little to work with, they quickly began to create a local fan favorite for many families in the neighborhood. 

    Our staff is here to ensure that you and your guests have a delicious dinner, cold drinks, and good conversation.

     

    In 2015, Jody and his son Terence expanded the bar/restaurant from 8 tables to upwards of 30 tables. Their goal was to create a larger dining room to allow for patrons to bring family, gather with larger groups, and create a family friendly dining experience.

    We host celebrations of all kinds:

    Birthdays

    Showers

    Baptisms

    Rehearsal Dinners

    Business Meetings and many more!

    Utilizing both rooms, we can accommodate up to 80 people. The new addition shares the same ambiance as our 'old' side.

    We look forward to serving you soon.
