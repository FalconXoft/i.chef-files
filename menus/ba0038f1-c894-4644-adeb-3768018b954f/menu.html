About

The Factory

The Factory, a New American restaurant located at 451 Communipaw Ave J Cy Cy, NJ 07304, offers a range of dining options for all preferences. With a trendy and classy vibe, The Factory is known for its casual, yet chic ambiance.

The menu at The Factory features a variety of appetizers such as Beet Salad, Thai Calamari, and Buffalo Calamari. For entrees, guests can enjoy dishes like Mofongos, Beef Burger, and Turkey Burger, each bursting with flavor and satisfaction.

Customers have praised the restaurant for its ambiance, reasonable prices, and delicious drinks. The diverse range of options, including vegetarian choices, ensures that there is something for everyone to enjoy.

Overall, The Factory provides a unique dining experience with excellent service, great music, and a lively atmosphere. Whether you're looking for a place for a date night, a group gathering, or a night out, The Factory is worth a visit. Don't miss out on their Happy Hour specials and flavorful desserts. Experience the culinary delights at The Factory today!
