Savor the Upper East Side

Welcome to the heart of our kitchen. At Silver Star Restaurant in Lenox Hill, we take pride in serving timeless American classics with a neighborhood charm. From our sizzling burgers and hearty entrees, we offer a variety of dishes. Each item is crafted with quality ingredients. Our menu also features fresh salads and classic wraps. We serve every meal with true hospitality. This is where the Upper East Side comes for satisfying meals that feel like home.
