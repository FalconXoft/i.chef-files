The Story of Yakiniku Futago

Yakiniku Futago began its journey in 2010 as a small barbecue restaurant in Tokyo, Japan, covering just 355 square feet. Our dream has always been to bring smiles to our guests and share the delightful flavors of Japanese barbecue. Today, we proudly serve over 120 locations worldwide, including Japan, the United States, Hong Kong, and Taiwan.

We aim to be the most memorable Yakiniku restaurant you visit. Our team is dedicated to providing a wonderful Yakiniku experience, where every visitor leaves feeling happy and satisfied. Come join us for tasty food, friendly service, and unforgettable memories!

GOLD TONG MEMBERSHIP

After visiting 10 times, you will become a Gold Tong Member. As a member, you will enjoy the following benefits:

  • A gold tong with your name engraved as a gift

  • A free Gold Tong Member dessert with every visit

  • A special birthday dessert during your birthday month

  • A free beef cake gift on your birthday

* To receive the free beef cake, a reservation must be made at least one week in advance, and you will need to show an ID with your date of birth. You must have made at least five visits within a 12-month period after becoming a Gold Tong Member.
