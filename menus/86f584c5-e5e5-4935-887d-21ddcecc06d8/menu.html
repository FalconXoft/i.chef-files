Cho Dang Gol is known as a "Tofu House" for its variety of homemade tofu-based dishes, freshly made every morning. Every meal begins with a complimentary tofu starter. In addition to traditional Korean fare, CDG also features creative homemade recipes inspired by the classics.
For those seeking more variety within Korean cuisine, weekly and seasonal specials offer something new to explore.
