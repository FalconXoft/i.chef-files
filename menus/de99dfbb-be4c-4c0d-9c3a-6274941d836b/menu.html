The Best Kosher Burger in NYC

Welcome to the original Burger Joint where we build the best burger you’ve ever had. We start with only the freshest ingredients and build it just the way you like.  

All our Burgers are GRASS FED, HORMONE & ANTIBIOTIC FREE, AKA“NEVER EVER”

At Mocha Burger Times Square, we take our craft seriously. Our talented chefs are devoted to the art of burger making, infusing each creation with a perfect blend of flavors and textures. We source only the freshest ingredients to ensure an unparalleled taste experience. Every bite is a celebration of culinary excellence.
