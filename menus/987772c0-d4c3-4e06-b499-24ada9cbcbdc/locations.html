Caribbean Starr

Welcome to Caribbean Starr

At Caribbean Starr, the essence of the Caribbean is served on every plate, invoking a vibrant culinary heritage that delights the senses. Nestled in Harlem, this casual yet classy eatery specializes in breakfast, brunch, and seafood, offering a plethora of dishes such as authentic jerk chicken and flavorful oxtail. Vegetarian options abound, ensuring inclusivity for all diners. The warm atmosphere, enhanced by Jamaican music, creates a welcoming space for friends and families. Customers rave about the well-seasoned fare, reflecting a commitment to traditional flavors. Whether you savor a sweet chili salmon or a comforting mac and cheese, every bite tells a story of love and culinary excellence.
