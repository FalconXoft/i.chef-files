About us

At Tinos, we share our heritage through food. As a family-owned restaurant rooted in Greek tradition, we bring authentic Mediterranean flavors and warm Greek hospitality to NYC’s Upper East Side. Each dish reflects our commitment to quality and the warmth of Greek cooking.

Reviews

Review by - Google

five star review by Rachael P:

Tino's is what makes nyc food scene incredible. Small place but packed on a Saturday night. The dips were flavorful and they didn't skimp on the pita. Meat was tender and juicy. Spanakopita was flaky and fresh. Dessert was well balanced. If you are more than two people I would recommend a reservation since it fills up fast.

Review by - Google

five star review by Jenniviv B:

LOVE this place!! the lady/ hostess it's so nice and sweet and even gave us free desserts. The octopus was the best and it was so flavorful. We had the pita with the different dips and the pita was so warm and the dips are so tasty. The grilled shrimp was good and the Greek salad was fresh crispy and delicious. Will be coming back - such a lovely service and food.

Review by - Google

five star review by Eunice K:

Highly recommend. I loved the tomato cucumber salad and branzino which came with lemon potatoes. The house provided complimentary pita and babaganoush. Service was great and very welcoming. There were three other parties there that all spoke Greek which was a great sign. If you are celebrating a birthday, they will bring out sparklers!

Review by - Google

five star review by Emily T:

I enjoyed dining at Tino's. I came alone and ordered a few dishes to try. The Horiatiki salad, grilled octopus, and seafood orzo were all standout dishes! The dessert and limoncello at the end of the meal were fantastic as well
