Ciao Amico

Popolari is a Retro/Chic Italian Pizza + Pasta Bar offering Neapolitan and Classic American Pizza, along with Homemade Pasta. Located in the Brookdale area bordering Montclair and Glen Ridge, it features 60 outdoor seats, indoor dining with a pasta bar, and ample parking. Awarded "Best New Restaurant" in New Jersey.
