Sandwiched between Lady Liberty and One World Trade, in the heart of Lower Manhattan, you will find the deliciousness that is Bravo Kosher Downtown.

At Bravo you have freedom of choice. We let you choose from our mouth-watering dairy or meat menus at side by side locations and all under the superior OK Kosher Supervision. Here, where peddlers once sold pastrami on Rye and people prided themselves on the best pizza in town you can now enjoy it all. From our 100% American Angus beef to a healing bowl of chicken soup Bravo Kosher Downtown has it all! So, to paraphrase the lady in the Harbor, Give me your hungry, your tired, your poor, your huddled masses. Eat something! You look hungry!
