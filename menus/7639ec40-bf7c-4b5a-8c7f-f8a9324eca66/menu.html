Private events

Book your private party in williamsburg today, we cant wait to host and to toast!

Whether its a casual birthday lunch, pizza making party, rehearsal dinner, celebration of a new birth or a celebration of life itself, Oregano loves nothing more than a great party. Oregano offers group dining packages in an intimate and warm rustic setting. Guests start with a feast of antipasto plates with varying meats and cheeses, spring mix salads, and a variety of our specialty pizzas, followed by individually chosen main courses featuring our fresh homemade pasta dishes. Dessert trays will follow in a family style fashion, with specialty desserts, including but not limited to our homemade tiramisu, cheesecake, and of course, our nutella pizza Unlimited house white and red wines are also available for those who love to indulge. For parties requiring more privacy, partial and full restaurant buyouts are available.
