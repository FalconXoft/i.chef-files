Welcome to Jongro BBQ

Located in the heart of Flushing, NY, Jongro BBQ offers a genuine Korean BBQ experience. Guests can savor in-house butchered meats, freshly marinated and grilled right at their table.

Our signature Beef Platter, complemented by an array of traditional banchan, remains a favorite among many.
