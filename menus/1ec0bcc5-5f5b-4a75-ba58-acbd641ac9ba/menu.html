About Us

Gaudio's was opened in 1958 in Astoria New York. It is still in its original location. Louie Gaudio, the founder and father of the current owner Dominic, came to the United States from Calabria, Italy.When he opened, a slice of pizza was 15 cents and a soda cost 10 cents.
