Welcome to L&B Spumoni Gardens A Brooklyn Tradition Since 1939

Discover the heart of authentic Italian-American cuisine at L&B Spumoni Gardens, a family-owned landmark nestled in the vibrant neighborhood of Brooklyn, NY. For over 80 years, weve been serving up our iconic Sicilian square pizza, creamy homemade spumoni, and timeless Italian dishes that bring people together.

Whether youre here for a slice of history, a family celebration, or a quick bite, L&B Spumoni Gardens is more than just a restaurant its where generations gather to share food, laughter, and memories. From the first bite of our perfectly crisp, cheesy Sicilian pizza to the last spoonful of our velvety spumoni, every visit is a taste of tradition.

Come for the food, stay for the experience where Brooklyn charm meets Italian soul.
