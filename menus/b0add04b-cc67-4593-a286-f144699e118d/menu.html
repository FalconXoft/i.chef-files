What Were All About

Dirty Pierres opened in 1995 and has been kicking it old school for over twenty years. Started by Le Cordon Bleu-trained Chef Christopher Lucien File and now run by his sister Jamie after his passing, this neighborhood mainstay has been rockin the Hills with mouthwatering burgers, white wine mussels to die for, and a tasty Sangria thatll knock you right out (because all youll want to drink is more!).
