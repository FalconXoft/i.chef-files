About

Jamaica Breeze

If you're wandering through Jamaica, New York, and find a craving for authentic Caribbean flavors, look no further than Jamaica Breeze. Nestled at 232-02 Merrick Blvd, this gem of a restaurant promises more than just a meal—it offers a vibrant dining experience fueled by the spirit of the Caribbean. With a lively atmosphere chock-full of rhythmic tunes, it’s a place where every visit feels like an island getaway.

One of the standout features of Jamaica Breeze is its unique buffet-style service. Customers can create their own personal plate from a varied selection of mouthwatering dishes, ensuring that everyone gets to savor the flavors they love. From succulent oxtails to spicy jerk chicken, and from delectable pepper shrimp to tender curried fish, the options are abundant. As one satisfied patron, Troy Gooden, noted, the energy and vibe of the place keep you alive and excited to enjoy every bite.

The reviews speak volumes about the kitchen's dedication to quality. Monefa Shaw raves about the consistent excellence of every dish, while Jada Mui highlights the establishment's cleanliness—a valuable trait that enhances the dining experience. It’s not just the food; it’s the entire atmosphere that makes Jamaica Breeze a little treasure, as described by its patrons.

And let’s not forget the value! Many customers agree that the pricing is reasonable considering the quality and quantity of food offered. You pay by the pound, which is a fantastic deal for the variety on your plate. Helen Proffes encapsulated the sentiment perfectly by wishing she could give more than five stars, praising the combination of tasty offerings and affordability.

In a city teeming with food options, Jamaica Breeze stands out as a must-try for anyone yearning for genuine Caribbean and soul food. Make your way to this lively spot, grab a plate, and let the flavors take you on a culinary journey. Your taste buds will thank you!
