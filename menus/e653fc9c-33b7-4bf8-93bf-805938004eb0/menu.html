ABOUT MOMO HIBACHI

Momo is a hibachi steakhouse located in Sheepshead Bay, NY

We serve a variety of Japanese cuisine including hibachi, sushi, and steak. Our hibachi is a live performance where our world-class chefs cook and perform on a flat grill in front of you. It is an interactive experience great for all ages.
