Pierre Loti's Story

About Us

Named after the turn-of-the-century French novelist, Pierre Loti Wine Bar & Restaurant located between Union Square & Gramercy Park offers an extensive menu of European small plates & wines. French varietals dominate the offerings, but the selection includes a healthy mix of other regions and grapes (Austria, Italy, and even Turkey make appearances).

Specialties

There are daily pocket-friendly specials, and each pour comes with a little extra in a fancy carafe. The wine-friendly food menu includes an impressive choice of cheeses and small plates ranging from Mediterranean dips, salads and a delectable choice of chocolates for dessert. Vibe-wise, the ultra-dim lighting ensures the place stays rife with snuggly couples as well as groups of friends looking for an enjoyable night out in your favorite restaurant near Union Square
