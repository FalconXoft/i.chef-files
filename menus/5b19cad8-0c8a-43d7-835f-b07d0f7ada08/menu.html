ACERCA DE NOSOTROS About Us

Para Ti Colombia Con Sabor is a Hispanic American casual dining restaurant specializing in Colombian cuisine.
With over 25yrs of experience, Para Ti Colombia Con Sabor has been bringing the authentic Colombian flavor and culture to its community.
Our wide and varied menu is inspired by our culturally rich and diverse homeland of Colombia. We buy fresh and make our dishes to order, which reflects in the flavors of every meal served.
