Weekend Lunch & Dinner

All December ordering the Trio House Pie will result in donations to Trio House Press. 

Trio House Press is growing! Since 2012, we've published poetry and prose by a variety of underrepresented voices, on timely and important topics and angles. We need your help to keep publishing more memoirs, essay collections, and poetry collections that can inspire, raise awareness, and broaden the conversation.

Emily Hyland, our namesake founder and poet, has her second book “My Little Ghost” forthcoming with trio press.

Text “Trio” to 53-555 to donate and learn more
