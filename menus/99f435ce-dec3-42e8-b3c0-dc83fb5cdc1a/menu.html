What We Serve

At Nagle’s Bagels, we serve a variety of delicious sandwiches made with fresh ingredients. Enjoy favorites like the New Yorker with fried eggs and applewood smoked bacon, or the Classic Nagle featuring sliced smoked salmon. For a spicy kick, try the Spicy Nagle with crumbled goat cheese and peppadews. We also offer vegetarian options like the Jerusalem with hummus and veggies. Explore our full selection of flavorful sandwiches, perfect for any taste!
