About us

Fantastic soul food restaurant BLVD Bistro in Harlem reopened July 10th, 2019, bigger, better, and tastier than ever. The American soul food restaurant is run by husband and wife team Carlos and Markisha Swepson and will feature items such as the double-cut pork chop, jumbo shrimp and grits, and a southern dinner with fried chicken, collard greens, potato salad, and homemade biscuit. Guests can enjoy traditional seating, a wrap-around bar, or a chef’s table that features a special menu.
