About

Specialties



Napoli Pizza & Pasta offers mouthwatering, oven-baked pizzas made with the highest quality ingredients and dedication in every pie.

Full with all of your favorite toppings that you love, we'll always be here to satisfy your hunger. Aside from our pizza, we offer toppings you love are ready to satisfy your hunger, and that's just the beginning. We offer a great selection of pastas with a range of sauces and oven baked specialties such as lasagna, calzones and ziti. Add to that classic entrees like shrimp scampi and fried calamari, and you're in for a taste of Italy made easy with our online ordering that brings it to your door or that's ready for you to pick up.

With a cozy ambiance, we'll always provide great service and home cooked meals. If you can't make it in we will deliver to you seven days a week twelve hours a day! Our upstairs restaurant is available for parties and gatherings and we provide full catering services for any event.
Let us help you make your next birthday, anniversary, or celebration special.

For an authentic taste of Italy, come down and join the family at Napoli Pizza & Pasta.

History



Established in 2010.

In just over five years, Napoli Pizza & Pasta has earned a reputation for quality ingredients and classic New York style!
