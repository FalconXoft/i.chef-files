Elegant Private Gatherings Near New York’s Iconic Landmarks

With semi-private seating for up to 25 guests and standing room for 35, Brasserie Cognac 58th & 7th offers an intimate space perfect for private dinners, corporate gatherings, or cocktail receptions. Situated just steps from Central Park and Carnegie Hall, our new location is ideal for hosting memorable events in the heart of Manhattan. For larger parties, the full restaurant is available for exclusive buyouts.


Brasserie Cognac provides the perfect setting for any occasion, blending classic French hospitality with the energy of New York's most iconic destinations.
