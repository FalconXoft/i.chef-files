AN AMERICAN BISTRO

PERSHING SQUARE

Pershing Square is the quintessential American bistro located across from Grand Central Terminal. Known for our legendary pancakes, breakfast here is an absolute must, but the magic doesnt stop there. Lunch, dinner, and weekend brunch menus are equally outstanding along with our exceptional cocktail program, featuring expertly crafted drinks to elevate the whole bistro experience. Every meal is a celebration, and every visit is a chance to savor the best of modern American dining.
