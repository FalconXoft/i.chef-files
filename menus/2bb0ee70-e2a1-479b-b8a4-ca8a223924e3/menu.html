Welcome

Eastern European Restaurant

Searching for a place that will bring you back to the mother-native Odesa, despite staying in the heart of Manhattan? Mishka Soho is the Eastern European restaurant NYC that brings your home, your roots, your soulful atmosphere and your favorite dishes to the center of New York City. 

If you feel like missing the taste of borscht from your childhood or just want to try something new, our restaurant blends elegant dining with comfort and tradition. For special occasions or just an ordinary evening, Mishka Soho does its best to keep every visit in your memory.

Menu

At Mishka Soho, we don’t just serve food; we bring back to life such familiar but perhaps slightly forgotten recipes of Eastern European cuisine. Each dish on our menu is prepared with the finest ingredients, following long-established traditions. From fluffy dumplings to rich goulash and golden potato pancakes, our chef prepares each dish as if for the family.

So, if you want to immerse yourself  in the homely, family and calm atmosphere of Eastern Europe, while enjoying your favorite dishes prepared in the best traditions, pay attention to our à la Carte menu and do not postpone your visit to Mishka Soho for later!

Gastronomy

Eastern European Cuisine

Eastern European food is more than just a meal. Instead, it’s a connection to history, culture, and community. At Mishka Soho, we are proud to bring this vision to Manhattan. 

Our recipes carry the spirit of family traditions, where food has always been a way to bring people closer together. That’s why at our table, you’re not just eating – you are either getting back or becoming part of the culture that values every moment shared over a meal with family and relatives.

Private Events
Looking for an ideal venue? Look no further. Corporate dinners, and special occasions, let us handle your needs.

    Bar

    If you have ever been to an Eastern European restaurant in Manhattan, you probably know that no dining experience will feel complete without high-quality drinks, accurately matching the dishes of your choice. Whether you prefer a sip of authentic horilka, a classic cocktail, or a glass of wine, we have options to complement every mood. Our bar menu is carefully designed to balance tradition with modern taste, making it easy to find something you love. A toast with friends or a quiet drink to unwind – our bar is ready to add that extra sparkle to your evening.

    Special Events

    Planning to celebrate something special? Mishka Soho’s banquet hall is the perfect choice for hosting private events. We are proud to offer this space that combines the spirit of Eastern Europe with the elegance of Manhattan in a unique way. Mishka welcomes you to celebrate a birthday, host a wedding or organize a small business gathering – for each of these occasions, we will carefully select the menu that matches the tastes of your guest and do our best so that you can enjoy the evening!
