About

Market Crates is the home to wholesome, healthy and delicious, local and sustainable foods in the heart of NYC. We pride ourselves on being sustainable with our food and are environmentally friendly. Healthy eating isn’t an option it’s a right!    

MISSION

We use high quality ingredients and create affordable, healthy meals - so you can feel good about eating the best, locally sourced, farm fresh & seasonal foods.

RESPONSIBILITY

MARKETCRATES provides locally grown greens & grains to help you achieve a better and richer lifestyle. We believe that sustainability is essential for our environment and the food industry. We use LED lights and biodegradable trays.


Market Crates is committed to giving back by supporting No Kid Hungry to end childhood hunger in America.
Through awareness and fundraising, we can help to fund the programs we all need to succeed in closing the doors on childhood hunger. For more in- formation go to
nokidhungry.org
