About

Our chefs specialize in authentic and delicious food. The staff at Triangolo Pizzeria is devoted to serving customers mouthwatering dishes that feature the highest quality ingredients available. Large Pie, Medium Pie and Small Pie are all prepared quickly and delivered to you right away.

Triangolo Pizzeria uses only the freshest ingredients around, ensuring that every dish is of the highest possible quality. Not sure what you're in the mood for? Try one of our specialties including Pizza Slice , Grandma Pie, Sicilian Pizza Slice, Pizza Slice with Topping, and more.
