about krave it

Krave It is a family-owned business, founded in 2015 by husband and wife Vishee and Jenna Mandahar. High school sweethearts, born and raised in Queens, NY with an equal love of food and creativity. The success of opening their first location in Bayside, Queens led to opening up 2 additional locations with hopes of even more expansion.

What sets Krave IT apart from others is our dedication to ALWAYS using fresh ingredients, and creating homemade, delicious, and unique dishes you can not find anywhere else. We currently operate multiple locations with an incredible range of menu options that will find you wanting a bit of everything.

We specialize in creative, out-of-the-box sandwich and pizza options that can not be replicated elsewhere.
