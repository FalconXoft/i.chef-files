Welcome to Don Alex: A Taste of Peru in Rego Park

Nestled in the heart of Rego Park, New York, Don Alex is a culinary haven that brings the rich flavors of Peru right to your plate. Located at 9705 64th Ave, this charming eatery has become a local favorite, offering a diverse menu that showcases the best of Peruvian cuisine.

Why Don Alex Stands Out

At Don Alex, the food is not just a meal; it's a celebration of authentic ingredients and traditional recipes. From the flavorful Pollo a la Brasa served with aromatic yellow rice and beans to the tantalizing Ceviche de Mariscos that dances on your palate, each dish is crafted with care and passion. The extensive menu promises something for everyone, including:

  • Appetizers: Salchipapas, Anticuchos, and Tamales that set the stage for your dining experience.
  • Ceviche: Delight in a variety of ceviche options, including Ceviche de Camarones and Ceviche Mixto.
  • Main Dishes: Savor the iconic 1/4 Pollo with rice and signature sides for a filling, satisfying experience.

Customer Experiences

Don Alex has garnered positive reviews from patrons who return time and again for its delicious offerings and inviting atmosphere. Customer Peggy Santaya remarks, "I know this restaurant for many years, really love this place, food is delicious, staff is amazing, I will keep coming here." Mark D emphasizes the value, stating, "The ambiance is cozy and clean, feels a bit fancy but the menu prices are affordable." It’s clear that the combination of great food and excellent service keeps guests coming back.

Ambiance and Service

Whether you're enjoying a meal inside the well-decorated spaces or outside on the patio, Don Alex provides a warm and welcoming environment. The staff is consistently praised for their attentive and friendly service, ensuring a pleasant dining experience. As Mike A puts it, "Just a very pleasant atmosphere with very good fresh food."

Final Thoughts

If you're seeking a dining experience that embodies both quality and authenticity, look no further than Don Alex. With generous portions, a pleasing ambiance, and dishes that transport you straight to Peru, it's a neighborhood gem that deserves a visit. Don’t miss out on the opportunity to indulge in the robust flavors and community spirit that make Don Alex special.
