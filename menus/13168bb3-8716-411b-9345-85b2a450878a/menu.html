Excellent place to eat Thai food! We tried the mango salad, pineapple fried rice, cashew nut, chicken satay, and tom yum soup. Absolutely everything was great! Even the atmosphere of the place is relaxing, and the service is friendly without being extra. All in all a great place.

★★★★★

High recommend you visit this Thai Restaurant. It isn't a big place, it holds up to about 30 people. It's small but big in flavor and portion size. Everything our table had was fresh, fresh, fresh. Vibrant Thia flavor. Crab fried rice had crab meat you could see. The chunks of crab were so tasty that you could taste the salt water it came out of. The noodle dishes with seafood so delicious. Deserts, yum. Try the fried ice cream. Warm coconut custard was very good. I can keep going on, but instead, get yourself over to this place so that you can experience the magic for yourself.

-Edgardo R.
