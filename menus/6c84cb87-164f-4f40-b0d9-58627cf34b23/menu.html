Shawarma. Kebabs. Bowls. All In

Everything on the shawarma menu starts fresh. Our meats are trimmed and marinated in-house. Shawarma is stacked and slow-roasted daily. Falafel is made by hand.  We offer plates, bowls, and pitas with a full lineup of sauces, grains, salads, and grilled vegetables, all made with seasonal ingredients.

Hosting something? Make it unforgettable. Ask about catering, private events, and the latest family meal deals. Whether you’re craving a beef shish kebab, chicken shawarma, or a ribeye steak, our menu is packed with options that deliver flavor, balance, and real satisfaction – no matter how you build your meal.
