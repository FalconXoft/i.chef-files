THE CUSTOM HOUSE INFORMATION

Biography

The Custom House is a warm and welcoming bar for both locals and tourists. A long classic marble bar is complimented by a fireplace and couch area. Enjoy our American and European influenced food in our spacious dining room or dine al fresco on our front patio and watch the world go by or sit in the cool shade of our back garden.

What will you Have?

We have a large selection of local & imported draft and bottled beers, an extensive selection of Bourbons and Scotches, large variety of wines by the glass or bottle or why not try a specialty cocktail.

Having a Leaving Party or Event?

Let The Custom House take all the hassle out of the planning. Our experienced staff will take care of you and your guests in a professional and friendly manner. We offer many different packages, so you can customize your event. Call us 718-643-600 or ask a member of staff for details.
