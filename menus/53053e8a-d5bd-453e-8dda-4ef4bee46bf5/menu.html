SHANGHAI FLAVORS & HANDMADE DIM SUM, FRESH EVERY DAY


Located in the heart of New York City, Hao Chinese Cuisine brings together the delicate elegance of authentic Shanghai cuisine and the bold, vibrant flavors of Chongqing specialties. We are dedicated to traditional craftsmanship, using only the freshest ingredients to create dishes that honor both heritage and innovation. From the refined techniques and subtle flavors of Shanghai dishes to the spicy, aromatic layers of Chongqing cuisine, every plate tells a story of passion and precision.

Our handmade dim sum series is crafted fresh daily with care and skill. Among our most beloved dishes are the soup dumplings (Xiao Long Bao), with delicate wrappers encasing rich, flavorful broth and tender meat, and our seafood pancake, known for its crispy texture and savory, ocean-fresh taste.

Our signature noodle selections are equally popular. The Dan Dan Noodles, with their perfect balance of nutty, spicy, and savory flavors, are a must-try, while the Sichuan Cold Noodles offer a refreshing yet fiery kick that keeps guests coming back for more. For those who crave bold, authentic tastes, our traditional mala (spicy and numbing) dishes capture the signature heat and complexity of Sichuan cuisine.

Over the years, Hao Chinese Cuisine has been featured in renowned publications such as The New York Times, The New Yorker, and 50 Best Discovery, and has been honored multiple times with the prestigious Michelin Bib Gourmand award for exceptional dining at great value.

At Hao, every meal is more than just dining—it’s a cultural and culinary journey. Whether you are gathering with family, hosting friends, or celebrating special moments, we strive to offer a warm atmosphere, heartfelt service, and dishes that embody the very best of Chinese cuisine.
