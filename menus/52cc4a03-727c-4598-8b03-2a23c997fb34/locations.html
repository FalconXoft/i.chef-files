About

Little Purity

If you're in the mood for a cozy and welcoming diner experience, look no further than Little Purity in Brooklyn, NY. This quaint breakfast and brunch spot offers a range of delicious options that will leave you satisfied and coming back for more.

Located at 390 7th Ave, Little Purity provides a diner vibe with friendly owners and staff, creating a charming atmosphere for diners. The menu features a variety of dishes, including fried fish fillet, grilled chicken sandwiches, and even assorted fruit pies and carrot cake for dessert lovers.

Customers rave about the tasty food at Little Purity, with one reviewer mentioning their love for the pancakes, tuna melt, mozzarella sticks, and chicken pot pie. Another satisfied diner praised the French toast with bananas as a top-tier dish, highlighting the well-made breakfast options available.

With options for takeout, delivery, and catering services, Little Purity is the perfect spot for a casual meal with friends or family. Whether you're craving a classic diner experience or looking to try something new, Little Purity has something for everyone. So, next time you're in the mood for a wholesome meal in a welcoming setting, be sure to check out Little Purity in Brooklyn.
