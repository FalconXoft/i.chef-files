Located in a landmark 1828 West Village brownstone, RedFarm serves innovative Chinese cuisine with playful presentations, bold flavors, and top-quality ingredients. Our menu features inventive takes on Chinese classics and whimsical dim sum, alongside a robust bar program with wine, sake, craft beer, and unique cocktails.

Designed with sharing in mind for groups small and large, RedFarm’s cozy yet lively setting features farmhouse-style décor, intimate dining rooms, and warm hospitality that make it a downtown favorite.

Every dumpling is crafted from scratch, with wrappers, fillings, and folds handmade in-house. We proudly offer a wide range of vegetarian and vegan dishes, and much of our menu—including many of our dumplings—is naturally gluten-free or can be made gluten-free upon request.

RedFarm West Village is open for dinner seven days a week, and for lunch and weekend brunch on Saturday and Sunday.
