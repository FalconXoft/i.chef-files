About

Rochdale Chicken And Fish

Welcome to Rochdale Chicken And Fish, a hidden gem located in the heart of Jamaica, NY. This vibrant establishment is not just your average fast-food joint; it’s a culinary experience, specializing in delicious chicken wings and soul food that warms the heart and satisfies the palate.

Situated at 11829 Guy R Brewer Blvd, Rochdale Chicken And Fish is the ideal spot for anyone craving a taste of Southern hospitality. Their menu is a celebration of comfort food, offering a variety of dishes such as Chicken and Grits, Shrimp and Grits, and even Fried Crab Legs at market value. The chef's mastery shines through in every dish, ensuring you get an authentic taste of soul food that rivals any homemade meal.

  • Customer Favorites: The chili wings come highly recommended, as noted by customer Marz M., who raves about their affordability and flavor.
  • Diverse Options: Not only do they excel in chicken, but their offerings extend to vegetarian options and seafood delights like Catfish with Grits and Lobster Tail with Grits.
  • Family-Friendly: It's a casual place that's great for kids and features many amenities including outdoor seating, free Wi-Fi, and even happy hour specials.
  • Convenience: Whether you choose to dine in, takeout, or have it delivered, Rochdale Chicken And Fish is equipped to cater to your needs.

In reviews, patrons consistently highlight the hearty portions and unforgettable taste of the dishes. Rebekah W. notes the oxtails as a standout, while Natasha M. can't get enough of their catfish and peach cobbler, a perfect ending to any meal. Despite some comments on portion sizes, the robust flavors more than compensate, leaving you eager for a return visit.

If you’re in search of a cozy spot that embraces the spirit of soul food while facilitating convenience, look no further than Rochdale Chicken And Fish. Their welcoming atmosphere, thoughtful service, and dedication to quality create a dining experience that will have you coming back for more.

To experience this culinary delight yourself, give them a call at (718) 712-5500 or stop by and immerse in the heartwarming offerings of Rochdale Chicken And Fish. Your taste buds will thank you!
