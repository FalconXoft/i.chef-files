In 2010, we set out to serve Washington Heights with the finest homestyle Italian cuisine, made with love. Saggios menu features handmade pasta, Italian-inspired cocktails, and an all-Italian wine list. Our cozy dining room is perfect for family dinner, date night, or any night. During the warm weather seasons, our back garden patio is the best spot in the neighborhood. Buon Appetito!
