About Yesfi Estiatorio

Welcome to Yefsi Estiatorio, the best Greek restaurant in the Upper East Side of the New York City’s Manhattan borough. Yefsi features an upscale selection of gyros, souvlaki, seafood, and Greek Mediterranean cuisine.

Yesfi is your haven for authentic Greek flavors in the Upper East Side of Manhattan. We pride ourselves on being one of the top Greek restaurants in Manhattan, bringing the vibrant tastes and warm hospitality of the Mediterranean to your neighborhood.

Yefsi is a top choice when searching for Greek restaurants near me or lunch on the Upper East Side. Whether you're craving a delicious souvlaki sandwich wrapped in a warm pita, Greek Mezedes and Taps, or more traditional Greek favorites like Pastitsio or Mousaka, we offer the best Greek lunch menu in the Upper East Side.

As a premier Mediterranean restaurant in Manhattan, Yefsi is passionate about using fresh, high-quality ingredients to create traditional Greek dishes bursting with flavor. From our perfectly grilled souvlaki to our tantalizing array of seafood, each plate is a culinary journey to the sunny shores of Greece.

Come and experience the true taste of Greece in a welcoming and lively atmosphere.
