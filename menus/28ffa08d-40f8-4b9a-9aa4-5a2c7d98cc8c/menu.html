YOUR LOCAL HIDEAWAY - CASK BAR & KITCHEN

Locally Sourced Crafted with Love

 

Your local Murray Hill Bar. No, not that one.
The other one, the one you love.

Cask Bar & Kitchen harks back to a bygone era. One of craftsmanship and authenticity, of dimly lit encounters and great conversations. Our well-informed barkeeps pour local craft beers, hand-drawn ales, wines, a wide range of spirits and liquors and carefully crafted cocktails. As for food, only the best seasonal and local ingredients are found under our roof - served simply and deliciously.
