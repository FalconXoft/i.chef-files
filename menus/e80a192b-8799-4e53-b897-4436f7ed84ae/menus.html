About Us

Jessica and Josh's restaurant experience, passion for the culinary industry and love for their home community of Weston, Connecticut has made their April 30, 2014 purchase of the Lunch Box restaurant a dream come true.

The central location of the Lunch Box in the beautiful small town of Weston is the driving force behind this neighborhood restaurant and gathering place.

Serving lunch, dinner, beer, wine, take-out and delivery, the Tolk family looks forward to building upon the Lunch Box's 37 year history and providing a comfortable and welcoming environment for all.

From the Merritt Parkway (Northbound)

Take Exit 42. At the bottom of the ramp, turn right on Route 57 North. Continue 2.5 miles to the Town Center on the left. The Lunch Box is the on the South corner of the plaza.

From the Merritt Parkway (Southbound)

Take Exit 42. At the bottom of the ramp, turn left on Route 57 North. Continue 2.3 miles to the Town Center on the left. The Lunch Box is the on the South corner of the plaza.

From Interstate 95 (Northbound)

Take Exit 18. At the end of the ramp turn left on the Sherwood Island Connector. Continue straight until the road ends at Route 1. Make a left onto Route 1 (Post Road) and go to the first light and turn right onto Roseville Road. (There is a McDonalds on the corner). Continue until the road ends. Turn left onto Cross Highway. At the stop sign, turn right. At the next stop sign, go straight. There is another stop sign before the Merritt Parkway. Continue straight on Route 57 North for approximately 2.5 miles to the Town Center on the left. The Lunch Box is the on the South corner of the plaza.
