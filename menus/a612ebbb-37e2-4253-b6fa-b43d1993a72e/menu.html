Menus,
Inspired by the idyllic Italian island of Capri, Mr. Capri brings the flavors of the surrounding coasts to New York Citys West Village.

The 75-seat restaurant fulfills a lifelong dream of Capri-natives owner Alessandro Ale Passante and his brother Mario Passante, who serves as culinary director.
Join Mr. Capri for dinner in New York City's West Village and be transported to the idyllic island of Capri and the southwest coast of Italy for the evening.
