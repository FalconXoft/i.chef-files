Check out our daily Mexican delights

You can count on 3 Chicas Mexican Kitchen to use fresh, high-quality products to prepare your authentic Mexican dish. Most of our dishes also have gluten-free options, just ask your server. Put your trust in our locally owned restaurant for delectable tacos, nachos, fajitas, and more! Call us at 201-848-4700 to reserve a table or to order a takeout.


Our food is all natural! No lard, trans fat, or preservatives. No microwaves or cans. Great food takes time so we appreciate your patience as we prepare your meal to order. Since we use market-fresh ingredients we thank you in advance for understanding our prices may change from time to time.
