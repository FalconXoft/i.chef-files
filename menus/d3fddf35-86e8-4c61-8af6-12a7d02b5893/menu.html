    OUR PIZZA

    THE REAL ITALIAN CRUST

    We are a family-owned pizzeria located in Astoria, and we are passionate about creating authentic and delicious pizzas using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients.

    At our pizzeria, we take pride in making everything in house or sourcing our ingredients locally from the finest artisans. What truly sets our pizzas apart is our constant pursuit of crust excellence. We believe that a great pizza starts with a great crust, and that's why we put so much effort into creating the perfect crust.
