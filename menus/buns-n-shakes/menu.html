Welcome to Buns N Shakes!

Satisfy Your Cravings

Welcome to Buns N Shakes, the top choice in New Brunswick, NJ, for irresistible American cuisine. Our menu features handcrafted smashed burgers, finger-licking Nashville hot chicken, and delicious milkshakes, all made with the freshest ingredients and finest sauces from scratch. Plus, we’re proud to offer a full HALAL menu.

Our chefs are passionate about creating unique dishes that delight your taste buds, and our friendly staff ensures you enjoy excellent service from start to finish. Whether you’re stopping by for breakfast, lunch, or dinner, you can count on a memorable dining experience.

Decadent Food Creations

Indulge in our juicy burgers, each patty formed by hand and cooked to perfection, served on warm, toasted buns with quality condiments and toppings. Don’t forget to try our sides like mozzarella sticks and onion rings.

Our milkshakes are the perfect treat, made with quality ingredients and topped with whipped cream for extra sweetness. Choose from classic flavors like chocolate and vanilla or adventurous options like lotus milkshakes.

Craving something different? Our chicken tenders are a hit, offering classic flavors that keep our customers coming back for more.

Visit Buns N Shakes today to satisfy your cravings or place an order online!
