Our customers love our mulligatawny soup ,bone in chicken madras ,samosa ,vegetable jalfrezi .

We service rose hill ,murray hill ,nomad ,kips bay  and surrounding areas. Dhaba Indian Cuisine is conveniently located to serve the entire region.

How do you prepare your food?

We prepare all our dishes fresh daily using authentic seasoned ingredients and traditional cooking techniques, following strict indian  standards to ensure quality and compliance.
