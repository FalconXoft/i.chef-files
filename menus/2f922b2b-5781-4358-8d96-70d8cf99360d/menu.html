What Our Customers Say

What a great, old-fashioned diner experience. The menu was huge and included all of the classic diner staples. There was a massive breakfast section, plus dinners and specials. My husband got a cheeseburger deluxe that was very good (but was missing the coleslaw and pickle sides). My spanakopita was quite good in flavor, the bottom crust was a little soggy but the top was flaky and delicious. They do charge for each soft drink refill. We would return in a heartbeat if we’re in the area.

Stefanie Walker, GOOGLE REVIEW

We beat the morning rush, arriving just after 9 a.m. on a Sunday. Happily shocked how quickly our food arrived. Real maple syrup was a real treat. Will be back every time we road trip through Connecticut.

Casey Vemis, GOOGLE REVIEW

Orem’s Diner in Wilton, CT is a classic gem – cozy, bustling, and brimming with charm. From the moment you walk in, you’re greeted by friendly, attentive staff who make you feel like a regular (even on your first visit). The extensive menu delivers diner comfort at its finest: hearty breakfast platters with fluffy pancakes and hefty omelettes, Greek specialties like flavorful chicken souvlaki, open-faced hot sandwiches smothered in gravy, and satisfying daily soups. Everything comes at a fair price, in a bright, clean space complete with shiny chrome accents and a classic bakery case full of tempting desserts. Whether you’re craving a lumberjack-sized breakfast, a comforting bowl of chicken‑noodle, or a timeless BLT with a side of fries, Orem’s consistently hits the spot – and keeps diners coming back for more.

Alexis Goncalves, GOOGLE REVIEW
