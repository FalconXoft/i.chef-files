ABOUT US

Every family has a passion. Ours is entertaining yours

The Waters Edge at Giovannis opened October 1987 as one of Stamford and Dariens premiere Steakhouses. Since then, weve grown our business to include not just our restaurant but also an award winning event space.

We offer our guests a timeless experience in a genuine, warm atmosphere perfect for special occasions, corporate events, or even a family night out.
