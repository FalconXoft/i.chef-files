Tasty's Diner! Where Every Meal Feels Like Home

Nestled in the heart of Ridgewood since the 1970s, Tasty’s Diner has been more than just a place to eat — it’s been part of the neighborhood’s daily rhythm for generations. From early morning breakfasts to late-night dinners, we’ve proudly served comfort food classics with a side of familiarity and friendly faces.

From Eggs to Entrees

Whether you’re grabbing pancakes on a Sunday morning, sliding into a booth for burgers after work, or just popping in for a donut and a fresh cup of coffee, you’re always welcomed like family. At Tasty’s, the coffee’s always hot, the portions are generous, and the vibe is always just right.

Order Online with Ease

Ordering online is quick, easy, and hassle-free. Simply browse our full menu on our website, choose your favorites, and with a few clicks your order will be on its way. Whether you’re craving our famous fluffy pancakes to start your day or a hearty burger and fries for movie night, we’ll have it hot, fresh, and ready for pickup or delivered right to your doorstep. No lines, no waiting – just the comfort food you love whenever you want it.

Where Ridgewood Eats

We’ve seen first dates, family traditions, after-school hangouts, and late-night laughs — and we’re still cooking up memories every day.

Come in, sit down, and make yourself at home. You’re always welcome at Tasty’s. Because its not just about food...its about family.
