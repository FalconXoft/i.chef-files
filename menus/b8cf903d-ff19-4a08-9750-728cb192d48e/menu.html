Welcome to Peking BBQ

OUR STORY:

Peking BBQ in Woodside, NY, is a hidden gem that offers a unique fusion of Peruvian and Chinese cuisine. The owner takes pride in providing delicious and authentic dishes, such as moist and juicy rotisserie chicken, flavorful spare ribs, and iconic Lomo Saltado. Customers rave about the unparalleled Peruvian Green Sauce, with many making long drives just to enjoy it. The no-frills, cash-only establishment offers quick service and generous portions, making it a go-to spot for both takeout and dining in. The owner is dedicated to providing top-quality food, evident in the rave reviews and loyal customer base.

Welcome to Peking BBQ, where we combine the flavors of Peruvian and Chinese cuisine to create a unique dining experience for our customers. Located at 58-11 Woodside Ave in Woodside, NY, we are proud to offer a variety of delicious dishes that cater to all tastes and preferences.

At Peking BBQ, we offer a wide range of Cantonese specialties, such as Sesame Chicken, Lemon Chicken, Beef Broccoli, and Seafood Delight. Our appetizers, including Egg Rolls, Chicken Wings, and Fried Shrimp, are perfect for sharing or enjoying as a starter before your main meal.

One of the highlights of our menu is our Peruvian rotisserie chicken, which has received rave reviews from our customers. The chicken is moist, juicy, and flavorful, and is best enjoyed with our unique and incomparable Peruvian green sauce.

In addition to our chicken, our Five Piece Spare Ribs and Lomo Saltado are also fan favorites. The ribs are tender and flavorful, while the Lomo Saltado pairs succulent beef with french fries for a satisfying meal.

Customer reviews speak volumes about the quality of our food, with many praising the taste and portion sizes. Our casual dining atmosphere, quick service, and affordable prices make Peking BBQ a go-to spot for lunch, dinner, and takeout.

Whether you're craving a classic Cantonese dish, Peruvian rotisserie chicken, or a fusion creation like our Lomo Saltado, Peking BBQ has something for everyone. Join us for a delicious meal and taste the unique flavors that set us apart from the rest.
