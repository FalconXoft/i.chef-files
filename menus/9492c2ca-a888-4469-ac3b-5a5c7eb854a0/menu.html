Lorraine H.:

Very attentive and accommodating server - following Covid protocols, and the music, atmosphere and food was terrific! It's small and intimate. The Ingredients on the pizza were fresh, they make their own pasta .... Def recommend this place!

review by - Yelp

J S.:

Former Ny-er who often comes to NY for the holidays, always looking for yummy Italian food that will appeal to my three older teens as well as myself . Bellini hit the mark in every way! The food (meatballs, caprese, various red sauce pastas, chicken parm) was delicious, with pasta served perfectly done and hot, Yummy homemade desserts, excellent and attentive service, cozy atmosphere...it was really an enjoyable dining experience. I want a Bellini in my neighborhood!

review by - Yelp

Emily F.:

My sisters and I were in NYC for a trip during Christmas time and we were looking for a restaurant one of the nights. After looking online, we came across Bellini and decided to try it! WOW!!! From the second we entered, the staff could not have been friendlier! They sat us immediately and were so kind! The service was great and the food was arguably the best Italian food my sisters and I have ever had! And we love Italian food! The Brussels sprouts were incredible, the chicken parm was delicious, and everything else we ate was so great! I could not recommend Bellini enough!

review by - Yelp

Jackie L.:

Delicious meal, incredibly reasonably priced. For the love of god GET THE PASTA! It's incredible - each pasta is homemade. I love the arrabiata sauce. The vodka sauce is also a homer in. The owner is incredibly kind, always checked in on us. Finished with the chocolate soufflé, which was heaven on a plate. It's a small, intimate restaurant and it deserves more buzz!!!!!

review by - Yelp

Lauren D.:

We live in the area and in our search for outdoor dining tonight, we stumbled upon Bellini! The people working there could not have been kinder or more gracious, and the food was great. We ordered the funghi bruschette to start- this was more for my husband as I am generally not a big mushroom fan but I ended up liking these! I ordered the Gorgonzola gnocchi as my main and it was SO yummy...full of flavor and not too heavy! My husband got the rigatoni a la Norma which he thoroughly enjoyed. We also ordered a side of grilled veggies and rosemary potatoes. Overall it was a lovely experience with tasty food and delightful hosts. We will be back for sure!
