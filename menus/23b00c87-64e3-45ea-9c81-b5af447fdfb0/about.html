About

Don Julio

Welcome to Don Julio, a culinary gem nestled in the heart of Elizabethport, NJ. This vibrant eatery specializes in a delightful fusion of flavors that represent the richness of Latin American cuisine. Whether you’re stopping by for an appetizer or a hearty main dish, Don Julio promises an unforgettable gastronomic experience that will have you coming back for more.

Located at 50 Marshall St, Don Julio stands out not only for its extensive menu but also for its warm, inviting atmosphere that beckons food lovers from all over. With a dedicated team ensuring quality at every step, it is no surprise that many patrons, like Joseph Machuca, have described the food as exceeding expectations.

  • Appetizers: Start your meal with highlights like the Ceviche De Pescado, featuring raw fish marinated in zesty lime juice, perfectly complemented by corn, potato, and red onions for $30.00. If you're looking for something more adventurous, try the Ceviche Mixto for a mix of fish and seafood for $33.00.
  • Soups: The Sopa Especial Don Julio is a standout, combining seafood with beef and chicken in a comforting broth for just $26.50. Perfect for those seeking warmth and flavor on a chilly day.
  • Signature Sauces: Elevate your dishes with their homemade sauces, such as the Crema Huancaina for $2.25. Each sauce adds a unique twist that enhances the overall dining experience.

What truly sets Don Julio apart is the generous portion sizes. Customers like Klau M. and Igor Torres rave about the ample servings that make every visit worthwhile. It’s not just about filling your stomach; it’s about relishing each bite in an authentically vibrant atmosphere.

Moreover, the positive reviews speak volumes about the dining experience at Don Julio. Aurora expressed her fondness for the establishment, stating, “I love this place! I love coming here to eat!” This sentiment is echoed by many who appreciate the friendly service and inviting environment.

So for those looking for a place where fantastic flavors meet fulfilling portions, Don Julio is the destination. A true culinary adventure awaits at this establishment, where every meal is not just about food—it’s about experience and connection.

Ready to savor the delightful offerings of Don Julio? Don’t forget to call them at +1 908-820-0100 for your next dining adventure!
