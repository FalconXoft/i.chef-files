About

Los Arcos

Welcome to Los Arcos! Nestled in the heart of Albertville, Alabama, Los Arcos is not just a restaurant; it's a vibrant celebration of authentic Mexican cuisine that has captured the hearts and appetites of many.

Located at 8606 US-431, Los Arcos combines excellent service with a delightful atmosphere that is both welcoming and lively. Their commitment to quality is evident in every dish, and diners rave about the flavors that evoke memories of the best Mexican meals across Arizona and California.

  • Signature Combinations: Savor delicious combinations like the One Burrito, One Tamale, & One Enchilada for just $10.49, or the tantalizing Fajita Bowl, which features your choice of fajitas with a melty white cheese sauce and a bed of rice for $13.99.
  • Vegetarian Delights: The Cheese Enchilada & Quesadilla combo, served with rice and beans, showcases the restaurant's ability to cater to all dietary preferences for $10.99.
  • Customer Favorites: Don’t miss the quesabirria tacos that have taken the menu by storm! Pair them with a plate of Nachos Mixed for a fiesta of flavor.

Reviewers like Maggie Cruz have praised the ambiance and cleanliness of the restaurant, reveling in dishes such as the Mixed Nachos that overflow with cheese, shrimp, and fajitas. Amanda Oliver highlighted the standout street tacos that left her and her son completely satisfied, declaring them the best she'd ever had.

Moreover, not only does the food shine, but Los Arcos also boasts a staff that is second to none. Tom Capron emphasizes the friendly and efficient service, making your dining experience a pleasurable one. Regulars like Carolann Hallman have made it their go-to spot, consistently enjoying amazing food and service over two years.

To top off your meal, indulge in one of their famous margaritas, as Jeremy Cole Hardeman suggests. The gold margaritas are a local favorite, and they perfectly complement the vibrant flavors of their dishes.

Whether you're a first-time visitor or a loyal patron, Los Arcos promises a dining experience filled with warmth, flavor, and community spirit. Make a trip today and discover why this charming spot is regarded as the best Mexican restaurant in this part of Alabama!
