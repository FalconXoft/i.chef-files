Welcome to Don Julio, a culinary gem located at 50 Marshall St, Elizabethport, NJ. This vibrant eatery specializes in authentic Peruvian cuisine, dazzling diners with an extensive menu that celebrates rich flavors and generous portions.

  • Entradas: Start your meal with delightful appetizers such as the Salchipapa ($12) or the succulent Choros A La Chalaca ($22.50), a dish of boiled mussels tossed with onions, tomatoes, and lemon.
  • Sopas: Dive into traditional soups like the creamy Sopa A La Minuta ($16), a hearty beef dish that warms the soul.

Customers rave about the flavors, with reviews highlighting the expansive menu and large serving sizes. Don Julio's inviting atmosphere coupled with its savory dishes truly make for a memorable dining experience. Come and indulge in the tastes of Peru!
