THE NEW ICONIC TASTE

Keumho Garden is a popular Korean BBQ restaurant that specializes in high-quality grilled meats and a variety of traditional Korean dishes. Located in NJ, Keumho Garden offers a cozy and welcoming atmosphere, with attentive service and an extensive menu. Some of the restaurant’s standout dishes include their marinated galbi (short ribs), samgyeopsal (pork belly), and bulgogi (thinly sliced beef), all of which are expertly grilled at the table. Vegetarian options, such as bibimbap (mixed rice bowl) and japchae (stir-fried glass noodles), are also available. With its delicious food and warm ambiance, Keumho Garden is a must-visit for anyone looking for an authentic Korean dining experience.
