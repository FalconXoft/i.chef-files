About

Sebaseba Bakery

Welcome to Sebaseba Bakery, a vibrant culinary oasis located in the heart of Jackson Heights, New York. Nestled on Northern Boulevard, this delightful bakery is more than just a place to grab a bite; it's a community hub where flavors and tradition come together to create an authentically Colombian experience. The friendly staff greet you with warm smiles, making you feel right at home from the moment you walk through the door.

The menu at Sebaseba Bakery is a cornucopia of delicious options, beautifully blending hearty breakfasts and flavorful lunches. Start your day off right with their signature Campesino Arepa, a large corn cake topped with hogao, scrambled eggs, and fresh cheese, perfect for just $15.25. For a taste of classic Colombian comfort, try the Bandeja Paisa, a generous plate of rice, beans, and a medley of meats for $24.45 that will surely transport you to the streets of Colombia.

  • Breakfast Highlights:
    • Arepa Grande con Queso y Mantequilla - A must-try for just $5.75.
    • Salchipapas - An irresistible combination of French fries, Creole potato, and hot dog for $15.00.
  • Lunch Delights:
    • Carne A La Parrilla - Succulent grilled beef steak accompanied by traditional sides for $19.50.

Reviews from satisfied customers consistently rave about the freshness and quality of the food. George L. Pabon notes, "The food is great, and the atmosphere is nice," while Mercado declares it to have "the best coffee on Northern Blvd." It’s evident that the dedication to excellent service shines through, with many patrons commenting on the bakery's warm and inviting atmosphere.

It’s heartwarming to know that Sebaseba Bakery not only serves delicious dishes but also connects people to their roots and memories. Angela F. beautifully sums it up with, "Es como volver a casa"—it’s like returning home. Whether you’re looking for a comforting breakfast or a satisfying lunch, Sebaseba Bakery offers a slice of Colombia right in your neighborhood. With reasonable prices and a welcoming ambiance, it’s no wonder that this bakery has become a cherished spot in Jackson Heights.
