Welcome to Yimo Sushi & Hibachi Japanese Restaurant in Monroe

Yimo Sushi & Hibachi Japanese Restaurant in Monroe, located at 357 Applegarth Road, Monroe, New Jersey, bringing with it some of the best Japanese Cuisine in New Jersey.

Besides our mouth-watering sushi we also make delicious authentic Japanese Cuisines. We use only the best quality products and the freshest ingredients. We invite you to dine-in and enjoy your meal in a charming Japanese Restaurant setting and promise you will not be disappointed.

Come enjoy the delicious, fresh, healthy and nutritious Sushi, Sashimi, Tempura, Teriyaki, Udon, Roll, Soup, Salad, Appetizer and Seafood. Dine in, take out and delivery are all available.

We look forward to seeing you at Yimo Sushi & Hibachi Japanese Restaurant in Monroe. Reservations are recommended. We also provide catering for all occasions - Business Lunches, Business Meetings, Office Parties, Retirement Parties, Birthday Parties and Holiday Parties. Please contact us to plan your next event.
