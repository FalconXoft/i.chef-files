About

Our chefs specialize in authentic and delicious food. The staff at F. Ottomanelli is devoted to serving customers mouthwatering dishes that feature the highest quality ingredients available. The Jam Burger, The Otto Cheeseburger and The Butcher Burger are all prepared quickly and delivered to you right away.

F. Ottomanelli uses only the freshest ingredients around, ensuring that every dish is of the highest possible quality. Not sure what you're in the mood for? Try one of our specialties including The Otto Veggie Burger , California Burger, Lettuce Wrapped, California Veggie Burger, and more.
