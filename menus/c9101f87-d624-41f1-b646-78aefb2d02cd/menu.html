Naomi's Kosher Pizza

Flushing, NY knows pizza - welcome to Naomi's Kosher Pizza! We're a renowned pizzeria that has been in business since 1970. You can find us on Main St where we offer a mix of delicious pies, Middle Eastern dishes, and more. Marked by the turquoise awning, our place is hard to miss. The interior is also neat, offering a cozy atmosphere with some cool paintings on the walls. Of course, the main draw is the food which we make only we the freshest ingredients available. So, let's dive into our menu and we'll start with our pizza selection. First on the list is our classic Cheese pie, but you can also create yours with the toppings you prefer. Next, we'd like to mention our Vegetable pizza. We make it with premium mozzarella, mushrooms, onions, green peppers, black olives, and tomatoes. Continuing with the classics, we serve a mouthwatering White pie. This one comes with ricotta, mozzarella, and parmesan cheese, and no sauce. Likewise, we offer an outstanding Sicilian pizza, as well as a number of other options. These include our Mushroom, Spinach, and Ziti combination, to name a few. But do check our complete menu for all the details, and you can make your pick from there. As mentioned earlier, we also specialize in Middle Eastern cuisine. Hence, the star of our menu is our famous Falafel. This dish combines Falafel balls, tossed salad, and tahina. You can pick between 3 and 6 pieces, and make it in the pita or on the side. We also include a Falafel platter i.e. Falafel balls, tossed salad, 2 scoops tahina, and pita on the side. Moreover, we serve a delectable array of appetizers to kickstart your meal. You can start with our mozzarella sticks or garlic knots, perfect for sharing or enjoying as a solo snack. For those craving something crispy and satisfying, try our fries. Our delicious French fries are deep-fried 'till golden brown, with a crunchy exterior and a light fluffy interior. Seasoned to perfection! And don't forget about our crispy onion rings, made with thinly sliced onions deep-fried until golden-brown. Moving on to our savory calzones and rolls, we offer options like cheese calzone, eggplant roll, and spinach roll. Each bite is packed with flavor and served with your choice of dipping sauce. For a taste of the Mediterranean, try our Turkish pita or cheese boreka. These savory pastries are sure to delight your taste buds. And don't miss out on our Mideast specials, including Jahnun and Shakshuka. The former features thinly rolled pastry dough, crushed tomatoes, sechug, and a hard-boiled egg. As for the latter, it consists of poached eggs in tomatoes, chili peppers, and onion sauce. These dishes offer a unique blend of flavors that you won't find elsewhere. Last but not least, our pasta dishes are sure to satisfy any carb craving. Enjoy classics like penne vodka and baked ziti. Additionally, try our eggplant parm, featuring breaded eggplant topped with mozzarella cheese and tomato sauce served with pasta. You're welcome to give us a visit at any time, our friendly staff is here to accommodate all your needs. But you can also order online for takeout or delivery if you find it more convenient.
