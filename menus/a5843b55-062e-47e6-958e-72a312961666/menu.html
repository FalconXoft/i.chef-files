    • Welcome! Benvenuti! いらっしゃいませ

    Behind a discreet door on South Fullerton Ave, pastaRAMEN is redefining the ramen shop. This Montclair BYOB blends the craft of Japanese noodle culture with the richness of Italian flavor, known as Wafu Italian. Led by James Beard-nominated Chef Robbie Felice, our intimate, moody space offers a dining experience that’s inventive, confident, and always surprising.

    Learn More

    The Menu

    The menu changes often, but the experience is always unforgettable. You’ll find mochi ramen carbonara, cacio e pepe gyoza, truffle miso pasta, and other dishes that push boundaries without losing comfort. Everything is made from scratch, inspired by the seasons and whatever Chef Robbie is fired up about. Come see what’s on tonight.

    Hours & Location

    pastaRAMEN is located at 6 S Fullerton Ave, Montclair, NJ 07042, just steps from the Church Street dining scene. We’re open for dinner seven nights a week, and reservations are strongly recommended. Whether it’s your first visit or your fifteenth, our team is here to make it memorable.

    Hours:

    • Monday – Thursday: 5:00 PM – 10:00 PM

    • Friday – Saturday: 5:00 PM – 11:00 PM

    • Sunday: 4:00 PM – 9:00 PM
