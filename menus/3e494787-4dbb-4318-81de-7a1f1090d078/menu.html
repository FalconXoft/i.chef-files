The Classic New York City Steakhouse Experience

Since opening in 2004, we have proudly established ourselves as one of New York Citys premier dining destinations. Renowned for our exceptional service and a timeless interpretation of the classic NYC steakhouse experience, we invite you to indulge in a culinary journey that celebrates the art of fine dining.

All of our prime steaks and chops are dry-aged on-site, ensuring unparalleled flavor and tenderness. Our dedicated team, each boasting a minimum of 10 years of experience in the steakhouse industry, is committed to providing exceptional service with a personal touch.

Join us to experience our renowned collection of fine wines, expertly crafted steaks and the freshest seafood that any New York City steakhouse has to offer. We are committed to making every dining experience with us nothing short of extraordinary.
