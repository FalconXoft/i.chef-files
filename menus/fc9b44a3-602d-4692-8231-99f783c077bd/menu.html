Houlihan’s in Secaucus is located in the Harmon Cove Plaza and is your place to wine and dine in New York. Whether you’re looking for a great meal for lunch or dinner, or just a place to unwind with the best New York happy hour, the Secaucus Houlihan’s restaurant provides a great dining experience. Drink up our happy hour specials, check out our current happenings or just find your favorite item on our menu.
