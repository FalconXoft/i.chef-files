Welcome to Manninos Italian Kitchen & Lounge
located in Commack, NY.

In the heart of Commack, Long Island stands a restaurant that will take your dining experience to the next level. Its Mannino’s Italian Kitchen & Lounge. Dine in a beautiful atmosphere where the cuisine complements your surroundings. We are known for our impeccable service and authentic Italian cuisine made with the freshest and finest ingredients. Mannino’s is the premier lunch or dinner destination featuring a menu with an outstanding array of Italian pasta classics as well as culinary delights like our fresh fish dishes, tender aged steaks, our famous stuffed veal chop and so much more! And of course, to enhance your exquisite dining experience, Mannino’s has a fully stocked bar including top-shelf liquors, crafted cocktails, and one of the most impressive wine lists on Long Island. Enjoy one on our rooftop lounge and be prepared for a dining experience like no other.
