MURRAYS CHEESE BAR & SHOP THANKS YOU FOR YOUR BUSINESS

Our Long Island City location is permanently closed. Please visit us at our Grand Central Terminal and 254 Bleecker Street locations!

Murrays Cheese Bar is closing on Sunday, September 15th.

Thank you for 4 years of great cheese and connection in Long Island City! We are incredibly grateful to our customers and team members for joining us along the way.

After deep consideration about the future of our business and the potential for sustainable growth of our Long Island City location, we have made the difficult decision to close our Murrays Cheese Shop and restaurant in Long Island City as of Sunday, September 15th.

All of our valued employees will be welcomed into roles in other parts of our business. We hope the cheese loving community from LIC will visit us at our Bleecker St. and Grand Central Market cheese shops in the future. We look forward to welcoming you soon.
