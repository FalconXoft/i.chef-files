Meet
The chef

Georgians consider that every guest is God-sent and love and respect for guests are passed from one generation to another.

Devoted and passionate Michelin-recognized chef, Maia Acquaviva – the co-founder of Oda House – is here to immerse you into the flavorful world of traditional Georgian dishes. Born and raised in Georgia, Maia has been treasuring hundred-year-old recipes to share them with you in our Georgian cuisine restaurant in New York. Since early childhood she developed a love for specialties that have been passed down for generations like khachapuri and khinkali since early childhood. So, when it comes to quality, we don’t compromise – only the best will do! Only heavenly delightful tastes! 

 

At Oda House, you can always expect to savor the richest and most delectable Georgian cooking delivered to you by a true Georgian chef.

Welcome to the

Oda House

Oda House is an authentic Georgian cuisine restaurant in the very heart of New York. Since 2013, we have been offering traditional Georgian specialties in Manhattan and in 2020, a new restaurant in Brooklyn opened its doors to visitors. Our staff strives to create an ambient and calming atmosphere in our Georgian food restaurants. Here, you can take a break from the daily grind and savor some peace, comfort and the choicest Georgian wine. Here, you can comfort your big family with a delicious dinner and a pleasant experience.

Now you don’t even need to leave New York to enjoy a genuine Caucasian spirit in a traditional Georgian restaurant near you. We at Oda House will serve you food that will definitely make you want to come back for more.

  • Organic food: meat and fresh vegetables from local farms served to your table.

  • Michelin recognized chef from Georgia

  • Natural Kvevri wine and flavorful spices imported directly from Georgia

  • Oda House value traditions and authenticity.
