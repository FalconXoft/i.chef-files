Join us with family and friends at Tokyo Japanese Hibachi Steakhouse and Sushi located close to Flushing, Queens for great food and the best show in town. Hibachi possesses all the qualities of a perfect dining experience. The speed and dexterity of our chefs will dazzle you and the exotic, elegant atmosphere will delight you. Enjoy the finest in steak and seafood skillfully prepared and presented by our chefs over your table top grill. Serving you hand butchered USDA filet mignon, sirloin steaks, chicken, scallops, fish, shrimp, vegetables from the grill.

Tokyo Hibachi SteakHouse also have vegetarian options for the hibachi or sushi, please let us know beforehand.

A children’s menu is available for children under 10.

We can accommodate large parties of 20. Reservations are recommended for parties of 8 or more.

You can order sushi at the separate seating area of the sushi bar or at the hibachi table. We serve up the finest grade sushi fish available. Our sushi bar serves from simple avocado rolls to black peppered tuna, to soft shell crab rolls, to exotic Uni (sea urchin), to fusion style foie gras and oysters on a half-shell appetizer.

If you are driving, take exit 25, for “188th Street and Utopia Parkway”, heading eastbound. Continue eastbound on Horace Harding Expressway, which is parallel to the Long Island Expressway until you reach us between 184th Street and 185th Street. Our address is 184-16 Horace Harding Expressway. If you are taking public transportation, we are three blocks from Utopia Parkway, take the Q88 or the Q17 bus.
