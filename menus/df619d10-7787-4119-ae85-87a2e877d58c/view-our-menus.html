‘Upholstery Store: Food and Wine’ is an intimate restaurant in West Village that brings a top-notch dining experience to a cozy setting. This quaint and romantic joint created by partners Kurt Gutenbrunner and Leo Schneemann redefines traditional seafood dishes and carries a wide variety of wines to please every palate.

The mixologists at the bar are also happy to craft bespoke cocktails for those who wish to experiment with more creative combinations.

Formerly a real Upholstery Store, the restaurant keeps true to its roots and name by embellishing the space with comfortable couches and leather touches.
