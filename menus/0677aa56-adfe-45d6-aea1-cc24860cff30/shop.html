From Sylvias Kitchen to Yours

 Since 1962, and inspired by soulful goodness and endless flavor possibilities. From the heart of NYC to that of your home, Sylvia's brings soul to every meal. Indulge in our large selection of seasonings, spices, sauces and mixes to experience the legendary taste of our family-owned and operated kitchen, right at yours. 

Love and care

Sylvia had always been dedicated to sharing her passion for food and community, believing each product should have quality consistency and love.

To this day, her philosophy and principles are still the soul of our success.
