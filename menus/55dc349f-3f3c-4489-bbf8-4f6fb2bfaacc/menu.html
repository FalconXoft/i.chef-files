    The Inn by Fumo, located in Harlem, serves New Yorkers tasteful New American dishes focused on comfort food. Join us for dinner, happy hour or brunch, or use our catering services, daily delivery, and takeout options. 

    Try everything from our Chicken Chicken Specialties like Chicken Wings and Tenders, Chicken Sandwiches, Chicken and Waffles, Peruvian Style Slow Cooked Rotisserie Chicken, to our delicious seafood dishes, featuring Pan Seared Catfish, Lobster Rolls, Lobster Mac and Cheese, Mussels and Fries, Lemon White Butter Sauce Grilled Salmon.

    Amazing selection of Italian Wines and delicious Cocktails, served in a Organic Modern decor and a dreamy ambiance.
