Classic New York Pizza, Perfected in Ridgewood

Here at Mano’s, we’re not here to reinvent New York pizza. We’re here to do it right.


Our dough ferments for five full days, giving every slice its signature crunch and light, airy bite. We use unbleached, unbromated Italian flour because great pizza starts with better ingredients.


Our sauce is simple, made with Italian tomatoes, Trapani sea salt, and olive oil. The balance of sauce to cheese is what makes it perfect: every bite crisp, flavorful, and unmistakably New York.


Mano’s is built on family, neighborhood pride, and respect for the craft. Every pie we make reflects the people behind it and the place we call home.

hot off the Press

Any neighborhood would be lucky to have a Manos.

The New York Times


The Times calls Manos a destination for fans of new wave pizza known for its tangy, fermented crusts that stand straight when folded. Owner Nick Manopella goes to great lengths to make sure every slice feels light, not heavy, fermenting the dough for several days and topping it with a simple, unsweetened tomato sauce.


Its all highly technical, but as the review says, it doesnt take much thought to enjoy a plain slice at Manos.

The Infatuation


The Infatuation calls Manos the kind of slice shop every neighborhood needs paper plates, red-checkered tables, and family photos on the wall.
