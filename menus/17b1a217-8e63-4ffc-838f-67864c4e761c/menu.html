Savory Puerto Rican recipes with modern creativity.

"Kiosko 787, a culinary haven where traditional Puerto Rican flavors are reimagined with a modern twist, offering a uniquely flavorful dining adventure."

Welcome to Kiosko 787 Puerto Rican Kiosko Style Casual Restaurant, where we invite you to embark on a culinary journey that blends the heartwarming traditions of Puerto Rican cuisine with a dash of contemporary innovation. Our restaurant is a celebration of the island's rich culinary heritage, where vibrant flavors and time-honored recipes meet modern creativity to create a one-of-a-kind dining experience from our cozy Brooklyn, NY Location, to bring you some of the Best Puerto Rican Food Around the New York City & Tri-state area.

Nestled in the heart of Gowanus, Brooklyn, NY, Kiosko 787 is more than just a restaurant; it's a testament to our passion for food and culture. With every dish we serve, we pay homage to the flavors that have been passed down through generations, infusing them with fresh perspectives and modern techniques to tantalize your taste buds.

Our menu is a carefully crafted tribute to Puerto Rico's gastronomic traditions, featuring iconic dishes like pernil, mofongo, camarones al ajillo, chillo frito, mamposteao, arroz con gandules and tripleta sandwiches prepared with the utmost respect for authenticity. But what truly sets us apart is our commitment to pushing the boundaries of flavor. Our talented chefs take these beloved classics and transform them into culinary masterpieces, offering a delightful fusion of tradition and innovation.

Upon visiting, you'll be enveloped by the vibrant ambiance that mirrors the lively spirit of Old San Juan, Puerto Rico. The building decor, the music, and the friendly faces of our staff all reflect the warmth and hospitality that our island is known for.

Whether you're a seasoned aficionado of Puerto Rican cuisine or a newcomer eager to explore its delights, Kiosko 787 is a place where tradition and modernity come together in perfect harmony. We invite you to join us on this gastronomic adventure, where each bite tells a story of our culture and creativity. Thank you for being a part of our culinary journey, and we look forward to sharing our passion for food with you.
