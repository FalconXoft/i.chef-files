OUR STORY


Mad For Chicken is a restaurant chain best known for its signature soy garlic chicken and unique Korean inspired dishes. Established in 2005, MFC has been flapping their wings ever since. With rustic designs and comfortable ambiance, the locations are known to be a “go to” place for groups of all sizes. In 2020, the company set forth to franchise and fulfill its worldwide mission of getting everyone to become “MAD” for their chicken.


DISCOVER


MFC SIGNATURE CHICKEN


MFC prides themselves on having the best fried chicken, with a crunch more satisfying than anything else. The signature chicken is made with a thin layer of flour, double fried to ghostwriter wein perfection, and then each piece is individually hand brushed with a secret sauce. MFC’s secret sauce comes in 2 unique flavors, soy garlic and spicy garlic. Crispy and crunchy on the outside, yet so juicy on the inside!
