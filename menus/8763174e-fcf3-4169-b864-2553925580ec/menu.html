Restaurant YUU – Modern French in Greenpoint, Brooklyn

greetings

Restaurant YUU was founded by a Japanese chef with the idea of offering a new form of French cuisine.
We offer traditional Japanese ” OMOTENASHI” and high quality French cuisine.
Restaurant YUU has 18 seats and is limited to 36 people per day.
Located in Greenpoint, which has two sides of calm atmosphere and youth culture in Brooklyn, New York.

Enjoy a special space with a modern interior and services provided using Arita ware, a traditional
Japanese tableware.
