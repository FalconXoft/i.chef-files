CATERING & EVENTS

Let I Love Paraguay Restaurant take the guesswork out of catering! Organize a unique event with the best food from Paraguay!

Our approach is fresh and experienced, and we love what we do. We have built our reputation strictly on word of mouth and genuine connections with our clients. This is not simply catering; it is much more for us. We strive to help create memorable moments for you and your guests. We're ready to cater to any occasion, from office meetings to happy hours to multi-course dinners, thanks to our passionate and talented team of chefs, bakers, and beyond. We'll take care of every last detail, so all you have to do is pick up a fork, raise a glass, and enjoy.
