This is your house, too.

Marie Eiffel Market is your local destination for fresh baked goods, organic produce, sandwiches, salads, prepared meals and catering right here on Shelter Island. Bring our food home, or stay and relax out back enjoying our dockside water view.

Our relaxed, cool, community atmosphere is decorated with home-made everything. Its perfect imperfection - just like a home. A quaint New York hotspot frequented by celebrities and casual foodies from all over, our store is both a reflection of the world and Maries taste for the curated.

Real & raw - just like her ingredients.

Marie Eiffel has become a Shelter Island legend and tourist attraction.

Born and raised in France, Marie moved to Manhattan in the early 2000s to pursue a career in consulting. A fatal car accident reincarnated her as a tastemaker, and over the past two decades, shes earned the reputation of excellence and being as bold as the coffee she serves.
