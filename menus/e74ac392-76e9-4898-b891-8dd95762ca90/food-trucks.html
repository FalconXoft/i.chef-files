FOOD TRUCKS

MR. KIM'S HIBACHI (AIN) FOOD TRUCK

Mr. Kim’s food truck offers hibachi entrées, tacos, bubble teas, boba floats and more!

View @sbu_eats on Instagram (you must be logged in to Instagram to view their page)

Accepted Payment Options:
Dining Dollars, Wolfie Wallet, Faculty/Staff Plan, Credit/Debit, Apple Pay, Google Pay

CHATIME

Chatime tea truck offers a wide variety of flavored beverages! Stop by for a refreshing cup of iced tea, bubble tea or the colorful Butterfly Pea Green Tea Latte!

View @ChatimeSBU on Instagram (you must be logged in to Instagram to view their page)

Accepted Payment Options:
Dining Dollars, Wolfie Wallet, Faculty/Staff Plan, Credit/Debit, Apple Pay, Google Pay

HALAL WAYZ

All of your Halal favorites including Chicken over Rice, Lamb over Rice, Gyros, Wings and more!

View @halalwayz_ on Instagram (you must be logged in to Instagram to view their page)

Accepted Payment Options:
Dining Dollars, Wolfie Wallet, Faculty/Staff Plan, Credit/Debit, Apple Pay, Google Pay

ANONA

The Anona truck offers plant-based smoothies, coffee, espresso, tacos and more!

View @anonablends on Instagram (you must be logged in to Instagram to view their page)

Accepted Payment Options:
Dining Dollars, Wolfie Wallet, Faculty/Staff Plan, Credit/Debit, Apple Pay, Google Pay

DAVID'S COOKIE TRAILER

David's Cookies Trailer is serving up fresh-baked treats and irresistible snacks.

Accepted Payment Options:
Dining Dollars, Wolfie Wallet, Faculty/Staff Plan, Credit/Debit, Apple Pay, Google Pay

BRANDED BUN

Branded Bun features a decadent menu of sweet and savory kolaches (stuffed pastries).

Visit their website at thebrandedbun.com to view their menu.

Accepted Payment Options:
Dining Dollars, Wolfie Wallet, Faculty/Staff Plan, Credit/Debit, Apple Pay, Google Pay

 

CARVEL

Enjoy all of your favorite ice cream novelties from Carvel®! The truck comes to campus for special events and when requested through the SBU Eats website.

Accepted Payment Options:
Dining Dollars, Wolfie Wallet, Faculty/Staff Plan, Credit/Debit, Apple Pay, Google Pay
