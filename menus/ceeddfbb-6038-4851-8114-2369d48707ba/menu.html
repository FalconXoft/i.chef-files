A LITTLE ABOUT US…..


True to its Irish owner's roots and renowned for its genuine hospitality, Cooper’s Craft and Cocktails is famous as a uniquely memorable experience in NYC.

Offering expertly crafted cocktails & carefully sourced local craft beer as well as an extensive selection of artisanal spirits, we have something for every taste. 

Stop by for a delicious lunch, dinner, or late-night snack; all freshly made in-house—our weekend brunch menu is also popular with perfectly paired cocktails.

Cooper's authentic hospitality makes it unique every time and ideal for any special occasion - whether it’s an intimate first date, a drink after work, a blowout birthday bash, or something in between.

Open seven nights a week with convenient locations in the heart of the East Village neighborhood.

Meet old friends or make new ones. Just pull up a seat, order a drink and stay for the good times.
