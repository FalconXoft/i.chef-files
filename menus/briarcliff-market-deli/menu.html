Welcome to Briarcliff Market, Deli, & Ice Cream!  We’re a family-owned market and deli conveniently located in the heart of Briarcliff Manor.  We’re open for breakfast, lunch, and early-dinner and offer a variety of delicious quick-serve foods including breakfast sandwiches, subs, salads, wraps, burgers, and more.  All of our food is prepared fresh to order with only the finest ingredients, and we work very hard to give you the best possible service.

Our specialties are our legendary chicken cutlet sandwiches and egg sandwiches, but whatever you order, it’s sure to be delicious!  Click below to order online, or come by and see us soon!
