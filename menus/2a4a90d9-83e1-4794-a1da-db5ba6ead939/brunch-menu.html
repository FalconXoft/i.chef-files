Frequently Asked Questions

 

What kind of food can I expect at this Mexican restaurant?
Youll find authentic Mexican cuisine with a modern twist, featuring traditional favorites like tacos and burritos. They also offer a unique Mexican brunch menu, combining classic flavors with creative dishes that keep customers coming back for more.

Are there any special events or themed days offered?
Yes, the restaurant hosts special events such as Taco Tuesday, Boozy Brunch, and festive Cinco de Mayo celebrations. These themed days create a lively atmosphere perfect for enjoying great food and drinks with friends and family.

Do you offer outdoor seating for customers?
Yes, we offer outdoor seating where guests can enjoy their meals in a relaxed, open-air environment. This option is especially popular during the warmer months and pairs perfectly with our happy hour specials.

Where can I find the best happy hour deals in Jersey City?
Jersey City offers many bars and restaurants with fantastic happy hour specials, especially in Downtown and Waterfront neighborhoods. These deals often include discounted drinks and appetizers, perfect for unwinding after work or socializing with friends.

How can I stay updated on happy hour deals and restaurant news in Jersey City?
Follow local restaurants on social media, subscribe to newsletters, and use apps like Yelp and Untappd for the latest updates on happy hour specials, events, and new restaurant openings.
