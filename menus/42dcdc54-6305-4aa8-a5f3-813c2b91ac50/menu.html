​Sevan was established with the goal of bringing Armenian cuisine to New York City. Having started as a small eatery, Sevan has since expanded and now also offers three beautiful event rooms to accommodate private parties of all sizes. Reach out to us, we would love to host your next event!

We specialize in private events including:
  • Birthday Parties
  • Mid-Size Weddings
  • Christenings
  • Bridal and Baby Showers
  • Kids parties
  • Corporate Events
  • Memorial Lunches and all else!

3 Beautiful Event Rooms Available!

We are also open for dining and takeout.
