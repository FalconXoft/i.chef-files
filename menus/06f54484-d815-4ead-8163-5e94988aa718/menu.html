Our Two Versions of the Medium Rare Story

Co-Founder Mark's Version

In 1812, a handsome young cattle rancher from Kansas City, Missouri, Malcom Rare, fell in love at a square dance with a beautiful farm girl in Idaho, Sara O'Frites, as he passed through on a cattle drive. As with many love stories of that time; theirs was a long and difficult journey to happiness.

Co-Founder Toms Version

Tom was running a publicly traded food manufacturing company. Mark was starting a new restaurant concept, BGR the Burger Joint. Tom desperately wanted to win back a major customer who had been lost 5 years before his arrival. The former customer would not even talk to him.
