Varietal: Grenache, Mourvedre, Cinsault and Tibouren. Very pale apricot color. The nose is fresh, fragrant with notes of peach and apricot accompanied by fine hints of grapefruit and mango. Mineral, the second opens a little more on yellow apple. The palate is suave, round with a nice acidity. Gourmand, there are notes of juicy peach, apricot and crunchy apple. The finish ends with a saline hint and notes of wild strawberries

Château Des Muraires- Côtes de Provence 2022 France 12%

Varietal: Syrah, Grenache and Carignan. Vines here are planted on south facing limestone terraces. The grapes are fermented at a low temperature in concrete tanks to retain freshness, then aged on the lees which gives a rounded, creamy profile. On the palate, red fruit dominates alongside a spicy acidity. There is a creamy note on the mid-palate, and a slightly bitter note develops on the finish.

Chateau Roubine Cotes De Provence, 2022, France, 13.5%

Varietal: Chardonnay. Adir Orange Chardonnay is a unique, skin-contact white wine that showcases the vibrant character of the Chardonnay grape.
