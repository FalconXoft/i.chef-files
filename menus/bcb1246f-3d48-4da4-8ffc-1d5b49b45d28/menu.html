    The Experience

    Step into a fusion of tradition and creativity found exclusively at Kissaki, where you’ll experience cuisine like never before. Whether you choose to order online or make a reservation Kissaki promises a dining experience that redefines traditional Japanese culinary practices with a modern touch.

    Treat yourself to the finest selection of sushi and a full menu of Japanese culinary delights sourced mindfully for your enjoyment. Our restaurants are designed to captivate your senses offering Omakase counters, indoor dining options and outdoor seating to soak in the city ambiance.

    Led by Executive Chef Edgar Valerio, our counter exudes warmth and hospitality ensuring every guest feels right at home. Come join us for a journey into the world of culinary excellence where tradition meets innovation.

    The journey doesn’t end there. Bring the flavors of Kissaki home, with our takeout delivery services or enjoy an At Home Omakase experience.

     

    Bowery

    Experience the most delicious Japanese food in a setting of your choosing at our flagship location. From our exclusive Omakase counter to indoor tables, choose the perfect setting for your dining pleasure. Make your reservation now for the best dining experience in NYC.

    Manhasset

    Located in the town of Manhasset our restaurant welcomes you with its atmosphere and promises of unforgettable taste sensations. Treat yourself to the flavors of cuisine from mouth watering Wagyu fried rice to decadent uni pasta and meticulously prepared sushi.

    Join us at Kissaki Manhasset for a true culinary experience with our brand new 6 Course Omakase Adventure! Let our talented chefs surprise and enchant your palate with a curated array of dishes, each one a masterpiece, in both taste and presentation.
