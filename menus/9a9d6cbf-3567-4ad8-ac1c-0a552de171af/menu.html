Your Local Taste of Italy

Crafted with Fresh, Traditional Ingredients

With several delicious recipes and our fresh tomato sauce made from scratch each morning, it is the perfect local taste of Italy. La Scala offers attentive dine-in service as well as convenient take-out. As a quarter finalist in the Newsday Pizza Smackdown 2022 competition, we take pride in our pizza as one of the best on Long Island.

The La Scala staff has extensive experience with catering for large events and hosting parties in our dining room. La Scala is a landmark in the community, a warm smile, an old school caring touch, but most importantly…a taste of tradition in a fast and ever changing world.
