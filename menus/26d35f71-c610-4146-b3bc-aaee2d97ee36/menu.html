Ciao !

For over 30 years, Villa Pizzeria Bar Ristorante has satisfied the cravings of hungry customers in Stratford and beyond with our authentic Italian dishes.  If you’ve never taken the occasion to experience our brand of dining and service, this is your invitation to visit us today.  Whether you join us in one of our two dining rooms, order take-out, or have us cater your next event on-location, we are confident you will love what we do and how we treat you.

Our customers typically become long-term friends – and we have many friends in the area!

Bottom line?  Our daily goal is – and has always been – to serve only the very finest Italian cuisine in Connecticut and to welcome you like family. In so doing, we’ve earned your trust, along with a reputation of being among the best Italian and Pizza restaurants in Stratford-CT and beyond.

We are truly thankful and humbled by that trust. We never take it for granted.

From our family to yours, we thank you for your patronage and look forward to your next visit!

One of the Premier Italian Restaurants in Stratford CT

Villa Pizzeria, Bar & Ristorante is proud to be family owned and operated, located at 411 Barnum Avenue Cutoff nestled in a small, cozy space right next to the LA Fitness in Stratford Square.  

Established in 1985, we’ve offered a wide menu of delicious, Authentic Pizza & Italian Cuisine.  Our menu of old-world, family recipes includes quite a variety of deliciousness!

Gourmet pizza and New York style pizza (including a Sicilian NY pizza.) A large selection of pasta dishes and baked dishes.  Chicken, veal, and seafood dinners. Specialty sandwiches including hot subs, cold subs, burgers, Philly cheesesteaks and calzones.  Plus, we have yummy appetizers, salads, a children’s menu, and a scrumptious dessert menu!

If you’re having any sort of gathering – personal, civic or business/corporate – remember we also have an outstanding catering service.

Come visit our Full Bar with a wide selection of beer and wine from all around the world, and our best selection of beer and wine from Italy.

Our chefs have created a catering menu that provides a large array of Italian gourmet specialties including appetizers, salads, and Italian entrees.You can completely customize your party or special occasion to your liking.

Call us to discuss your specific catering needs and we’ll take care of the rest.
