CATERING MENU

We can serve any type and size of on-location gathering or event:
‍
- Personal Events; such as family reunions, birthdays, anniversaries, bar and bat mitzvahs, etc.
- Business or corporate events such as training sessions, luncheons, client events, etc.
- Civic group or church events.

Our catering menu below provides some of our most popular items for such events.  Pricing shown reflects both small or large containers.  To see information about these specific items, please refer to our regular MENU pages.

Call us in advance to discuss your needs including dates, time, and approximate number to be served.  We can actually help you in your planning stages to meet your needs.  Since we do this often, we can provide insight on how best to accommodate the number of people you’ll have, yet helping you be efficient with your budget.

Custom catering quotes are also available.
