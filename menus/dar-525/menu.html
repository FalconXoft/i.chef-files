AUTHENTIC MEDITERRANEAN CUISINE WITH A BROOKLYN FLAIR

Greenpoint Menu

Bedstuy Menu

 

 

DAR525 is an authentic Mediterranean restaurant in Brooklyn. Our dishes are derived from our roots, prepared fresh and served in a genuine, pleasant and cozy atmosphere.

DAR (دار) means HOME in arabic. And just like back home, as you enter DAR525, you will be greeted as family, not just as a customer. From our Wine & Beer selection to the freshly baked pita bread, we want you to taste true Mediterranean food without ever leaving Brooklyn.

We also offer catering and special event services for intimate and large scale affairs, offering custom quotes dedicated for your party.
