French Fries

Premium potatoes, perfectly fried to a crispy golden brown on the outside with soft, fluffy comfort on the inside. Lightly seasoned with high-quality salt, they’re an ideal complement to any meal or a savory snack on the go!

Seasoned Fries

Crispy and golden, perfectly fried on the outside and taken to the next level with garlic seasoning, parmesan cheese, and parsley flakes. Each bite is packed with bold, savory flavor that makes these fries the perfect side or a delicious snack!

Onion Rings

Onions, lightly battered and fried to golden perfection, with a seriously crunchy outside and a delicious, tender, and flavorful inside. Pair them with a Bonchon dipping sauce for the ultimate taste experience.

Coleslaw

Crisp cabbage and shredded carrots dressed in a creamy sauce. Bonchon Coleslaw blends a delicate, slightly sweet tang with a satisfyingly crunchy texture that you won’t soon forget.

Kimchi

Fermented vegetables and cabbage, salted and seasoned with a bold blend of spices. This iconic staple of Korean culture delivers a salty, sour, and spicy kick to any of our main dishes.

Pickled Radish

Daikon radish, chopped into small cubes and pickled in a sweet, sour, and lightly salty brine, is a staple of the Korean table. Pickled radish is supremely crunchy and a refreshing companion to any dish.

Steamed Rice

Fluffy, white grains provide a neutral flavor base for any dish. A classic addition, it pairs effortlessly with bold Korean flavors. Try adding any of our sauces to delight your tastebuds.
