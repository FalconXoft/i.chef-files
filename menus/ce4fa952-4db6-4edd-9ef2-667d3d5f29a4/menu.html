The Best Indian Food in Midtown ManhattanAbout Darbar

Darbar Grill is the best Indian restaurant in Midtown Manhattan and offers unparalleled service and mouth-watering Indian cuisine prepared with fresh ingredients. Owner Mike Irani describes Darbar Grill as a contemporary Indian restaurant in New York City with a menu that focuses on traditional Indian dishes while also bringing some fresh ideas to your plate. Our romantic, friendly, and modern ambiance is perfect for date night, small groups, or business dinners. We have an in house delivery team and feature Indian food delivery in the midtown area with great prices and great deals to order direct from our website. We also offer a wide variety of vegetarian and vegan dishes. Visit Darbar Grill for the best Indian food in Midtown East!


Events & Catering

Located near luxury hotels, we're the perfect spot for a private dining event in midtown east with a second floor dedicated to private parties that can be combined with the 1st floor to seat up to 140 guests comfortably. We also offer Indian cuisine catering service for off premise parties and have considerable experience with weddings, corporate events, and more. We are happy to build you a custom menu.
