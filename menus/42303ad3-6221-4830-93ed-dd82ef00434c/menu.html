Welcome to Maruzzella!

Maruzzella is located in Manhattan’s Upper East Side.  Maruzzella has been a neighborhood restaurant serving fine Italian Food in New York City since 1988.  Join us for lunch or dinner and enjoy your favorite Italian dish or come try one of our daily specials.
