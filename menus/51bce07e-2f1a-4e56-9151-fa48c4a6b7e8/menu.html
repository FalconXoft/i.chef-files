SERVING NYC'S SMARTEST, MOST FUN AND INFLUENTIAL FOLKS, FRIENDS, AND FAMILIES FOR OVER 60 YEARS!

First opening in 1960, Dorrian's Red Hand has been a staple of Manhattan's Upper East Side for decades, dutifully serving delicious American fare and popular beers and cocktails to generations of neighbors, locals, and their families.

In the 50+ years since it opened, DRH (NYC) — as we are also known as — has expanded from a humble little corner bar serving loyal regulars to a fully restaurant space with a smaller, intimate back room with its own bar.

We are now a venue that can offer the comforts of a wonderful meal and tasty drinks to everyone from individuals and couples to small parties and large events.

We have maintained that “old neighborhood” charm of Yorkville, while adapting with the tastes and energy of Today’s customers.

KARAOKE TUESDAY Nights!

Dorrian’s Tuesday Night Karaoke has been going strong for over 20 years as it continues to be the Upper East Side’s premiere karaoke night!

Dan “the Man” has been are popular KJ since the very beginning, and he still gets the very best out of our Tuesday Night rockstars, and getting the whole room vibe jumpin’!

Dorrian’s Red Hand (NYC) Is honored to team up with the NYC Backgammon Club by hosting backgammon enthusiasts every Wednesday from 6:30–11pm.

The NYC Backgammon Club provides all the boards, Dorrian’s provides the setting, food, and drinks, and YOU provide the skill, fun, and camaraderie!

Whether you consider yourself a novice or a pro, we welcome all to engage in and experience the thrill, gamesmanship, and conviviality of your fellow backgammon-lovers!

OFFICIAL UES Badgers BAR!

Dorrian’s Red Hand (NYC) hosts the Wisconsin Alumni Association’s Big Apple Badgers chapter for all Wisconsin Badgers football games this season.

We’ve created a special Wisconsin Badgers game time menu of food & drinks — including a “Berger” burger with brat and red cabbage, a “Badgers” pizza, and bottles of Leinenkugel’s Summer Shandy.

Join us and the rest of the large crowd of Badgers Fans each and every game time.
