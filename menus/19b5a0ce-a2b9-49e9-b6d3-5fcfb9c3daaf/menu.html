Your health is our priority

We follow CDC protocols, taking multiple steps to maximize your safety including:

  • Daily Professional Sanitization
  • Health Screening
  • Constant Disinfection

Daily Professional Sanitization

We thoroughly sanitize our restaurant nightly, as recommended by the CDC.

Health Screening

We take employee temperatures when they arrive at work, ensuring they are symptom free.

Masks & Gloves

Employees wear masks and gloves at all times.

Constant Disinfection
