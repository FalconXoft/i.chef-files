Welcome to PHEASANT, one of New York Citys premier catering companies. We specialize in providing seasonal menus and show-stopping displays that leave a lasting impression on your guests. With our years of experience, impeccable organization, and creativity, we are excited to accommodate all ideas and visions.

Pheasant offers an extensive range of catering services tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients, ensuring that every occasion is elevated and unforgettable. Whether you are planning a glamorous gala, a joyful wedding, or a festive holiday party, our team is dedicated to providing exceptional culinary experiences characterized by meticulous attention to detail.

In addition to full-service event catering, we also provide thoughtful drop-off and staffed office catering solutions. This flexibility allows us to cater to corporate functions and meetings with the same level of care and quality as our larger events. Our menu features locally sourced, seasonal, and organic ingredients, allowing for a variety of dietary restrictions to be accommodated with ease.

At Pheasant, we pride ourselves on our warm and inviting staff, who collaborate closely with clients to ensure a seamless and enjoyable experience from start to finish. Every dish is crafted with intention and served with finesse, making your event not just a meal, but a memorable experience for you and your guests.

 
 
 
 

At PHEASANT, we believe that the details matter. From the intricacies of flavor pairings to the presentation of each dish, we meticulously craft every aspect of your catering experience. However, we never lose sight of the big picture creating an event that surpasses your expectations and delights your guests.

We are committed to sustainability, green practices, and community, and we carry those values with us into every event. Whether youre looking for your wedding, corporate conference, backstage, or private catering, were here for you.
