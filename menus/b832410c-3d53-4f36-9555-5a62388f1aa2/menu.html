Empellón Taqueria is a local, Mexican inspired bar and taqueria from Chef Alex Stupak, located in the heart of the West Village on the corner of West 4th Street and 10th Street.

The menu consists of mash up bar snacks and tacos served in a casual environment. The drinks list features margaritas and cocktails crafted from artisanal spirits, along with a selection of canned beer and shot specials.
