Ayat is a Michelin Guide-featured Palestinian restaurant bringing bold flavors to Bay Ridge's historic 3rd Avenue. Where Brooklyn brownstones meet Middle Eastern hospitality, our cozy bistro serves the best of both traditional Palestinian cuisine and NYC spirit.

All Halal, all delicious – join us at our welcoming tables, order online for takeout and delivery, or let us cater your next event. Run by a husband and wife duo, we serve everything from Mansaf or Maklouba feasts to Shawarma on the go...and every plate we serve tells our story. Good food, real talk, no filter.
