    Chama Mama Upper West Side

    Chama Mama Upper West Side is where positivity naturally gathers, creating an inviting and uplifting atmosphere. With its high ceilings, exposed brick walls, and artistic touches, the space feels both open and intimate, offering a warm and welcoming setting that reflects the heart of Georgian hospitality. Every detail has been thoughtfully designed to showcase the rich, authentic flavors of Georgian cuisine.

    Dining in Upper West Side

    This location embodies Chama Mama’s signature blend of tradition and modern creativity, making it the ideal setting for unforgettable dining experiences. Whether you’re joining us for a casual meal, a special occasion, or simply to explore something new, Chama Mama UWS wraps you in an ambiance as vibrant and comforting as the dishes we serve.
