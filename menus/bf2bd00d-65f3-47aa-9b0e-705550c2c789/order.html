Aldo's Ozone Park

Welcome to Aldo's Ozone Park - the right place for a delicious pie in Ozone Park, NY! At our pizzeria, you can expect to find outstanding food and service. In fact, that's the very reason why we have so many loyal customers in Queens. And we hope you become one of them, too. So, don't hesitate to give us a visit. Let's take a look at our selection of flavorsome pizzas. For a start, you can get our round or Sicilian cheese pie, or even create yours with the toppings you prefer. But we also have a number of specialty combinations. These include our Grandma, White, Hawaiian, Penne Vodka, and Margherita, to name a few. And if you'd like to grab some on the go, then our pizza by the slice is the right choice for you. Furthermore, we got fresh appetizers, salads, heroes, wraps, pasta, seafood, desserts, and others. Plus, if you have an upcoming event, we offer great catering services, too. Feel free to join us for lunch or dinner at any time. Or you can also order us for pickup or delivery on Slice.
