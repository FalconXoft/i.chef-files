REVEL & RYE BAR & RESTAURANT
Familiar, comforting and sneakily sophisticated, our new Revel & Rye Bar & Restaurant offers up an intimate dining experience tucked away from the hustle and bustle of Broadway. With a New York inspired menu, extensive American whiskey list and over 20 wines by the glass, you'll never be short on choices. So dig in, pull up a seat at the bar, and revel in the New York energy that's just within reach.
