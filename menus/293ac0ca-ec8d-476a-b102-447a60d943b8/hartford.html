Hartford EVENT SPACES

Located at 25 Front Street, Hartford, Bear’s Smokehouse BBQ offers authentic Kansas City-style wood-smoked BBQ.  This location is perfect for smaller corporate and networking events, receptions, cocktail parties and family milestone events such as baby/bridal showers or rehearsal dinners.

 
 

ROOM SIZE AND FEES

The Bear Den is a private room which features one long community table that will seat 12-14 guests.  For groups up to 40, a section of the store can be stanchioned off to accommodate a buffet style option.  In addition to food and beverage costs, a room rental fee or minimum revenue will apply along with a service fee for staffing. A rental contract will be provided with all associated costs listed.
