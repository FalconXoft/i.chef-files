About 
SHAW-NAES HOUSE

Shaw-nae's House is an intimate dining experience that has garnered rave reviews from critics and the local community due to its soul-stirring dishes served with love. The seasonal menu is the brainchild of American Chef and Food Alchemist Shaw-nae Dixon who infuses herbs grown on-site, local produce, and even CBD into her creations. Based in Staten Island, New York, Shaw-nae's House provides meals for the homeless and is also available for private events.
