Mama Rosa Pizza & Pasta

Looking for some of the best pizza in Rutherford, NJ? No worries, you're at the right place - welcome to Mama Rosa Pizza & Pasta! We're a local pizzeria offering amazing food and service to all our customers. So, if you're around Bergen County, don't hesitate to give us a try. Let's take a look at our selection of flavorsome pizzas. For a start, you can try some of our regular options such as our cheese, Sicilian, and gluten-free pie. Or you can even create yours and select all the toppings you like. In addition, we serve a number of specialty pizzas. These include our Margherita, Meat Lovers, Hawaiian, White, and Veggie Special pie, to name a few. What's more, we have fresh and hot pizza by the slice which is a great option to grab on the go. Besides our pies, we also offer a range of other delicious dishes. Thus, you can try any of our appetizers, salads, calzones, and Stromboli. Or perhaps go for our pasta, heroes, seafood, wraps, desserts, and others. Of course, everything we make here is with the freshest ingredients, so whatever you pick - you're in for a real treat. We look forward to having you as a guest, and we hope you enjoy our food and friendly service. Or you can also order us for pickup or delivery on Slice!
