About Sabrosura restaurant

Our History

At Sabrosura 2, a casual Chino-Latino restaurant in the Bronx, we prepare and serve a mix of traditional Caribbean dishes and innovative interpretations of Latino, Chinese, and American seafood cuisine. Our kitchen combines traditional cooking methods with novel ingredients to create innovative meals just for you. With fresh flavors, beautiful presentation, and creative pairings, we transport guests to vibrant, exciting destinations all over the world. Join us, and embark on a fun culinary journey you won’t soon forget. 

We are available with some of the finest recipes by our experienced chef. We have been serving you our best selling fusion dishes from years. Stop by and give it a try!
