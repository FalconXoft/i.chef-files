  • About Us

    One of Bed-Stuy/Clinton Hills most beloved restaurants, Rustik Tavern welcomes you to enjoy top-notch food in Brooklyn's coolest neighborhood. We have a dope patio and a fiercely flavorful menu that will remind you of mama's home cooking. Come see our sister location in Building 77 at the Brooklyn Navy Yard!

  • Dinner for Two

    Every Wednesday to Sunday from 7-11pm, you and your accompanying guest can wine and dine the Rustik way with 2 dinner entrees, 1 dessert to share, plus a bottle of wine! All for $40 per-person.

  • Dinner

    A perfect place for first dates, dinner with friends or simply unwinding at the end of a long day with happy hour drinks. Dinner at Rustik Tavern provides upscale food in a casual atmosphere with top-notch service. Reserve today!

  • Cocktails

    Innovative cocktails specially crafted by Brooklyns top bartenders - Rustik Tavern is the perfect place to grab drinks! Bottomless available at brunch. Good times are sure to be had by all.

  • Brunch

    Sip and savor on Fridays Saturdays and Sundays with our Boozy Brunch special at Rustik Tavern! Enjoy unlimited Bloody Marys, Mimosas and Bellinis for up to 90 minutes with the purchase of an entree. Reserve today!
