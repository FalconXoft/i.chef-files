Reviews

Review by - Google

Adam:

The food was as good as the pictures show. The portions are generous for the price. The service was fast and very friendly. The environment was decently busy for a rainy day. They had multiple drink options as well. The BBQ was good for everything that we ordered and it came very quickly. The food was flavorful and very moist and tasty. Nothing really bad here. Unfortunately we were too full to try the dessert.
