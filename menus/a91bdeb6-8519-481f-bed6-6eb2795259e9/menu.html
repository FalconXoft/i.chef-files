Welcome to
River Palm Edgewater


We are proud to announce that the River Palm in Edgewater, NJ is celebrating its 40th year. It is a privilege to be known as one of the best steakhouses in New Jersey, serving the best quality Dry Aged Steaks, Seafood, Pasta, and Sushi along with an extensive wine list and cocktails.

Enjoy one of New Jersey's best steakhouses.
The River Palm Terrace

What sets us apart is “quality, quality, quality”.
We are dedicated to serving you the finest quality ingredients that we bring into our restaurants. Our distinguished, professional wait staff is considered the highest rated butler service in the area and pride themselves in creating a memorable dining experience for you.

Our beef is hand selected from the best-fed cattle by the finest stockyard packing companies in the west. The steaks are center cut only from U.S.D.A. Prime Beef, aged on the premises to the mellow point of perfection and then hand crafted by our in-house butcher for incomparable tenderness and flavor.

Even our prime rib is prime. All our prime beef is tender and juicy beyond imagination.
Search the world over and you'll find no other meats or seafood to compare by quality, taste, and savor, with the superb selection at the River Palm Terrace.

This is our assurance and promise. Find out why we continue to be voted New Jersey’s “Best Steakhouse” and “Best Seafood” Restaurant year after year.
