About us
Frank’s Trattoria

Welcome to Franks on First, classic northern style Italian cuisine, with a well-varied and balanced sort of pasta, chicken, veal, and seafood dishes, as well as all types of pizzas. We have a quiet and calm dining room, together with a pizzeria in the front. We are opened and deliver 7 days a week.
