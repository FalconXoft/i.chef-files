Each dish is inspired and taken directly from Chef Elyas' mother's (Oti) hand written cookbook. Meant to be eaten together, each dish compliments the other. Spicy, savoury, refreshing, and salty.
Shared & Small Tings
Broken Burrata
Creamy burrata filled with imported Romanian Telemea Cheese, dill and lovage herb blend, yuzu juice, and cream. Served on roasted tomatoes and a sweet tomato consomme.
Romanian Stuffed Pepper
Traditional ardei umpluti w/ pork, rice, fresh green herbs, roasted in our creamy tomato sour cream broth and finished with smokey paprika sour cream.
Pickled Mushroom Toast
Thick cut sourdough toast soaked in beef jus and loaded with housemade bone marrow ricotta and pickled market mushrooms.
Mici
Herbed and Spiced beef and lamb with fresh garlic and our famous miso-infused fermented mustard. 3 per order.
Zacusca
Fire roasted aubergine, capsicum, with caramelised onions and tomato. Served as a dip with bread.
Roasted Pickled Peppers
Pickled spicy peppers, roasted with fresh herbs.
Spiced Pickled Red Grapes
Habanero house pickle brine. A load of spices (cinnamon, anise, dill, peppercorns, cumin, fennel...to name a few)
Fasole Batuta
A creamy whipped bean dip laced with garlic and finished with red carmelised onions.
Mamliga
Our creamy, decadent, house polenta is made with bone broth and miso.
Wine
We serve only Eastern European / Romanian wines. Our wines rotate often... come in to find out what's fresh.
