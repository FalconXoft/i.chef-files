Review by - Google

five star review by Gabrielle B:

Great food! Our server was super kind and the food came out in a timely manner. Our group enjoyed every dish we ordered! The lamb was perfect. Highly recommend!

Review by - Google

five star review by Linda and Garrin T:

WOW!!! Best spinach pie ever! My wife spent time living in Greece and ate a spinach pie every day for lunch. She has been trying to find a similar pie for me to enjoy since I was not there. We have had spinach pies from the West coast to NYC. Here we found the best she has ever had and me as well. It was huge, thick, full of fresh spinach and cheese, flaky outside, hot, and served with style! Thank you!

Review by - Google

five star review by Alexandra R:

Had a Sunday lunch for five (we booked ahead). It was just such a fun and delicious experience. The waiter was really friendly and helpful, recommended some good dishes but also was honest with portion sizes (huge!) so we didn't order too much. We had: Mixed dips mezze starter, moussaka (traditional and veggie), spanakopita, calamari, dolma and a Greek salad with chicken. There was so much food, we all took home a doggy bag. It was all delicious, there was nothing that didn't feel fully authentic. Definitely recommend this place.
