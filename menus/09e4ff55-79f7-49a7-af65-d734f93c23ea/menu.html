Salma. Authentic Lebanese Grill. Salma is located in East Village NY, offering authentic Lebanese cuisine. We serve an array of famous Lebanese plates such as Hummus, Tabbouleh along with many other specialties. We cater to all tastes and food choices, including vegetarian and vegan.

Salma's Food

Salma is health conscious, committed to offering healthy and soulful food through quality ingredients and ethical cooking.

FRESH INGREDIENTS

All the ingredients we use in our meals are organic and fresh.

VEGETARIAN OPTIONS

Vegans and vegetarians will find an array of delicious and healthy options.

TASTY FOOD

Lebanese cuisine is known for its diverse and delicious meals and dishes. We make sure that our recipes are authentic with a touch of our secret mixes.
