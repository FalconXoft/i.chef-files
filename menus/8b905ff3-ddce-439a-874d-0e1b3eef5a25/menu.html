    Where Tradition Meets Mastery
    Experience Hunan Cuisine at Its Finest

    Young and Rich NY is a Hunan cuisine restaurant dedicated to honoring the natural deliciousness of every ingredient. Our chefs masterfully balance the art of seasoning to enhance each dish, ensuring that every bite fully absorbs the essence of the spices. With a focus on authenticity, we deliver bold, flavorful dishes that celebrate the rich culinary heritage of Hunan. Our signature dishes, such as the House Special Fish Head with Hunan Pepper and House Special Double Fish Head with Chili Pepper, highlight the depth of flavors that make our cuisine stand out. At Young and Rich NY, we strive for the perfect unity of taste and flavor in every dish we serve.

    Taste the Heat
    Signature Dishes That Define Hunan Cuisine

    Our specialties are a testament to the depth of Hunan cuisine, bursting with bold and unforgettable flavors. The House Special Fish Head with Hunan Pepper brings the heat with its fragrant spiciness, while the Sichuan Green Peppercorns stir-fried with chicken deliver a numbing, savory kick. For the adventurous palate, our House Special Pig Intestine & Bullfrog Pot is a rich, flavorful dish that perfectly blends textures and spices for an exciting culinary experience.

    A Symphony of Flavors: The 9 Cold Dishes Appetizer

    Our specialty appetizer, the 9 Cold Dishes, is a delightful medley of refreshing and savory flavors, each dish carefully curated to offer a unique taste experience. From the spicy pickled vegetables to the tender cold cuts, this beautiful spread is a true celebration of traditional Chinese cold appetizers, perfect for starting any meal.
