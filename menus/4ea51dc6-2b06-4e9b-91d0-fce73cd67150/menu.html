Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we provide full catering services for events of all sizes. Our catering menu features our most popular dishes and can be customized to meet your specific needs. Please complete our catering form and someone will help you prepare for your event!

What are your hours of operation?

Wok in the Clouds is open seven days a week from 11:00 AM to late evening. Sunday through Thursday, the restaurant closes at 11:30 PM, while Friday and Saturday feature extended hours with closing time at 12:00 AM Holiday hours may vary - please call ahead to confirm.
