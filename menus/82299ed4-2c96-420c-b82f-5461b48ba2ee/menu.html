THIS IS LALIBELA ETHIOPIAN RESTAURANT

Heritage on a Plate

At Lalibela Ethiopian Restaurant, we celebrate the rich heritage of Ethiopia through every dish. From our time-honored recipes to the vibrant spices that define our cuisine, each plate tells a story of tradition and culture.

Whether youre enjoying our injera or flavorful stews, you’re tasting the essence of Ethiopian heritage, crafted with care and passion to bring an authentic experience to New Haven.

“Our mission is to bring you the healthiest food in town, crafted with love and tradition. Our authentic Ethiopian cuisine is made with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients to nourish both body and soul.”

– Shilimat Tessema, Owner and Executive Chef

What are your opening hours?

We are open from 11:30 AM to 10:00 PM, Monday through Thursday, 11:30 AM to 11:00 PM, Friday through Saturday, and 5:00 PM to 10:00 PM on Sundays. On holidays, our hours may vary, so please check our website or call ahead.

Do you offer vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes, we offer a variety of vegetarian and vegan dishes on our menu. Our staff can also help customize certain dishes to fit your dietary needs.

Is there parking available at your restaurant?

There is street parking available by the restaurant. There are also parking garages nearby.

Do you take reservations?

Yes. Please call us at (203) 789-1232

Do you offer delivery or takeout services?

Yes, we offer both delivery and takeout options. You can place your order online through the link near the top of the homepage. Delivery areas and times may vary.
