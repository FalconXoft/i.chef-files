Located on the historic site of the Brotherhood Winery in Washingtonville New York, the Vinum Café occupies the ground floor of the original winery building circa 1839. The extensive renovation revealed the original brick wall giving a new life to this landmark, together with a brand new 140 seat Fine Dinning Restaurant.

Our Award Winning Chef has created Vinum Café to share his love for fine food and gourmet cooking. 25 years of experience and a continuous commitment to provide the utmost quality food and service, has earned our chef some of the highest recognition in the Hudson Valley including consistent high ratings in the Zagat Restaurant Guide. The new eatery boast a fine dining Restaurant, a Cafe with lighter fare,, a full service Bar and
a large outdoor Patio.
