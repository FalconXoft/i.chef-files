GG's Wood Fired Pizza

Our History

GG’s Wood Fired Pizza is committed to a vision of using only the best seasonal, locally grown, organic product whenever possible. With a passion for pizza and Italian food with 35 years of training, GG’s is a restaurant fueled by years of watching, learning and a deep love for what we do.

Learn More About Us

Menus

Our menus include only the best seasonal, locally grown, organic product whenever possible. For our pizzas we serve a thin crust artisan pizza baked in our wood fired Mugaini oven at a temperature up to 1000 degrees and only local natural hardwoods. Our doughs and pastas are all natural and made from 00 Caputo flour hand grown in the valley of Campania Italy. And always, our oven is of the highest quality: the smoky flavor imparted on the pizza, the way our Pizzaiolo’s develop an intimate relationship, all give GG’s pizza it’s unique characteristics.
