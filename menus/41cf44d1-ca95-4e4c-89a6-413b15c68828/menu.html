OUR CUISINE

Enter Yama House and immediately you will find that Yama takes sushi very seriously. In a spotless and well lit surrounding, our friendly and efficient staff will be pleased to serve you a wide variety of excellent sushi and sashimi.

You can choose between a number of set menus but if you prefer, you can assemble your meal a la carte. Yama House only uses the freshest and best ingredients to ensure that you enjoy a taste of Japan every time you visit us.
