About Us

Lumix Hibachi & Sushi possesses all the qualities of a perfect dining experience. Enjoy the finest in steak and seafood skillfully prepared and presented with great flair by Master Hibachi chefs over an open flame on your table top grill.

Our executive Sushi Chefs utilize the finest seasonal ingredients to emphasize the elements of color, shape, flavor and texture. Each dish is carefully chosen and arranged to enhance your dining pleasure. The speed and dexterity of our chefs will dazzle you and the exotic, elegant atmosphere will delight you.

A Perfect Location for Birthdays, Showers, Corporate Meetings, Holiday Parties Banquet facilities can accommodate up to 100 guests.
