About Us

2004 brought the opening of a new dining classic: Ricardo’s. Inside the exposed brick walls of this Harlem restaurant, Ricardo’s beautifully blends the traditional downtown steakhouse with the rich culture of Harlem

The modern open kitchen displays the chefs’ quality cooking and lining the walls are art pieces from local artists, capturing Harlem’s essence. Low lighting combined with a lively, energetic ambience assure an experience that will keep you coming back.

Located on 2nd Avenue and East 110th Street, Ricardo’s doors are open for candle-lit dinners, family get-togethers, and special occasions. We hope to see you there!
