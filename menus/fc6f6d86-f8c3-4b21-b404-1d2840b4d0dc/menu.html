– Bar & Restaurant –

[ Murray HILL ]

 

 
 

Bubo combines the elegance of an old world cocktail lounge with the warmth of a local speakeasy. It is an intimate space for dinner and pairing courses with our constantly evolving mix of international wines, artisanal cocktails and craft beers. Our small dishes, all enjoyed at the bar or the hidden back-room lounge, are inspired by the cuisines of the Mediterranean region.

 
 
 
 
 

Private Events 

 
 

Looking for a location for your next party or event? Our full venue or intimate private back room can be reserved for any affair. We have a max capacity of 75 people and offer a variety of options including catering, open or cash bar and other special touches that can be added for your party.  Inquire by filling out this form, or feel free to call us if you have further questions. 

Parties over 6, minimum spend $50 per person.
