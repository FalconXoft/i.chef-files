Welcome To Caffe Napoli Ristorante

Established by Anna Silvestri and Family in 1972, Caffe Napoli introduced the sidewalk cafe in Little Italy.

What started as a modest cafe serving only coffee and pastry has matured into a full dinning establishment catering to New Yorkers and tourist from across the seas. We invite you to experience New York history in the Heart of Little Italy for over 50 years.
