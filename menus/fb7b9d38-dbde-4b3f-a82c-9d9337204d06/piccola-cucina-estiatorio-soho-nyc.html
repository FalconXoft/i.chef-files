FISH MARKET

Piccola Cucina Estiatorio

There is a piece of Chef Philip Guardione’s Catania, precisely the Etna fish market, in the third venue recently opened in the heart of the Village, near Soho: Piccola Cucina Estiatorio.

The Greek name refers to the most elaborate and elegant taverns but inside, a Sicilian bee becomes a display of fresh fish that the customer can choose from.
