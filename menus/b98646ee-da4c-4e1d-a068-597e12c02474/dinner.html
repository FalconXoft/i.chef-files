من إيدي Min Eediyé: A Chef’s Tasting Menu

A special family-style journey featuring the vibrant, lovingly crafted dishes “Min Eediyé” meaning “From my Hands.” This seasonal tasting menu is required for reservations of 8 to 12 guests and is served at $85 per person (excluding beverages, tax, and 20% gratuity). We kindly request that you inform us of any dietary restrictions in advance. If you would like to request substitutions, additional dishes, or request a larger group reservation, please email the restaurant at events@boutrosbk.com or submit an event inquiry in our contact us section. Substitutions and additional dishes are subject to supplemental fees. Menu is in the Need to Know section, all items are included in the Prix Fixe.
