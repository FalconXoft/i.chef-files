About Us

Welcome to Precious Restaurant, a trusted name in Hoboken, New Jersey for authentic Japanese and Chinese cuisine. Located in the heart of the city, we proudly serve a menu that combines tradition, freshness, and creativity. From flavorful Chinese classics to beautifully prepared sushi, every dish is crafted with care to provide both taste and presentation.

Our sushi menu features popular favorites such as the Samurai Roll and the Fancy Phoenix Roll, along with many other sushi rolls that highlight the artistry of Japanese cuisine. For those who prefer Chinese specialties, our kitchen offers comforting dishes perfect for sharing with family and friends.

Whether you’re dining in, ordering takeout, or celebrating a special occasion, Precious Restaurant is dedicated to warm hospitality and memorable flavors. Contact us at Tel: 201.798.8827 or Fax: 201.798.8768—we look forward to welcoming you soon.
