    Arthur Avenue, spanning from about 184th to 188th Street in the Belmont neighborhood of the Bronx, New York, provides an immersive experience in Italian culture. Nestled in the heart of it all sits Enzo’s of Arthur Avenue.

    A family-owned restaurant where tradition and authenticity combine to elevate your palette. Each dish is crafted with originality and expression. From the must-have dishes, to the gems that have been a staple to the restaurant's inception in 2005, Enzo’s of Arthur Avenue’s robust menu will bring Italy to your afternoon or evening visit.
