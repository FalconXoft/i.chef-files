    Welcome to Jungly

    Tucked away in a heritage building in Long Island City, Queens, Jungly is an amalgamation of all things New York. We take inspiration from our home, aptly nicknamed the Concrete Jungle, in an attempt to bring the food, culture, diversity, and timeless energy of New York City to Long Island City.

    A stone's throw away from Manhattan and Brooklyn, and sitting squarely in this popular Queens destinationJungly is brings the best of the Concrete Jungle in one spot.
