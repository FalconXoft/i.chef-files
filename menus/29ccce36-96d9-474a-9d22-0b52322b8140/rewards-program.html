Details

1. Get Started

To begin earning points, you must first create an account through our online ordering page. If you already have an account, please sign in either at the beginning of your online order or at checkout. 

2. Earn Points 

Order online through our website or app and earn 1 point for every dollar you spend. In order to receive your points, you must be signed in to your online ordering account. If you checkout as a guest, your order will not be valid for earning points.

3. Your Dashboard

When you're signed in to your online ordering account, you will be able to view your total points at the top of our online ordering menu. Click on this to access your Loyalty Dashboard where you can view your total points, points needed to redeem rewards, available rewards for you to claim, and recent activity.

4. Redeem rewards

To claim your rewards you must first be signed into your online ordering account. To claim, just click on your eligible reward and it will automatically be added to your order - no promo code needed! Your eligible rewards will show at the top of our online menu (next to your points) so you can either claim right from there, your Loyalty Dashboard, or within your cart by selecting it from the discount drop down. 

5.Double & Triple Bonus Points

Earn double or triple the amount of points when you place an online order during these bonus point days!

Be sure to check your email from us because you never know when we'll be running our bonus point specials!
