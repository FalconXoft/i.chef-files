About

Tevfik Başhelvacı (Dayı) opened Dayi’nin Yeri in 1994, he was the first and only restaurant in New Jersey to serve Lahmacun & Pide (Turkish pizzas). You can also find a wide variety of traditional Turkish food, Mediterranean and Middle Estern flavors.
Come visit us for family-friendly dining in a relaxed, comfortable atmosphere. Feel free to bring your own wine, and enjoy a good meal at Dayı’nın Yeri..
