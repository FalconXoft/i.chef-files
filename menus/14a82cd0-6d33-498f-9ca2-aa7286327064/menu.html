an upscale Italian restaurant

Indulge yourself in NYC's most elegant Northern Italian restaurant that offers the finest food, wine and service to which one can perfectly pair our impressive wine collection from around the world. Under the careful direction of one of Manhattan's mastermind restaurateurs you are guaranteed a fabulous dining experience.

Valbella At The Park is located in the heart of Midtown Manhattan at 3 Bryant Park | 126 W 42nd Street, New York, New York 10036. The NY Times has described Bryant Park as one of Manhattan’s loveliest public spaces.

Grounded in hospitality, our staff is committed to provide you with a most memorable experience the moment you walk through the tall dramatic walnut door with the Valbella insignia.

You will be introduced to a bi-level architectural gem, modern yet classic New York style. Whether you choose our breathtaking private Wine Room, surrounded by 1500 labels from all over the world or our private Scotch Room which features rich woods, beautiful floral arrangements, dramatic lighting while showcasing the finest Scotches and Bourbons. Or if you just want an evening of intimate dining, you will be dazzled!

Completing the total sensory experience in Valbella At The Park is the exquisite photography by Gürdal Bibo adorning the walls. Adjacent to this, the Rooftop Lounge with its lit waterfall along with an expansive bar featuring our specialty cocktails and world class Sushi will offer you a tranquil setting to gather with friends and take in the spectacular views of the city.
