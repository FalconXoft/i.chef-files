Sipsteria - At a Glance

Read More

Espresso & Natural Wine Bar/Restaurant

Sipsteria is a one-of-a-kind espresso and wine bar with two locations in New York City—Morningside Heights and the Upper West Side. We serve specialty coffee from Colombia and Ethiopia by day, and in the evening, our spaces transform into cozy wine bars/restaurants featuring organic and natural wines from around the world. Enjoy French pastries, artisanal sandwiches, tapas, cheese & charcuterie, and Georgian-inspired plates.

 

Every Saturday, Sipsteria Morningside comes alive with Jazz Night, where talented local artists bring warmth and rhythm to the evening.

At Sipsteria, there’s always something to savor—morning to night.
