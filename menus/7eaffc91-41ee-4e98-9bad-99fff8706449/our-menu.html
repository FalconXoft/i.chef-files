The Fleetwood Deli & Cafe
The Fleetwood Deli & Cafe in Mount Vernon, NY, located on the corner of Grand St and Gramatan Avenue, is a cozy neighborhood gem known for its homestyle cooking, baking, and BBQ. The deli takes pride in offering a nostalgic dining experience with a menu that includes everything from freshly baked bagels and hearty sandwiches to delicious breakfast items and salads. The friendly staff and welcoming atmosphere make it a favorite spot for locals looking for a quick bite or a relaxing meal with family and friends. Whether you're in the mood for a classic egg on roll or a specialty sandwich, The Fleetwood Deli & Cafe has something to satisfy every craving.
