Curbside

Try our contactless Curbside To-Go Service! It's as easy as 1, 2, 3!

1. If ordering online, you must select Curbside, provide a cell phone number and vehicle information. If you call the restaurant at 845-727-1000 to place your order, request Curbside and provide cell phone and vehicle information.
2. When you arrive to pick up your order, park in our parking lot.
3. We will text you when your order is ready and will bring it out to you. Or, when you arrive, call the curbside phone number at 845-727-1046 to let us know you have arrived.
