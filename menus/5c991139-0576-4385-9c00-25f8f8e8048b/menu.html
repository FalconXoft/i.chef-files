FROM UPSTATE TO YOUR PLATE

Our New York native ingredients are sourced from local vendors, harvested in peak season, and thoughtfully curated into creative and flavorful dishes from our Executive Chef.

Our Menus

SATURDAY

JAZZ BRUNCH

Experience the vibrant fusion of live jazz, exquisite cuisine, and exceptional whiskey at Great Jones Distilling Co.’s Jazz Brunch, held every Saturday in the heart of Manhattan. From 11:30am to 3pm, indulge in a carefully curated menu paired with craft cocktails, all while enjoying performances by New York’s top jazz duos in our main dining room.

PROHIBITION HOUR

AT GREAT JONES

Join us at the Tasting Room Bar for Happy Hour every Tuesday – Friday from 3:30pm – 6:00pm. Explore dishes and cocktails that highlight our locally sourced ingredients, including the Great Jones whiskey that’s distilled right here within our walls. Check out our $11 cocktails, $2 oysters, $3 wings and more.
