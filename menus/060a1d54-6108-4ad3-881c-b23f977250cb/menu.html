OUR STORY

Encourage someone you care about to eat well!

Claires new cookbook, 50 Vegetarian Recipes from 50 Years at Claires Corner Copia, celebrates 5 decades of delicious, nourishing meals shared right here in downtown New Haven, Connecticut - across from Yale University. Pick up your copy in person at 1000 Chapel Street for a true taste of Claires heart and kitchen, or find it online at Amazon and bookstores everywhere.

While youre here, grab a gift card for the food lover in your life - its the perfect way to share the love, one wholesome bite at a time. Gift cards are available in-store. If you cannot stop by, give us a call and well mail one to you! Please dont order our gift cards on line!

Organic, sustainable, Vegetarian, Kosher Dairy, vegan and gluten-free dishes. ALCOHOL FREE.
