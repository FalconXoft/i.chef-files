Il Divo

New York

Rodolfo Valentino

Il Divo mimics the magical and elusive qualities that made actor Rudolph Valentino a legend.

Carefully decorated, the restaurant offers a balance between quality Italian cuisine with a taste of Hollywood glamor.

From antique furniture, to glistening chandeliers, to original movie posters hanged throughout the establishment, guests will feel transported back to the 1920s.

Everything is flawless

Philosophy

We could sum up in five points our professional philosophy: friendly reception, exceptional service, quality cuisine, comfortable environment, affordable price.


In terms of the gourmet experience, the strong points are the local and traditional cuisine, simple and genuine; the quality, that does not come at the expense of quantity, and a very dedicated attention to the customer who is “spoiled” from the very first moment he enters the premises.


Two things customers will never pay for at Il Divo are courtesy and kindness: those will always be served for free and in abundance!
