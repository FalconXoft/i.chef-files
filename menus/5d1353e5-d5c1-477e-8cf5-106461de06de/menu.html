Experience the Taste of the Himalayas at Kathmandu Fusion


Welcome to Kathmandu Fusion, where authentic Nepali and Tibetan flavors come alive in the heart of Jackson Heights. Our restaurant is a true culinary gem, offering a taste of the Himalayas with every bite.

Helmed by renowned NY Chef Jeevan Bom, our dishes are crafted with passion, tradition, and the finest ingredientsjust like youd find in the homes and streets of Nepal and Bhutan. From savory momos and aromatic thukpa to thenthuk and tender sekuwa, each meal is a journey through the mountains.

At Kathmandu Fusion, we dont just serve foodwe bring the warmth and hospitality of Nepal to your table. Whether youre craving a taste of home or exploring Himalayan cuisine for the first time, we invite you to dine with us and experience true authenticity.
