About

ROMA RESTAURANT

Roma Restaurant is located in Tuckahoe, New York and has been a mainstay of the community for over 80 years. Roma specializes in classic Italian food but has a varied menu including salads, appetizers, seafood, steaks, chops, pasta, nightly Blackboard Specials, and of course our famous brick oven pizza. All menu items are available for takeout.
