Welcome to Stone St. Tavern; a historic old world niche gem, that like its street namesake, has stood the test of time. Our pub-style menu offers richly satisfying comfort food, indulgent four-cheese baked mac-and-cheese. But the real triumph is our legendary Hangover Burger, which is topped with bacon, cheddar, and a sinful fried egg. Select from craft beers, cocktails, burgers, apps, at affordable prices. Stop in to enjoy our outdoor seating on this historic cobblestone street in FiDI.
