ABOUT KANTU

Located in the borough of Queens, our cozy venue inspired by the traditional colors and landscapes of Peru offers an intimate atmosphere and our personalized customer service brings people closer to one of the most outstanding cuisines in the world. Our goal is to get involved with the community and make them proud of our commitment to high quality food and service, with a diverse menu that goes from the famous ceviche to different preparations of meats, sauces and drinks, we provide multiple options to those who trust Peruvian cuisine as a healthy and delicious option. K'antu offers a menu made with fresh and authentic ingredients faithful to the essence of Peruvian cuisine
