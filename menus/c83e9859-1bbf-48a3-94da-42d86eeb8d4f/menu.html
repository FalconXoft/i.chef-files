Frequently Asked Questions

Which pasta dishes at Jane Doe make it one of the best pasta spots in Jersey City?
Our menu features standout pasta dishes like linguine with sunflower seed pesto, rigatoni with braised pork shoulder, and mafaldine in Japanese curry vodka sauce with smoked chicken. Each dish is crafted with fresh ingredients, offering a unique fusion of flavors that sets us apart in Jersey City’s dining scene. It’s one of the many reasons we’re also considered a top choice for Romantic Restaurants Jersey City diners love.

What are the happy hour specials at Jane Doe in Jersey City?
Join us for happy hour, Tuesday to Saturday from 4-7 PM and Sunday from 12-7 PM. Enjoy $10 classic cocktails at the bar. On Pasta Tuesdays, from 5-10 PM, savor 50% off select pasta dishes when you dine with us and pay cash. Whether you’re winding down after work or planning a date night, our Happy Hour is the perfect start.

Is Jane Doe a bar and restaurant in Jersey City that serves craft cocktails?
Yes, we pride ourselves on offering an exceptional dining experience complemented by our signature craft cocktails. Our mixologists meticulously prepare each drink, blending flavors to create unique beverages that perfectly pair with our modern American cuisine infused with Asian influences.

Does Jane Doe offer a mix of Italian and Japanese flavors as part of modern American cuisine in Jersey City?
Absolutely, our menu is a fusion of modern American cuisine with Italian and Japanese influences. We offer a variety of small plates and hearty pasta dishes, all crafted with the freshest ingredients to provide a unique and memorable dining experience.

Are there vegetarian or gluten-free pasta options at Jane Doe in Jersey City?
Yes, we offer vegetarian and gluten-free pasta options. Dishes like our linguine with sunflower seed pesto and spaghetti pomodoro can be prepared to accommodate dietary preferences. Please inform your server of any dietary restrictions, and we’ll ensure a delightful dining experience tailored to your needs. Whether you’re looking for a special dinner or seeking Romantic Restaurants Jersey City couples recommend, we have something for everyone.
