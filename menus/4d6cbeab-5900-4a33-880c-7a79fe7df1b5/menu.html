Located in Prospect Lefferts Gardens, DjonDjon is a purveyor of fine Haitian American Cuisine.

Embark on a culinary journey that seamlessly blends tradition and innovation at Djon Djon, a Haitian-American restaurant that is making waves in the gastronomic scene. Djon Djon's commitment to authenticity shines through its menu, offering traditional Haitian dishes like LAMBI, a flavorful nod to Haitian heritage.

What sets this eatery apart is its inventive twist on modern cuisine, incorporating the exquisite Haitian djon djon mushroom into pasta and seafood dishes. As a cornerstone of Haitian culinary culture, the djon djon mushroom infuses a unique and rich flavor profile into every dish, creating an experience that is both familiar and refreshingly novel. Djon Djon restaurant invites you to savor the essence of Haiti through a tapestry of flavors that celebrates the past while embracing the future.
