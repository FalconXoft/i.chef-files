SIGNATURE BOWLS

Hawaiian

Hawaiian Style Poké with wild-caught ahi tuna, sweet onions, cilantro tossed in spicy mayonnaise, topped with green onions, shredded nori, and crispy onions.

Miso Salmon

Hawaiian Style Poké featuring salmon, sweet onions, diced mangos, and cilantro tossed in creamy miso, topped with garlic chips.

Sweet and Spicy Shrimp

Shrimp, corn, cilantro, jalapeños, and sweet onions tossed in garlic oil, topped with wonton crisps.

Pokemoto Tofu

Organic tofu adds a twist to Hawaiian style poké with sweet onions, and edamame tossed in Pokémoto Chojang, topped with green onions, toasted rice puffs, and sesame seeds.

Sesame Ginger Chicken

Chicken, sweet onions, and shredded carrots tossed in sweet sesame ginger sauce topped with avocado, cashews, and tempura flakes.
