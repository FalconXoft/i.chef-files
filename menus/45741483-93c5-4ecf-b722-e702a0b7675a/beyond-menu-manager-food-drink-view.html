Manage Your Restaurant with Ease – Anytime, Anywhere Take control of your restaurant’s online orders with the Beyond Menu Manager app. Designed for restaurant owners and managers, this app streamlines order management, menu updates, financial reporting, and more—right from your phone or tablet. Key Features: - Order Management – View, accept, and manage online orders in real time. - Menu Control – Update prices, availability, and items on the go. - Store Settings – Customize hours, pickup and delivery settings, and pause orders. - Financial Insights – Track sales, order reports and review payouts. - Push Notifications – Stay updated with order alerts and important updates. Whether you’re on the go or in the kitchen, the Beyond Menu Manager app keeps your business running smoothly. Download now and take charge of your restaurant!
