    About Us

    Our flagship restaurant, The Onion Tree in Sea Cliff, has become a beloved culinary destination renowned for its creative menu and welcoming atmosphere.


    AtThe Onion Tree Pizza Co., we continue this tradition of culinary excellence and innovation. Our menu features a tantalizing array of pizzas inspired by both Italian and Indian flavors. From traditional Napoletana and Romana styles to inventive Indian-influenced creations, each pizza is a culinary masterpiece, crafted with the finest ingredients and Chef Jays signature spiced finishing oil, Tadka.

    But our commitment to exceptional dining goes beyond the plate. Our restaurant is a reflection of our passion for great food, great company, and great music. From the vibrant decor to the rock n roll-inspired ambiance, every detail has been carefully curated to create a dining experience that is both memorable and immersive.


    Located at 214 First Avenue, The Onion Tree Pizza Co. invites you to join us on a culinary journey like no other. Whether youre craving a classic Margherita pizza or eager to explore our Indian-inspired specialties, we promise a dining experience that will tickle your senses.

    Our Restaurant

    Drawing inspiration from Chef Jays upbringing in Gujarat, India, and his extensive culinary journey across eight countries, The Onion Tree Pizza Co. offers a tantalizing fusion of Italian and Indian flavors. From traditional Napoletana and Romana pizzas to inventive Indian-inspired creations, each dish is a celebration of culinary creativity and cultural diversity.

    Unforgettable Catering

    Elevate your next event with our unique fusion of Italian and Indian cuisine. Our catering services bring unforgettable flavors directly to your doorstep, ensuring a culinary experience that will delight your guests and leave a lasting impression.
