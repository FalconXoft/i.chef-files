Your Neighborhood Indian Restaurant • Since 1990

Welcome to Taste of India II Staten Island, the Island's first Indian restaurant, where a treasure trove of true, authentic Indian flavors awaits you.

Proudly serving Staten Island since 1990, we are located in the heart of New Dorp Lane and have been a beloved culinary destination for over three decades. Chef and owner Raman Kumar personally designs each dish on our menu, ensuring a genuine and flavorful Indian dining experience. We offer dine-in, takeout, delivery, and catering services to suit every occasion and preference.

We specialize in traditional North Indian cuisine, prepared with the finest ingredients and time-honored recipes. Our menu offers true Indian taste without any fusion—from aromatic curries and naan to sizzling tandoori dishes. We also feature a full bar with Indian specialty drinks to complement your meal.

Whether you're craving a hearty meal, celebrating a special occasion, or simply exploring new flavors, our warm and welcoming atmosphere is the perfect place to enjoy a true taste of India.
