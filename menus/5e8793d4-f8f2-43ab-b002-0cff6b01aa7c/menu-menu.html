Lonches Bañados

Lonches Bañados are traditional lonches (sandwiches) dating back to 1953 in Guadalajara, MX. Traditionally it is a pulled pork sandwich, served on birote (mexican-style baguette), bathed in warm, hearty, slightly spicy tomato sauce. The recipe for this sauce has been in our family for 60 years.
