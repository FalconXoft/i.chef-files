About Us

Our Restaurant offers a variety of fresh seafood, chicken and veal dishes, preparred in the Italian tradition. Start your dinner with our Rock Shrimp Limoncello, or Grilled Portabello, followed by Mozzarella Caprese, and finally one of our signature main courses. Our Vetello Benito Veal, Linguine Fantasia or Pollo Bellisimo are loved by all!
