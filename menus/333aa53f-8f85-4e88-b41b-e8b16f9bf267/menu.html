Welcome to El Continental Restaurant

At El Continental Restaurant, we celebrate the vibrant culinary heritage of El Salvador. Nestled in Brooklyn, NY, our restaurant offers a warm and inviting atmosphere, perfect for families and friends. Our menu boasts authentic Salvadoran dishes, featuring delectable pupusas, tamales, and hearty breakfast options like Huevos Rancheros. With a commitment to quality, we provide fresh, flavorful ingredients that transport you straight to the heart of Central America. Ideal for casual dining, group gatherings, and takeout, El Continental is where tradition meets taste. Join us as we share the rich flavors of Salvadoran cuisine in a welcoming setting that feels like home.

Welcome to El Continental Restaurant, a culinary gem nestled in the heart of Brooklyn, NY. Specializing in authentic Salvadoran cuisine, this vibrant eatery offers a delightful journey into Central America through its rich flavors and traditional dishes. Located at 672 5th Ave, El Continental is dedicated to providing a warm and casual atmosphere, making it an ideal spot for friends, families, and food enthusiasts alike.

At El Continental, the dining experience is not just about food; it's about community. The restaurant features a welcoming vibe with moderate noise levels, making it perfect for groups or intimate meals. With options for outdoor seating, takeout, and delivery, patrons can enjoy their meals in a way that suits their lifestyle. Plus, the restaurant accepts a variety of payment methods, including credit cards and Apple Pay, for added convenience.

What sets El Continental apart? The menu is a treasure trove of authentic Salvadoran delights. Start your morning right with their breakfast options, like the hearty Huevos Rancheros, served with potatoes, sausage, and beans, all topped with a zesty salsa for an authentic touch. And let’s not forget the delectable pupusas — thick corn tortillas filled with savory ingredients such as beans, cheese, and pork. Each bite transports you to El Salvador with its rich texture and explosive flavors.

  • Appetizers: The restaurant offers a range of appetizers, including fried chicken tacos and shrimp ceviche, perfect for sharing or enjoying solo.
  • Soups: Try the flavorful seafood soup or beef tripe soup for a comforting and hearty experience.
  • Kid-Friendly: With options suitable for children, El Continental ensures that every member of the family is well-fed and happy.

El Continental Restaurant is more than just a place to eat; it’s a community hub that celebrates Salvadoran traditions with every dish. The team behind the restaurant takes pride in creating a welcoming ambiance, where everyone can enjoy authentic cuisine and feel at home. Whether you’re a local or just passing through, make sure to stop by El Continental — your palate will thank you!

To reserve your table or inquire about catering options, call (718) 832-1327. Discover the flavors of El Salvador right in Brooklyn!
