Welcome to Mr. Chau’s Chinese Food

At Mr. Chau’s Chinese Food, the essence of authentic Chinese cuisine comes alive. Situated in San Jose, this inviting eatery is perfect for families, groups, and anyone wishing to indulge in delightful flavors. Renowned for its extensive menu, Mr. Chau’s offers something for everyone, with a few vegetarian options sprinkled in. The cozy atmosphere, complemented by outdoor seating and a welcoming vibe for pets, makes it a great spot for casual dining. With convenient delivery, takeout, and reservation options, your next culinary adventure is just a phone call away. Experience the warmth and tradition at Mr. Chau's, where every dish tells a story.

Welcome to the culinary charm of Mr. Chau’s Chinese Food, a delightful treasure nestled in the heart of San Jose, California. Located at 1608 McKee Rd, this restaurant brings the authentic tastes of Chinese cuisine to your table, offering everything from savory stir-fries to delicate dumplings.

As a food reviewer at Restaurant World, I had the pleasure of dining at Mr. Chau's, and I can honestly say it left a lasting impression. This establishment stands out not only for its delectable dishes but also for its warm ambiance—ideal for families, groups, and even those looking for a quiet meal. With options for delivery and takeout, it suits both casual diners and those who prefer a cozy night in.

  • Reservations: Mr. Chau’s happily accepts reservations, making it easy to plan your visit.
  • Vegetarian Options: While the limited vegetarian selections may not cater to everyone, the available dishes are crafted with care.
  • Family-Friendly: The restaurant is undoubtedly good for kids, featuring a relaxed environment that welcomes families.
  • Outdoor Seating: Enjoy a lovely meal with nature; Mr. Chau's offers outdoor seating that’s perfect for warm Californian evenings.
  • Dog-Friendly: And yes, dogs are welcome, which is a nice touch for pet owners.

One of my personal favorites from the menu was the Kung Pao Chicken, boasting the right amount of heat with tender chicken and crunchy peanuts, perfectly balanced by a hint of sweetness. The dumplings, steamed to perfection, melted in my mouth, showcasing the chef's dedication to authentic flavors. Each dish is a testament to the culinary art that Mr. Chau has brought to life.

If you’re looking for a place that combines quality food with a comfortable atmosphere, Mr. Chau’s is undoubtedly worth a visit. Whether you want to watch the game on their TV while savoring some well-cooked noodles or enjoy a peaceful dinner with loved ones, you won't be disappointed.

In summary, if you find yourself in San Jose, make sure to stop by Mr. Chau’s Chinese Food. The comfort, service, and flavors make it a definitive stop for anyone craving traditional Chinese dishes. For inquiries or to place an order, feel free to call (408) 258-1897. Get ready to delight your taste buds!
