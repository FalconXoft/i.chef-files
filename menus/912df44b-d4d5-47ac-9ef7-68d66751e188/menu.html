Why the Change?

Becoming 100% Halal has been a project weve worked on since the owner converted to Islam in Ramadan 2024. We didnt want anyone missing out on a delicious cuisine and the owner got tired of seeing pork in his store. January 1st he took the leap and got rid of the remaining pork items from his menu. the flavor has not been altered and he has worked an entire year making sure every item is exceptional before doing the switch.
