Casa Bella Restaurant

Welcome to Casa Bella Restaurant | Little Italy, NYC

Nestled on historic Mulberry Street in Little Italy, New York City, Casa Bella invites you to experience the flavors of authentic Italian cuisine in our newly renovated, intimate dining room.

Join us for lunch and dinner, seven days a week, and enjoy classic Italian specialties—from handmade pastas to traditional seafood and meat dishes—prepared with the freshest ingredients and a passion for true Italian hospitality.

Whether you’re celebrating with family, sharing a romantic meal, or simply exploring the best restaurants in Little Italy, Casa Bella is the perfect place to relax, savor, and create lasting memories.

We look forward to welcoming you soon!
