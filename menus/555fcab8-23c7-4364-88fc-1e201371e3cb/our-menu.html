About Taco Joes and reviews

No preservatives, lard, transfats, or MSG. We fry in 100% canola oil. All of our food is prepared and cooked on premise. Nothing artificial is used or added. Only fresh, top quality ingredients. Nothing but delicious Southwest style foods.

Taco Joe’s comes from a healthy background: Our founders originally started the Yogurt & Such Cafe chain in 1982 and were among the pioneers of the healthy foods revolution on Long Island, New York.  Taco Joe’s was founded in 2008 in Massapequa, NY under the same principals as its sister company; Yogurt & Such Cafe; to give our customers the freshest quality ingredients, great value and quick service in a nice comfortable atmosphere!
