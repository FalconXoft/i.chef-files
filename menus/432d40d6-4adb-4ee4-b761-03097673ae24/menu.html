MOMENTS THAT CREATE MEMORIES

Our award-winning cuisine, attentive customer service, and inviting ambiance have made the Appian Way a favorite restaurant and event venue in New Jersey for over 45 years.

Dine with Us

One step into the Appian Way and you realize you’re not just in a restaurant, but in the La Banca family’s home.

Here, we cook with passion and creativity rather than precision. True to the Italian ways, we invite you to take your time to thoroughly enjoy our food and wine. You’re encouraged to relax, linger and savor the experience.

From our family to yours…welcome.
