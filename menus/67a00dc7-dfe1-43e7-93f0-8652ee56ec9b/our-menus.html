Welcome To Bay Ridge Diner

We are located in the Bay Ridge neighborhood of Brooklyn at 8017 5th Avenue Brooklyn, NY 11209. We are a family owned and operated restaurant serving our community delicious foods for many years. We are a perfect place for breakfast, lunch or dinner. We hope to see you all soon.

Everything we serve is prepared fresh daily using only the best ingredients.
