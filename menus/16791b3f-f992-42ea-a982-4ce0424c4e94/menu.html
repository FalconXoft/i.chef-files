Welcome To Vinny's

A cherished Carroll Gardens neighborhood staple for over 25 years. We serve heartfelt Italian-American dishes with generous portions, family-style warmth, and a true Brooklyn spirit. Join us for classic flavors, good company, and a place that feels like home.

Celebrating 25+ Years of Neighborhood Flavor

For more than two decades, Vinny’s has been a cornerstone of Carroll Gardens, proudly serving authentic Italian-American dishes that bring comfort and joy to every table.

Generous Portions Crafted with Heart

From crispy fried calamari to our delicous pastas every dish is made fresh daily to satisfy your cravings and warm your soul.

Your Brooklyn Home for Good Food and Warmth

At Vinny’s, we’re more than just a restaurant — we’re a welcoming gathering place where neighbors become friends over timeless classics and genuine hospitality.

MENU

Vinny’s of Carroll Gardens serves up honest, straightforward Italian-American cooking—the kind that’s been part of family dinners and neighborhood traditions for generations. Our menu stays true to the classics house-made sauces, timeless pasta dishes, and familiar favorites prepared with care.
