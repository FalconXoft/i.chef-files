Welcome to B2 Bistro
North Brunswick

Dining in Park-Like Splendor

Immerse yourself in the rustic yet sophisticated vibe at B2 North Brunswick, a beautifully-situated, full-service restaurant and banquet facility neatly nestled on the shore of picturesque Lake Farrington in Middlesex County. Our private banquet space can accommodate up to 150 people (120 with dance floor) and provides access to our lovely park-like setting. Please reach out to our event managers to see how we can help make your event extra special.
Based on the "garden to table" concept, our ​Executive Chef and his talented team transform the freshest locally-sourced ingredients into delectable culinary creations reminiscent of the American and Mediterranean bistro-style of cooking. Our bar features an eclectic wine list, a continuously rotating selection of draft beers, and a raft of expertly crafted cocktails. We specialize in barrel-aged spirits and use fresh seasonal fruit in our drinks.

Private Events

Our private dining room is the perfect choice for special events, meetings, and parties. Our event space features access to the outdoor veranda in a park-like setting which can serve as a perfect photographic backdrop for event attendees. This beautifully-appointed room can easily accommodate a bar, dance floor, band or DJ, multiple seating configurations, and up to 150 guests (120 with dance floor). This space is an excellent choice for weddings, large birthday parties, graduations, business meetings and conferences, and all manner of social and celebratory events.
​​Our catering specialist will help you select the perfect menu, table décor, and required service for your event. Our executive chef and the B2 culinary team will create a customized menu that is sure to make your event memorable. And of course, our hospitable and fun bartenders can create those breezy coastal cocktails that bring everything together for a perfect occasion.

​Please reach out to our private events team at (732) 297-3803 or via the event inquiry email form below to see how we can help you plan your next event.
