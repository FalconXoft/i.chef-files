Welcome to Anthony David's

Come Together Over the Finest Italian Food in Hoboken

More than 25 years ago Anthony David’s transformed Hoboken’s dining with its distinctive epicurean offerings in a charming corner location.

Led by Chef Anthony Pino, we deliver an authentic trattoria experience with Italian Classics and Mediterranean influences. Our all-day brunch, late afternoon Antipasto, and evening dinners feature a blend of classic and innovative dishes, using fresh, local ingredients.

The warm atmosphere, highlighted by a beautiful patio, a thoughtfully curated wine list, and creative cocktails, makes Anthony David's ideal for any meal.

Recognized for exceptional Hoboken catering, we extend our culinary expertise to events of all sizes, ensuring the same quality and attention to detail. Visit us often to see why Anthony David’s is a favored spot in the neighborhood.
