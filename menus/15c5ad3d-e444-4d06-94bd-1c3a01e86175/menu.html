In the evenings, Okonomi becomes YUJI Ramen and offers an a la carte menu of seafood-rich ramen and mazemen.

Okonomi serves an authentic Japanese breakfast, with a focus on the traditional Ichiju Sansai made with the finest local sources.

Ichiju Sansai showcases a delicate balance of flavors and textures, highlighting the natural beauty of each ingredient, from the silky tamagoyaki to the crisp vegetables. With every bite, diners are transported to mornings in Japan, when simple pleasures of a hearty breakfast are savored and celebrated.

As diners savor Ichiju Sansai, they are experiencing a centuries-old tradition that celebrates the art of Japanese cuisine. The restaurant’s commitment to using locally sourced ingredients reflects a deep respect for the land and the sea, creating a sustainable dining experience that honors both the past and the future.

At Okonomi, authenticity and local flavor combine to create a dining experience like no other.
