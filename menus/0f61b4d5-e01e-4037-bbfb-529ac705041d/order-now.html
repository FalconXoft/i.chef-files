Neat represents a new era of eating with brighter, better and fresher fast-food.

We’re feeding a positive change in global eating habits, catering to diverse tastes and preferences with our menu of sustainable, plant-based food.
Based on the ethos of simplicity, honesty and transparency, we’re cooking from scratch using whole ingredients, made inhouse or sourced from chefs we know and partners we trust. Neat is place where vegans, veggies and meat-eaters alike can come together to enjoy a meal - it's all about eating what comes natural, better for you, better for your health and for the better for the environment.
Good mood food.

our patty

our new signature shroom + bean patty is rich in plant-based proteins, dietary fibre and essential nutrients, while being low in saturated fats. these contribute to heart health, digestive well-being, and overall balanced nutrition.

we've combined five key components to create the delectable, high protein, meaty experience you crave without compromise: protein, fats, carbs, minerals and flavours.

ingredients include oyster mushroom, pea protein, chickpeas, mung beans, white onion and quinoa.

simply put: it’s meat, made from plants. better for you, better for animals, better for the environment.

our produce

cooking from scratch, we’re creating flavour with local and seasonal vegetables: pickling, spicing, seasoning and slow-roasting.

we take great pride in cooking from scratch using real, whole ingredients sourced locally from farmers we know and partners we trust.

our menus are 100% free from animal products and packed with natural nutrients, proteins, fibres, vitamins and antioxidants to keep your body stronger for longer.

our packaging

it's not just the food that's kind to the planet. we are committed to sustainability and are a proud partner of Packaging Environmental.

our packaging is 100% made from plants! Designed to be eco-friendly, 100% recyclable and biodegradable.
