Shake-ing It Up in Harlem

With a hint of nostalgia and a clear vision for the future, Harlem Shake® is proud to serve as your go-to community hangout for retro-style, food-filled fun… with a huge side of peace, love, and respect.

Here at Harlem Shake®, your favorite memories of our vibrant, uniquely historical corner of NYC are re-created and preserved with the help of crave-worthy Burgers and Shakes, crafted using only high-quality ingredients.
