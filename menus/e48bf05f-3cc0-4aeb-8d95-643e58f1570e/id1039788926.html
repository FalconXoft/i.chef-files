Do you love to order food online from your favourite restaurant? Use this app to keep in touch faster, easier and better with the restaurant you eat frequently from. If you are a loyal customer of one of our member-restaurants, this is the online food ordering app for you. Features: - order online your food from your chosen restaurant(s) - based on location, the app automatically displays the restaurant(s) you order from - you can see if restaurant is ready to take orders now and quickly browse their menu - your contact details will be pre-filled so next time you order easier and faster - your order confirmation includes pickup/delivery time estimation, provided by the restaurant staff especially for you and your order. - your order will be confirmed in real time as member restaurants are directly handling your order immediately. No middle-man, no confusing call-center, no over-promises. This is just between you and your chosen restaurant.
