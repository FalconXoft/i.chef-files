ABOUT US

Le Fleur Rouge brings the soul of Chinese cuisine and the artistry of French technique together in the heart of Brooklyn. From our signature Lobster Bisque Noodles and Black Truffle Beef Noodles to Tea-Infused Duck and Steak au Poivre, every dish is a harmony of comfort and elegance. Whether you’re dining in or enjoying our freshly prepared takeout, we serve warmth, creativity, and refined flavor in every bite — no passport needed, just your appetite.
