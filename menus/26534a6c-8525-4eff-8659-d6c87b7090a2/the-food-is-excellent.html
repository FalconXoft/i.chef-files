Authentic Italian Menu in New York

After 47 years this proud, family-run restaurant continues to offer large portions at modest prices.

Villa Mosconi’s famous pastas are still homemade on the premises daily using a pasta machine imported from Italy. Their rustic, earthy, full-flavored entrees never cease to be authentic Italian cuisine.

In addition to the traditional menu choices there are plenty of specials prepared daily depending on what is procured at the market, guaranteeing freshness.
