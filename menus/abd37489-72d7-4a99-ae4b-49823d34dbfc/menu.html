Pasquale’s been serving your favorite Italian food in Boonton since 1989. 

 

With the combination of high-quality ingredients, great food, and affordable prices it’s no surprise that Pasquale’s Pizza has been a local favorite for thirty-two years. 

Whether you want to grab a quick Grandma’s slice after baseball practice, sit around at home with friends eating good food, and drinking wine or need a corporate lunch catered Pasquale’s has options for everyone! Don’t just take our word for it. Pasquale’s won a Pizza throwdown in Boonton taking home bragging rights of “Best Crust”, “Best Sauce”, “Best Regular Sicilian”, “Best Grandma’s” and “Best Overall”. We look forward to serving you.
