About Us

Bienvenidos


Avocado Haven Mexican Fresh is a quick service Mexican restaurant in New Haven, Connecticut


We are the perfect place to pick up a quick meal and also a great option for your next catering event.


Our first location at 39 Elm St. New Haven offers a wide selection of fresh and delicious food from Mexico. We pride ourselves on only selecting the freshest ingredients. 


Avocado Haven was founded by Carmen and Robert Klinger the owners of the New Haven Salad Shop


The New Haven Salad Shop and Avocado Haven are planning to expand to other cities in and out of Connecticut


We hope to see you soon!
