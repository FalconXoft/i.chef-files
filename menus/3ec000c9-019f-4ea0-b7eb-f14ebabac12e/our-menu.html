Shama Restaurant
Shama Restaurant, located in the heart of New York City, offers a delightful blend of authentic Indian and Pakistani cuisine. Nestled near the vibrant neighborhoods of Astoria and Long Island City, Shama is known for its rich flavors and welcoming atmosphere. Guests can enjoy a variety of traditional dishes, from savory curries to freshly baked naan, all crafted with the finest ingredients. Whether you're dining in or ordering takeout, Shama promises a memorable culinary experience just minutes away from local parks and cultural hotspots.
