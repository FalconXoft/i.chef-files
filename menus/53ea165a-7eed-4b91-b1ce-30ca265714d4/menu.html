Thanksgiving Prix-Fixe Menu

Thanksgiving at Levant on Smith – Thursday, November 28, 2024

Join us for a special Thanksgiving prix fixe dinner at Levant on Smith in Brooklyn.
Enjoy a cozy, 3-course meal featuring seasonal favorites and a complimentary glass of wine, cocktail, or mocktail.


First Course

One Choice Per Guest

  • Butternut Squash Soup

    Homemade butternut squash soup for Thanksgiving Day.

  • Chicken Croquettes

    Crispy chicken croquettes (3pcs) with a creamy filling,served with homemade aioli

  • ZUCCHINI PANCAKES

    Lightly fried savory blend of fresh shredded zucchini,vegetables and feta cheese .Served with tomato sauce and sour cream

  • SHRIMP FLAMBÉ

    Jumbo shrimps flambéed with Pastis Parisian brasserie-style and garlic parsley.

Main Course

One Choice Per Guest

  • Traditional Turkey Dinner

    Oven-Roasted Turkey with mashed potato Roasted Brussels Sprouts , Cranberry Sauce and pan Gravy

  • Steak Frites

    Tender hanging tenderloin steak grilled to perfection Served with French Fries

  • Seabass

    Fillet of sea bass served with mashed potato, sauteed baby spinach with red wine jus

  • Légumes Rôtis

    Roasted seasonal fresh vegetables, tossed with garlic, extra virgin olive oil, and lemon juice.Vegan, Vegeterian,Gluten Free

Dessert

  • Homemade Pumpkin Pie

    Enjoy our homemade pumpkin pie: creamy pumpkin filling

  • Creme Brulee

    Creamy custard flavored with espresso, topped with caramelized sugar

Beverage

  • Complimentary Beverage

    (Limit of 1 Glass Per Guest) Enjoy a complimentary glass of your choice—wine, cocktail, beer, or mocktail
