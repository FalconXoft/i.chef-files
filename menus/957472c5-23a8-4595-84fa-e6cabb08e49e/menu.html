About SkyIce

SkyIce Thai Food Restaurant & Ice Cream Shop

Launched in Park Slope, Brooklyn in 2011 offering an exotic destination for satisfying your sweet and savory spirit. We specialize in all-natural homemade small batch hand crafted ice cream and provincial authentic Thai home cooking. A wild and culinary delightful combination not encountered anywhere else in the world.


Family owned and operated, our mission is to create a distinguished gastronomical experience transporting your tastebuds to new heights, with a focus on healthy eating while doing our part to be environmentally responsible.


Our menu was carefully crafted to encompass the unique flavors and dishes from the 83 provinces and six regions of Thailand. Whether it's Khao Soi from the North, Larb from the East, Crab Curry from the South each region has it's own unique flavor breathing life into each dish.  We are 100% halal.


SkyIce brand ice cream lives somewhere in between ice cream and gelato. While we make your familiar favorites like Caramel Sea Salt and Belgian Chocolate Brownie we are most known for our Asian inspired classics like Black Sesame Seaweed, Durian, White Miso Almond, and our most popular ice cream Thai Tea. 


Of course, we have something for everyone! A plethora of options awaits eaters of all kinds: vegans, vegetarians, and gluten free. Even our ice cream and sorbet. 


While you sit back and take cool photos in our ice cream shop or snack on one of our homemade appetizers for Instagram let us dazzle your taste buds and take you where no other ice cream and Thai food has taken you before.


Stop by our ice cream shop and Thai restaurant today and feast!
