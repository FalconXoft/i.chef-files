AboutGreat Food and Live Music

Welcome to Woodside Cafe! Our family is proud to offer a unique dining and entertainment experience that celebrates the rich cultural heritage of the Newari and Nepali community. Our menu features a wide variety of traditional dishes that are made from the freshest local ingredients and prepared with love and care. Whether you're in the mood for savory momos, spicy chow mein, or sweet lassi, you're sure to find something to satisfy your appetite!

In addition to our delicious food, we also offer a full bar with an extensive selection of beers, wines, and cocktails. We believe that good food and good drinks are best enjoyed with good company, and we strive to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels like family. Enjoy live music every Friday through Sunday starting at 8:30pm!
