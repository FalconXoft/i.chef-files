Catering Menu

We are Catering Specialists!

Whether it is a wedding, company picnic, or dinner party, our expert team of chefs and professional staff has a proven track record of delivering an outstanding range of menus elegantly served to your satisfaction.

We offer three (3) different catering packages and a Wedding package with extensive item selections that is customizable for all budgets.

We have done and can cater up to 3,000 people per day for indoor or outdoor events. Our custom delivery truck makes it all possible. Look for it on our next outing!

We also cater to states in the Tri-state area such as New Jersey, Pennsylvania, Connecticut, Massachusetts and as far south as Washington DC and Virginia.
