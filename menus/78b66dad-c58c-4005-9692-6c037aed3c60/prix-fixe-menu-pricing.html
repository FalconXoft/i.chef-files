Prix Fixe Menu Pricing for Large Groups

Looking to book a party for thirteen or more guests?

Each of our 4 dining rooms can seat from 30-45 guests and can be available in either shared or private configurations for your next event! Please consider the following Prix Fixe menu options.

Weekday- Luncheon- (2 hour seating* for 4 or 5 course Menu)

$37.50 p/person + tax + 20% gratuity & 5% administration fee

 Mesclun Salad and Soup (Cold or Hot Seasonal Options, choice at table)
Assorted Pizzas served family style AND/OR
      + $10.00 for assorted Lasagna flights served family style (as additional course or served instead of pizzas)
  *Pizza and/or Lasagna determined at time of booking*
  Tiramisu with choice of Coffee or Tea
  Soft drinks

     Add Wine & Beer ($)
        +$9.50 for an allocation of up to 1 glass of wine or beer p/person (guests have a choice of 3 reds, 3 whites, 2 demi-secs, dessert wines including Champagne and Prosecco and a selection of 8 beers)

Wine & CHeese Event- (2 HOUR SEATING*)

$40.00 p/person + tax + 20% gratuity & 5% administration fee

  Cheese, Affettati and Fruit Platters with warm Focaccia Crisps
  Soft drinks
  Up to 2 servings of wine or beer p/person (guests have a choice of 3 reds, 3 whites, 2 demi-secs, dessert wines including Champagne and Prosecco and a selection of 8 beers)

      Add an Entree ($)
        + $15.50 for assorted Pizzas served family style OR
        + $18.75 for assorted Lasagna flights served family style
     Add More Wine & Beer ($)
        +$9.50 for an allocation of up to 1 glass of wine or beer p/person (guests have a choice of 3 reds, 3 whites, 2 demi-secs, dessert wines including Champagne and Prosecco and a selection of 8 beers)

Brunch/Dinner Menu- (3 hour seating* for 4 or 5 course menu)
$50.00 p/person + tax + 20% gratuity & 5% administration fee

  Cheese, Affettati and Fruit Platters with warm Focaccia Crisps
  Mesclun Salad
  Assorted Pizzas served family style AND/OR
          + $10.00 for assorted Lasagna flights served family style (as additional course or served instead of pizzas)
    *ALL Pizza and/or ALL Lasagna determined at time of booking*
  Tiramisu with choice of Coffee or Tea
  Soft drinks

        Add Wine & Beer Only ($)
                  +$9.50, +19 or +$28.50 for an allocation of up to 1, 2 or 3 serving(s) of wine or beer (guests have a choice of 3 reds, 3 whites, 2 demi-sec, Champagne and Prosecco)

        Add Full Bar ($)
                  +$55.00 for an allocation of up to 3 servings of wine, beer, tier 2 mixed drinks, plus designate two specialty cocktails from our martini, hot stuff or seasonal cocktail list

*Additional seating time may be added to your reservation in advance via increased purchase minimum. Parties that exceed their seating allocation are charged a higher hold-over fee p/quarter hour beyond their original booking. Room charge(s) may be assessed for dining room(s) booked privately. Purchase minimum, hold-over fee and room charge(s) vary depending on day and time of reservation.
