About

Get ready to embark on a gastronomic journey to Turkey without leaving New Jersey. Our menu boasts a enticing array of Turkish Mediterranean specialties, from juicy kebabs to mezzes, ensuring there's something to please everyone. Each dish is crafted with care, using fresh ingredients and traditional recipes passed down through generations
Whether you're a regular looking for your Turkish fix or a newcomer eager to explore Mediterranean flavors, Dervish Restaurant is the perfect destination for your next meal. Our cozy restaurant is the ideal setting for a casual lunch with friends or a relaxed dinner with loved ones. Can't stay? No problem! Simply place your order online and swing by to pick it up, or sit back and let us bring the flavors of Turkey to you.
