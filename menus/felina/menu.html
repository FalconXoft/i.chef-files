Venue Showcase | Felina Ridgewood

Iconic Dining & Event Space

Felina Ridgewood’s neoclassical bank space is home to Felina, one of New Jersey’s most celebrated restaurants and iconic event spaces including the Treasury and Bank Vault, all combining to impress and delight the senses while creating moments never to be forgotten.

  • Location: 18 Prospect St Ridgewood, NJ 07450
  • Phone:  (551)-276-5454

Eat

Felina is one of New Jersey’s most celebrated restaurants. The airy industrial space houses a restaurant that is both sleek and reflective of Italian-inspired cuisine. The interplay of textures and color bring life and vibrancy that solidifies its place in the center of the Village of Ridgewood. Felina’s hip and beautiful setting lends itself to an energetic scene in downtown.

With delicious food that balances seasonality and technique, and expertly paired vintage spirits and a Wine Spectator awardwinning wine list, you’ll be drawn in even more. Our food and beverage program at Felina work in unison with the seasonal harvest, showcasing each in-season ingredient, perfectly.

LA TERRAZZA

Nestled above a historic bank in the upscale community of Ridgewood, New Jersey, this rooftop bar is inspired by traditional Italian cafes. An outdoor oasis with views of the town below, La Terrazza features highly curated cocktails and handmade pizza from our outdoor brick oven. Filled with instagrammable moments, this rooftop bar captures the beauty and essence of the Italian seaside, bottled up for New Jersey.

La Terrazza is weather dependent and is first come, first served.
