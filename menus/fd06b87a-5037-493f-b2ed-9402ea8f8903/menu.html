ABOUT

Jing Li is a modern Chinese fusion restaurant nestled in the heart of Long Island City, Queens, just a short train ride from Manhattan. Inspired by Szechuan, Shanghai, and Cantonese cuisines, Jing Li brings a unique twist to global flavors. Each dish is expertly crafted by the executive chef with fresh ingredients and meticulous attention to detail.
