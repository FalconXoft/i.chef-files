Specials

Long Branch Food Specials

  • Mango Habanero Boneless Wings

    Our juicy hand breaded boneless wings tossed in our sweet and spicy mango habanero sauce with fresh chopped mangoes, finished with a sprinkle of chopped scallions and served with creamy ranch dressing for dipping

  • Stuffed Chicken

    A juicy panko breaded chicken breast stuffed with mozzarella cheese and Grandma’s sausage & apple stuffing, oven baked then sliced and served over a bed of mashed potatoes and finished with a ladle of rich brown gravy

  • Poutine Pizza

    Our homemade pizza dough rolled in sesame seeds then topped with our rich brown gravy, French fries, natural cheddar cheese curds and shredded mozzarella cheese then oven baked till bubbling

  • Homemade Pozole Soup

    Abuela’s traditional Mexican soup, rich with hand pulled oven roasted chicken, Mexican corn, dried chilis, garlic, onions and cilantro, served piping hot, topped with sliced avocado and a side of crispy tortilla chips

  • Texas BBQ Turkey Sandwich

    Thick slices of fresh oven roasted turkey breast drizzled with hickory barbecue sauce then topped with caramelized onions, melted cheddar cheese, and homemade coleslaw served on grilled Texas toast with a side of shoestring fries and Richard’s famous macaroni salad

  • Shrimp & Gnocchi

    Jumbo U15 shrimp sautéed with sweet oven roasted tomatoes and fresh spinach tossed with delicate potato gnocchi in our homemade roasted tomato cream sauce, finished with a drizzle of fresh basil pesto and served with a side of garlic bread

  • Dueling Quesadillas

    Two 10” flour tortillas, 1 stuffed with juicy steak carnitas, fresh jalapeños, cilantro, onions, mozzarella and cheddar cheese the other stuffed with flame grilled chicken breast, bell peppers, onions, mozzarella & cheddar cheese then toasted on our hot plancha and served with fresh salsa roja and salsa verde
