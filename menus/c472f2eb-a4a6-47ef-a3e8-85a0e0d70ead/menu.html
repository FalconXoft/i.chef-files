About Naples Pizzeria

We offer the best in Italian Cuisine.  Bring a bottle of wine and enjoy our quaint dining area.  Take-out or utilize our fast and free delivery service.  Our signature items include Buffalo Chicken Pizza, Chicken Caesar Salad, and Penne in Vodka Sauce.  We also offer many other unique Gourmet Pizzas along with your favorite Italian classics, a large variety of subs, calzones, and appetizers.
