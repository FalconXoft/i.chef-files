New York Bagels + Delicatessen

New York Bagels + Delicatessen

Good food, every day.

GERTIE is a modern “Jew-ish” appetizing shop and delicatessen inspired by New York’s great bagel and brunch institutions, a space for our community to gather and celebrate.

A home away from home to catch up with friends and family, or simply sit with a coffee and your thoughts— just like at Grandma’s.

OUR STORY

Our offerings

Freshly baked bagels, bialys, and pastries, a latke bar with customizable toppings, riffs on NY deli sandwiches and sides, big salads, house-smoked fish and meats, matzoh ball soup, and more “Jew-ish” inspired comfort foods.

Our drink counter features espresso from Parlor Coffee, teas from Spirit Tea, a full bar with inventive brunch cocktails, nostalgic sodas inspired by Dr. Brown’s, and local wines and beers.
