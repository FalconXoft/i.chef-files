ABOUT US

Nestled in the vibrant heart of New York, at 64-18 Fresh Pond Road, lies an authentic slice of Italy waiting to be explored: welcome to Mon Cheri. This chic Italian restaurant, located in the lively and multicultural borough of Queens, offers a true gastronomic escape from the pulse of the city.

Surrounded by an area rich in history and cultural diversity, Mon Cheri blends seamlessly into the neighborhoods unique character. Here, tradition meets innovation, creating a dining experience that mirrors the communitys dynamic spirit a place where the flavors of Italy feel right at home.

A full day with us

Discover how you can enjoy Mon Cheri at any moment of the day.
Start your morning with a personalized breakfast featuring printed cappuccinos and Italian pastries. For lunch and dinner, savor authentic Italian specialties prepared with care. As the evening begins, our automatic cocktail machine crafts the perfect aperitif. After dinner, unwind in a relaxed, sophisticated atmosphere with sweet treats and expertly prepared cocktails.
At Mon Cheri, every hour is the perfect time to visit an experience thats memorable from morning to night.
