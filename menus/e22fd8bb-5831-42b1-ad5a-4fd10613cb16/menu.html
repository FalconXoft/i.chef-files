Al Fresco Dining

Matunuck Oyster Bar is now open for al fresco dining. We are located at the marina parking lot across the street from our restaurant, and are looking forward to welcoming guests to our tented dining area in Matunuck Marina overlooking the Salt Pond.

Matunuck Oyster Bar will offer table side dining, as well as grab and go options. Our operating hours for outdoor dining will be daily from 11am-10pm. Click here for reservations and take-out. Guests will now pull into the marina parking lot where our valet team will be ready to assist.

We are incredibly grateful to our municipal and state leaders for their partnership in helping us execute this plan. This outdoor dining space will allow us to welcome guests back this summer, as well as keeping many of our valued team members employed.

We look forward to seeing you under the Matunuck Oyster Bar tent, at 650 Succotash Road, East Matunuck.
