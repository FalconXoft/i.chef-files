                About Us

                Our Chef spiro and Staff

                  Athena Mediterranean Cuisine is a family-owned Greek restaurant whose sole purpose is to provide our community in
                our Park Slope neighborhood with 
                Greek food with a twist.

                Our menu is comprised of recipes passed down from generation to generation as well as our new recipes giving a twist to our Greek flavor, that is guaranteed to take you to a mythical
                Greek island with every bite.

                At Athena Mediterranean Cuisine we pride ourselves in offering high-quality dishes made with the finest ingredients at affordable prices;
                because food this good should be accessible to all.

                We hope to see you soon and look forward to serving you and your loved ones. your dining experience is top-notch!
