About

We are a family run business since 2011 serving various communities in Brooklyn, Staten Island & Queens. Since we opened our first location, our mission has been to deliver the best service along with the tastiest food to our customers. We look forward to seeing you soon

Our Customers

We would like to thank all of our amazing customers. We have had the pleasure of meeting so many great people over the years and we are beyond thankful for the support. We look forward to continue serving everyone for many years to come. Thank you everyone!

Our Food

We specialize in Paninis, Sandwiches, Wraps, Salads, Crepes, and much more. We use only the finest and freshest ingredients and we thrive on serving only the best food with bold flavors that will make your taste buds dance.
