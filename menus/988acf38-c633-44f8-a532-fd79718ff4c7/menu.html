Founded by French owner,  Robert Arbor, in 1992 on Macdougal Street in Soho.  Le Gamin expanded to become a local chain of cafes known for its authentic casual French ambiance, popular neighborhood feel, fresh food and signature crpes.

Now located in Greenpoint Brooklyn and Sharon Connecticut, Le Gamin continues to serve fine French comfort food made from seasonal and locally sourced ingredients.
