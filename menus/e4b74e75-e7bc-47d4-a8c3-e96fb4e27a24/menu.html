    Your Table Is Waiting

    Nestled in the heart of Lower Manhattan, Morton's The Steakhouse, World Trade Center features USDA prime-aged steaks, succulent fish and the freshest seafood in Manhattan, all served in an atmosphere of unmatched refinement. Morton's is centrally-located, near many of New York's most exciting attractions. The 9/11 Memorial is just steps away, while The Statue of Liberty, Ellis Island, Wall Street and City Hall are all less than a mile away. Morton's is the perfect destination for an evening on the town, a romantic dinner or a productive business meeting. Our distinctive private boardrooms are ideal for any private event, while Bar 12-21 provides a sleek and sophisticated gathering spot for after work drinks and Bar Bites.

    General Manager: Charles Villanueva
