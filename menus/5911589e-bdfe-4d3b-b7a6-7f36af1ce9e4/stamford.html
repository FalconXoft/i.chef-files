About Our Stamford Studio

Food & Drinks

Enjoy a wide variety of soft drinks, wine, beer, craft cocktails/mocktails and gourmet coffee. Premium wines are also offered by the bottle.

View the bar menu

Classes by Age

Read class descriptions carefully before booking. Generally, children must be accompanied by an adult. Kids ages 6-13 are generally allowed in "Little Brushes" and "Family Day" classes.

Directions & Parking

We are located at 25 Harbor Point Rd (between Fortina and Bare Burger). FREE GARAGE PARKING is available at 2200 Atlantic Street. We are also a short walk from the Stamford train station.
