Let the Good Times FLOW!

Pinot's Palette Stamford boasts a full-service bar with a wide range of top-shelf liquors, specialty cocktails, beer, and wine by the glass or bottle. Our extensive non-alcoholic offerings include craft mocktails and sodas, Athletic NA Beer and CleanCo nonalcoholic spirits. You can also enjoy fresh iced or hot drip or espresso drinks from the gourmet coffee bar which features sustainably sourced organic beans. We carry bagged snacks and sweets, including Sweet P Bakery cookies (coming Fall 2025).

In addition to our standard bar menu, we feature exciting bar specials like red or white Sangria, Sake tasting, and many more! Stop by and see what's new!
