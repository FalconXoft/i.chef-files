Welcome to Ladybird

A lush wonderland in the heart of the East Village. Our vibrant vegetable bar serves whimsical plant-based tapas in a dreamy space filled with velvet banquettes, golden accents, and hanging ferns. From NYCs best bao buns to playful cocktails and a wine list featuring all female-owned producers, Ladybird is the go-to for date nights, girls nights, and unforgettable evenings out.

Now kosher. Walk-ins and solo diners are always welcome.
