About Us

Simply Jerk® Jamaican Grill is a family-owned and operated restaurant that was created by the owners of Jamaican Delight and The Green Grotto restaurants, which were staples in the heart of New Brunswick for 15 years.


In 2014, Simply Jerk® was created as the answer to an increasing demand for a healthier fast-food without sacrificing quality.


Simply Jerk® is a trending restaurant concept with a modern-rustic ambiance that is welcoming to all walks of life. It utilizes the finest and freshest ingredients to provide its guests with authentic Jamaican cuisine and an unforgettable experience, from start to finish.
