    WHO  WE  ARE

    Discover

    We are Marani, the only glatt kosher Georgian restaurant in the USA. Brimming with flavor, we are dedicated to bringing the culture we grew up with right unto your plate. The Georgian culture is rich and full of history, braving many empires, it's still standing strong. A small nation with a lot of life, Georgian culture is deeply intertwined with hospitality. At Marani, we bring those values to you; come with an appetite and get comfortable because if theres one thing Georgians love more than a good time, it's eating! Explore our rich cuisine, where you're bound to fall in love with some juicy Khinkali, or maybe get lost in our Chashushuli! If you're thirsty we have refreshing cocktails or even better, Georgian wine!

    Did you know Georgia is one of the oldest countries to have started making wine? Maybe even the first! Safe to say that we've perfected the recipe by now and you're lucky enough to be able to try it right here at Marani!

    Across the world, but it feels like home.
