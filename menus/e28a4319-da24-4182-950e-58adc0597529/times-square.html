About us

Empanada Mama is known for having the biggest and best empanadas in NYC. Empanada Mama serves over 40 unique flavors of empanadas at three locations in NYC; Hell's Kitchen, Lower East Side, and Times Square. Empanada Mama offers delivery and catering, too. Tap on the Order Online button above to find out if we deliver Empanadas to you in NYC. Every empanada, appetizer, and entre is made with the finest and freshest ingredients. We believe in fast, delicious and flavorful meals that don't break the bank.
