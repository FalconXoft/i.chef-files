Our story

La Cabaña Argentina has been proudly serving the Queens community since 1971, bringing the rich flavors and warm traditions of Argentine cuisine to New York City. We began our journey on Roosevelt Avenue and 95th Street, where we built a loyal following with our authentic grilled meats and welcoming atmosphere. In the year 2000, we opened a new location at 86th Street and Northern Boulevard, which continues to be our home today. As the only remaining location, this beloved spot carries on our legacy, offering guests a true taste of Argentina with every visit.
