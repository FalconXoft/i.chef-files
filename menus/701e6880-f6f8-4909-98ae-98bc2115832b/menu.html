Real Madrid Restaurant

Exquisite Cuisine from the Heart of Spain

Starting in 1986,

Real Madrid Restaurant opened it’s doors as one of the first Spanish restaurant on Staten Island. It still stands as the oldest Spanish restaurant thanks to the consistency of the food and service. Your hosts, Miguel & Mario greet you as you come into Real Madrid with friends & family to connect over great sangria and Spanish food.
