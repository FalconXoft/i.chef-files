Welcome to Le Petit CAFE!


Le Petit Cafe is the perfect neighborhood spot. The atmosphere is always friendly and the food is consistently good!! This is also one of the most reasonably priced places in Brooklyn which, given the current economy, is a real gift.

We offer a wide selection of soups, wraps, sandwiches, coffees, salads, entrees, pastries & desserts, and we serve breakfast, lunch, brunch, and dinner.

Our newly expanded backyard is a great place to relax and try our wonderful Fruit and Vegetable Juices (like the Ginger Grant: apple, carrot & ginger) or our Fruit Smoothies (like the Studio Smoothie: raspberry, pineapple, pear, apple & banana). Or try one of our Fresh Homemade Soups!
