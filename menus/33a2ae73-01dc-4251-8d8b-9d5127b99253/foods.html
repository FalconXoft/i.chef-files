A heartwarming taste of
Tokyo to Manhattan

Loved by
locals since 1985

Sake Bar Hagi is New York’s first authentic izakaya: a cozy Japanese tapas bar that
serves everything from yakitori to sushi.

Founded and owned by Japanese foodies, our mission is simple—to bring a heartwarming
taste of Tokyo to Manhattan.

Inspired by Showa Era under-the-bridge izakayas, our interior features old records and
trinkets reminiscent of an earlier time.

Whether you’re with friends, co-workers, or on a casual date, come step into
Sake Bar Hagi for a comforting taste of old-school Japan.

萩は、1985年にNY市初の日本スタイル本格居酒屋をオープンしました。
当店自慢の創作料理や、寿司・焼き鳥などに加え、バラエティに富んだ日本酒や焼酎をご用意。
NYで日本居酒屋を彷彿とさせる味と雰囲気をご堪能ください。

A place for
sake lovers

We take our sake seriously, carefully choosing
the best of the best from all across Japan. We’ve also got shochu,
umeshu, wine and plenty of spirits to go around.

日本各地から厳選した銘柄日本酒を取り揃えております。
また、焼酎や梅酒、ワインなども種類豊富。
お料理と相性抜群の一杯で乾杯！
