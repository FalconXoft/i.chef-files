Killmeyer’s Beer Garden

Usually open from early May through Mid-Fall, our beer garden delivers a wonderful experience. You can be dining outside on a beautiful summer evening, while enjoy a cocktail and listen to live music on the beer garden stage; or watching a game on either of our flat screens while sitting at the outdoor bar. The beer garden can even be reserved for private events.

Finest European and Craft Beers

Killmeyer’s is one of only a small handful of NYC bars to win the prestigious Good Beer Seal. The Beer Menu changes for every season. Taste our amazing craft, German and seasonal brews that flow through our 16 taps inside and 12 taps in the beer garden. We also have over 100 bottles including craft brews, amazing selections from all over the world.
