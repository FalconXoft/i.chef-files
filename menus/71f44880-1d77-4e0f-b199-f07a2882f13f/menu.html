2-Course Brunch ($30)

2025 NYC Restaurant Week
2-Course Brunch for $30
(July 21st - August 31th, 2025)


On Weekends, celebrate NYC Restaurant Week with a Bold Georgian Brunch! 
We're inviting you to discover the vibrant and bold flavors of Georgian cuisine with our exclusive $30 two-course brunch. 

Start your brunch journey with one of our flavorful appetizers, Khinkali Dumplings or Kveri Dumplings (Suglani cheese & spinach soup dumplings). Move on to a brunch favorite such as: Shrimp Walnut Kharcho (Shrimp in a garlicky walnut sauce with Elarji polenta) or Chirbuli Shakshuka, our Georgian twist on shakshuka. 

Whether you're new to Georgian food or a longtime fan, our menu is crafted to delight every palate, offering vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options alongside our hearty, authentic classics. Reserve your table today and go on a weekend adventure!
