    Giving Back

    The Spaniard is proud to be partnering with charity: water to contribute to their efforts to bring clean water to families in need. 663 million people on our planet lack access to life’s most basic need, something we all take for granted: clean and safe drinking water. Access to clean water improves health, gives children the chance to go to school and offers new employment opportunities. We’re working with charity: water because 100% of donations fund clean water projects.
