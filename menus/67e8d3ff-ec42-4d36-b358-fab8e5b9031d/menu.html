About

Oasis Diner Restaurant

Welcome to Oasis Diner Restaurant, the heart of comfort food nestled at 2132 Flatbush Ave, Brooklyn, NY. This quaint eatery serves as a beacon for locals and visitors alike, enticing them with the promise of hearty meals and a warm, inviting atmosphere.

At Oasis Diner, you can expect a delightful blend of traditional diner fare alongside exciting new creations. The menu is expansive, featuring everything from fluffy pancakes and succulent burgers to vegan delights for those seeking alternatives. Customers rave about the generous portion sizes—something you’ll find is a hallmark of this beloved establishment. A regular diner-goer, Rob H., aptly noted that it offers “good, reliable comfort food” that never disappoints.

The atmosphere at Oasis is both casual and welcoming, making it an ideal spot for family gatherings, brunch with friends, or a late-night snack. With outdoor seating available, you can enjoy your meal al fresco, enhancing that neighborhood feel that patrons like Rachel C. have come to love. She noted the “wonderful neighborhood vibe,” which is complemented by friendly service that makes you feel right at home.

Accessibility is a priority here, with wheelchair access, bike parking, and various payment methods accepted, including Android Pay and Apple Pay. The restaurant is kid-friendly and also invites four-legged companions to join the dining experience. Whether you're stopping by for breakfast, brunch, lunch, or dinner, Oasis Diner has you covered.

As a food reviewer, I can definitively say that Oasis Diner is a place you should not miss when in Brooklyn. Whether you're biting into a classic BLT with an impressive helping of bacon or savoring the authentic flavors of their chicken souvlaki, you’ll leave with a satisfied palate and a smile on your face. So gather your friends and family, and make your way to Oasis Diner for a meal that is sure to comfort and delight!
