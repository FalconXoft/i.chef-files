Everybody Eats

Our way of saying no one goes home hungry - a mentality that applies to more than just food.

Welcome to Comptons: a neighborhood sandwich shop that believes good people deserve good food.


In 2018, Alex Compton opened his first shop in Queens, NY with the goal of serving food that tastes as good as it looks. With a menu that stayed true to his New York roots and had a little something for everyone, Comptons quickly became a go-to neighborhood spot.

Since opening our doors in 2018, Comptons has expanded to four locations; and our attention to detail and focus on quality has only grown. From the music playing when you come in, to the board games on the counters, to the products on our shelves, we want everyone who stops by to feel like theyve stumbled on a secret they cant help but share.

By using the highest quality ingredients in our classic sandwich staples and getting creative with guilty pleasure recipes, our menu reflects the energy and diversity of the neighborhoods we serve. We love when customers tell us their visit was worth the hype.

As we continue to grow and partner with new communities, our commitment to originality and authenticity remains at our core. Our bread is a Comptons exclusive recipe you wont find anywhere else, and our house-made condiments are flavors you wont come across in other sandwich shops. We're proud of everything that sets us apart, but the honest truth is we only know how to serve food that we enjoy ourselves.

As we like to say, whether youre grabbing a quick bite between meetings, fueling up after a night out, celebrating a win, nursing a hangover, sticking to a clean-eating plan, catching up with a friend, or just following your daily routine everybody eats.
