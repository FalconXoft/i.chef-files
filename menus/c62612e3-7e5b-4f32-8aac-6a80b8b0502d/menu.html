  • Sharon Man

    star star star star star 20 November 2025

    Great all you can eat hot pot with many soup options and you can have a split pot so you can try two different soups (we typically like spicy beef and mushroom+seaweed). Their medium is actually very spicy, so you may want to get the mild and spice it up as needed at the sauce bar, which is well stocked. There is also a great ice cream and soft drink selection included in the price. Fun experience.

  • Alex Blitstein

    star star star star star 19 November 2025

    I really am really happy this is in my city. I think they have even managed to step up their game since my last visit. Given that it is all you can eat hotpot, you would think that the staff could be a little bit slower, this is not the case at all, they were buzzing at full pace, and super friendly. The selection of meats, veggies and broths are really quite good, and all the free drinks are great as well. We love coming here with just 2 of us, because we can get so much variety. This is just an overwhelmingly positive experience. we will certainly be back.
