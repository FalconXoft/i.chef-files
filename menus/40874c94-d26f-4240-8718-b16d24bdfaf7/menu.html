SELECTION OF MEATS

Main Course

Steak

  • Picanha / Prime part of Top Sirloin

    Our signature cut, a gourmet delight. Served over a garlic crust or sea salt.

  • Alcatra / Top sirloin

    Served thinly carved, this tender cut comes from the same section as the Top Sirloin.

  • Fraldinha / Flank Steak

    Known as the bottom of the sirloin, this cut has an extra rich taste and distinctive texture.

  • Picanha com alho / Garlic steak

    expertly grilled and infused with garlic

Chicken

  • Coxa de frango / Chicken thighs

    Lightly marinated in a mixture of onion, garlic, beer, salt, and pepper, our chicken will set the tone for the most authentic Brazilian food to come

  • Coração de frango / Chicken hearts

    Skewered little hearts, roasted to chewy perfection.

Pork

  • Pork Loin/ Lombo

    Our "meatier" than the usual Pork Ribs are rubbed in a special blend of spices that tenderizes the meat.

  • Linguiça de Porco / Pork sausage

    Perfectly seasoned and deliciously crispy.

Lamb

  • Picanha de Carneiro / Lamb Picanha

    This lean and tender cut is also known as lamb’s prime steak.

More
