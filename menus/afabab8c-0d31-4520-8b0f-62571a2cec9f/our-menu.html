About us

The restaurant was opened in Feb 21st, 1991 in the Westchester Area – Mount Vernon, New York.

Specializes in authentic Cajun food, hot spicy sauces and strong drinks, absolutely incredible nightly live music shows.

We are always happy to have you at our restaurant.
