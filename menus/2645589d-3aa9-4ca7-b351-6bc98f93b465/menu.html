Dinner Menu (Tuesday-Sunday)

Please note, our kitchen is available:

Tuesday: 4pm-10pm

Wednesday & Thursday: 4pm-11pm

Friday & Saturday: 4pm-1am

Sunday: 2pm-8:30pm

To ensure all our guests have the opportunity to dine with us, we have the following seating times:

(1-2 guests: 1.5 hours) (3-6 guests: 2 hours)

(7-11 guest: 2.5 hours) (12-18 guests: 3 hours)

Of course, if we don't have another reservation for your table, we are delighted for you to enjoy a longer visit with us! Please note, the above referenced seating times are simply a guideline.

Thank you.
