Billy's Pizza & Pasta

Every pizza lover knows that there's nothing like the taste of a freshly baked pie ordered from a family-owned restaurant. From the secret recipe passed down for generations to the hand-made dough and sauce, Billy's Pizza & Pasta embodies all of the above. We opened our doors back in 2007 at 1626 Bedford Ave in Brooklyn, and we have been serving you delicious pizzas ever since. The main goal of our restaurant remained the same throughout the years – to use top of the line products to serve our customers the best and finest food available. You should try our signature gourmet pizzas, but we also offer some other fantastic dishes on the menu. Our buffalo wings are the talk of the neighborhood, and the garlic knots are genuinely lip-smacking. Moreover, if you love Italian food, our pasta should definitely be on your list. Order your favorite pizza, pasta, salad, and more, all with the click of a button. We accept orders online for pickup and free delivery!
