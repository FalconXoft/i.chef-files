We are a classic New Orleans-inspired neighborhood restaurant nestled in Prospect Heights, Brooklyn

 
 

CHEF AND PROPRIETOR, JOHN VERLANDER

 

We're here to pay homage to the joints that make New Orleans the best food city in America. The places where you can delight in freshly shucked oysters on the half shell without pretense, feel happy with a hot roast beef po-boy dripping with gravy, enjoy a steaming bowl of seafood gumbo, and wash it all back with an ice cold beer.

Our food is pure Creole soul made fresh daily from scratch.

And we're nice. Come hang out.
