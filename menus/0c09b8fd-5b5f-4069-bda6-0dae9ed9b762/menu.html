Vegetarian Appetizers

  • Aloo Papri Chaat

    Wheat crisps topped with chick peas, potatoes, yogurt, roasted cumin, mint and tamarind chutneys.

  • Aloo Tikki

    Potato patties seasoned with ginger and spices.

  • Benarsi Kachori

    Crispy flour puffs served with tomato salsa, potatoes and chutneys.

  • Bhel Puri

    Puffed rice crisps topped with chopped onions, potatoes, avocado, corn kernels, and tossed with chutneys.

Non-Vegetarian Appetizers

  • Aachari Chicken Tikka

    Pickle flavored boneless chicken cooked in tandoor.

  • Chicken 65

    Stir-fried chicken salted with chilies, onion, ginger and garlic.

  • Chicken Tikka

    Boneless chicken marinated in yogurt and spices.

  • Crab Chettinadu

    Lump crab meat seasoned with garlic, ginger, roasted coconut, and spices

  • Fish Amritsari

    Fish marinated in ginger, garlic, garam masala, and yogurt fried to perfection.

  • Mixed Vegtables

Goat & Lamb Entrees

  • Barrah Masaladar

  • Kadhai Goat

    Goat cooked with fresh tomatoes, onions, ginger and garlic.

  • Keema Mutter (Minced Lamb)

    Minced lamb and green peas cooked with fresh ground spices.

  • Laal Maas

    Lamb cooked in slow fire with yogurt, chili, turmeric, onion, ginger, garlic and cardamom.

  • Lamb Korma

    Tender pieces of lamb cooked in rich cashew nut and almond cream gravy.
