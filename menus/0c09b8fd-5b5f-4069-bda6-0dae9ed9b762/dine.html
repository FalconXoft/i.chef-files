Dine in Menu

At Benares, our Dine-In Menu offers an extensive selection designed to satisfy every palate. Begin your meal with our rich, flavorful soups and fresh salads, followed by a variety of vegetarian and non-vegetarian appetizers.

From crispy vegetarian delights to succulent chicken and tender lamb, our appetizers are the perfect introduction to the bold flavors of Indian cuisine. Choose from a wide range of entrées, including vegetarian, vegan, chicken, goat, lamb, and seafood options, each prepared with fresh, authentic ingredients and expertly blended spices.

For those who prefer the Indian tandoori menu, our Tandoori Grill entrées offer a smoky, charred flavor that brings a unique touch to their meals. Enjoy them with freshly baked naan, aromatic rice, or fragrant biryani. For the perfect ending, indulge in our delectable desserts and refreshing beverages.

Whether you're dining casually or celebrating a special occasion in a non-vegetarian or vegetarian Indian Dine-In Restaurant, Benares promises an unforgettable experience that brings the best of Indian flavors to your table.
