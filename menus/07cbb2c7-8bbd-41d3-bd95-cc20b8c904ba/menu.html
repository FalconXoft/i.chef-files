Welcome to Fresco’s

Fresco’s Pizzeria is proud to bring high-quality ingredients and lovingly made food to this neighborhood. We thank you for your continued patronage and hope to bring our best.

On February 9, 2022, National Pizza Day, we were officially recognized by Grubhub as the pizzeria with the top number of orders in all of New York City. NYC itself is the pizza capital in the country, and probably the world, so this puts us on the map in a global way...

...all thanks to you, Washington Heights!

We appreciate you keeping us in business.
