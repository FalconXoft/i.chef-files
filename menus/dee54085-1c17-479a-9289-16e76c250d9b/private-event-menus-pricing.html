Palo Santo has private event spaces available to rent for up to 60 people and is the perfect location for your next party! Our restaurant is located in a brownstone on a tree lined block in Park Slope, Brooklyn and our charming space can provide you with an ideal setting for your gathering. We provide excellent food and service to create a memorable occasion.

All are welcomed!

RESTAURANT BUY-OUTS


If you are planning a large party to celebrate a special occasion please consider renting the entire restaurant; our maximum seating capacity is 60 people. Please inquire regarding pricing and availability.


 Pricing & Planning

A room rental fee is due at the time of booking, in order to reserve a specific section or the entire restaurant for the exclusive use of your event. The per person food & beverage pricing, along with NY state sales tax & gratuity, is due on the day of your event.

To ensure availability of all menu selections, any alterations must be made 14 days prior to your event. A final head count is required two days before your event.

You are welcome to decorate the space! If you plan to decorate, please schedule additional time to do so and remove all decorations by the end of your scheduled event time.
