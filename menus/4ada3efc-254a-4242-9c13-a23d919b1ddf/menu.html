Welcome to Yanni’s Pizza & Restaurant! Located in the heart of Newington, Connecticut, we’re a family-owned restaurant that’s been serving Greek, Italian, and American favorites for over 20 years.

Our menu is a delicious blend of cultures, offering everything from zesty Greek salads and hearty Italian pastas to classic American burgers.

Looking for something new? Check out our daily specials for a tasty surprise, or let us cater your next event with a menu that’s sure to please everyone.

Yanni’s isn’t just a place to eat; it’s a place to gather with family and friends. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or just craving a delicious meal, we’re here to make it memorable.

Come join the Yanni’s family today – we can’t wait to serve you!
