About Us

Savory Thai is a cornerstone restaurant in Williamsburg, which has been recognized for its outstanding cuisine, excellent service and friendly staff. Our Thai restaurant is known for its modern interpretation of classic dishes and its insistence on only using high quality fresh ingredients.

Savory Thai offers delicious dining, take-out and delivery to the Williamsburg neighborhood.
