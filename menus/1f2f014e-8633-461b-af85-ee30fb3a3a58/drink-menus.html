aromas of blackberry, plum and a hint of mocha. Dark fruit flavors intertwine with notes of spice, and chocolate to create a full-bodied Malbec. A soft mouthfeel lead the way to a long, velvety finish.
Louis Martini - Cabernet Sauvignon, Napa Valley, 2018
Rated 93 by James Suckling
A full bodied red with walnut, berry, chocolate and spice on the nose. A chewy tannins and a rich finish. A little firmer and tighter, which equates to a more serious red.
Orin Swift 8 years in the Dessert - Zinfandel/Grenache Blend, Napa California
RP93 Robert Parker's Wine Advocate
Medium garnet-purple colored, the Zinfandel Blend 8 Years in the Desert delivers notions of stewed plums, baked raspberries and fruitcake with hints of powdered cinnamon, clove oil, tree bark, hoisin and dusty soil. Medium to full-bodied, the palate is chock-full of ripe, spicy black and red fruits, supported by chewy tannins and tons of freshness, finishing long and earthy.
Frank Family Napa - Cabernet, California
Rated 91 by Vinous
The 2018 Cabernet Sauvignon (Napa Valley) is fresh, vibrant and fruity, all of which make it a terrific choice for drinking now and over the next handful of years. Ripe red berry fruit, wild flowers, mint and sweet spice are front and center. This pliant, inviting Cabernet has so much to offer.
