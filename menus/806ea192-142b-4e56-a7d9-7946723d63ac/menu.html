five star review by Miles S:

Ordered a lamb gyro. Food came out within 5 minutes. The food was delicious, tender, and well flavored. The service was quick and the restaurant was clean with a warm atmosphere. The food is reasonably priced especially for Jersey City...

Review by - Google

five star review by Midas M:

Best quick, easy, and very healthy spot in JC. Fantastic flavors, food is ALWAYS fresh, and everything is high quality. You can tell they use good olive oil too. The salad and the bowl are awesome deals. Spanakopita and Tzatziki is delicious...

Review by - Google

five star review by Teresa C:

I ordered Moussaka and Tzatziki without any big expectations on a random Monday night. Let me tell you... the best Moussaka I ever had, just like the one I had during my trip in Greece!!! The salad that comes with it was amazing too, perfect dressing and delicious olives. I will order again!!!

Review by - Google

five star review by Maureen B:

Been here a few times now, dine in and takeout. Portions are huge, food is super fresh and well seasoned. I'm obsessed with their tzatziki sauce, and they definitely have the most delicious feta. Excellent spot on Newark, hope they're here for a long time!

Review by - Google

five star review by Erin L:

So glad I discovered this place! Ela has been our go-to restaurant and this review is long overdue. Food is fresh, large portions and tasty - takes us to Greece without the passport! Our favorite dishes are chicken souvlaki, horiatiki, chopped salad, baklava and always with extra pita & tzatziki.
