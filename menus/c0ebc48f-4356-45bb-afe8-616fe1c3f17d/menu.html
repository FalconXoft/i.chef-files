    • The very famous restaurant in Brooklyn

    Gage & Tollner was open 1879 – 2004,
    and was scheduled to reopen in 2020,
    before the pandemic closed New York City restaurants. Then on April 15, 2021, Gage & Tollner finally returned to Downtown Brooklyn, in all its glory.


    To stay in-the-know about the latest offerings and happenings at Gage & Tollner, please sign up for our mailing list below.

    2 Exciting Experiences, 1 Historic Space

    Gage & Tollner

    An oyster and chop house for the 21st century that upholds the timeless tradition of serving top-quality ingredients, simply yet perfectly prepared.
