Restaurant Policy

LUNCH OPTIONS: Monday - Friday 11:30 am - 2:45 pm, excluding holidays

There is a 3% credit card service fee.

Notify servers of any food allergies or dietary restrictions

100 MIN SEATING LIMIT - 30% upcharge per additional 30 minutes

CHILDREN: under 4ft: Half price | under 3 1/2ft: Free

Automatic 20% gratuity will be added to parties of 5 or more

Any food leftover is NOT available to take home and will result in an additional charge

Our servers come around and help you cook but you're more than welcome to help yourself. If you'd like to cook on your own, please notify your server.

Maximum 3 forms of payment including cash.

We take reservations all throughout the week. We have a 10 minute hold policy on reservations and any changes must be notified beforehand.
