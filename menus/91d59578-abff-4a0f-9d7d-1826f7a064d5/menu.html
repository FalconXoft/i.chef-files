Welcome to Guantanamera

Guantanamera, is known as one of the best Cuban restaurants in NYC. Guantanamera's vibrant Cuban restaurant offerings include traditional Cuban dishes, fabulous mojitos, and live Cuban music. Our Havana-inspired Cuban restaurant atmosphere features exposed brick, candle-lit tables, and Cuban folkloric murals. Our guests enjoy the fresh Cuban specialties and superb service. Our bar serves fine wines, rums, cocktails, and appetizers, making it a lively spot where Cuban food, drinks, and music come together. Experience Guantanamera, experience the best Cuban restaurant in New York City.
