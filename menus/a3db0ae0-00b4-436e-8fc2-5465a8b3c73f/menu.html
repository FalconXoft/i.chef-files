Nestled in the heart of Co-op City at 149 Dreiser Loop, Bronx, NY 10475, Capri II Pizzeria stands as a beloved cornerstone of the community—a testament to authentic Italian-American cuisine and unwavering dedication to excellence. For years, this family-owned establishment has been crafting what many consider to be among the finest pizza in the entire Bronx, earning a reputation that extends far beyond the neighborhood's boundaries. The moment customers step through the doors, they're transported to an era when quality mattered more than speed, when recipes were passed down through generations rather than mass-produced in corporate kitchens. The old school ambiance immediately embraces visitors with its warm, nostalgic atmosphere—checkered tablecloths, vintage photographs adorning the walls, and the intoxicating aroma of fresh dough, melted cheese, and carefully selected herbs wafting from the kitchen. What truly sets Capri II apart isn't just their phenomenal pizza, though those generous slices have certainly earned their legendary status among locals. Each pie represents a perfect marriage of crispy-yet-tender crust, rich tomato sauce made from the finest ingredients, and cheese that stretches magnificently with every bite. The portions are notably substantial—each slice practically a meal in itself, satisfying even the heartiest appetites while maintaining the delicate balance of flavors that keeps customers returning year after year.

Capri II Pizzeria offers an extensive menu centered around their legendary pizza—widely regarded as among the Bronx's finest. Their kitchen crafts exceptional hot heroes and sandwiches, with chicken parmesan standing as a customer favorite. The establishment also serves expertly prepared Philly cheesesteaks, fresh salads in generous portions, and unique offerings like pepperoni rolls that distinguish them from typical neighborhood pizzerias.
