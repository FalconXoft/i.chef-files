Welcome to N'ata Storia: A Slice of Naples in the Heart of Brooklyn

Step into N’ata Storia and be transported to a magical atmosphere, where the authentic flavors of Neapolitan cuisine meet the warmth and hospitality of our homeland.
An Unforgettable Culinary Experience
Here, every dish tells a story, a journey through the traditions and aromas of Naples. Our pizzas, with their high, soft crusts, our pasta dishes, made with homemade pasta and rich sauces, and our entrees, featuring fresh, genuine ingredients, are prepared with passion and skill by our chefs to give you an unforgettable culinary experience.
A Unique Atmosphere
The welcoming and familiar environment, the intoxicating aroma of freshly baked pizza, and the sound of chatter and laughter create a unique atmosphere where you’ll feel right at home.
N’ata Storia: More Than a Restaurant, an Experience
N’ata Storia is the perfect place for a romantic dinner, a family night out, or a gathering with friends. Come and visit us and let yourself be captivated by the flavors and atmosphere of Naples.
We look forward to welcoming you in Brooklyn!
