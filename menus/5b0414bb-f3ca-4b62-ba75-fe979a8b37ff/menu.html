About

Las Margaritas

Welcome to Las Margaritas, a vibrant gem nestled in the heart of Jackson Heights, New York. Located at 83-22 37th Ave, this inviting sports bar and restaurant promises an unforgettable culinary journey infused with a lively atmosphere. Whether you’re a local or just passing through, you’re in for a treat.

Las Margaritas is not just a restaurant; it’s a destination for those seeking a delightful mix of taste and entertainment. With commendations from satisfied diners, it’s clear that the establishment takes pride in its offerings. From their mouth-watering fish and BBQ chicken salad to the well-crafted margaritas, every dish reflects a dedication to flavor. One happy patron noted that the food is not only tasty but served in generous portions—a perfect combination for both families and sports enthusiasts.

The ambiance at Las Margaritas is as impressive as the menu. Decorated thoughtfully, the space transforms throughout the year, even getting into the spirit of holidays like Valentine’s Day, creating a welcoming environment for all. Customers have praised the high-energy vibe, especially during the evening when laughter and excitement fill the air.

Drinks, particularly the margaritas, have received standout reviews. One visitor remarked that they serve "the best margarita," while others have enjoyed unique options like the espresso martini, crafted by the friendly bartender, Isabel. For those looking to unwind with friends or celebrate special occasions, Las Margaritas delivers exceptional cocktails that elevate the dining experience.

Service at Las Margaritas has also caught the attention of satisfied patrons. Although the restaurant occasionally sees longer wait times due to its popularity, the attentive staff ensures that every guest feels valued. Reviews highlight the professionalism of the servers who are both knowledgeable about the menu and eager to enhance your experience with recommendations.

If you find yourself in Jackson Heights or simply craving a fun and flavorful meal, Las Margaritas is undoubtedly worth a visit. Offering a blend of exceptional dishes, delightful drinks, and a welcoming atmosphere, this local treasure captures the spirit of community dining. So gather your friends, savor the dishes, and enjoy the company—Las Margaritas is ready to impress.

“ Walked in here as I've heard good things about this place and had a good experience for the most part! Waddling in at almost 40 weeks preggo, we were seated pretty much immediately around noon on a Saturday. Our server came around to take our drinks order while we waited for the rest of our party to arrive. We scouted the menu to see if it was to our liking and they offered a variety of Colombian, Mexican, and some American dishes so we decided to stay. Ordered a Colombian coffee, Shrimp salad, tostones con guacamole, pargo frito, calamari, bandeja típica. Everything came out decently quick! Pargo Frito was omggg.. so crispy yet not oily and same with calamari. I didn't get heartburn like I usually do with oily foods so I took it as a really good sign. Toastones pairs so so well with the guac... salivating just thinking about that again. You also can't go wrong with the Bandeja Típica. Classic Colombian dish with a mix of steak, chicharrón, arepita, guac, chorizo, served with rice. Los todos fue muyyyy chimba. My only complaint is when I got up to look for the bathroom, I literally stood in front of two waitress and was totally ignored while they took their sweet time chatting to each other about whatever... which is fine like I don't care but it'd be nice if they acknowledged a first time patron needed help to find the bathroom. This is not reflective of our actual server - she was amazing and so attentive and constantly came to clear our tables (maybe a teeny bit rushed as we were still eating and the restaurant wasn't super full but nbd).
