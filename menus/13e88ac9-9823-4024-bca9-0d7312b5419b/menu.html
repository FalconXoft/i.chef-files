Welcome to Muteki Udon, where Japanese culinary art meets a passion for exciting flavors.

With over 20 years of collective experience in the restaurant industry, our team has honed our skills and expertise across various culinary styles, including Ramen Noodle, Hawaiian Poke Bowl, Sushi, Udon, and Soba. Now, experience our newest concept focusing on rich, comforting homemade Udon noodles, savory Donburi, and delicious homemade appetizers.

Located at 234 Flatbush Avenue, Brooklyn, NY 11217, our menu is developed by Master Shuichi Kotani, Jian Wei He, and Zhi Yang, ensuring the highest quality and authenticity. Join us for an unforgettable dining experience, complemented by a selection of Japanese sakes and beers. Our Homemade Udon Noodles are crafted from the finest ingredients and tasty at any temperature. Elevate your dining experience with our handcrafted unique bowls and plates. Discover the essence of Japanese culinary artistry at Muteki Udon.
