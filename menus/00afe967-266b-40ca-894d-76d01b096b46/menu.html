LOCATED IN THE HEART OF FORT GREENE, DINO IS A NORTHERN ITALIAN RESTAURANT SERVING SIMPLE, SEASONAL AND FRESH COMFORT FOOD.

The menu focuses on traditional dishes and includes homemade pastas and desserts accompanied by daily specials, innovative cocktails and a hand-picked affordable wine selection. Outdoor dining is available on the front patio or in the large, airy back garden and a private dining room is available for large parties and special events.

Close to BAM, the Pratt Institute and Fort Greene Park, Dino is the perfect choice for lunch, brunch or dinner.
