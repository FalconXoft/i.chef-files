One of Manhattan’s most impressive banquet facilities, and the ideal space for hosting exceptional private events in any season.

Event Venue

The Hudson’s extensive boutique facilities include indoor and outdoor spaces for parties of 503,000, a full-service on-site kitchen, and knowledgeable planners experienced in executing events of any size. No other venue in the tri-state area can offer such a dramatic setting just short drive (or boat ride) form midtown Manhattan.

Highlights

Full service restaurant, lounge and events space, nestled on the bank of the Hudson river, just below the Cloisters.

  • Easy access for charter boats and busses, valet parking service and convenient public transit.
  • The Hudson’s Dining Room has fully retractable floor to ceiling windows on all four sides.
  • State-of-the-art multi zone sound system compatible with DJs and live bands.
