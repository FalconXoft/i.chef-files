A little something about us

Setting up shop in Murray Hill, Nom Nam is a preservation of the Lien family culinary legacy. Its a refugee story elevated through home-style Vietnamese recipes served up by a vibrant staff. Simply put, Nom Nam pays homage to the traditional while also creating some new signatures.
