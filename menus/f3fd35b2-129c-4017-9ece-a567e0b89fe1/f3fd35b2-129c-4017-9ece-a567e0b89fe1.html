Experience Spanish Flavors in the Heart of New York City

Discover Addictive NYC, a hidden gem nestled in a historic narrow building in the bustling Midtown Manhattan. Since its establishment in 2015, Addictive NYC has been dedicated to offering an exceptional dining experience centered on warm hospitality and delicious cuisine. The menu showcases a diverse selection of tapas and flavorful paella, crafted using the finest seasonal ingredients. Complementing the culinary delights is a thoughtfully curated wine list featuring over 100 exquisite wines from across the globe, with a special emphasis on Spanish varieties. Whether you seek a laid-back meal or an elegant dining affair, Addictive NYC provides the ideal setting to savor the essence of Spain amidst of Midtown New York City.

Come try our exceptional quality food at Addictive!

We are proud to offer a wide range of fresh meats and seafood, all prepared daily to ensure the highest standards of quality. Indulge in our imported specialties from Spain, including guindilla, quescrem, chorizo, and octopus.

As a Certified Sommelier, I have been fortunate to taste thousands of exquisite wines from diverse regions of Spain and Europe while curating an extensive and diverse wine list. My team and I would be thrilled to assist you in selecting the perfect wine to complement your meal.
