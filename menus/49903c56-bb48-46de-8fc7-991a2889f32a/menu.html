More than 10 years after opening in Tel Aviv and becoming a cornerstone of the local food and music scene, Port Sa’id has finally arrived in NYC.

Located on 350 Hudson, Port Sa’id is a place where the flavors of the Mediterranean live in harmony with rich sounds from around the world. Equipped with a larger than life sound system and a hand curated vinyl library, Port Sa’id aims to provide a unique listening experience alongside the creative and produce-driven food of Eyal Shani. Here, guests interact directly with kitchen and staff to create a tandem sensory performance like no other. 

Walk-ins always welcome.
