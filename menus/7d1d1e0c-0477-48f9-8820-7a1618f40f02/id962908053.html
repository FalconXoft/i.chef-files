Menufy by HungerRush. Find and order delivery or carryout from Restaurants around you! We offer the easiest online ordering experience, with no account creation needed and a wide array of secure online payment method options. We accept: Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal, Venmo, Cryptocurrency, and all major Credit Cards. For more information visit Menufy.com.

  • Needs improvement

    03/03/2023

    AlexJAuer

    To start, when you open a menu, it actually opens a web view of the menu. So iOS suggests you use the Menufy app with a banner at the top. Which makes no sense because you’re in the app! This feels very amateur-ish. Next, if you want to filter to saved favorite restaurants, you have to toggle that on every time you want that list. My favorites are often buried under several others. It would be nice if users didn’t have to open a separate app menu and select a toggle, just to see the restaurants you want to filter to. The UI/UX could use some attention. It isn’t the prettiest aesthetic and it feels a little clunky at times. Other similar apps like Toast are much more enjoyable to use. Ultimately, it’s fine at best. If they charge restaurants less, I’m all for it. Just not the greatest app ever.

    Developer Response

    Thank you for taking the time to provide your feedback. We are currently working on an updated app that addresses your suggestions. And for context, we do not charge our restaurants for our online ordering or web-building services, including our app. In addition, we do not take a cut of menu sales, unlike other companies. We hope you continue supporting restaurants through their preferred online ordering site.

For further assistance regarding your order, contact our 24/7 support team by phone at (913) 738-9399 or by email at support@menufy.com. We apologize for any frustration and want to help if possible.
