The Chatwal, New York

130 W 44th St, New York, NY 10036, USA | +1 (212) 764-6200 | Map | Contact

In the heart of Manhattan, most conveniently located on 44th Street, The Chatwal, New York is steps from the best of entertainment, dining, shopping, Broadway theaters and the city’s media and financial giants.

The Chatwal, New York’s 76 keys includes 28 stunning Suites and 14 ‘themed’ suites with spacious designer terraces as well as bespoke services including a team of professional Butlers, in-room dining and distinct private bars.
