Additional Information

Experience behind-the-scenes movie magic of Oscar winning Forrest Gump at Bubba Gump Shrimp Co. located on Broadway in Times Square. Escape the bright lights of Times Square and experience down-home, Southern comfort food just like mama used to make it. From fried shrimp, juicy burgers, and frozen cocktails Bubba Gump Shrimp Co. has something to satisfy the whole family.

Bubba Gump Shrimp Co. captures the charm and American spirit that made “Forrest Gump” a smash hit, featuring down-home décor reminiscent of the movie’s setting in Alabama. Now branded as an American icon, the restaurant showcases memorabilia and still-photos from the movie, along with reproductions of script pages, storyboards and even some costumes.

At our core, we are a seafood restaurant committed to providing a casual environment where everyone can enjoy a great meal and have some fun too.

Food is the hero at the Bubba Gump Shrimp Co., we serve food that makes our employees proud and will make you want to return. Our menu features shrimp, of course. And once you've had one of our margaritas hand-shaken at your table, you're sure to be a fan.

At Bubba Gump Shrimp Co. we’re always ready for great food, great fun, and a great party!
