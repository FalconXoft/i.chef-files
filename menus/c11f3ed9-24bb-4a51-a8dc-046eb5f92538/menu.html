ABOUT

Papatzul´s ambiance is warm and gently urges us to journey into all that Mexico City has to offer. The fragrant aromas of traditional Mexican spices fill the air, beautiful Posters of the Golden age of Mexican Cinema and traditional Mexican Masks adorn the space.

 

Also featured along the dining room walls, guests will notice the presence of three commanding candle lit iron trees of life, which were custom ordered specially for Papatzul by an artist in Mexico City. At Papatzul, you truly become submerged into a world that allows you to embrace the beauty of Mexico City.
