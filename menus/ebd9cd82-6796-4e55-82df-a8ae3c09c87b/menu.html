Bold Flavors. Fast Casual Vibes.

At Smashburger, our menu is all about bold flavor and better ingredients—crafted fresh and served fast. As a fast casual restaurant, we combine the quality and care of sit-down dining with the speed and convenience you crave. From smashed-to-order burgers made with 100% Certified Angus Beef® to crispy chicken sandwiches, savory veggie burgers, and our signature rosemary SmashFries®, every item is designed to deliver serious comfort food satisfaction.

Looking for something quick? We’ve got plenty of quick bites for life on the go—like snackable sides, kid-friendly meals, and handspun Häagen-Dazs® shakes that hit the sweet spot. Whether you’re dining in or grabbing takeout, the Smashburger menu makes it easy to enjoy big flavor without the wait.
