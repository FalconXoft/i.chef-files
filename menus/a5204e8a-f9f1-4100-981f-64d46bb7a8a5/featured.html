FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What meals do you serve?
At Teriyaki R Us, we offer a diverse menu featuring Japanese and Asian Fusion cuisine, including signature teriyaki dishes, fresh sushi rolls, hearty ramen, and flavorful bento boxes. Our menu highlights favorites like Chicken Teriyaki, Beef Teriyaki, Salmon Teriyaki, and Tofu Teriyaki, as well as a variety of dumplings, spring rolls, and tempura. We also serve delicious salads, fried rice, noodles, and vegetarian options to cater to every palate. Sushi lovers can enjoy classic rolls such as California Roll, Spicy Tuna Roll, and Salmon Avocado Roll. Whether you crave a quick lunch or a satisfying dinner, Teriyaki R Us has something for everyone.
Do you offer delivery or takeout?
Yes, we offer both delivery and takeout options through trusted platforms like DoorDash for your convenience.
