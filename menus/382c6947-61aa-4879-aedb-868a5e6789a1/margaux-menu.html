The Margaux Wines Collection

Discover a thoughtfully curated French wine list and menu that celebrates both heritage and craft. From crisp Sauvignon Blancs and elegant Chardonnays to deep Bordeaux blends and prestigious Grands Crus, each bottle is chosen to perfectly complement your fine French dining experience and authentic French menu at Margaux by La Sirène, the premier French restaurant in Midtown NYC. Whether you’re savoring a glass of Picpoul on a sunny afternoon or indulging in a rare vintage Margaux alongside signature dishes like Escargots, Cassoulet, or Hanger Steak Rossini, our Midtown French wine selection and menu brings together timeless classics and modern favorites for every palate.
