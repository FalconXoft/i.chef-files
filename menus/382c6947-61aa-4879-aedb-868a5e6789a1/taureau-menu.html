La SIrene SOHO: Taureau's

Experience Taureau's Decadent Fondue!

 

Welcome to Taureau by La Sirène SoHo, where French dining meets the warmth of traditional fondue. Located in the heart of New York City, we offer an authentic French experience with a modern twist. Our menu features a selection of fondue varieties, from classic cheese and savory broths to decadent chocolate, all prepared with the finest ingredients.

At Taureau by La Sirène SoHo, we celebrate the joy of sharing. Our cozy ambiance is perfect for intimate dinners or lively gatherings, making every meal a culinary journey to the heart of France.
