Italian Restaurant serving Milford & Fairfield, CT
We invite you to take a look through our diverse menu selection of delicious Spanish & Italian cuisine

Our Italian restaurant's menu has been expertly crafted by our chef to suit a range of culinary desires. We use only the freshest local produce, meat and poultry in and around Milford to bring you traditional tastes of Spain and Italy. From fresh ceviche to eggplant parmigiana, tasty pizzas to tiramisu, our menu selection at Bin 100 Restaurant is an invitation to not only savor great Mediterranean food, but also to enjoy choosing from an extensive list of fine wines. We don’t want you to have just a good night; we want you to have a memorable night. This is why we have fine-tuned our creativity, hospitality and expertise in rich cuisine to bring you the best menu selection in Milford. Stop by our inviting Italian restaurant today for an evening in the Mediterranean.
