Dinner Menu

Enjoy a Delicious Dinner at Our Italian Restaurant in Milford, CT

At Bin 100 Restaurant, we pride ourselves on providing Milford, Stratford, Fairfield, Bridgeport, CT, and the surrounding areas with a delicious and authentic Italian cuisine. Our dinner menu has a little something everyone will enjoy, such as flatbreads, pasta salads, seafood, steak, cannoli’s, and more. Plus, we make sure the ambiance is just right for your perfect private dining experience. Browse our menu items below and call our Italian restaurant today to book your reservation!


Our staff looks forward to providing you with a memorable indoor or outdoor dining experience! If you are planning a get together or event, we also offer a host of catering menu options and party packages to best meet your specific needs. We offer the perfect party venue in Milford, CT for your next get-together with friends, family, or co-workers!
