Browse our menu below to see what we’re serving or contact us today!


Thai food dishes are known for including flavors of salty, sweet, sour, and spicy. A meal will typically consist of meat or fish, vegetables, noodles, and soup. Ingredients are combined together in the signature "steel wok," prepared with unique seasonings and served with a side of rice. Grab some chopsticks and enjoy the incredible taste of Thai food.

Check Out Our Thai Food Menu!

Choose from a selection of all your favorite, classic Thai food entrees, prepared and served to perfection. We've got Pad Thai, Khao Pad, Gaeng Daeng, Tom Yum Goong, Som Tum, and plenty of options in between. We can customize the spice and hotness level of any dish to your taste as well!
