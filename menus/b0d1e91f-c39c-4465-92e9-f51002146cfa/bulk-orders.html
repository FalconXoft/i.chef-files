Bulk order inquiries

＜Notice of New Year's holiday closure＞

We apologize for any inconvenience, but regarding shipping and inquiries during the New Year holidays, we will be closed during the following periods:

Closure period: Saturday, December 28, 2024 - Sunday, January 5, 2025

For large corporate orders, if we receive confirmation of payment by the morning of Monday, December 23rd, we will ship the order by the day before the holiday period .

* Depending on the order status and quantity, your order may be shipped after Monday, January 6, 2025. If you are in a hurry, please place your order as soon as possible.

*Please note that it may take some time for us to issue an invoice and confirm your payment.

GoGo Curry can accommodate large orders from corporations.
If you are considering using our products as gifts, prizes for events or giveaways, or if you would like to have your products delivered to multiple addresses, please contact us using the inquiry form below.

About bulk orders

We accept bulk orders of 100 boxes or more.

The product covered is "GoGo Curry Medium Spicy."
If you would like to purchase other products, please contact us using the inquiry form.

Payment can be made by bank transfer (deferred payment).

We will issue an invoice, so please make the payment by the due date.

We can also create your own original packaging.

GoGo Curry has worked on a number of collaborative products in the past.
We accept orders for 500 boxes or more. Please contact us first!
