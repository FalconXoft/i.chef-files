Your neighborhood spot for fresh, bold food made from the highest quality ingredients

Filled with Flavor / Crafted with Love

Located in the Lafayette neighborhood of Jersey City, Mordi’s is the place to go when you want great food in a Casual AtmosPhere.

Our menu is filled with a large variety of Israeli-inspired dishes: hand-pounded, breaded, crispy schnitzel, mouth-watering specialty sandwiches and burgers, fresh, flavorful pitas and salads, house-made sauces, and generous portions. We craft made-to-order comfort food from fresh, quality ingredients in a community we love and love to support. 

Let us make your taste buds happy! Stop by our sandwich shop, located at the corner of Communipaw Avenue and Pine Street in Jersey City, and dine with us today!
