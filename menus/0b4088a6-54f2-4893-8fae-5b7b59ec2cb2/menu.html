A Taste of Home

Since 2017, Mama Kitchen has been bringing the authentic flavors of Tripolitanian cuisine to New York. Inspired by grandmother’s traditional recipes and combined with Chef Shira’s culinary magic, we serve genuine home-cooked food, prepared with love and patience.

About Us

Welcome to Mama Kitchen

 where every meal is a heartfelt celebration of homemade goodness. At Mama Kitchen, we believe that food is more than just nourishment—it’s about family, tradition, and the joy of coming together. Our restaurant is a cozy haven where friends, families, and neighbors gather to enjoy the warmth of home-cooked meals made with love.

Each dish we serve is inspired by the timeless recipes of home kitchens

Using fresh ingredients and a generous sprinkle of passion. We take pride in preparing everything from scratch, just like Mama used to, to bring you flavors that are comforting, familiar, and unforgettable.
