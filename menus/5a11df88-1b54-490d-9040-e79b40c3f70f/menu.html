Our Story

Since its inception, Shoo Loong Kan Hotpot has always persisted in its business principle of “flavors from the soul, quality with integrity” and dedicated itself to providing customers with hot pot soup bases with genuine flavors, fresh and healthy ingredients and a diverse and innovative array of products. and adopts an even more fashionable, trendy and diversified brand image to create a more varied and novel dining experience for the modern lifestyle.

  • Where We From

    Shoo Loong Kan, which translates literally as Little Dragon Ridge, is named after a place in Shapingba district in Chongqing which has a mountainous region that resembles a dragon. The traditional Chongqing hotpot originated from this area approximately 100 years ago. To commemorate its place of origin, it was later named Chongqing Xiao Long Kan Hotpot.

    Established in 2014, Shoo Loong Kan Hotpot originated from Sichuan. It started from merely a handful of hotpot tables and in just 5 years, we expanded rapidly through our business franchise model. In 2017, we successfully expanded overseas and managed to open 1,000 outlets worldwide by 2020.
