A French Gem Hidden In Tribeca.

Housed in a 200-year-old former Seafarers Inn near the Hudson, Yves blends minimalist Tribeca cool with the timeless charm of Parisian cafés. A sister restaurant to Smith & Mills, Yves offers a relaxed, neighborhood feel within its sleek, art-filled space.


The menu features modern French dishes—refined yet approachable, comforting but fresh.
Join us for drinks or dinner—we’ll save you a seat.
