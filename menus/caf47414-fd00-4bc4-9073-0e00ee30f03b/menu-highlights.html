Signature Lanzhou Beef Noodle

传统兰州牛肉面

The authentic recipe straight from Lanzhou, China. A selection of hand-pulled noodles swim in a clear, savory beef broth flavored with over 20 spices, simmered for hours.

Topped with thin-sliced beef, radish, cilantro, garlic sprout, and a splash of chili oil, it’s light yet deeply satisfying. Each slurp brings comfort, history, and the perfect chewy bite.

Spicy Oil Spill Noodle

油泼辣子面

A bold staple of Northwestern Chinese cuisine. Thick, belt-like  hand-pulled noodles are boiled, then piled high in a bowl and topped with garlic, scallions and chili flakes.

The magic happens when scorching-hot oil is poured over the toppings, releasing an irresistible aroma of toasted chili and garlic. The dish can come with a variety of additional toppings to satisfy your cravings.

Monstrous Plate Chicken

大盘鸡

A feast-worthy dish with bold flavor and bold portions. Tender chunks of bone-in chicken are stewed with potatoes, bell peppers, and chili in a fragrant, spiced sauce.

Served with wide, chewy noodles that soak up the rich, savory gravy, it’s perfect for sharing—or not.

Braised Lamb Shoulder

黄焖羊肉

A rich and warming stew made for colder days and big appetites. Bone-in lamb is slow-braised with ginger, garlic, and earthy spices until fall-apart tender.

The golden broth is deep and comforting, clinging to every piece of meat and ladled generously over wide noodles.

Mutton on the Bone

手抓羊肉

Rustic, hearty, and unapologetically meaty. This Northwestern lamb dish is simmered with aromatic spices, then served bone-in for maximum flavor.

Meant to be eaten with your hands, each piece is juicy, tender, and packed with deep, lamb-forward richness. A true carnivore’s delight.
