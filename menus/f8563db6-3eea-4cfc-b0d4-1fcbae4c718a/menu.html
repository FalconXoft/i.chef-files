    Additional Information

    Positioned in the heart of Midtown Manhattan, Mastro’s Steakhouse New York is moments away from Rockefeller Center and Times Square. This impeccable location offers an upscale culinary experience near Broadway theaters, luxury shopping destinations, and cultural attractions.

    Dress Code

    To maintain our elegant atmosphere, we kindly request: No athletic wear, lounge wear, overly casual clothing, or exposed undergarments. No excessively revealing, sheer, or clothing with offensive language, imagery, or odors. No beanies, baseball caps, visors, or bandanas. Collared shirts and/or sport coats for gentlemen are always appreciated.  

    Management reserves the right to determine compliance. Thank you for helping us create a refined dining experience for all.
