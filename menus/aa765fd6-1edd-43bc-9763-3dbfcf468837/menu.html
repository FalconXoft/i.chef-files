About us

Arcadia Bar and Kitchen

Welcome to Arcadia Bar & Kitchen, your neighborhood gathering place in the heart of Astoria, Queens. Founded by a born and raised Astorian, Arcadia was built on a love for good good, great drinks and the vibrant sense of community that makes Astoria so special. From the start, our mission has been simple - to create a welcoming space where friends, families, and neighbors can relax, connect and celebrate life's everyday moments. Whether you are joining us for dinner, happy hour, brunch or a special celebration, Arcadia is all about friendly service, warm hospitality, and genuine neighborhood charm. At Arcadia we are more than just a bar and kitchen - we're part of the Astoria community, and we're proud to welcome everyone who walks through our doors as part of the family. Eat well, Drink happily. Feel at home. That's Arcadia.

Reviews

Review by - Yelp

Sha S:

Such a pretty restaurant, with such nice Christmas decor. The food was so good. The shrimp tacos were amazing (minus the fried onions but that's because I don't like onions). They have an assortment of flatbread's, the feta/olives one is really good. Lastly, their mac & cheese is to die for. They have happy hour from 5pm to 7pm so get your drinks in during this time.

Review by - Yelp

John K:

I have been driving by this place for years, especially at night , and it caught my eye due to its great curb appeal, from the lights and bar at night. Well finally went in with a friend . We sat at the yard, at the rear of the restaurant , and truly enjoyed it. Our choices were a burger, Cesar salad, shrimp tacos and fries. We were very happy with all including the dirty martinis...

Krysten L:

I love this restaurant it's beautiful. The patio is perfect for days with great weather. All the food was very delicious. No complaints whatsoever. Since it was Mother's Day they have my mom a rose which was the loveliest touch. I will definitely be bringing more people here.
