Torrisi

About

Torrisi serves an à la carte menu that reflects a singular approach to Italian cuisine. It combines the highest levels of execution and the most spectacular ingredients with inspired creativity and a deep respect for New York City’s culinary history. In addition to our classics, a range of nightly specials will be listed in each dining room during your visit.

Andy Wang - Forbes

Our Iconic Address

Torrisi is housed within the iconic Puck Building on Mulberry Street, right in the heart of Nolita. With vaulted ceilings, incredible artwork, and interior design by Garrett Singer Architecture, it is a grand New York stage for what has become one of the most essential dining destinations in the United States.

For the comfort and enjoyment of all our guests, we maintain an adults-only dining experience. We regret that children under three are not accommodated in our dining rooms. We are unable to accommodate strollers, high chairs, or a children’s menu.

We welcome guests to bring bottles of their own wine for a corkage fee of $95 per 750ml bottle ($190 for a magnum). If you are interested in bringing more than 3 bottles, please contact reservations@torrisinyc.com to coordinate with our team.

Dress Code

Torrisi is a fine dining restaurant, please dress for the occasion. Athletic wear is prohibited.

Reservations

Reservations for the dining room at Torrisi are available 30 days in advance for parties of up to 6 guests exclusively on Resy. Torrisi takes walk-ins on a first come, first served basis at our Bar Counter. We recommend booking a reservation for the dining room.

Please note that reservations for Torrisi can be made directly through our website, authorized reservation platforms such as Resy or Dorsia, or by contacting our restaurant teams via email at reservations@torrisinyc.com. We cannot guarantee the validity of reservations made through any other channels and hereby disclaim all liability in connection with your use of unauthorized channels to book a reservation. If you are not sure whether a reservation is valid or have any questions related to making a reservation, please contact us at reservations@torrisinyc.com.
