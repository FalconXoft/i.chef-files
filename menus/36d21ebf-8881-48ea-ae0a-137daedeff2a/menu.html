Welcome to Glin Thai Bistro

We are located at the heart of Fort Green, Brooklyn. We proudly represent a combination of cultures between North and South Thailand. Chef Sunny, the chef-owner, said 'Make your day more special with our food'. We are focusing on making real Thai authentic, bold flavors of traditional and non-traditional dishes using the freshest ingredients. Let the aroma of delicious Thai food surround you.
