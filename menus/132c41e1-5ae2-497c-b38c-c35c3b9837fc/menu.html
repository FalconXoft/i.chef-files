About Us

Welcome to Spaghetti Incident, where the charm of Italy meets the vibrant energy of the Lower East Side. Located on Eldridge Street, Spaghetti Incident is more than just a restaurantits a journey to the rustic streets of Italy, right in the bustling streets of New York City. Our intimate setting transports you to a cozy trattoria, with warm lighting, exposed brick walls, and a welcoming atmosphere that invites you to relax and indulge in authentic Italian cuisine.

Named after the iconic Guns N Roses 1993 album, Spaghetti Incident embodies the spirit of rock n roll alongside the traditions of Italian culinary excellence. Our menu boasts amazing pasta dishes crafted with care and passion. From classic favorites to innovative creations, every dish is a testament to our commitment to Italian home cooking, always fresh and bursting with flavor.

Owner Giovanni Gentile’s vision for Spaghetti Incident extends beyond just serving delicious pasta; it’s about creating a space where diners feel at home. As a beloved local spot, we pride ourselves on being a gathering place for friends, family, and neighbors alike. Whether you’re a regular or a first-time visitor, you’ll always feel like part of the family.
