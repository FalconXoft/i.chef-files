FAQ

  • What type of pizza do you serve?

    Traditional New York style slices and whole pies, including square pies and slices.

  • Do you have vegan options?

    Yes, we do have vegan pies and slices available, both round and square.

  • Do you offer takeout and delivery?

    You can now place takeout and delivery orders exclusively via DoorDash or Caviar. There are some weekend restrictions.

    For any issue with your DoorDash or Caviar order, please call DoorDash customer service at 855-973-1040.

  • Do you have gluten free options?

    We do not offer gluten-free pizza.

  • What is your your phone number?

    Our phone number is 929-337-6385 for general inquiries.

    PLEASE NOTE: We do NOT accept orders over the phone.

    For all other communication our email address is: sliceinfo@pauliegee.com

    For any issue with your DoorDash or Caviar order, please call DoorDash customer service at 855-973-1040.

  • Can I purchase Paulie Gees Slice Shop gift cards?

    Yes, you can purchase Paulie Gee’s Slice Shop eGift cards in person at our slice shop.

    Sorry, eGift Cards can not be used with Doordash or Caviar.

  • Do you serve alcohol?

    We serve wine, beer, and cider.

  • Do you make the pizzeria available for private events or buyouts?

    So as not to inconvenience other guests we do not.

  • Are you hiring?

    We are always looking for talented individuals to join our team. Please send your resume to jobs@pauliegee.com for consideration.
