Jamaican & American Cuisine

At Johnny's Classic Quizine, our menu offers a variety of American, Caribbean and Jamaican food, made fresh every single day. Some of our favorites include steamed fish, Rasta Pasta, curry, shrimp, jerk shrimp, jerk chicken, BBQ and fried chicken, oxtail, stir-fried vegetables, fried dumplings, Ackee, fried Red Snapper, and much, much more. To explore these wonderful selections in a fine family restaurant setting, please contact us or stop by Johnny's Classic Quizine, today.
