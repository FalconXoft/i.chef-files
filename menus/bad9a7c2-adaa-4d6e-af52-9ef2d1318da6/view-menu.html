Welcome to Sfoglia

Nestled on an unassuming stretch of the Upper East Side, Sfoglia is a rustic trattoria with exposed brick walls in a chandelier-lit dining room. Farmhouse tables warmed with votives and fresh flowers makes for a cozy, romantic yet relaxed atmosphere. Inside this charming setting, you will find passionate hospitality and a creative, ever changing seasonal Italian menu inspired by local produce. True to the name Sfoglia which means “to thumb through” in Italian and refers to a variety of handmade fresh egg pasta dough, we set out to accomplish just that throughout our whole journey. Deeply rooted in our community for the past 17 years, Sfoglia will continue to inspire and create memorable moments with those walking through our doors.

Private Dining

Sfoglia can host an array of different private events, depending on your needs. Both luncheon and dinner events are accommodated upon availability.All events and groups of 10+ guests will be required to set up a family-style event from our set menu tiers, which will be provided upon request.

For small groups of up to 12 guests maximum: Tucked away above Sfoglias main dining floor, our private dining room is the perfect space to accommodate parties in this gorgeous intimate room in complete privacy. The private dining room is designed by neighborhood store Blue Tree, and has hosted many small weddings, celebrations, celebrities and families.


Our two main dining rooms are also available for private dining events, one seating up to 30 and the other up to 40 guests, respectively. Alternatively, the full restaurant could be booked for a complete private event for groups ranging from:

20 – 60 guests for a formal sit down dinner
20 – 75 guests for an informal buffet style gathering
