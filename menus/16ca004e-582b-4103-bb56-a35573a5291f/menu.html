About

Roti on the Run

Welcome to Roti on the Run, your go-to spot for authentic Caribbean flavors nestled in the heart of Brooklyn at 3140 Fulton St. This enchanting eatery specializes in a variety of West Indian and Caribbean dishes that will transport your tastebuds straight to the sun-kissed islands.

At Roti on the Run, customization is key. Patrons can build their own plates, selecting from an array of vegetable options such as the delightful 3 Veggie Meal, featuring your choice of three vibrant vegetables for just $12. For those craving protein, the menu boasts mouthwatering options like Curry Goat and Grilled Jerk Chicken, both served with the perfect accompaniments of channa and potatoes. You can experience these flavors for $14.50 to $16.75, proving that good taste comes at a fair price.

Customer reviews rave about the excellent service and fresh, flavorful food. Vance Green highlighted the ability to customize his meal, enjoying rice and peas paired with ackee and salt fish. Meghan Snell spoke highly of the friendly staff, who go the extra mile to ensure satisfaction, while Muslimatic University praised the restaurant for capturing the essence of Caribbean cuisine.

Not to be missed, Roti on the Run also offers delectable desserts. Indulge your sweet tooth with a slice of creamy cheesecake or dive into a tender homemade Tres Leches cake for only $3.00. Each bite is a delightful cap to an already fantastic meal.

Whether you're grabbing a quick lunch or enjoying a leisurely dinner, Roti on the Run promises an unforgettable culinary escape. With an array of dishes that cater to diverse tastes, including halal options, this gem makes it easy to savor the richness of Caribbean cuisine. So, the next time you find yourself near Brooklyn, don't miss the chance to visit Roti on the Run. Your taste buds will thank you!
