Welcome to Gourmet Express, a culinary haven located in Pelham, NY, known for its extraordinary menu that appeals to all palates. This gem features a delightful fusion of healthy options, including fresh salads, vibrant vegetarian and vegan meals, and an impressive variety of proteins, ensuring everyone finds something to savor.

  • Vegan Delights: Notable highlights include a tempting vegan carrot cake and a newly introduced vegetarian/vegan section.
  • Sea Bass Sensation: Dive into the exceptional Lemon Sea Bass, offered in multiple delicious preparations.
  • Fresh Juices & Salads: Accompany your meal with their refreshing juices that uplift the dining experience.
  • Quality Assurance: Enjoy meals made with love and crafted from fresh ingredients, ensuring maximum flavor and satisfaction.

Gourmet Express not only satisfies cravings but also creates a warm atmosphere where health meets indulgence. With its buffet-style service, the quality and variety reflect a dedication to excellence. Don't miss out on discovering your new favorite dish!
