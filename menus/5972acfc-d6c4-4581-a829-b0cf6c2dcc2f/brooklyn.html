Burger Village is a popular restaurant located in park Slope at 222 7th Ave Brooklyn, NY 11215. The restaurant is known for its delicious and high-quality organic grass-fed burgers made with fresh, all-natural ingredients. Burger Village is committed to offering customers a healthier alternative to traditional fast food, with a menu featuring organic, grass-fed, antibiotic-free beef and plant-based and vegan burger options.

In addition to our signature burgers, Burger Village also offers a variety of other menu items, including salads, sandwiches, fresh hand-cut French fries, and shakes. Customers can choose from a range of toppings and sauces to customize their meal to their liking or make it simple and delicious by picking up the signature pairs.

Whether you’re in the mood for a classic burger or something a little more exotic, Burger Village has something for everyone. With its high-quality ingredients and commitment to sustainability, Burger Village has become a go-to destination for burger lovers in Park slope and beyond.

Burger Village is a great spot for foodies who enjoy a good drink. In addition to the delicious and healthy food options, the restaurant offers a wide variety of signature cocktails, fine draft beers, local beers, and organic wines to complement your meal.

The signature cocktails at Burger Village are made using only the freshest ingredients and are carefully crafted to offer a unique and flavorful experience. You can choose from a range of options, including classic cocktails, like the Manhattan or Old Fashioned, or try one of their original creations, like the Spicy Mango Margarita or the Cucumber Cooler.

For beer enthusiasts, Burger Village offers a selection of fine draft beers and local beers from some of the best local breweries. Beer is available for every taste preference, from pale ales to Ipa’s.
