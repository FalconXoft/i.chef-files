About

Our chefs specialize in authentic and delicious food. The staff at Sumi Sushi is devoted to serving customers mouthwatering dishes that feature the highest quality ingredients available. Sushi Regular, Sashimi Regular and Sushi Deluxe are all prepared quickly and delivered to you right away.


Sumi Sushi uses only the freshest ingredients around, ensuring that every dish is of the highest possible quality. Not sure what you're in the mood for? Try one of our specialties including Sashimi Deluxe , Sushi Sashimi Combo, Chirashi, Sashimi Don, and more.
