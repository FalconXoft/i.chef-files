Welcome

Mediterranean food with a twist, serving in the heart of Brooklyn's Fort Greene.

Miss Ada is an inviting neighborhood restaurant in the heart of Brooklyns vibrant Fort Greene. Led by Israeli-born Chef/Owner Tomer Blechman, Miss Ada uses local and seasonal ingredients to create a unique twist on modern Mediterranean cuisine. In the warmer months, fresh herbs from the sun-drenched backyard garden add an extra element of flavor to the dishes and cocktails.

 The name, MISS ADA, originates from the Hebrew word for restaurant, MISADA. The root of the word, SEUDA, represents coming together to the dinner table with family and friends to relax and discuss the day together.

Book your Miss Ada reservation on Resy

DIning at Miss Ada

Is the sidewalk caf back? Yes! Our sidewalk caf has returned and is available for walk-ins and reservations! Most tables (including all reservable tables) are covered and protected in case of rain. Some walk-in tables are weather dependent.

Do you take reservations? We take reservations for indoor and outdoor dining on Resy. Reservations become available 14 days in advance at 9 am for groups of 2 to 6 people. The majority of our tables are reservable, and most of our walk-in space is at the bar.

Can you accommodate larger parties? Absolutely! We love to host parties of 7 to 20 guests with a set menu for the table. We book large parties up to 2 months in advance. Want to hear more? Shoot us an email at info@missadanyc.com.

How should we book a reservation with kids? We LOVE having kids dine at Miss Ada! In order to accommodate children we need the guest count to include all babies and children, even if they are not dining. For example, 3 adults, 2 kids and a baby would be a party of 6. We are not able to accommodate strollers in the dining area but can provide stroller parking.

What is outdoor dining like in the summer? The back patio is fully covered and protected from the rain. In case of severe rain we have waterproof siding to ensure the dining area is protected. The back patio is shaded and has fans for days that are hotter and humid. It is reflective of the outdoor temperature.

What if we are running a bit late? We are always able to hold tables for 15 minutes in case of lateness, but after that may give away spots to guests on our waitlist. If you are running late please text us at 929-271-1189. Even with advanced notice we are only able to hold reservations for 30 minutes. If you expect to be later than 30 minutes well be able to add you to the waitlist upon arrival.

Can we come without a reservation? We hold our bar seats and sidewalk caf tables for walk-ins. We arent able to accommodate all walk-ins but we try our best!

Do you run a waitlist? Absolutely! Once our walk-in space is full we begin a waitlist and will text when something opens up. The waitlist is for bar seats and the sidewalk caf but can sometimes include indoor or outdoor tables if someone cancels.

Can I join the waitlist for a specific dining area? Generally our waitlist is for the entire restaurant so we arent able to take on dining locations but well try our best!

Can I bring a dog to dinner? We welcome service dogs in all areas of our restaurant. Additionally pet dogs are welcome to dine on the sidewalk caf.

We look forward to seeing you!

Private events

 

We are thrilled to welcome larger parties for a customizable dining experience on our back patio. We offer these events year round, and work to create a selection of food, beverage and music perfect for you and your group! Our back patio is protected against rain and enclosed in the winter months to allow for year round celebrations. Wed love to help you plan the perfect rehearsal dinner, graduation, birthday party, baby shower, or work dinner.

Private Events in our Back Patio

Our back patio buyouts are offered year-round for parties of 20 to 35. We offer both cocktail party and seated events.

The space offers private dining, with full use of the back patio.

Customize a menu of family style mezze, hummus, appetizers and entrees along with an open bar of Miss Ada cocktails, wine and beer!

We love to work with you on customizations and ensuring the event has any special moments you want to include! That may mean specific music, organizing a toast, a cake, or speeches.

Pricing is based on a food and beverage minimum based on the type of event (weekday, weekend brunch).

For larger groups we offer full restaurant buyouts for parties of 30 to 70.

Private Events at Thea

Looking to host a fully private event indoors for a smaller group?

We are thrilled to offer customizable private parties at Thea, our sister space on Greene Ave.  We offer seated dinners and cocktail parties for groups of 15 to 30 guests. Enjoy a menu of Miss Adas mezze, skewers, and family style entrees with cocktails, beer and natural wine. 

Our team will work with you to develop the menu, drink offerings, and style of service. We can accommodate allergies and restrictions, and customize the event for birthdays, anniversaries, office parties and work events. 

The menus have room for customization to fit your needs! The space is available 7 days a week, for parties from 6:30 pm to 9:30 pm. There is no space fee, and final pricing is based on the menu cost per person plus tax and tip.
