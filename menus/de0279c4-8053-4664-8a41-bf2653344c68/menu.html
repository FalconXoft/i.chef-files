Menus

Barito Tacos & Cocktails, in downtown Port Jefferson, New York, is serving up authentic Mexican cuisine with a modern twist. Chef Steven Scalesse & Chef Kevin Breeden stay committed to using the freshest ingredients in a menu featuring House Made Guacamole with home made tortillas made fresh daily. Try the mouth watering tacos, tostadas, tortas, burritos, pupusas, enchiladas, quesadillas, & many other authentic Mexican dishes.
