Welcome to East Met West

East Met West on Metropolitan Avenue in Brooklyn offers a mix of Chinese and American cuisine with a focus on fast delivery and takeout options. Customers rave about their Wonton Soup, Egg Roll, and Lo Mein, praising the perfect noodle to meat ratio and generous portions. The menu includes many vegetarian options and dishes like beef with mushrooms and hot and sour soup that are packed with flavor. While some reviewers note inconsistencies in food quality, overall, East Met West is a popular spot for those seeking quick, tasty Chinese-American dishes.

Welcome to East Met West, a hidden gem located on Metropolitan Avenue in Brooklyn, NY. This Chinese restaurant offers a unique fusion of East and West flavors, creating a delightful dining experience for all. With options for delivery, takeout, and reservations, East Met West caters to your dining preferences with ease.

What sets East Met West apart is its extensive menu featuring many vegetarian options and delicious dishes that cater to various palates. From the savory beef with mushrooms to the flavorful hot and sour soup, each dish is crafted with care and attention to detail.

Customers rave about the speedy delivery service and generous portions, making it a go-to spot for those craving traditional Chinese-American cuisine. Whether you're in the mood for wonton soup, egg rolls, or lo mein, East Met West is sure to satisfy your cravings.

With a cozy, divey atmosphere and moderate noise level, East Met West is the perfect spot for a casual dinner or a quick takeout meal. Indulge in their tasty offerings while enjoying outdoor seating or take advantage of their catering services for your next gathering.

Don't miss out on the chance to try the dishes that customers cannot stop raving about, such as the chicken with broccoli, lo mein, boneless spare ribs, wonton soup, and crab rangoons. Each bite is a burst of authentic flavors that will leave you craving more.

So, whether you're a loyal customer or a first-time visitor, East Met West is guaranteed to leave a lasting impression on your taste buds. Experience the fusion of East and West flavors at this beloved Brooklyn restaurant.
