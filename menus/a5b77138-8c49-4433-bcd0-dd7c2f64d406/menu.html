About Take Care

A New Casual Neighborhood Bistro Thats Serious About Making Your Day

IGC Hospitality, known for Refinery Rooftop, Parker & Quinn, and the Rockaway Hotel, announces Take Care, a modern American bistro in the Shelburne Sonesta New York Hotel that promises to take care of its guests from morning until night. The theme is about serving fresh, consistent, and well-prepared food with thoughtful menu items that highlight seasonal preparations with health and wellness in mind.

The menu, by Chef Bryan Noury, boasts fresh and seasonal dishes that showcase the best of cafe cooking like Crispy Artichokes, a classic Chopped Salad, and a special house burger, along with a local Grilled Berkshire Pork Chop, Broiled Salmon, and Prime Bavette Steak, all with extensive menu items for those who are gluten-free, vegetarian, and vegan. The beverage program focuses on cocktails with fresh fruit & vegetables, low and no abv cocktails, and freshly pressed juice mocktails, along with organic wines and esoteric varietals from different regions.

IGC Hospitality is committed to creating vibrant and unparalleled hospitality destinations with venues in NYC (Manhattan and Rockaway Beach, Queens) and Orlando, FL. Over the past decade, IGC Hospitality has reinvented itself with each new location, giving a unique personality to its diverse venues. The Shelburne Sonesta Hotel, a boutique hotel on Lexington Avenue, offers spacious rooms and suites with kitchens and terraces overlooking the Empire State Building. This partnership and addition of Take Care is designed to serve not only the hotel guests but also the dynamic locals of Murray Hill in Manhattan.
