About

Our chefs specialize in authentic and delicious food. The staff at Falafel On Broadway is devoted to serving customers mouthwatering dishes that feature the highest quality ingredients available. Shish Tawook Platter, Kufta Kebob Platter and Gyro Platter are all prepared quickly and delivered to you right away.


Falafel On Broadway uses only the freshest ingredients around, ensuring that every dish is of the highest possible quality. Not sure what you're in the mood for? Try one of our specialties including Shish Kebob Platter, 2-Piece Lamb Chops Platter, Half BBQ Chicken Platter, Hummus and Babagnush, and more.
