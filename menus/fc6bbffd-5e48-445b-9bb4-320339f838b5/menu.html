About

THE LEXINGTON SOCIAL is located in the up & coming "SPANISH HARLEM", the heart of East Harlem, in 1634 LEXINGTON AVEnue.  The Lexington Social is an intimate friendly neighborhood TAPAS WINE BAR offering MEDITERRANEAN-STYLE cuisine from ITALY & SPAIN, specialty HAND-CRAFTED COCKTAILS & a short list of BEERS. You are sure to enjoy the delicious Mediterranean style Tapas, extensive wine list, 8 tap beers (3 which of are seasonal), seasonal cocktails & SANGRIA all which will leave your taste buds wanting more. The decor feels vintage romantic while the atmosphere is both upbeat & intimately social. Its easy to meet new people, relax with a group of friends while enjoying a signature cocktail. Host your next party with us or ask about our CATERING. We plan events to suit your needs.
