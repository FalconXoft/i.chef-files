A SoHo original since 2003.
Rooted in Aegean recipes, antique charm, and a love for making people feel at home.

From hand-picked chandeliers to dishes passed down through generations, every detail invites you to stay a little longer.

Introducing you to a place that feels discovered, not designed.

Set among SoHos cast-iron corners, Antique Garage blends the flavors of the Mediterranean with the mood of old New Yorkdim lighting, vintage charm, and jazz in the air. It's not just a dinner spot, its a feeling: lived-in, candlelit, and quietly unforgettable. A downtown original, made for nights that dont follow a script.

Our Story

Antique Garage SoHo began in 2003, when chef and founder Utku Cinel reimagined a forgotten mechanics garage as a place for food, music, and memory. The menu draws from his Aegean rootsfamily recipes, seasonal ingredients, and a deep respect for where each dish comes from. What we serve is personal: plates meant to be shared, flavors that linger, and a space where every detail has a story.
