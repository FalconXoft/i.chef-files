About Niko's SouvlakiGo Greek!

The best kalamaki in town from the finest Greek meats.

We pride ourselves on our Greek kalamaki (skewers), meats, gyro, salads and much much more. We season our meats with only the best Greek herbs and slow roast them to perfection.

The ambiance of the is top notch, with bright light, wall murals, and a killer outdoor patio heart of the city. Enjoy our food inside or on the go with friends, family or co-works.
