About

Emporium Brazil offers a true taste of Brazil in the heart of Midtown NYC. Just steps from Times Square, our restaurant serves authentic Brazilian dishes like feijoada, picanha, moqueca, and po de queijo in a warm, inviting atmosphere. Enjoy churrasco-style meats, fresh caipirinhas, and tropical sides for lunch, dinner, or private events. Whether youre Brazilian or simply a fan of bold flavors, Emporium Brazil is your go-to destination for unforgettable cuisine.
