About us

Established in 1934, East Side Restaurant is a New Britain institution serving up delicious German cuisine in a warm and inviting atmosphere. Our menu features classic German dishes made with fresh ingredients, as well as a wide selection of German beers. Whether you’re looking for a hearty meal or a place to celebrate with friends and family, East Side Restaurant is the perfect spot.

We are proud to be a family-owned and operated business, and we look forward to serving you soon!

HOST YOUR NEXT EVENT IN OUR MUNICH ROOM!

The Munich Room at East Side Restaurant is a vibrant and lively space that captures the essence of a traditional German beer hall. With its warm wood paneling, colorful murals, and long communal tables, the room exudes a festive atmosphere that’s perfect for gatherings of friends and family.
