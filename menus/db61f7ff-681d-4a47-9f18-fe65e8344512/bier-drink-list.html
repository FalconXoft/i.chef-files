Biers & Drinks

Largest German Bier Selection in CT

Prost!

View our selection of biers below

East Side Restaurant boasts a delightful selection of German Biers, carefully chosen to represent the rich brewing traditions of Deutschland. From crisp and refreshing Pilsners to malty and satisfying Dunkels, and perhaps even a Weizenbier with its characteristic fruity notes, there’s a bier to complement every dish and palate. Prost!

GERMAN DRAFT BIERS

Weihenstephaner Hefeweizen

ABV 5.4%

A classic Hefeweizen brewed by the world’s oldest brewery, with notes of banana, clove, and malt sweetness, finished with a refreshing crispness. 

WEIHENSTEPHANER ORIGINAL LAGER

ABV 5.1%

The long maturation period provides the beer with a strong character that pairs well with hearty dishes but also with salads, poultry and stews.

Weihenstephaner KellerBier

ABV 5.6%

A traditional unfiltered lager, offering a smooth, full-bodied flavor with earthy malt sweetness and a refreshing, slightly hoppy finish.

WARSTEINER PILSNER

ABV 4.8%

A refreshing, pale golden pilsner with a clean taste, balanced by hints of barley malt, mild yeast tones, and subtle hop bitterness. 

WARSTEINER Dunkel

ABV 4.9%

A smooth, dark beer with rich malt flavors of caramel and toffee, balanced by subtle hop bitterness for a full-bodied yet smooth finish.

Spaten Oktoberfest

ABV 5.9%

A rich, amber Märzen with a malty sweetness and a smooth, crisp finish, perfect for celebrating the season. 

East Rock Brewing Co. Lager

ABV 5%

Brewed locally in New Haven, CT, East Rock Lager is a crisp, clean lager with a smooth malt profile and balanced hop bitterness, delivering a refreshing, easy-drinking finish. 

Premium Draft Biers

Weihenstephaner Hefeweissbier Dunkel

ABV 5.3%

A rich, dark Hefeweizen with notes of caramel, toasted malt, subtle banana, and clove, balancedby a smooth, slightly sweet finish.

Ayinger Pilsner

ABV 5.3%

A crisp and refreshing pilsner with a clean malt backbone and a perfect balance of hop bitterness, offering a smooth, dry finish. 

Ayinger Celebrator Doppelbock

ABV 6.7%

A rich, full-bodied doppelbock with deep malt flavors of caramel, toffee, and a hint of dark fruit, balanced by a smooth, warming finish.

Konig Ludwig Weiss Bier

ABV 4.9%

A smooth, well-balanced beer with malty sweetness, subtle hops, and a crisp finish. Brewed with traditional Bavarian craftsmanship.
