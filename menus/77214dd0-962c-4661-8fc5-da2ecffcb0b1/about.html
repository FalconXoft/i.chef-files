Welcome To Quechua Nostra Peruvian Food

Quechua Nostra Peruvian Food is a hidden gem in Spanish Harlem, offering a wide array of authentic Peruvian dishes. Located at 1634 Lexington Ave, this casual yet charming spot boasts a menu filled with flavorful appetizers like Salchipapas and Anticucho, soul-warming soups such as Chupe de Camaron, and refreshing salads like the Avocado Salad. Customer favorites like Lomo Saltado and Pollo Saltado are must-try dishes that never disappoint. The friendly staff and cozy atmosphere make this place a perfect spot for a delicious lunch or dinner experience. Highly recommended for Peruvian food enthusiasts seeking a taste of traditional flavors.

About

Quechua Nostra Peruvian Food

Quechua Nostra Peruvian Food, located in Spanish Harlem, is a hidden gem serving up authentic Peruvian cuisine. With a diverse menu offering options for vegetarians and meat-lovers alike, this restaurant is a must-visit for anyone looking to explore the flavors of Peru.

From their flavorful Salchipapas to their comforting Peruvian soups like the Sustancia de Carne, Quechua Nostra delivers on traditional dishes with a modern twist. The Lomo Saltado, a customer favorite, is a must-try for its juicy skirt steak and bold flavors that will leave you craving more.

The casual yet welcoming atmosphere at Quechua Nostra makes it the perfect spot for a lunch date or a group outing. The attentive staff and top-notch service only add to the overall dining experience, making this restaurant a standout in the neighborhood.

Whether you're a Peruvian food enthusiast or a first-time visitor, Quechua Nostra Peruvian Food is sure to impress with its delicious dishes and friendly ambiance. Stop by for a taste of Peru right in the heart of New York City.
