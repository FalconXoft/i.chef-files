Welcome to Lava.

Join us as we infuse traditional Spanish cuisine with an Italian-inspired menu featuring courses everyone will enjoy.  Our star chef, Chef Mariano prepares exquisite dishes with his many years of experience around the world. 

Lava, not only has the most impeccable Spanish cuisine but consists of unbeatable service, a trendy atmosphere, and a new evolutionary nightlife style in Staten Island. From a choice of sitting at the bar, dinner table, or in the lounge, Lava offers great food and drinks for lunch or dinner weekly, enjoying the DJ from Thursdays to Saturday nights, or having the option of enjoying a music-filled brunch on Sunday.
