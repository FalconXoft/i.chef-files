Praised by reviewers as “must try” and “a welcome addition” to Manhattan’s vast pizza scene, Emmy Squared Pizza introduced our one-of-a-kind fare to the East Village in July 2018. Founded on an unconditional love of well-made food, Emmy Squared combined a New York style “grandma” pie with a Detroit style pizza, and then topped it off with an added dose of culinary creativity. But we didn’t stop at our pizza, Emmy’s Le Big Matt Burger has been recognized as one of "The Best New Burgers in NYC" (Gothamist) and one of the "20 Best Burgers in NYC" (Infatuation). Besides award-winning pizzas and burgers, you’ll find delectable appetizers, sandwiches, and salads on our extensive menu. We look forward to welcoming you to Emmy Squared East Village and sharing our unique take on American “comfort food.”
