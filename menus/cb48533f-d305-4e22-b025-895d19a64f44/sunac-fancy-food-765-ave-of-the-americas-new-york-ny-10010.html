Sunac Fancy Food

Welcome to Sunac Fancy Food

Sunac Fancy Food in New York City's Chelsea neighborhood offers a diverse range of fresh and flavorful deli options for breakfast and lunch. With a focus on quality and convenience, they provide a self-serve buffet, grab-and-go items, and made-to-order dishes like rare roast beef sandwiches, breakfast sandwiches, and chicken pad Thai. Customers praise the wide selection of vegetarian options, freshly cut fruit, and endless snack aisles. Whether you're craving a hearty sandwich, a healthy smoothie, or a quick bite to go, Sunac Fancy Food delivers on taste, value, and friendly service in a clean and organized setting.

As a food reviewer of Restaurant World, I am excited to introduce you to Sunac Fancy Food, a delightful deli specializing in breakfast and brunch options. Located at 765 Ave Of The Americas in New York, NY, Sunac Fancy Food offers a range of amenities including delivery, takeout, catering services, and even accepts cryptocurrency as payment.

Customers rave about the fresh and flavorful lunch options, with plenty of vegetarian choices available. The buffet offerings change regularly, ensuring that diners always have something new to try. The service is top-notch, and the establishment is known for its cleanliness and organization.

One customer praised the value and quality of the food, mentioning favorites like the Cuban sandwich and breakfast sandwich. Another highlighted the fruit options, beverages, and snack aisles, making it a great spot for a quick bite to go.

Personal favorites include the rare roast beef sandwich with roasted red peppers, horseradish, cheddar, and lettuce on a tomato basil roll. The breakfast sandwich with bacon, egg, cheese, and avocado was also a hit, as well as the delicious chicken pad Thai for lunch.

Although some find the prices a bit steep, the variety and quality of the offerings make Sunac Fancy Food worth a visit. With options ranging from specialty items to prepared foods, there is something for everyone's taste. Whether you're in the mood for a salad, seafood, or a hearty meal, Sunac Fancy Food has you covered.

Overall, Sunac Fancy Food stands out for its diverse menu, friendly service, and convenient location. Whether you're grabbing a quick meal on the go or dining in, this deli has something for every discerning foodie.
