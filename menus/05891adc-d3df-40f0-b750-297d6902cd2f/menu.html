PlantMade By Double Zero & Bar verde

PlantMade brings a healthy perspective to the world's most popular food. which showcases a vibrant selection of Latin and Mediterranean favorites, alongside inspired new creations.

PlantMade is known for its warm, comfortable ambiance, communal seating and extensive group of regulars who socialize along with dining. Since its opening, PlantMade has garnered an enormous amount of support and praise for its innovation, creativity and use of high quality, seasonal ingredients. This includes recognition from The Michelin Guide as a Bib Gourmand Restaurant for its 5 time this past year.

Marlon Tran

Bar Verde was exceptional with their twist on Mexican cuisine with everything being vegan and gluten free! With all the dishes we ordered, we couldn’t tell it was just all vegetables. Let’s start with the guacamole and it was simply fresh 👌🏽 The drinks we ordered were massive and worth the NY prices. Spicy cauliflower enchiladas were phenomenal along with the Maitake Enmolada, which was purely just mushrooms that had the similar Umami flavor and good for my taste buds. Churros were a game changer knowing it was gluten free. The three stars rating is really a 3.8 stars because the space is small not spacious, service was good but our original waiter vanished after giving out entrees. Also we weren’t aware everything was already premade and couldn’t really substitute something out. Other than that we will come back if craving vegan Mexican food.

Our Mission

The journey of vegan food has been nothing short of revolutionary. From humble beginnings with basic plant-based substitutes to today's sophisticated alternatives, the evolution reflects a growing global consciousness towards ethical, sustainable eating habits. We’re hoping to help make a global difference by spreading plant-based options to as many people as possible.
