Aahar Menu (Updated December 2025)

Welcome to Aahar, a vibrant Indian restaurant located in the heart of New York at 60 Pearl St. This café is a delightful sanctuary for those seeking an authentic taste of India, with a menu that encapsulates the diverse flavors of Indian cuisine.

Aahar offers a mouthwatering selection of non-vegetarian entrées such as the Shrimp Vindaloo—a fiery curry bursting with the tang of cider vinegar and red chili, and the Tandoori Salmon Tikka, perfectly marinated for a tender finish. For those who prefer vegetarian options, the Paneer Tikka and golden Samosas are not to be missed.

Not only is the food consistently praised by patrons, who describe it as "fresh and filled with flavor," but the warmth of the staff enhances the overall experience. Customers have highlighted the attentive service, with staff members ensuring your dining experience is enjoyable and satisfying.

Whether you’re indulging in a fixed-price lunch special or savoring a leisurely dinner, Aahar’s blend of superb flavors and inviting atmosphere makes it a must-visit spot for any food lover. Make sure to include Aahar in your NYC culinary adventures—it's an experience worth savoring!
