A staple of the Historic South Street Seaport, Dorlans Tavern is where the neighborhood comes together for refreshing drinks and delicious food. A homegrown bar that welcomes locals, visiting tourists, and the FiDi business community, we embody the eclectic essence of New York City - and have a great time in the process. Come for a meal, a drink, or a private celebration in our beautiful event space. And if you're a newcomer to Dorlans Tavern or visiting the Seaport for the first time, dont worry - well make you feel right at home.
