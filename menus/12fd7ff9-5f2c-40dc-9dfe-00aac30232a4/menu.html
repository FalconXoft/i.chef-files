Sarabeth's (Upper West Side)

Nestled in the cultural heart of the Upper West Side, Sarabeth's has been a beloved culinary sanctuary for over four decades. Known for our homestyle cooking, welcoming atmosphere, and attentive service, we've won the hearts of generations. Surrounded by landmarks like the American Museum of Natural History, Beacon Theater, and Children's Museum of Manhattan. Just a stroll from Central Park and Riverside Park, Sarabeth's Upper West Side invites locals and visitors to enjoy an unforgettable culinary journey amid this vibrant New York neighborhood.
