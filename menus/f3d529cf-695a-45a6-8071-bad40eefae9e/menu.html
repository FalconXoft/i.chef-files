About

FROM A FAMILY CAFE AT THE TURN OF THE CENTURY IN LYON, FRANCE, TO THE HEART OF MANHATTAN’S UPPER EAST SIDE TODAY, CAFE BOULUD IS CHEF DANIEL BOULUD’S CONTINUAL EXPLORATION OF HIS MOST FUNDAMENTAL CULINARY INSPIRATIONS AND A HIGHLY PERSONAL, SOULFUL EXPRESSION OF HIS CRAFT.

LEARN ABOUT CAFE BOULUD

Our Menu

OUR FOUR ESSENTIAL MUSES REMAIN CONSTANT: LA TRADITION (FRENCH CLASSICS), LA SAISON (SEASONAL SPECIALITIES OF THE MARKET), LE POTAGER (THE VEGETABLE GARDEN), LE VOYAGE (EXOTIC FLAVORS FROM THE WORLD’S GREAT CUISINES). YET WITH EACH SEASON, NEW VARIATIONS APPEAR, HARMONIZING TRADITION AND DEEPLY-ROOTED FRENCH CLASSICISM WITH CULINARY INNOVATION AND AN INTREPID, GLOBALLY-INSPIRED SENSIBILITY.

EXECUTIVE CHEF ROMAIN PAUMIER AND EXECUTIVE PASTRY CHEF KATALINA DIAZ JOIN WITH CHEF DANIEL BOULUD TO LEAD GUESTS ON A JOURNEY OF CULINARY DISCOVERY, WITH ELEMENTS OF BOTH FAMILIARITY AND NOVELTY, GROUNDED IN CLASSIC TECHNIQUE AND EXCEPTIONAL SEASONAL INGREDIENTS.
