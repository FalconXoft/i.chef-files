Seba-Seba Coffee Shop

Introducing Seba-Seba Coffee Shop - A Colombian Delight in Jackson Heights

Located at 7928 37th Ave in Jackson Heights, NY, Seba-Seba Coffee Shop is a hidden gem offering an array of traditional Colombian dishes that will transport your taste buds to South America. This cozy coffee shop not only boasts a welcoming atmosphere but also a menu that caters to all your cravings.

From hearty breakfast options like the Seba Seba Breakfast Rice and beans with any style eggs, corn cake, and your choice of meat, to mouthwatering appetizers like fried shrimp and calamari, Seba-Seba Coffee Shop has something for everyone. The gourmet corn cakes, or arepas, are a must-try, with tantalizing options like Seba Seba Corn cake topped with guacamole, beans creole sauce, sausage, and pork skin.

But what sets Seba-Seba Coffee Shop apart from other Colombian restaurants in the area? According to loyal customers like Miguel C., it's the consistency in both taste and service. The staff at Seba-Seba Coffee Shop goes above and beyond to ensure a seamless dining experience, with quick service and a focus on customer satisfaction.

Maria R. raves about the daily specials and the friendly service, while Yeimi D. reminisces about the traditional Colombian flavors that remind her of home. And even though Crystal L. had a minor issue with her order, she still appreciates the excellent bakery offerings at Seba-Seba Coffee Shop.

Next time you find yourself in Jackson Heights craving authentic Colombian cuisine, make sure to stop by Seba-Seba Coffee Shop. Whether you're in the mood for a comforting bowl of soup or a savory arepa, this charming coffee shop has it all. And don't forget to grab some baked goods or a cup of their delicious coffee to-go - you won't be disappointed!

Our Guestbook

“ So good, so close, so consistent. Jackson Heights is no stranger to Colombian restaurants and cafes; they are all over the place. There's a few things that set Seba Seba away from the others though. I love their location. It's in the heart of Jackson Heights and right on a corner. You're basically always passing by it which makes it great to pop in quickly while out and about. It's consistent. Some Colombian places will be good today and not so great tomorrow. After having been here dozens of times, I can say that I always know what I'm getting. The soup is gonna be good, the bunuelos are going to be good, the steak is going to be good. Consistency is huge to setting up a familiar experience for customers. The customer service is also exceptional. Seba Seba manages to move things along way faster that other Colombian spots. If I wanna pop in quickly for an empanada to go, I'm likely not going to be enthusiastic about waiting a long time. Seba Seba really does a great job focusing on getting people what they want quickly, which makes their consistency even more impressive. If I had one quibble, it's just that their pan de bono is way drier than other places. But that's just not that big a deal. I'm going to keep going to Seba Seba for all my Colombian food desires. They're great. PS: If you're sick, be sure to pick up a large soup to go. It always makes me feel better. ”

- Miguel C.
