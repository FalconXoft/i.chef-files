About us

A Taste of Italy in the Heart of the Bronx

Michaelangelo's Restaurant is one of Arthur Avenue’s hotspots and it's a must-see place. We are dedicated to providing authentic Italian-American food in the atmosphere you would experience in Italy. Our coal-fired brick oven, dating back to the early 1900 's, has been restored and is one of New York's largest brick ovens. The flavors it creates are second to none. Our chefs use only the freshest ingredients and have created a classic menu composed of pizza, pasta, seafood and meats. Open year round, our outdoor patio is a great place to plan an event or to enjoy an intimate dinner with a loved one.
