Frequently Asked Questions

What type of food do you serve?

We specialize in Kofta with popular dishes like Buffalo Chicken Wrap, Vegan Meatball Sub, Hummus Falafel Bowl Gf.

What are your most popular dishes?

Our customers love our Hummus Falafel Bowl Gf, Buffalo Chicken Wrap, Vegan Meatball Sub, Hummus Falafel Bowl Gf.

Do you service East Village?

Yes, we service East Village, Alphabet City, Stuyvesant Town, Noho and surrounding areas. Vegan Grill is conveniently located to serve the entire region.

How do you prepare your food?

We prepare our dishes using traditional techniques including Baked, Fried, Grilled. Our Kofta specialties are carefully Sauted to ensure the best flavors.

Do you offer catering services?

Not at this time.

What are your hours of operation?

We're open Tuesday through Sunday from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM. We're closed on Mondays for kitchen prep and staff training. Holiday hours may vary - please call ahead to confirm.
