FIND US !

At the Edge of Little Italy , Chinatown, and the Ever-iconic Broadway, Landmark Coffee Shop is exactly what it sounds like — a true landmark serving breakfast that slaps and coffee that hits, with a front-row seat to the hum of our city.

Tucked onto one of the quieter streets of the city that never sleeps - we’re here every day — same corner, same crew, same mission:

Fuel your day, feed your soul, and maybe remember your order next time.

Heading down Lafayette from Washington Square Park ?
Coming up from the Brooklyn Bridge past the old courthouses?

You're just a few blocks out.

And when that warm, buttery smell of pancakes hits you on the street — that’s us.
