Williamsburg

Located in the heart of Williamsburg, our Brooklyn shop is conveniently situated half a block from the Bedford Avenue L Train stop. Known for its acclaimed dining, waterfront parks, and wide range of local & vintage shops, Williamsburg is one of Brooklyn's most bustling neighborhoods. After dinner or a walk in the area, stop in to 16 Handles and choose from 16 flavors of soft serve, including frozen yogurt, ice cream, and vegan optionsalways available. Delivery is also available throughout Williamsburg and Greenpoint.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the 16 Handles app?

Downloading the new 16 Handles app is like installing a celebration button on your phone. It's easier than ever to treat yo'self to your favorite flavors, toppings, and other sweets & snacks from 16 Handlesand to get rewarded for it! Scan at checkout in one of our shops, order ahead and skip the wait, or get 16 Handles delivered to your door! Whether you swirl in store or order ahead, you'll be able to earn and redeem loyalty points with your purchase.
With the 16 Handles app, you can:

  • Order ahead for pickup or delivery and get rewarded!
  • Scan your app in store and earn points.
  • Link all your purchases under the same loyalty account to earn FREE FRO-YO and other perks.
  • Get access to exclusive offers and promo days for our rewards members.
  • Easily find flavors and treats that work for your dietary choices or restrictions.
  • Enable notifications to find out about special promotions, events, and featured flavors.
  • Find your nearest 16 Handles shop.

How does 16 Handles delivery work?

We receive your order in the store, then swirl your requested flavors and blend any drinks ordered. Our team then freezes your order in a special blast chilller that is designed to keep your soft serve frozen through the delivery process. Toppings are packed fresh in individual containers and kept on the side to maintain the best quality. After your order leaves our shop, the delivery or courier service gets it to you as quickly as possible. When you receive your order, you get to top your sundae however you choose, just like in our shops.
