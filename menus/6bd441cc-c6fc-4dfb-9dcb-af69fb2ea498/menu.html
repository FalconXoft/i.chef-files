Chi Siamo

About Us

✻

We love people. We love food. We love our neighborhood.

Soleluna is the result of a partnership between Valerio, a restaurateur from Milan, Italy, his wife Michele from Venezuela and Gina and Francesca, also restaurateurs from Cisternino, a small village in the heart of Puglia, Italy.

After years of friendship and working together we decided to make our dream come true, and we opened Soleluna restaurant in Sunnyside, a welcoming and growing neighborhood of Queens, NY.

Our menu is genuine and traditional and our goal is to bring the real flavours of Italy to you.
