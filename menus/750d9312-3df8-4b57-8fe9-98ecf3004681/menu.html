THE BUTCHERS DAUGHTER IS A VIBRANT, PLANT-FORWARD RESTAURANT, CAF, AND JUICE BAR.

 WE ALSO LIKE TO THINK OF OURSELVES AS A VEGETABLE SLAUGHTERHOUSE.

We take pride in our ever-evolving, seasonally-changing menu, which is 100% vegetarian, with numerous vegan and gluten-free options.

Fall slows us down. Its a time to gather, rest, and return to what feels real. The harvest brings roots, gourds, and greens that nourish us through the colder months and remind us why we care so much about fresh, honest food. Were grateful to be part of your table.

Reserve a Table Order Online

We believe that vegetables are rad - and should take center stage in elevating your everyday lifestyle. We create nourishing, seasonal dishes, housemade breads and pastries, and organic cold-pressed juices all served alongside locally-roasted coffee and craft cocktails.
