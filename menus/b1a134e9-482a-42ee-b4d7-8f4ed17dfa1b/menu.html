One Kitchen For Every Craving.

Grass Roots Kitchen brings you the highest quality foods you love

with the ease and speed of quick service, or what we like to call fresh-casual.

Whether you are vegan, gluten-free or an unabashed omnivore,

we serve what you crave morning and night.

Come put some roots down.
