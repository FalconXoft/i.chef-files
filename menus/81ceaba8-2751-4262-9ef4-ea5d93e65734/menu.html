  • Rohit Nayak

    Amazing little hidden gem in Manhattan! Loved the Egyptian food experiences. Their three appetizer platter was fantastic. Try the fava beans- I loved it! Hummus was perfect and the moussaka - delicious with a kick!

  • Eli Rivera

    Amazing place great atmosphere and extremely welcoming owners. Hookah and strawberry mojito was great. You should also try their Egyptian tea. Definitely will be back soon!

  • leila salarpour

    It was a lovely experience. The waitress was very friendly and helpful. Hookah was nice and we had the mezza platter which was tasty. They serve alcoholic and nonalcoholic drinks and the black tea with mint (recommended by the staff ) was very pleasant! Overall, we had an enjoyable time!

  • Mathias Amarillo

    This place was amazing! Me and my partner went and had such a great time! Drinks are very strong and the hookah is absolutely superb!!! This place a must go to if you're in the area, even if you live in Manhattan you should still stop by at least once! 5/5 would recommend.

  • Renita Gidarisingh

    Went for bottomless brunch.... drinks are good and pretty strong. Given to us in a pitcher... food was very delicious. Hookah is good but strong for about an hour... Mina gave us great customer service from when we walked in until we left... place is very comfortable and welcoming.
