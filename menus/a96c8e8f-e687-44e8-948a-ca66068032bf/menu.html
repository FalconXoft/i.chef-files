Come Together.
Eat. Repeat.

Fortuna began with a handful of recipes and a whole lot of heart. When Nonna moved here, she didn’t bring much, but she brought what mattered. Spices wrapped in paper. Stories stitched into every step. And a deep belief that good food can hold a family together. Today, we still cook that way. Kosher. Dairy-free. Always with purpose. Always with love.


Our menu is simple – warm, familiar, and full of flavor, a true Mediterranean and Middle Eastern experience with roots stretching across Africa and beyond. It’s the food you remember. The kind that makes you feel like you’re exactly where you need to be.

 
Cozy, comforting, and full of soul – just the way Nonna would’ve made it.
